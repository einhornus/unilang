ча́яние -> hope, expectation
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: ча́яния
Genitive: ча́яния



Etymology: чаять (čájatʹ) +‎ -яние (-janije)



Definitions (English):
  • expectation, hope, dream, aspiration,
    • паче / сверх ча́яния ― páče / sverx čájanija ― beyond expectation, contrary to expectation, unexpectedly
