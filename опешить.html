опе́шить (8988)


Etymology

From о- (o-) + пе́ший (péšij, “unmounted, pedestrian”) + -ить (-itʹ); original sense "to be thrown off a horse".

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈpʲeʂɨtʲ]

Verb

опе́шить • (opéšitʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial) to be taken aback
