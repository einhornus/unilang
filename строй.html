строй (1727)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stroj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1

From Old East Slavic строи (stroi), from Proto-Slavic *strojь. Cognate to Russian строи́ть (stroitʹ, “to build, construct”).

Noun

строй • (stroj) m inan (genitive стро́я, nominative plural строи, genitive plural стро́ев)

  1. order, array
  2. line
  3. system, order, regime
  4. (music) tune

Etymology 2

Verb

строй • (stroj)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of строи́ть (stróitʹ)
