строй -> system, structure, military formation
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: стро́и
Genitive: стро́я



Etymology: From Old East Slavic стро́и (stroi), from Proto-Slavic *strojь. Cognate to Russian стро́ить (stroitʹ, “to build, construct”).



Definitions (English):
  • order, array
    • line
      • system, order, regime
        • (music) tune
          • second-person singular imperative imperfective of стро́ить (stróitʹ)
