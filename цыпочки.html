цы́почки (5640)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈt͡sɨpət͡ɕkʲɪ]

Etymology 1

Noun

цы́почки • (cýpočki) f inan pl (genitive цы́почек, plural only)

  1. tiptoe

Etymology 2

Noun

цы́почки • (cýpočki) f anim or f anim pl

  1. genitive singular of цы́почка (cýpočka)
  2. nominative plural of цы́почка (cýpočka)
