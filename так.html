так -> so

Etymology: From Old East Slavic тако (tako), такъ (takŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tako (adverb), from Proto-Slavic *takъ (adjective). Cognate with Ukrainian так (tak), тако (tako) Belarusian так (tak) Bulgarian така (taka) Serbo-Croatian тако, так Slovene tаkо, tàk Czech and Polish tak Upper and Lower Sorbian tak Lithuanian tóks, tokià and Latin tālis. Akin to Old Church Slavonic pronoun тъ (tŭ).
