шпга -> sword
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: шпги
Genitive: шпги



Etymology: Borrowed from Polish szpada, szpaga, from Italian spada (“sword”), from Latin spatha (“sword”), from Ancient Greek σπάθη (spáthē, “broad blade”).



Definitions (English):
  • small sword, epee, foil, rapier
