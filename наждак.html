нажда́к (31569)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ottoman Turkish ناجاق (nacak, “hatchet”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐʐˈdak]

Noun

нажда́к • (naždák) m inan (genitive наждака́, nominative plural наждаки́, genitive plural наждако́в, related adjective нажда́чный)

  1. emery (stone or powder)
  2. (dated) big club, battle axe (16th to 18th century)
