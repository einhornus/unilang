Verb[edit]

позва́нивать • (pozvánivatʹ) impf

  1. (also impersonal) to ring periodically, to tinkle
    позванивает в уша́х ― pozvánivajet v ušáx ― my ears are ringing (literally, “there is ringing in the ears”)
  2. (colloquial) to call (on the phone) periodically
