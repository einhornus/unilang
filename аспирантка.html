аспира́нтка (24970)


Etymology

From аспира́нт (aspiránt) +‎ -ка (-ka).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐspʲɪˈrantkə]

Noun

аспира́нтка • (aspirántka) f anim (genitive аспира́нтки, nominative plural аспира́нтки, genitive plural аспира́нток, masculine аспира́нт)

  1. female postgraduate, graduate student (a person continuing to study after completing a degree)

Related terms

  • аспиранту́ра (aspirantúra)
