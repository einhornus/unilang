шорох -> rustle
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: шорохи
Genitive: шороха



Etymologies:
  • Onomatopoeic.
  • May be the same as 1. Cognate to Ukrainian ше́рех (šérex, “grease ice”).


Definitions (English):
  • rustle (soft, crackling sound)
    • (dialectal) rough surface
      • (dialectal) thin broken ice grease ice, pancake ice
