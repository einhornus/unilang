печа́ль -> sorrow
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: печа́ли
Genitive: печа́ли



Etymology: From Old East Slavic печа́ль (pečalĭ, “grief, censern, loathing”), from Proto-Slavic *pečalь, from *pekti (“to bake”).
