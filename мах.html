мах (7803)


Etymology

Deverbal from маха́ть (maxátʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [max]
  • Rhymes: -ax

Noun

мах • (max) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ма́ха, nominative plural ма́хи, genitive plural ма́хов)

  1. single rapid movement in the air, swing, wave, flap, stroke
  2. (sports) rapid forward, backward or sideways movement of the foot or hand
  3. (mechanics) single revolution, swing, stroke
  4. step (of an animal, usually a horse, when running)

Related terms

  • маха́ть impf (maxátʹ), махну́ть pf (maxnútʹ)
