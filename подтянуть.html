подтянть -> to tighten, pull up
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я подтян
Ты подтянёшь
Past: подтянл/подтянла



Etymology: под- (pod-) +‎ тяну́ть (tjanútʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to pull (to), to pull up (to), to haul up (to)
    • подтяну́ть бревно́ к берегу ― podtjanútʹ brevnó k béregu ― to pull the log to the shore, подтяну́ть бревно́ к кры́ше ― podtjanútʹ brevnó k krýše ― to haul the log onto the roof
  • to bring up, to move closer up (of troops, etc.)
    • to tighten
      • (colloquial) to tuck in
        • (colloquial) to pull up,
          • вам надо подтяну́ть английский ― vam nádo podtjanútʹ anglíjskij ― you must pull up your English (to a better standard)подтяну́ть дисципли́ну ― podtjanútʹ disciplínu ― tighten up discipline
        • (colloquial) to help catch up with the rest
          • (colloquial) to join in singing, to join in a song, to sing along, to harmonize
