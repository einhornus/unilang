пробо́ина (13964)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈboɪnə]

Noun

пробо́ина • (probóina) f inan (genitive пробо́ины, nominative plural пробо́ины, genitive plural пробо́ин)

  1. (rupture) hole, breach, leak (e.g. in a ship or airplane)
