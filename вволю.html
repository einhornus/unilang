вво́лю (17325)


Etymology

From в (v) +‎ во́лю (vólju), accusative of во́ля (vólja, “will”); approximate sense "to (your) will".

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvːolʲʊ]

Adverb

вво́лю • (vvólju)

  1. (colloquial) (in) plenty, as much as is wanted, a lot
    есть вво́лю ― jestʹ vvólju ― to eat plenty, to eat one's fill

Synonyms

  • вдо́воль (vdóvolʹ), всласть (vslastʹ)

Related terms

  • в (v)
  • во́ля (vólja)
