хлопок (10247)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxlopək]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

хлопок • (xlópok) m inan (genitive хлопка, nominative plural хлопки, genitive plural хлопков, related adjective хлопчатый or хлопковый or хлопчатобума́жный)

  1. cotton (plant or material)
  2. cotton (cloth)
Synonyms
  • хлопчатник (xlopčatnik)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [xlɐˈpok]

Noun

хлопок • (xlopók) m inan (genitive хлопка, nominative plural хлопки, genitive plural хлопков)

  1. crack, snap, pop (dull sound of impact, explosion or dramatically expanding gases)
Related terms
  • хлопание (xlopanije), вы́хлоп (výxlop)
  • выхлопно́й (vyxlopnój)
  • хло́пать (xlópatʹ), прихло́пнуть (prixlópnutʹ)
