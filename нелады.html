нелады́ (29991)


Etymology

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪlɐˈdɨ]

Noun

нелады́ • (neladý) m inan pl (genitive неладо́в, plural only)

  1. (colloquial) disagreements, inability to get alonǵ

Related terms

  • ла́диться (láditʹsja)
