судомо́йка (30971)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʊdɐˈmojkə]

Noun

судомо́йка • (sudomójka) f anim or f inan (genitive судомо́йки, nominative plural судомо́йки, genitive plural судомо́ек)

  1. (animate) scullery maid (a woman who washes dishes)
  2. (inanimate) scullery (room for washing dishes)
