стонать -> to groan
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Я сто́ну́, сто́на́ю
Ты сто́нешь, сто́на́ешь
Past: стонал/стонала



Etymology: From Old Church Slavonic стонати (stonati), from Proto-Slavic *stenàti. Compare Bulgarian стон (ston), Czech stonati. Non-Slavic cognates include Ancient Greek στόνος (stónos, “a sighing or groaning”), Latin tonō (“to thunder, to resound”) and Sanskrit स्तनति (stanati, “to roar, to cry”).



Definitions (English):
  • to groan, to moan
    • to suffer, to languish
