пома́да (12743)


Etymology

Borrowed from French pommade.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈmadə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

пома́да • (pomáda) f inan (genitive пома́ды, nominative plural пома́ды, genitive plural пома́д)

  1. lipstick
  2. (archaic) pomade

Synonyms

  • (lipstick): губна́я пома́да (gubnája pomáda)
