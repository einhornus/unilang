понтно -> understandably, I see! I understand!

Etymology: поня́тный (ponjátnyj) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • understandably, comprehensibly
    • clearly, plainly
      • short neuter singular of поня́тный (ponjátnyj, “understandable, intelligible, clear”)
