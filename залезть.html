зале́зть (4259)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ лезть (leztʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈlʲesʲtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

зале́зть • (zaléztʹ) pf (imperfective лезть or залеза́ть)

  1. to climb up (finished action)
  2. (also figuratively) to get intó, to scramble
    зале́зть в долги ― zaléztʹ v dolgí ― get intó debts
  3. to meddle
