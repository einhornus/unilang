щено́к -> puppy
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: щеня́та
Genitive: щенка́



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic щеня (ščenja), from Proto-Slavic *ščenę.



Definitions (English):
  • puppy
    • (wild animals) cub, whelp
      • (derogatory) snot son of a bitch.
