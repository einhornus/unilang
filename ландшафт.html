ландша́фт -> landscape
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ландша́фты
Genitive: ландша́фта



Etymology: Borrowed from German Landschaft.



Definitions (English):
  • landscape (portion of land or territory which the eye can comprehend in a single view)
