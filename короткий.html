коро́ткий -> short
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *kortъkъ. Doublet of кра́ткий (krátkij), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • short, brief
    • (figuratively) intimate
      • (short form only) too short, shorter than necessary (of clothing and shoes)
        • мне э́ти штаны́ коротки ― mne éti štaný korotkí ― these trousers are too short for me
