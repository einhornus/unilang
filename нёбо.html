нёбо -> sky, heaven
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лето)

Plural: нёба
Genitive: нёба



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *nebo, from Proto-Indo-European *nébʰos (“cloud”). Doublet of не́бо (nébo), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • palate,
    • мя́гкое нёбо ― mjáxkoje njóbo ― soft palate, твёрдое нёбо ― tvjórdoje njóbo ― hard palate
