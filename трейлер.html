тре́йлер (18573)


Etymology

Borrowed from English trailer.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtrɛjlʲɪr] (phonetic respelling: трэйлер)

Noun

тре́йлер • (trɛjler) masculine gender">m inan (genitive тре́йлера, nominative plural тре́йлеры, genitive plural тре́йлеров)

  1. trailer (vehicle towed behind another, used for carrying equipment)
  2. trailer (preview of a film)
