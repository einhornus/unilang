изможденный (15382)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic измъжденъ (izmŭždenŭ), past passive participle of измъждити (izmŭžditi). Related to мозжить (mozžítʹ, “to ache dully but persistently”), мозглый (mózglyj, “dank; frail”), dialectal мозгнуть (mózgnutʹ, “to spoil, to perish”), мзга (mzga, “wet weather”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɪzmɐʐˈdʲɵnːɨj]

Adjective[edit]

измождённый • (izmoždjónnyj)

  1. haggard, peaked
