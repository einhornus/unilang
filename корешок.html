корешо́к (9366)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kərʲɪˈʂok]

Etymology 1

ко́рень (kórenʹ) +‎ -шок (-šók)

Noun

корешо́к • (korešók) m inan (genitive корешка́, nominative plural корешки́, genitive plural корешко́в)

  1. Diminutive of ко́рень (kórenʹ): rootlet
  2. spine, back (of a book)
  3. counterfoil
  4. (banking) a bundle of 100 banknotes / bills of the same face value

Etymology 2

кореш (kóreš) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Noun

корешо́к • (korešók) m anim (genitive корешка́, nominative plural корешки́, genitive plural корешко́в)

  1. (colloquial) Diminutive of кореш (kóreš): male friend; buddy, pal, mate, crony
