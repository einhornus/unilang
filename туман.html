тумн -> fog, mist
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: тумны
Genitive: тумна



Etymology: Attested since the 17th century.Borrowed from a Turkic source. Ultimately, probably from Proto-Turkic *dum (“cold”), or from Avestan 𐬛𐬎𐬥𐬨𐬀𐬥‎ (dunman, “fog, mist”), 𐬛𐬎𐬎𐬄𐬥𐬨𐬀𐬥‎ (duuąnman, “cloud”).
