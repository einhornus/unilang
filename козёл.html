козёл -> goat
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: козлы
Genitive: козла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic козьлъ (kozĭlŭ), from Proto-Slavic *kozьlъ (“he-goat”), from *koza (“she-goat”) +‎ *-ьlъ



Definitions (English):
  • goat, he-goat, billygoat,
    • козёл отпуще́ния ― kozjól otpuščénija ― scapegoat
  • (slang, derogatory) idiot, blockhead jerk, bastard, ass.
    • Вот ведь козёл! ― Vot vedʹ kozjól! ― What a bastard!
  • (criminal slang, very offensive) an inmate who is an informer and collaborates with authorities
    • (criminal slang, very offensive) homosexual (usually passive)
      • (colloquial) game of dominoes,
        • забива́ть козла́́ ― zabivátʹ kozlá ― play dominoes
      • (gymnastics) buck
