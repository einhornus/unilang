замкну́ть (18010)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ -мкнуть (-mknutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐmkˈnutʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

замкну́ть • (zamknútʹ) pf (imperfective замыка́ть)

  1. to close
  2. to surround
  3. (electricity) to create a closed circuit, to short-circuit
  4. to lock

Related terms

  • замыка́ние (zamykánije)
