орло́вский (19724)


Etymology

Perhaps from Орёл (Orjól) + -о́вский (-óvskij).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐrˈlofskʲɪj]

Adjective

орло́вский • (orlóvskij)

  1. (relational) Oryol (city in Russia)

Related terms

  • Орёл (Orjól)
