ус -> whisker
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: усы́
Genitive: у́са



Etymology: From Old East Slavic усъ (usŭ), from Proto-Slavic *ǫsъ < *vǫsъ < *vǫdsъ, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *wónsa, from Proto-Indo-European *wondʰsom, s-expansion of root *wendʰ- (“hair, wool, beard”).



Definitions (English):
  • (usually in the plural) moustache
    • whisker
      • feeler, antenna
        • tendril, awn
