подрез́ать (15083)


Etymology 1

под- (pod-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈdrʲezətʲ]

Verb

подрез́ать • (podrézatʹ) pf (imperfective подрез́ать or подрезывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut, to clip, to trim, to prune, to lop
    подрез́ать кры́лья кому-либо ― podrezatʹ krýlʹja komú-libo ― to clip smb's wings
  2. (colloquial) to add, to cut some more
  3. (tennis) to undercut, to chop, to slice (the ball)
  4. (colloquial) (car) to cut in front (of)
