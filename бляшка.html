бля́шка (22570)


Etymology

бля́ха (bljáxa) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈblʲaʂkə]

Noun

бля́шка • (bljáška) f inan (genitive бля́шки, nominative plural бля́шки, genitive plural бля́шек)

  1. (colloquial) endearing diminutive of бля́ха (bljáxa, “badge, name plate”)
  2. (medicine) plaque

Related terms

  • бля́ха (bljáxa)
