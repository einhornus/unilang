скова́ть (12473)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ кова́ть (kovátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [skɐˈvatʲ]

Verb

скова́ть • (skovátʹ) pf (imperfective ско́вывать)

  1. to forge, to hammer out
  2. to forge together
  3. to chain
  4. (military) to hold, to fix
    скова́ть огнём ― skovátʹ ognjóm ― to pin (down) by fire
  5. (figuratevly, with ice) to lock, to beset
    мороз/лёд скова́л реку ― moróz/ljod skovál réku ― the river is frozen over; the river is icebound
  6. to fetter
    страх скова́л мне душу ― strax skovál mne dúšu ― I was overcome/seized with fear

Related terms

  • кова́ть (kovátʹ)
  • ко́ваный (kóvanyj)
  • ковкий (kóvkij), ковка (kóvka)
