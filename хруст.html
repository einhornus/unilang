хруст (7599)


Etymology[edit]

Onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [xrust]

Noun[edit]

хруст • (xrust) m inan (genitive хру́ста, nominative plural хру́сты, genitive plural хру́стов)

  1. crunch, crackle

Related terms[edit]

  • хрусте́ть (xrustétʹ)
  • хрусткий (xrustkij)
