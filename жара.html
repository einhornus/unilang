жара -> heat, hot weather
Noun, declension 1b
Plural: жары
Genitive: жары



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *žārь, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷher- (“to heat”). Cognate with Hindi गर्म (garma) and Hindi गर्मी (garmī), Albanian zjarr, Old Armenian ջեր (ǰer), Latin fornāx, Old Norse varmr, Old Prussian goro (“hearth stove”).



Definitions (English):
  • heat, hot weather, hot spell
    • genitive singular of жар (žar)
