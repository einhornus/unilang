невданный -> unprecedented
Adjective



Etymology: From не- (ne-) +‎ ви́данный (vídannyj), past passive participle of вида́ть (vidátʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • previously unseen or unknown
    • extraordinary, exceptional, unprecedented
