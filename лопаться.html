лпаться -> to burst
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я лпаюсь
Ты лпаешься
Past: лпался/лпалась



Etymology: лопать (lópatʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to pop, to burst (to break from internal pressure)
    • (colloquial) to crash, to go bankrupt (e.g. of a company, of an idea)
      • (colloquial) to be depleted (of patience)
        • (colloquial) to express one's opinion harshly
          • passive of лопать (lópatʹ)
