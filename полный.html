по́лный -> full
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *pьlnъ, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *pílˀnas, from Proto-Indo-European *pl̥h₁nós.



Definitions (English):
  • full, complete,
    • по́лный пансион ― pólnyj pansión ― full board, American plan
  • absolute, perfect
    • exhaustive
      • stout, chubby
