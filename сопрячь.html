сопря́чь (23583)


Etymology

со- (so-) +‎ -прячь (-prjáčʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈprʲæt͡ɕ]

Verb

сопря́чь • (soprjáčʹ) pf (imperfective сопрягать)

  1. (engineering) to join, to couple, to conjugate, to mate
  2. (informatics) to interface

Related terms

  • пря́жа (prjáža)
  • пря́жка (prjážka)
