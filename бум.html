бум (12877)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [buḿ]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from English boom.

Noun

бум • (buḿ) m inan (genitive бума, nominative plural бумы, genitive plural бумов)

  1. (media) sensation, racket
  2. (economics) boom

Etymology 2

Onomatopoeic.

Interjectioń

бум • (buḿ)

  1. thud, crash
