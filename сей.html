сей -> this (=этот) (bookish, used in set phrases)

Etymologies:
  • Inherited from Proto-Slavic *sь (“this”). Doublet of сий (sij), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.
  • From Proto-Uralic *śüdäme.


Definitions (English):
  • (archaic, poetic, literary, humorous) this, this here.
    • Прочита́л я сей докуме́нт, ну и где там упомина́ется сто́имость? Pročitál ja sej dokumént, nu i gde tam upominájetsja stóimostʹ? I've read this document, well, where is the cost mentioned there?
  • second-person singular imperative imperfective of се́ять (séjatʹ)
    • Tundra Nenets
      • heart
