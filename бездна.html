бе́здна -> abyss, (coll) (+g) great quantity
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: бе́здны
Genitive: бе́здны



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *bezdъna, equivalent to без- (bez-) +‎ дно (dno).



Definitions (English):
  • abyss, deep (a bottomless or unfathomed depth)
    • vast number
