бездна -> abyss, (coll) (+g) great quantity
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: бездны
Genitive: бездны



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *bezdъna, equivalent to без- (bez-) +‎ дно (dno).



Definitions (English):
  • abyss, deep (a bottomless or unfathomed depth)
    • vast number
