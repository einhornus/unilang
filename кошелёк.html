кошелёк -> purse (for money)
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: кошельки́
Genitive: кошелька́



Etymology: From кошель (košélʹ) +‎ -ок (-ok), from Proto-Slavic *košelь, from *košь, from Proto-Indo-European *kosyo-, from root *kos-, apparently from *kes-.

  • purse, wallet, billfold
