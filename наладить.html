налдить -> to put in working order, set going, adjust, put in order
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я налжу
Ты налдишь
Past: налдил/налдила



Etymology: на- (na-) +‎ ла́дить (láditʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to adjust, to repair, to set right
    • (colloquial) to organize, to arrange, to establish,
      • нала́дить дела ― naláditʹ delá ― to set things going
    • (colloquial) to normalize, to smooth (relations)
      • нала́дить сотру́дничество ― naláditʹ sotrúdničestvo ― to enter into cooperation, нала́дить хоро́шие отноше́ния ― naláditʹ xoróšije otnošénija ― to establish good relations to get along well
    • (colloquial) to tune, to adjust to a certain harmony (of a musical instrument, song, etc.)
      • (low colloquial) to drive away
        • (low colloquial) to keep talking about, to harp on (perfective only)
