нареза́ть (7660)


Etymology 1

на- (na-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈrʲezətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

нареза́ть • (narézatʹ) pf (imperfective нареза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut, to slice
  2. to rifle, to thread
  3. (colloquial) to get hurt (by cutting), to leave a painful impression on one's body
