потом -> then, next

Etymology: по (po, “after”) +‎ том (tom, “that”)



Definitions (English):
  • afterwards, afterward, after that
    • then
      • later.
        • Я э́то сде́лаю потом. Ja éto sdélaju potóm. I will do that later.
      • instrumental singular of пот (pot)
