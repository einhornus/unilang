нае́здник (21502)


Etymology

наездить (najézditʹ, “to run over, cross, ride”) +‎ -ник (-nik).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈjezʲnʲɪk]

Noun

нае́здник • (najézdnik) m anim (genitive нае́здника, nominative plural нае́здники, genitive plural нае́здников, feminine наездница)

  1. horseman, equestrian
  2. (horse) trainer, jockey
  3. (zoology) ichneumon-fly
