наба́т -> alarm bell
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: наба́ты
Genitive: наба́та



Etymology: Borrowed from Arabic نَوْبَة‎ (nawba, “change of the guard”).



Definitions (English):
  • alarm, tocsin (also figuratively)
