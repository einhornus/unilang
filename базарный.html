база́рный (12099)


Etymology

база́р (bazár) + -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈzarnɨj]

Adjective

база́рный • (bazárnyj)

  1. (relational) market, marketplace
  2. (colloquial) noisy, loud, rude, prone to shouting or arguing (over insignificant matters)

Related terms

  • база́р (bazár)
