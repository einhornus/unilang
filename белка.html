бе́лка -> squirrel
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: бе́лки
Genitive: бе́лки



Etymology: From Old East Slavic бѣлъка (bělŭka, “squirrel”), diminutive of бѣла (běla, “squirrel”) (see -ка (-ka)), from бѣла вѣверица (běla věverica, “white squirrel”). See белый (bélyj, “white”), веверица (véverica, “squirrel ermine, stoat”).
