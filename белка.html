Etymology: From Old East Slavic бѣлъка (bělŭka, “squirrel”), diminutive of бѣла (běla, “squirrel”) (see -ка (-ka)), from бѣла вѣверица (běla věverica, “white squirrel”). See бе́лый (bélyj, “white”), ве́верица (véverica, “squirrel ermine, stoat”).
