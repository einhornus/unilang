рубль -> rouble
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: рубли
Genitive: рубля



Etymology: From Old East Slavic рубль (rublĭ), synchronically analysable as руб- (rub-) +‎ -ь (-ʹ). Literally 'a cut' (of a grivna bullion).

  • ruble, rouble (a monetary unit of Russia equal to 100 kopecks)
