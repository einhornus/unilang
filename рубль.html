рубль (588)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic рубль (rublĭ), synchronically analysable as руб- (rub-) +‎ -ь (-ʹ). Literally 'a cut' (of a grivna bullion).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rublʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

рубль • (rublʹ) m inan (genitive рубля́, nominative plural рубли, genitive plural рубле́й)

  1. ruble, rouble (a monetary unit of Russia equal to 100 kopecks)

Related terms

  • рублишко m (rublíško)
