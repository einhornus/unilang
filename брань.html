брань -> swearing, abuse
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: бра́ни
Genitive: бра́ни



Etymologies:
  • Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic брань (branĭ), from Proto-Slavic *bornь. Compare the inherited Old East Slavic боронь (boronĭ).
  • браный (brányj) +‎ -ь (-ʹ)


Definitions (English):
  • scolding, invective, swearing
    • (obsolete) battle, fight,
      • поле бра́ни ― póle bráni ― battlefield
    • cloth with fancy patterns
