зао́чно (17356)


Etymology

зао́чный (zaóčnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈot͡ɕnə]

Adverb

зао́чно • (zaóčno)

  1. without́ seeing, sight unseen
  2. (law) by default, in absentia
    приговори́ть кого-либо зао́чно ― prigovorítʹ kovó-libo zaóčno ― sentence smb in his absence
  3. (education) by correspondence, extramurally

Related terms

  • зао́чный (zaóčnyj)
  • заочник f (zaóčnik)
