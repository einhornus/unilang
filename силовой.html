силово́й (7919)


Etymology

си́ла (síla) +‎ -овой (-ovój)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪlɐˈvoj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

силово́й • (silovój) (no comparative)

  1. (sports) strength-based
    силово́е упражне́ние ― silovóje upražnénije ― strength exercise
  2. (relational, physics) force
    силово́е по́ле ― silovóje póle ― force field
  3. (relational) power (energy-producing)
    силова́я ста́нция ― silovája stáncija ― power station
  4. (politics) force-based, using troops
    силово́е реше́ние ― silovóje rešénije ― force-based solution (to a conflict)
