сучи́ть (21698)


Etymology

(This etymology is missing or incomplete. Please add to it, or discuss it at the Etymology scriptorium.)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʊˈt͡ɕitʲ]

Verb

сучи́ть • (sučítʹ) impf

  1. to spin, to twist, to throw
  2. (colloquial) to jerk, to work
    сучи́ть но́жками (о ребёнке) ― sučítʹ nóžkami (o rebjónke) ― to kick

Synonyms

  • скать (skatʹ)
