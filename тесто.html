тесто -> pastry, dough
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: те́ста
Genitive: те́ста



Etymology: From Old East Slavic тѣсто (těsto), from Proto-Slavic *těsto.



Definitions (English):
  • dough, paste, batter
