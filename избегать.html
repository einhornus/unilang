избгать -> to avoid
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я избгаю
Ты избгаешь
Past: избгал/избгала



Etymologies:
  • From избегнуть (izbégnutʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ) or from из- (iz-) +‎ бегать (bégatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ).
  • из- (iz-) +‎ бегать (bégatʹ)


Definitions (English):
  • to avoid, to evade, to elude
    • to refrain from
      • to keep off, to steer clear, to shun
        • to escape (from trouble, misfortune, punishment, etc.)
          • (colloquial) to run (many places), to run about, to run all over
