Verb[edit]

навороти́ть • (navorotítʹ) pf (imperfective навора́чивать)

  1. (low colloquial) to heap up (a large quantity of something, in a disorderly way)
  2. (colloquial) to overcomplicate (something), to make (something) overly convoluted
  3. (low colloquial) to make a mess of (something), to do (something undesirable or erroneous)

Related terms[edit]

  • навёртывать impf (navjórtyvatʹ), наверну́ть pf (navernútʹ)
  • навёртка (navjórtka)
  • навёртывание (navjórtyvanije)
  • наверчивать impf (navérčivatʹ), навертеть pf (navertétʹ)
  • наверчивание (navérčivanije)
