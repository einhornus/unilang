лощи́на (14709)


Etymology

From лоск (losk, “dell, hollow, lowland (dialectal)”) +‎ -ина (-ína).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lɐˈɕːinə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

лощи́на • (loščína) f inan (genitive лощи́ны, nominative plural лощи́ны, genitive plural лощи́н)

  1. hollow, depression, dell, glen, ravine

Synonyms

  • ложби́на (ložbína)

Related terms

  • лоск (losk)
