брандма́уэр (28247)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Brandmauer.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brɐndˈmaʊɪr]

Noun

брандма́уэр • (brandmáuer) masculine gender">m inan (genitive брандма́уэра, nominative plural брандма́уэры, genitive plural брандма́уэров)

  1. firewall, party wall
  2. (computing) firewall
