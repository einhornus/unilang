шутя́ (19456)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂʊˈtʲa]

Participle

шутя́ • (šutjá)

  1. present imperfective adverbial participle of шути́ть (šutítʹ)

Adverb

шутя́ • (šutjá)

  1. in jest, for fun
    не шутя́ ― ne šutjá ― seriously, in earnest
  2. easily, lightly
    э́то мо́жно сделать шутя́ ― éto móžno sdélatʹ šutjá ― you could do it in your sleep
    шутя́ отде́латься ― šutjá otdélatʹsja ― to get off lightly
