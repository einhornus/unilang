бля́ха (13872)


Etymology

Borrowed from Polish blacha, from Old High German bleh (“metal plate”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈblʲaxə]

Noun

бля́ха • (bljáxa) f inan (genitive бля́хи, nominative plural бля́хи, genitive plural бля́х, diminutive бля́шка)

  1. badge, plaque
  2. metal plate used in a buckle or for decoration
