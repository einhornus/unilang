мол -> he/she/they said
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: мо́лы
Genitive: мо́ла



Etymologies:
  • Borrowed from French môle.
  • From молвить (mólvitʹ, “to say”).


Definitions (English):
  • jetty, mole, breakwater, pier (protective structure to protect areas from sea waves)
    • (colloquial) he says, they say (used informally to mark reported speech)
      • Он мне говори́т, мол, дава́й де́ньги! ― On mne govorít, mol, daváj dénʹgi! ― He says to me, he says, "give me the money!"
