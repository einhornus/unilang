ха́халь (22101)


Etymology

Probably of expressive origin, also reminiscent of words like коха́ти (“to love”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxaxəlʲ]

Noun

ха́халь • (xáxalʹ) m anim (genitive ха́халя, nominative plural ха́хали, genitive plural ха́халей, feminine ха́халишка)

  1. (low colloquial) suitor, lover
