подтя́гивать (12741)


Etymology

подтяг- (podtjag-) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈtʲːæɡʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

подтя́гивать • (podtjágivatʹ) impf (perfective подтяну́ть)

  1. to pull (to), to pull up (to), to haul up (to)
    подтя́гивать бревно к берегу ― podtjágivatʹ brevnó k béregu ― to pull the log to the shore
    подтя́гивать бревно к кры́ше ― podtjágivatʹ brevnó k krýše ― to haul the log ontó the roof
  2. to bring up, to move closer up (of troops, etc.)
  3. to tighten
  4. (colloquial) to tuck in
  5. (colloquial) to pull up
    вам на́до подтяну́ть английский ― vam nádo podtjanútʹ anglíjskij ― you must pull up your English (to a better standard)
    подтя́гивать дисципли́ну ― podtjágivatʹ disciplínu ― tighten up discipline
  6. (colloquial) to help catch up with the rest
  7. (colloquial) to join in singing, to join in a song, to sing alonǵ, to harmonize

Related terms

  • подтя́жка (podtjážka), подтя́жки (podtjážki)
  • подтянутый (podtjánutyj)
