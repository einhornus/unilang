разбу́хнуть (19283)


Etymology

раз- (raz-) +‎ бухну́ть (búxnutʹ, “to swell”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐzˈbuxnʊtʲ]

Verb

разбу́хнуть • (razbúxnutʹ) pf (imperfective разбухать)

  1. to swell, to distend
  2. to grow excessively/immoderately, to inflate
    книга разбу́хла от́ примеча́ний ― kníga razbúxla ot primečánij ― the size of the book is made excessively large by too many footnotes

Synonyms

  • набу́хнуть (nabúxnutʹ)

Related terms

  • бухну́ть (búxnutʹ)
