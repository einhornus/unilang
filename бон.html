бон (14110)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bon]

Etymology 1

From French bon.

Noun

бон • (bon) m inan (genitive бо́на, nominative plural боны, genitive plural бонов)

  1. voucher
  2. bon (an early type of paper currency)
  3. any paper currency collected by notaphilists

Etymology 2

Shortening of бонхед (bonxéd), from English bonehead.

Noun

бон • (bon) m inan (genitive бо́на, nominative plural боны, genitive plural бонов)

  1. (slang) a Neo-Nazi skinhead
