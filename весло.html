весло́ -> oar
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: вёсла
Genitive: весла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic весло́ (veslo), from Proto-Slavic *veslo (“oar”).



Definitions (English):
  • oar, paddle, scull
    • (criminal slang) spoon
      • (slang) rifle, especially a sniper rifle (which looks like an oar)
