весло́ (4175)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic весло́ (veslo), from Proto-Slavic *veslo (“oar”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪsˈlo]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

весло́ • (vesló) n inan (genitive весла, nominative plural вёсла, genitive plural вёсел)

  1. oar, paddle, scull
  2. (criminal slang) spoon
  3. (slang) rifle, especially a sniper rifle (which looks like an oar)

Related terms

  • вёсельный (vjóselʹnyj)
