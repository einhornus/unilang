буря -> storm, gale
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: бури
Genitive: бури



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *buřa, akin to Bulgarian бу́ря (burja), Slovene burja, Polish burza, Serbo-Croatian бура/bura, Slovak búrka (“tempest”) and búrať (“to crush”). Non-Slavic cognates include Old Norse byrr (“fair wind”), Latin furo (“I rage, rave”), Sanskrit भुरति (bhurati, “stir, palpitate”). Compare also Turkish bora (“squall”).

  • present adverbial imperfective participle of бу́ри́ть (burítʹ)
