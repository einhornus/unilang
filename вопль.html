вопль (3570)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [voplʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

вопль • (voplʹ) m inan (genitive во́пля, nominative plural во́пли, genitive plural во́плей)

  1. cry, wail, howl, yell, scream (prolonged cry of distress or anguish)

Related terms

  • вопи́ть (vopítʹ), завопи́ть (zavopítʹ)
