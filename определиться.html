определи́ться (10885)


Etymology[edit]

определи́ть (opredelítʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɐprʲɪdʲɪˈlʲit͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

определи́ться • (opredelítʹsja) pf (imperfective определя́ться)

  1. to take shape, to be / become formed (of one's character); to clarify itself
  2. to find / determine one's position
  3. to enter, to enlist (in); to find a place, to get employment
  4. passive of определи́ть (opredelítʹ)
