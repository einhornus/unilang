о́ный (12342)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *onъ

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈonɨj]

Determiner

о́ный • (ónyj)

  1. (high register) that, that very, the aforementioned
  2. (obsolete) that other (as opposed to this)
    во вре́мя о́но ― vo vrémja óno ― of yore; in olden days; once a very long time ago
    во времена́ о́ны ― vo vremená óny ― once upoń a time; in the old days; once
    во дни́ о́ны ― vo dní óny ― once upoń a time; in olden days; once
  3. (obsolete) otherworldly, unearthly

Synonyms

  • тот (tot)
