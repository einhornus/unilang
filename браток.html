брато́к (10925)


Etymology

брат (brat) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brɐˈtok]

Noun

брато́к • (bratók) masculine gender">m anim (genitive братка́, nominative plural братки́, genitive plural братко́в)

  1. (colloquial) brother, man, bro
  2. (colloquial) mobster

Synonyms

  • брати́шка (bratíška)

Related terms

  • брат (brat)
  • братва́ (bratvá)
