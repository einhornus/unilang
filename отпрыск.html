Noun[edit]

о́тпрыск • (ótprysk) m inan or m anim (genitive о́тпрыска, nominative plural о́тпрыски, genitive plural о́тпрысков)

  1. (inanimate, botany) offshoot
  2. (animate, archaic, now ironic) offspring
