сплочённый -> united
Adjective


Definitions (English):
  • past passive perfective participle of сплоти́ть (splotítʹ)
    • connected in bundles or rafts (of logs or boards)
      • joined closely together, cohesive,
        • спплочённые ряды́́ ― sppločónnyje rjadý ― closed ranks
      • (figuratively) united, unanimous
