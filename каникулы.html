кани́кулы (6667)


Etymology

From the Latin Canicula, meaning "small dog", an alternative name of the star Sirius, which from the 24th of July to the 24th of August rises and sets at the samé time as the sun, thus concerning this summer period. See dog days for more information.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈnʲikʊlɨ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

кани́кулы • (kaníkuly) f inan pl (genitive кани́кул, plural only)

  1. (school, university, parliament) vacation (U.S.), holiday (UK)
