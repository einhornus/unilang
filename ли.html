ли -> whether [question word]
Definitions (English):
  • (interrogative particle, placed after the first word in a sentence or phrase) Do/does/did...? Is/are/was/were...?
    • Зна́ете ли вы? ― Znájete li vy? ― Do you know? Хорошо́ ли ему́? ― Xorošó li jemú? ― Is he okay? Отпра́вил ли я письмо́? ― Otprávil li ja pisʹmó? ― Did I send the letter?
  • whether, if
