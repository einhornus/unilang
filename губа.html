гу́ба -> lip
Noun, declension 1f
Plural: губы
Genitive: гу́бы



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *gǫba.
  • From Proto-Slavic *guba (“fold”), action noun of *gъbnǫti (“bend”). Alternatively, per Vasmer, from губа́́ (gubá, “lip”), originally "river mouth".
  • Shortening of губва́хта (gubváxta), a corruption of гауптва́хта (gauptváxta).


Definitions (English):
  • (anatomy) lip.
    • У вас губа́́ не ду́ра. ― U vas gubá ne dúra. ― You have good taste.
  • (obsolete) polypore, sponge
    • ( North Russia) bay, inlet
      • (military, slang) stockade (prison)
        • (military, slang) detention in a stockade (as a punishment)
          • са́жа́ть/отправля́ть на губу́́ ― sažátʹ/otpravljátʹ na gubú ― to confine to stockade
