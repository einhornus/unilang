родни́ть (24640)


Etymology

Perhaps from родня́ (rodnjá) +‎ -ить (-ítʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐdʲˈnʲitʲ]

Verb

родни́ть • (rodnítʹ) impf (perfective сроднить or породнить)

  1. to make related
    Perfective: породнить (porodnítʹ)
  2. to bring closer together
    Perfective: сроднить (srodnítʹ)
  3. (imperfective only) to be in common (between), to create an affinity (between)
    подру́г роднила любо́вь к му́зыке ― podrúg rodníla ljubóvʹ k múzyke ― their love of music created an affinity between the friends
