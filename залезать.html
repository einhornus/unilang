залеза́ть (10888)


Etymology

зале́зть (zaléztʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəlʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

залеза́ть • (zalezátʹ) impf (perfective зале́зть)

  1. to climb up
  2. (also figuratively) to get intó, to scramble
  3. to meddle

Related terms

  • лезть impf (leztʹ), поле́зть pf (poléztʹ)
