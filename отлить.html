отли́ть (13054)


Etymology

от- (ot-) + лить (litʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtˈlʲitʲ]

Verb

отли́ть • (otlítʹ) pf (imperfective отлива́ть)

  1. to pour off, to pump out
  2. to found, to cast
  3. to flow back, to ebb
    кровь отлила́ от лица́ у кого́-либо
    krovʹ otlilá ot licá u kovó-libo
    smb's face paled [grew pale; turned bloodless; went white]
  4. (colloquial) to take a leak, to make water

Related terms

  • лить impf (litʹ)
  • отливка (otlivka), литьё (litʹjó)
