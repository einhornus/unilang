оголте́лый (21736)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐɡɐlˈtʲelɨj]

Adjective

оголте́лый • (ogoltélyj)

  1. unbridled, rampant, frenzied, wild, extreme
    оголте́лый национали́зм ― ogoltélyj nacionalízm ― unbridled nationalism

Usage notes

  • Short forms оголтел, оголтела, оголтело, оголтелы theoretically exist, but are usually avoided due to their appearing similar to past-tense forms.
