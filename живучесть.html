живу́честь (29311)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ʐɨˈvut͡ɕɪsʲtʲ]

Noun[edit]

живу́честь • (živúčestʹ) f inan (genitive живу́чести, nominative plural живу́чести, genitive plural живу́честей)

  1. vitality, survivability, viability, survival capability
  2. durability
