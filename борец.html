Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: борцы́
Genitive: борца́



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *borьcь, from *borti +‎ *-ьcь synchronically derivable from бороть(ся) (borótʹ(sja)) +‎ -ец (-éc).
