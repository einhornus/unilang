поминть -> to mention, recall
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я поминю
Ты поминешь
Past: поминл/поминла


Definitions (English):
  • (dated) to remember, to recall
    • (colloquial) to mention,
      • не помина́й меня лихом! ― ne pomináj menjá líxom! ― remember me kindly! (sometimes used when someone leaves for a very risky venture or leaves for good)
    • (religion) to pray for the health living or the repose of the soul of the dead, to commemorate
      • to squeeze, to press, to wrinkle for awhile.
        • Он стоя́л до́лго, помина́я перча́тки, не зна́л, что отвеча́ть. On stojál dólgo, pominája perčátki, ne znál, što otvečátʹ. He stood for a long time, wrinkling his gloves, and did not know how to reply.
