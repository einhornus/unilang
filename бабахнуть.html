баба́хнуть (30920)


Etymology

Onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈbaxnʊtʲ]

Verb

баба́хнуть • (babáxnutʹ) pf (imperfective бабахать)

  1. (colloquial) to give a resounding slap, to go off with a bang, to pop, to explode (transitive and intransitive)
