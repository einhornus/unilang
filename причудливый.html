причудливый -> odd, whimsical
Adjective



Etymology: причу́да (pričúda) +‎ -ливый (-livyj)



Definitions (English):
  • fancy, complicated
    • cute, quaint
      • bizarre, weird, peculiar (strangely unconventional)
        • (dated) whimsical
