лесору́б (17655)


Etymology

From лес (les) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ -руб (-rub), combining form of руби́ть (rubítʹ, “to chop”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪsɐˈrup]

Noun

лесору́б • (lesorúb) masculine gender">m anim (genitive лесору́ба, nominative plural лесору́бы, genitive plural лесору́бов)

  1. lumberman, lumberjack, logger, woodcutter

Synonyms

  • дровосе́к (drovosék)

Related terms

  • лес (les)
