поде́лка (27553)


Etymology

поде́лать (podélatʹ, “to make, to do”) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈdʲelkə]

Noun

поде́лка • (podélka) f inan (genitive поде́лки, nominative plural поде́лки, genitive plural поде́лок)

  1. a (handmade) article
  2. (derogatory) a makeshift article
  3. an odd job
