моль (13002)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [molʲ]
  • Rhymes: -olʲ

Etymology 1

From Old East Slavic моль (molĭ), from Proto-Slavic *moľь.

Noun

моль • (molʹ) f anim (genitive моли, nominative plural моли, genitive plural мо́лей)

  1. clothes moth (Tineidae)

Etymology 2

Noun

моль • (molʹ) masculine gender">m inan (genitive моля, nominative plural моли, genitive plural мо́лей)

  1. (music) minor
Synonyms
  • мино́р (minór)

Adjective

моль • (molʹ) (indeclinable)

  1. (music) minor
Synonyms
  • минорный (minórnyj)

Etymology 3

Noun

моль • (molʹ) masculine gender">m inan (genitive моля, nominative plural моли, genitive plural мо́лей)

  1. (chemistry) mole, gram molecule
