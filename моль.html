Etymology 1[edit]

From Old East Slavic моль (molĭ), from Proto-Slavic *moľь.

Noun[edit]

моль • (molʹ) f anim (genitive моли, nominative plural моли, genitive plural мо́лей)

  1. clothes moth (Tineidae)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

моль • (molʹ) m inan (genitive моля́, nominative plural моли, genitive plural мо́лей)
