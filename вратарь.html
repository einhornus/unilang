врата́рь (19675)


Etymology

From врата́ (vratá, “gate”) +‎ -арь (-árʹ), calque of German Torwart (“goalkeeper”, literally “gate guardian”). Compare Belarusian варатар (varatár), Serbo-Croatian vràtār / вра̀та̄р, etc.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vrɐˈtarʲ]

Noun

врата́рь • (vratárʹ) m anim (genitive вратаря́, nominative plural вратари́, genitive plural вратаре́й)

  1. goalkeeper
  2. goaltender
