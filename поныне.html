поны́не (14804)


Etymology

по (po) +‎ ны́не (nýne)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈnɨnʲe]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

поны́не • (ponýne)

  1. (literary or dated) up to the present (time), to this day

Synonyms

  • до си́х по́р (do six pór)

Related terms

  • ны́не (nýne), ны́нче (nýnče), ны́нешний (nýnešnij)
