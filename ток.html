ток -> electric current
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: то́ки
Genitive: то́ка



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *tokъ.
  • Borrowed from French toque.

  • (water, electricity) current
    • (agriculture) threshing floor
      • mating place (of birds)
