завя́зывать -> to tie, start, get involved in
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я завя́зываю
Ты завя́зываешь
Past: завя́зывал/завя́зывала



Etymology: завяза́ть (zavjazátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to tie up, to fasten
    • (figuratively) to start, to strike up,
      • завя́зывать знако́мство ― zavjázyvatʹ znakómstvo ― to strike up an acquaintance
    • (colloquial) to quit (a bad habit or activity)
