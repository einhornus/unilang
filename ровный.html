ро́вный -> level, even, equal
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *orvьnъ. Cognate with Belarusian роўны (róŭny). Doublet of ра́вный (rávnyj), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • even, level, flat, smooth
    • straight
      • equal, balanced
