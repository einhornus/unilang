ду́шить -> to strangle, perfume
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я ду́шу
Ты душишь
Past: ду́шил/ду́шила



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *dušiti.
  • духи (duxi) +‎ -ить (-itʹ)


Definitions (English):
  • to smother, to stifle, to strangle, to throttle,
    • души́ть в объя́тиях ― dušítʹ v obʺjátijax ― to press to one's bosom, to embrace warmly
  • to oppress, to stifle, to repress, to suppress
    • to choke, to suffocate,
      • зло́ба ду́шит его́́ ― zlóba dúšit jevó ― he is choking with anger
    • to perfume, to scent
