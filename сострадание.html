сострадание -> compassion
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лето)

Plural: сострадания
Genitive: сострадания



Etymology: со- (so-, “with”) +‎ страда́ние (stradánije, “suffering”), perhaps a calque of Latin compassiō.



Definitions (English):
  • compassion, sympathy
