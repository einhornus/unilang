живо́й -> alive, lively
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *živъ, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷih₃wós (“alive”). Cognate to English quick and Latin vīvus.



Definitions (English):
  • living, live, alive,
    • живо́й звук ― živój zvuk ― live sound (for television programs)
  • lively, vivid, vivacious, animated
    • quick, nimble, brisk
