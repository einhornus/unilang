погодть -> to wait a little
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я погож
Ты погодшь
Past: погодл/погодла



Etymology: From по- (po-) +‎ год (god) +‎ -ить (-itʹ), from the original meaning of Old Church Slavonic годъ (godŭ, “time, stint”).



Definitions (English):
  • to wait a little (commonly used in the imperative)
    • to delay the start of something
