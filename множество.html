мно́жество -> large number
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: мно́жества
Genitive: мно́жества



Etymology: From Old East Slavic мъножьство (mŭnožĭstvo), from Proto-Slavic *mъnožьstvo, from *mъnogъ (“much, many”) + *-ьstvo. Equivalent to мно́гий (mnógij) +‎ -ество (-estvo).



Definitions (English):
  • multitude, mass
    • (mathematics) set
