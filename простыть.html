просты́ть (14876)


Etymology

про- (pro-) +‎ стыть (stytʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈstɨtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

просты́ть • (prostýtʹ) pf (imperfective простывать)

  1. to get cold
    его́ и след просты́л ― jevó i sled prostýl ― there's no trace of him (literally, “his track has gotten cold”)
  2. to catch a cold

Synonyms

  • (to catch a cold): простуди́ться (prostudítʹsja)
