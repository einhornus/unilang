ржа́вый -> rusty
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic ръжавъ (rŭžavŭ), from Proto-Slavic *rъďavъ, from *rъďa + *-avъ. Equivalent to ржа (rža) +‎ -авый (-ávyj).

  • rusty (affected by rust)
