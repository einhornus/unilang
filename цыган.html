цыга́н -> gypsy
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: цыга́не
Genitive: цыга́на



Etymology: Ultimately from Byzantine Greek τσιγγάνος (tsingános, “Gypsy”), from a word meaning "untouchable".
