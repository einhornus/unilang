не́чего -> there is nothing
Definitions (English):
  • there is nothing to (negative genitive of что (što))
    • мне не́чего де́лать ― mne néčevo délatʹ ― there is nothing for me to do.
  • there is no need, (one) need not, (it is) no use,
    • не́чего жа́ловаться ― néčevo žálovatʹsja ― stop complaining (literally, “it is no use complaining”)
