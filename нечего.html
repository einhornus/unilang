не́чего (836)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnʲet͡ɕɪvə] (phonetic respelling: нечево)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Pronoun

не́чего • (néčevo)

  1. there is nothing to (negative genitive of что (što))
    мне не́чего де́лать ― mne néčevo délatʹ ― there is nothing for me to do.
  2. there is no need, (one) need not, (it is) no use
    не́чего жа́ловаться ― néčevo žálovatʹsja ― stop complaining (literally, “it is no use complaining”)

Synonyms

  • нефиг (néfig) (colloquial, rude)
  • нехуй (néxuj), нехер (néxer) (vulgar)

Usage notes

  • The preceding noun or pronoun is in the dative case; the verb that follows is in the infinitive.
  • Prepositions are inserted betweeń не (ne) and the form of что (što): не для чего́, не к чему́, не с чем.
