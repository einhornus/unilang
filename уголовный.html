уголо́вный -> criminal (adj)
Adjective



Etymology: From у- (u-) +‎ голова́ (golová) +‎ -ный (-nyj). In Old East Slavic, голова́ (golová) also meant "dead man" compare Latin capitālis (“of the head deadly”), as in "capital punishment".



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) crime criminal, penal (being against the law)
    • уголо́вный престу́пник ― ugolóvnyj prestúpnik ― criminal, уголо́вное дело ― ugolóvnoje délo ― criminal case, уголо́вный ко́декс ― ugolóvnyj kódɛks ― criminal code
