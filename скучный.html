ску́чный -> boring
Adjective



Etymology: ску́ка (skúka) +‎ -ный (-nyj)



Definitions (English):
  • dull, boring, tedious, tiresome (inciting boredom)
    • Ску́чен день до вечера, коли де́лать не́чего. Skúčen/Skúšen denʹ do véčera, kóli délatʹ néčevo. Day-by-day a day goes by.(literally, “The day is boring until the evening if there's nothing to do.”)
  • bored, listless
