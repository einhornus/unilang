осторожно -> carefully, cautiously

Etymology: Cognate with Belarusian асцяро́жна (ascjaróžna).



Definitions (English):
  • carefully, cautiously, with caution, with care
    • guardedly, warily
      • prudently
        • short neuter singular of осторо́жный (ostoróžnyj)
          • look out!, look sharp!, watch out!, be careful!
            • Осторо́жно! Ты чуть не поскользну́лся. ― Ostoróžno! Ty čutʹ ne poskolʹznúlsja. ― Look out! You almost slipped.
          • beware, danger!
            • Осторо́жно, высо́ко́е напряже́ние! ― Ostorožno, vysókoje naprjažénije! ― Danger! High voltage.
