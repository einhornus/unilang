гро́хнуть (8639)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *groxnǫti.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈɡroxnʊtʲ]

Verb[edit]

гро́хнуть • (gróxnutʹ) pf (imperfective гро́хать)

  1. (colloquial) to bang
  2. (colloquial) to drop with a crash/bang, to bang down, to break, to smash
  3. (colloquial) to roar (with laughter)
  4. (criminal slang) to kill, to murk, to waste
    Synonyms: кокнуть (kóknutʹ), укокошить (ukokóšitʹ)

Related terms[edit]

  • грохот (gróxot)
  • грохота́ть (groxotátʹ)
