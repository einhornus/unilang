погря́знуть (20314)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ грязнуть (grjáznutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈɡrʲaznʊtʲ]

Verb

погря́знуть • (pogrjáznutʹ) pf (imperfective погрязать) (в (v) + prepositional)

  1. (alsó figuratively) to get stuck in, to become mired in, to bog down in, to get bogged down in
  2. (figuratively) to get carried awaý by, to become completely absorbed in
  3. (figuratively) to become involved in (reprehensible deeds)

Related terms

  • грязнуть impf (grjáznutʹ), погря́знуть pf (pogrjáznutʹ)
  • грязь (grjazʹ), грязевой (grjazevój)
  • грязнить (grjaznítʹ)
  • гря́зный (grjáznyj), гря́зно (grjázno)
  • грязнуля (grjaznúlja), грязнуха (grjaznúxa)
