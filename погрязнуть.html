погря́знуть (20316)


Etymology

по- (po-) + гря́знуть (grjáznutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈɡrʲaznʊtʲ]

Verb

погря́знуть • (pogrjáznutʹ) pf (imperfective погряза́ть) (в (v) + prepositional)

  1. (also figuratively) to get stuck in, to become mired in, to bog down in, to get bogged down in
  2. (figuratively) to get carried away by, to become completely absorbed in
  3. (figuratively) to become involved in (reprehensible deeds)

Related terms

  • гря́знуть impf (grjáznutʹ), погря́знуть pf (pogrjáznutʹ)
  • грязь (grjazʹ), грязево́й (grjazevój)
  • грязни́ть (grjaznítʹ)
  • гря́зный (grjáznyj), гря́зно (grjázno)
  • грязну́ля (grjaznúlja), грязну́ха (grjaznúxa)
