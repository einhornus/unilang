хан (5029)


Etymology[edit]

From a Mongolic source, compare Mongolian хаан (haan), ultimately from the same Turkic source as каган (kagán).

A possible intermediary is Chagatai خان‎ (xan).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [xan]

Noun[edit]

хан • (xan) m anim (genitive ха́на, nominative plural ха́ны, genitive plural ха́нов)

  1. khan

Synonyms[edit]

  • каган (kagán)
