непреме́нно -> without fail

Etymology: непреме́нный (nepreménnyj) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • without fail, certainly
    • by all means
      • short neuter singular of непреме́нный (nepreménnyj, “indispensable, permanent”)
