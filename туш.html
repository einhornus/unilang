туш (15251)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tuʂ]
  • Homophones: тушь (tušʹ)

Etymology 1

Borrowed from German Tusch, probably from French touche.

Noun

туш • (tuš) m inan (genitive туша, nominative plural туши, genitive plural ту́шей)

  1. (music) flourish

Etymology 2

Noun

  1. genitive plural of туша (túša)
