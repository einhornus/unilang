обли́чье (14568)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic обли́чье (obličĭe), from Proto-Slavic *obličьje, from *oblikъ + *-ьje, from *ob- + *likъ. Cognate with Belarusian аблічча (ablíčča). Synchronically analyzable as о́блик (óblik) +‎ -ье (-ʹje).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈblʲit͡ɕje]

Noun

обли́чье • (oblíčʹje) n inan (genitive обли́чья, nominative plural обли́чья, genitive plural обли́чий)

  1. appearance, look
  2. face; guise
