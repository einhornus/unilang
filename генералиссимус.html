генерали́ссимус (19006)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin generalissimus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡʲɪnʲɪrɐˈlʲisʲɪmʊs]

Noun

генерали́ссимус • (generalíssimus) m anim (genitive генерали́ссимуса, nominative plural генерали́ссимусы, genitive plural генерали́ссимусов)

  1. (military) generalissimo (a supreme commander of the armed forces of a country, especially one who is also a political leader.)
