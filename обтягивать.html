обтя́гивать (18513)


Etymology

обтяг- (obtjag-) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐpˈtʲæɡʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

обтя́гивать • (obtjágivatʹ) impf (perfective обтянуть)

  1. to cover (furniture)
  2. to fit close (to)
  3. (nautical) to bowse taut, to bowse down
