о́блик (3662)


Etymology

об- (ob-) +‎ лик (lik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈoblʲɪk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

о́блик • (óblik) m inan (genitive о́блика, nominative plural о́блики, genitive plural о́бликов)

  1. face, look, aspect, appearance, figure, image
  2. mindset, temper, character, make-up

Related terms

  • лицо́ (licó)
  • обли́чье (oblíčʹje)
