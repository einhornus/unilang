гогота́ть (16110)


Etymology

Onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡəɡɐˈtatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

гогота́ть • (gogotátʹ) impf

  1. to cackle (of geese)
  2. (colloquial) to roar with laughter

Related terms

  • го́гот (gógot)
