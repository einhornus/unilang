гнус (25793)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *gnusъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡnus]

Noun

гнус • (gnus) masculine gender">m inan (genitive гну́са, uncountable)

  1. (collective) mosquitoes, midges, horseflies and other bloodsucking insects

Related terms

  • гнусить (gnusitʹ)
  • гну́сный (gnúsnyj)
