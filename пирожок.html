пирожо́к (7916)


Etymology

пиро́г (piróg) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪrɐˈʐok]
  • audio

Noun

пирожо́к • (pirožók) m inan (genitive пирожка́, nominative plural пирожки́, genitive plural пирожко́в)

  1. diminutive of пиро́г (piróg): Russian pastry (a small pastry baked with any sort of filling, from meats, fish, eggs, rice, cabbage or mushrooms to all kinds of fruit, cottagé cheese, raisins or nuts), pirozhki
