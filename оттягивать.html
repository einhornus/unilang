оття́гивать (12242)


Etymology

оттяг- (ottjag-) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈtʲːæɡʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

оття́гивать • (ottjágivatʹ) impf (perfective оттяну́ть)

  1. to draw off, to draw back, to pull aside
  2. to weigh down
  3. to delay, to procrastinate, to put off

Related terms

  • оття́жка (ottjážka)
