салю́т -> salute, firework display (in honour of s.o. or sth)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: салю́ты
Genitive: салю́та



Etymology: First attested in 1720. Via German Salut or directly from French salut, from Latin salūs.
