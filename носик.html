но́сик (9312)


Etymology[edit]

нос (nos) +‎ -ик (-ik)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈnosʲɪk]

Noun[edit]

но́сик • (nósik) m inan (genitive но́сика, nominative plural но́сики, genitive plural но́сиков)

  1. diminutive of нос (nos): (little) nose
    Ма́ма, у меня но́сик болит. (child's speech) ― Máma, u menjá nósik bolít. ― Mummy, my nose hurts.
  2. bill, beak (of an insect or bird)
  3. spout (a tube through which liquid is poured or discharged)
  4. face (forward part of some tools)
  5. toe (that part of a shoe or sock covering the toe)
