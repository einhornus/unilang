но́сик -> small nose, toe (of shoe), spout
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: но́сики
Genitive: но́сика



Etymology: нос (nos) +‎ -ик (-ik)



Definitions (English):
  • diminutive of нос (nos): (little) nose.
    • Ма́ма, у меня но́сик болит. (child's speech) ― Máma, u menjá nósik bolít. ― Mummy, my nose hurts.
  • bill, beak (of an insect or bird)
    • spout (a tube through which liquid is poured or discharged)
      • face (forward part of some tools)
        • toe (that part of a shoe or sock covering the toe)
