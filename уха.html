уха -> fish-soup
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: ухи
Genitive: ухи



Etymology: From Old East Slavic уха (uxa), from Proto-Slavic *juxa, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *yew(ǝ)- (“to blend, mix (food), knead”). Cognates include Latin ius, Sanskrit यूष (yūṣa).
