шипя́щий (16353)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂɨˈpʲæɕːɪj]

Participle

шипя́щий • (šipjáščij)

  1. present active imperfective participle of шипе́ть (šipétʹ)

Adjective

шипя́щий • (šipjáščij)

  1. hissing
  2. (linguistics) sibilant, of hushing sounds in Russian orthography (relating to /ʂ/, /ʐ/, /ɕː/, and /ʑː/)
Related terms
  • шипе́ние n (šipénije)
  • шипе́ть impf (šipétʹ)
