донести́сь (3766)


Etymology

донести́ (donestí) +‎ -сь (-sʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dənʲɪˈsʲtʲisʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

донести́сь • (donestísʹ) pf (imperfective доноси́ться)

  1. to get / be worn out (of clothes, shoes)
  2. to reach one's ears, to be heard; to be carried by the wind
  3. passive of донести́ (donestí)
