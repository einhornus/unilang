выпира́ть (14204)


Etymology

вы- (vy-) + -пира́ть (-pirátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɨpʲɪˈratʲ]

Verb

выпира́ть • (vypirátʹ) impf (perfective вы́переть)

  1. to bulge out, to protrude, to stick out
  2. (colloquial) to be too prominent/obvious
  3. to push out, to shove out
  4. (colloquial) to chuck/turn/drive out (of)

Related terms

  • пере́ть (perétʹ), пере́ться (perétʹsja)
