бо́дрый -> cheerful, in good form
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic бъдръ (bŭdrŭ), from Proto-Slavic *bъdrъ (“alert, cheerful”), from *bъděti (“to be awake”) + *-rъ.



Definitions (English):
  • cheerful
    • brisk
      • sprightly
        • awake
          • vigorous, vivacious
