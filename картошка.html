карто́шка -> potatoes (coll)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: карто́шки
Genitive: карто́шки



Etymologies:
  • From картоха (kartóxa) +‎ -ка (-ka), a dialectal form of карто́фель (kartófelʹ).Ώ] Recorded since the middle of the 19th century. Synchronically analyzable as карто(фель) (kartó(felʹ)) +‎ -шка (-ška).
  • Borrowed from Russian карто́шка (kartóška).
