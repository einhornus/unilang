зараста́ть (23770)


Etymology

зарасти́ (zarastí) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zərɐˈstatʲ]

Verb

зараста́ть • (zarastátʹ) impf (perfective зарасти́)

  1. to be overgrown, to be covered with vegetation, to hair, to fur, etc.
  2. to heal, to skin over (of a wound)

Related terms

  • расти́ (rastí)
