рост -> growth, increase, height
Noun, declension 2c (pattern город)

Plural: роста
Genitive: роста



Etymologies:
  • Inherited from Old East Slavic ростъ (rostŭ), from Proto-Slavic *orstъ. Synchronically derivable as deverbal from расти́́ (rastí, “to grow”) note that stem-stressed forms of this verb begin with рос- (ros-).
  • Compare Persian راست‎ (râst) ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₃reǵtós.
  • From Tajik.


Definitions (English):
  • growth (in a living organism)
    • growth, increase, rise (in a quantity, price, etc.)
