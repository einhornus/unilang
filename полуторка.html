полу́торка (19574)


Etymology

полу́тора (polútora, “one and a half (oblique case)”) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlutərkə]

Noun

полу́торка • (polútorka) f inan (genitive полу́торки, nominative plural полу́торки, genitive plural полу́торок)

  1. (colloquial, historical) GAZ-AA-brand truck with a 1.5-ton payload
  2. (colloquial) small apartment consisting of a medium-sized room and a small room

Related terms

  • полтора́ (poltorá), полуторный (polútornyj)
