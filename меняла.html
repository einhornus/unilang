меня́ла (8969)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈnʲalə]

Etymology 1

Noun

меня́ла • (menjála) m anim or f anim (genitive меня́лы, nominative plural меня́лы, genitive plural меня́л)

  1. money changer (person who will exchange currency)

Etymology 2

Verb

меня́ла • (menjála)

  1. feminine singular past indicative imperfective of меня́ть (menjátʹ)
