checklist (0)


Etymology

From check +‎ list.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtʃɛklɪst/

Noun

checklist (plural checklists)

  1. A list of tasks to be completed, names to be consulted, conditions to be verified and similar.
  2. An inventory, especially of species.
    a checklist of the plants of the Guiana Shield
