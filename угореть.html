Verb[edit]

угоре́ть • (ugorétʹ) pf (imperfective угорать)

  1. to be poisoned by fumes (such as monoxide or charcoal)
  2. (figuratively) to lose the ability to think
  3. (slang) to laugh loudly, to LOL, to be very amused
