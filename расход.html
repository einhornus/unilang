расхо́д (3094)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈsxot]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ot

Noun

расхо́д • (rasxód) masculine gender">m inan (genitive расхо́да, nominative plural расхо́ды, genitive plural расхо́дов)

  1. expense, expenditure
  2. pl. charges, expenses, exps, outlay
  3. debit
  4. consumption

Related terms

  • расхо́доваться (rasxódovatʹsja)
