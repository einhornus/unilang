мести (18720)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *mestì.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈsʲtʲi]

Verb

мести • (mestí) impf (perfective подмести́ or замести́)

  1. to sweep
  2. to scatter
  3. to swirl
  4. (impersonal) to snow strongly, to blizzard
    Метёт. ― Metjót. ― There's a snowstorm.
Related terms
  • метение n (meténije)
  • метла́ (metlá)
  • мете́ль f (metélʹ)
