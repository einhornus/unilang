вы́борка (29437)


Etymology

Deverbal of вы́брать (výbratʹ) + -ка (-ka), based on the nominal combining stem выбор- (výbor-), derived from the present tense stem выбер- (výber-) with Indo-European o-vocalization characteristic of deverbal nouns.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨbərkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

вы́борка • (výborka) f inan (genitive вы́борки, nominative plural вы́борки, genitive plural вы́борок)

  1. selection, excerption, sampling

Related terms

  • выбира́ть impf (vybirátʹ), вы́брать pf (výbratʹ)
