ла́зить (8757)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *làziti.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlazʲɪtʲ]
  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

ла́зить • (lázitʹ) impf (perfective полазить)

  1. to climb, to clamber
  2. to creep

Usage notes

  • ла́зить (lázitʹ) and полазить (polázitʹ) are Russian abstract verbs. Their counterparts, лезть (leztʹ) and поле́зть (poléztʹ), are concrete verbs.
  • The imperative forms ла́зь (lazʹ) / ла́зьте (lázʹte) (including derivatives with prefixes) are rarely used and considered illiterate, and are not recommended for use. The recommended imperative forms are suppletive forms полезай (polezáj) / полезайте (polezájte).

Related terms

  • вы́лазка (výlazka), лаз (laz), лазанье (lazánʹje), лазу́тчик (lazútčik)
  • обле́злый (oblézlyj)
