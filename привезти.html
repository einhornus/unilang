привезти́ (1247)


Etymology

при- (pri-) +‎ везти́ (veztí).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪvʲɪˈsʲtʲi]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Homophone: привести́ (privestí)

Verb

привезти́ • (priveztí) pf (imperfective привози́ть)

  1. to bring, to fetch by vehicle ((transitive) to transport toward somebody/somewhere)

Related terms

  • приво́з (privóz)
