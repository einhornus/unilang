филиа́л (7296)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fʲɪlʲɪˈal]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

филиа́л • (filiál) m inan (genitive филиа́ла, nominative plural филиа́лы, genitive plural филиа́лов)

  1. branch office, branch (office of an organization with several locations)
  2. subsidiary
  3. affiliate

Synonyms

  • отделе́ние (otdelénije)

Related terms

  • филиальный (filialʹnyj)
