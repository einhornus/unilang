шлях (24220)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Ukrainian шлях (šljax) or Belarusian шлях (šljax), cf. Polish szlach or szlak.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ʂlʲax]

Noun[edit]

шлях • (šljax) m inan (genitive шля́ха, nominative plural шля́хи or шля́хи, genitive plural шля́хов or шля́хов)

  1. (historical, Ukraine, Southern Russia) path, steppe road
