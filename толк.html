толк -> sense, point, understanding
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: тлки
Genitive: тлка



Etymology: From Old East Slavic тълкъ (tŭlkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tъlkъ



Definitions (English):
  • sense, use, judgment
    • talk, rumor
      • (obsolete) doctrine, trend, sort
        • push, shove, thrust
