тя́га -> locomotion, (к +d) thirst for, attraction towards
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: тя́ги
Genitive: тя́ги



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic тя́га (tjaga), from Proto-Slavic *tęgъ or *tęga



Definitions (English):
  • draft/draught, pull, draw
    • traction, pulling, hauling
      • thrust
        • pull rod, connecting rod
          • stay, brace
            • laboratory hood
              • bent, desire
                • (hunting) roding, flight of woodcock in mating season
