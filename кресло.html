кре́сло (801)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *krěslo.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkrʲeslə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

кре́сло • (kréslo) n inan (genitive кре́сла, nominative plural кре́сла, genitive plural кре́сел)

  1. armchair, easy chair
  2. theater stall
  3. responsible position, post
