ко -> eye (bookish, poetic)
Noun, declension 2e
Plural: чи
Genitive: ка



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *oko, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃ekʷ-.



Definitions (English):
  • (dated or poetic or literary) eye (used in proverbs and expressions)
    • о́чи чёрные ― óči čórnyje ― dark eyes. Ви́дит о́ко, да зуб неймёт. ― Vídit óko, da zúb nejmjót. ― The eye sees (it) but the tooth cannot take it.
