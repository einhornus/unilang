ви́снуть (24938)


Etymology

A primary verb in -нуть (-nutʹ). Compare висе́ть (visétʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʲisnʊtʲ]

Verb

ви́снуть • (vísnutʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to droop, to hang down, to dangle
  2. (colloquial, derogatory) to hang on (to), to cling (to)
    ви́снуть у кого-либо на ше́е ― vísnutʹ u kovó-libo na šéje ― to hang on smb's neck
  3. (colloquial, computing) to freeze up
    Perfective: зави́снуть (zavísnutʹ)

Synonyms

  • висе́ть (visétʹ)

Related terms

  • ве́шать (véšatʹ)
  • вис (vis)
