ты́кать -> to prod, jab (sth into sth)
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я ты́каю
Ты ты́каешь
Past: ты́кал/ты́кала



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *tykati (sę).
  • ты (ty) +‎ -кать (-katʹ)


Definitions (English):
  • to poke, to prod, to jab, to thrust
    • (vulgar) to screw, to copulate with. Ты меня не ты́чь,
      • я тебя́ не ты́кал. Ty menjá ne tyčʹ, ja tebjá ne týkal. Don’t get so informal with me, I haven’t screwed you (play on words, to screw someone and to thou someone)
    • to thou, to thee and thou, to treat with familiarity
