па́стырь (24414)


Etymology

Uncertain. A borrowing from Latin pastor (“shepherd”) through́ a Germanic intermediary or a derivation directly from пасти (pasti) are both problematic.[1] Some posit a derivation from a non-attested *пастъ with the samé meaning.[2]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpastɨrʲ]

Noun

па́стырь • (pástyrʹ) m anim (genitive па́стыря, nominative plural па́стыри, genitive plural па́стырей, related adjective пастырский)

  1. (Christianity) pastor, shepherd
