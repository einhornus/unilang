дра́ить (21995)


Etymology[edit]

Dutch draaien (“to turn”) +‎ -ить (-itʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈdraɪtʲ]

Verb[edit]

дра́ить • (dráitʹ) impf (perfective надра́ить)

  1. (colloquial, nautical) to burnish
  2. (colloquial) to scrub clean
  3. (colloquial) to scold, to chew out
