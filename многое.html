Pronoun[edit]

мно́гое • (mnógoje) n inan (genitive мно́гого, uncountable)

  1. (in the singular) many things, many a thing, much
    Мно́гое есть что сказа́ть ― Mnógoje jestʹ što skazátʹ ― There is much to tell.
    Мно́гого не жди́те от него́ ― Mnógovo ne ždíte ot nevó ― Don’t expect too much from him.

Usage notes[edit]

In origin, this is the neuter singular nominative/accusative of многий (mnógij, “much, many”). As an adjective, this is mostly used in the plural. See многий (mnógij) for the full adjectival declension and more usage examples.
