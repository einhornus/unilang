стропи́ло -> rafter, roof beam
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: стропи́ла
Genitive: стропи́ла



Etymology: From стропить (stropitʹ) +‎ -ло (-lo), from строп (strop) (related to English roof)



Definitions (English):
  • rafter (series of sloped beams)
    • truss
