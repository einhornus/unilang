стропи́ло (22887)


Etymology

From стропить (stropitʹ) +‎ -ло (-lo), from стро́п (strop) (related to English roof)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [strɐˈpʲilə]

Noun

стропи́ло • (stropílo) n inan (genitive стропи́ла, nominative plural стропи́ла, genitive plural стропи́л)

  1. rafter (series of sloped beams)
  2. truss
