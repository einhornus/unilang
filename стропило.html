стропило -> rafter, roof beam
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лето)

Plural: стропила
Genitive: стропила



Etymology: From стропить (stropitʹ) +‎ -ло (-lo), from строп (strop) (related to English roof)



Definitions (English):
  • rafter (series of sloped beams)
    • truss
