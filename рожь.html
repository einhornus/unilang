рожь -> rye
Noun, declension 3b
Plural: ржи
Genitive: ржи



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *rъžь, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wrugʰyo-. Compare Serbo-Croatian and Serbo-Croatian raž, Bulgarian ръж (rǎž), Latvian rudzi, German Roggen, English rye.



Definitions (English):
  • (singular) rye (grass grain)
    • (usually in the plural) stand or field of rye
