рожь (21326)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *rъžь, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wrugʰyo-. Compare Serbo-Croatian and Serbo-Croatian raž, Bulgarian ръж (rǎž), Latvian rudzi, German Roggen, English rye.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [roʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

рожь • (rožʹ) f inan (genitive ржи, nominative plural ржи, genitive plural ржей)

  1. (singular) rye (grass; grain)
  2. (usually in the plural) stand or field of rye
