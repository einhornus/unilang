мече́ть (18166)


Etymology[edit]

From a Turkic language (compare Kazakh мешіт (meşit), Tatar мәчет (mäçet), Turkmen metjit), ultimately from Arabic مَسْجِد‎ (masjid).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈt͡ɕetʲ]

Noun[edit]

мече́ть • (mečétʹ) f inan (genitive мече́ти, nominative plural мече́ти, genitive plural мече́тей)

  1. mosque
