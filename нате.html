нте -> here you are, take it (coll)

Etymology: Imperative plural of на (na).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) here you are! there you are!, here! (said formally or to a group of people when giving someone something, i.e., "take it!")
    • (colloquial) (an exclamation of surprise)
