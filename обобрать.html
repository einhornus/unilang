обобра́ть (21371)


Etymology

обо- (obo-) +‎ брать (bratʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐbɐˈbratʲ]

Verb

обобра́ть • (obobrátʹ) pf (imperfective обира́ть)

  1. to pick, to collect (from a tree or garden)
  2. to clean out (to cheat someone out of their possessions)
  3. (low colloquial) to gather in folds or ruffles
  4. (low colloquial) to rob

Related terms

  • брать impf (bratʹ), взять pf (vzjatʹ)
  • бра́ться impf (brátʹsja), взя́ться pf (vzjátʹsja)
