девя́тка (12360)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪˈvʲatkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

девя́тка • (devjátka) f inan (genitive девя́тки, nominative plural девя́тки, genitive plural девя́ток)

  1. (colloquial) (digit) nine
  2. group of nine
  3. (card games) nine
    козырна́я девя́тка ― kozyrnája devjátka ― nine of trumps
  4. (colloquial) No 9 bus (tram, etc)
  5. (sports) top corner of the goal
