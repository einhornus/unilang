вексель (18176)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Wechsel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʲeksʲɪlʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

вексель • (vékselʹ) m inan (genitive векселя, nominative plural векселя or ве́ксели, genitive plural векселей or векселей, related adjective вексельный)

  1. (finance) bill of credit, bill of exchange, promissory note
