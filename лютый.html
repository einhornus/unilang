лю́тый (9351)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic лютъ (ljutŭ), from Proto-Slavic *ľutъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲutɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

лю́тый • (ljútyj) (comparative (по)лютее or (по)лютей)

  1. fierce, cruel, grim
