горшок -> pot
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: горшки
Genitive: горшка



Etymology: From a diminutive form of Proto-Slavic *gornъ (“(flower)pot”), out of which Macedonian грне (grne, “ceramic pot”) comes. Compare Belarusian горщо́к (horщók), Ukrainian горщо́к (horščók)
