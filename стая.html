стая -> flock, pack
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: стаи
Genitive: стаи



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic стаꙗ (staja, “cattleshed, shelter, tent”). Cognate to сто́я́ть (stojátʹ), стать (statʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • flock, herd, pack, troop
