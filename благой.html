благй -> good
Adjective



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic благъ (blagŭ). Displaced the inherited East Slavic form, from Proto-Slavic *bolgъ (“good”).



Definitions (English):
  • (stilted, ecclesiastic or poetic) good,
    • благи́ми наме́рениями вымощена доро́га в ад, blagími namérenijami výmoščena doróga v adthe road to hell is paved with good intentions
