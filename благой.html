благой (8998)


Etymology

Borrowed from Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic благъ (blagŭ). Displaced the inherited East Slavic form, from Proto-Slavic *bolgъ (“good”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [blɐˈɡoj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

благой • (blagój) (no comparative)

  1. (stilted, ecclesiastic or poetic) good
    благи́ми наме́рениями вымощена дорога в ад
    blagími namérenijami výmoščena doróga v ad
    the road to hell is paved with good intentions

Synonyms

  • хоро́ший (xoróšij)

Related terms

  • бла́го n (blágo)
  • благо- (blago-)
