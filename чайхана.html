чайхана́ (25574)


Etymology

Borrowed from Persian چای‌خانه‎ (čâyxâne).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡ɕɪjxɐˈna]

Noun

чайхана́ • (čajxaná) f inan (genitive чайханы́, nominative plural чайханы́, genitive plural чайхан)

  1. (Central Asia, Iran, Azerbaijan) teahouse, teashop (a cafe that sells tea)

Synonyms

  • ча́йная (čájnaja)
