поединок -> duel
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: поединки
Genitive: поединка



Etymology: Borrowed from Polish pojedynek (“duel, rencounter”), from po- + jeden (“one”) + -ek. Cognate with еди́ный (jedínyj), оди́н (odín).



Definitions (English):
  • duel, rencounter
    • combat, fight, battle.

    • (figuratively) dispute, quarrel, argument
