мле́чный (16557)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *melčьnъ; synchronically, from млеко (mléko) +‎ -ный (-nyj). Doublet of моло́чный (molóčnyj).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈmlʲet͡ɕnɨj]

Adjective[edit]

мле́чный • (mléčnyj)

  1. (poetic, literary or dated) milky
