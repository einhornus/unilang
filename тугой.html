туго́й -> tight
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic тугъ (tugŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tǫgъ (“hard, strong”), from Proto-Indo-European *tengʰ-.



Definitions (English):
  • tight, taut (under high tension)
    • туго́й на у́хо (colloquial) ― tugój na úxo ― hard of hearing
