пролете́ть -> to fly through or past
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я пролечу́
Ты пролети́шь
Past: пролете́л/пролете́ла



Etymology: про- (pro-) +‎ лететь (letétʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to fly (by, past, through), to fly (over)
    • to overfly, to overshoot,
      • мы пролете́ли знак "стоп" ― my proletéli znak "stop" ― we overshot the [stop] sign
    • to cover, to pass rapidly
      • (time) to fly by,
        • пролете́ли две́ неде́ли ― proletéli dve nedéli ― two weeks flew by
      • (colloquial) to be passed over (for), to miss the mark, to miss the boat,
        • он пролете́л с повыше́нием ― on proletél s povyšénijem ― he was passed over for
