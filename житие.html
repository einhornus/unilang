житие -> life, biography
Noun, declension 2b
Plural: жити
Genitive: жити



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic житиѥ (žitije, “life”), from Proto-Slavic *žitьje, from Proto-Slavic *žiti (“to live”). Doublet of житьё (žitʹjó), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • (philosophy, ecclesiastic, dated) life, existence
    • (philosophy, ecclesiastic) life, biography,
      • жития́ святы́х ― žitijá svjatýx ― hagiography
