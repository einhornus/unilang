фен (7218)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Föhn.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fʲen]

Noun

фен • (fen) m inan (genitive фе́на, nominative plural фе́ны, genitive plural фе́нов)

  1. hairdryer (electrical appliance for drying hair)
