Noun[edit]

шестёрка • (šestjórka) f inan (genitive шестёрки, nominative plural шестёрки, genitive plural шестёрок)

  1. (colloquial) six
  2. group of six
  3. (card games) six
    козырна́я шестёрка
    kozyrnaja šestjórka
    six of trumps
  4. No 6 bus tram, etc
  5. six-in-hand
  6. six-oar boat
  7. (slang) underling, minion

Related terms[edit]

  • шестерить (šesteritʹ)
