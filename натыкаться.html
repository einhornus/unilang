натыкться -> to run into, stumble upon, meet unexpectedly
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Etymology: From на- (na-) +‎ -тыкать (-tykátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja), with meaning following the corresponding perfective наткну́ться (natknútʹsja).
