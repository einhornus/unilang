хозин -> master, boss, owner
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: хозева
Genitive: хозина



Etymology: Ultimately from a Turkic language. From Old East Slavic хозя (xozja), Chuvash хуҫа (huśa). Cognates include Persian خواجه‎ (xâje), Turkish hoca, Slavic: Ukrainian хазяїн (xazjájin), Belarusian хадзяін (xadzjáin).



Definitions (English):
  • owner, proprietor
    • landlord, housekeeper
      • boss, master, chief, principal, manager, employer, hirer
        • man, husband
          • (computing) host, master
            • (biology) host
