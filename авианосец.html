авиано́сец -> aircraft carrier
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: авиано́сцы
Genitive: авиано́сца



Etymology: First attested in 1927. From авиа- (avia-) +‎ носи́ть (nosítʹ) +‎ -ец (-ec), an inexact calque of German Flugzeugträger.



Definitions (English):
  • aircraft carrier, flattop
