ломка (24773)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlomkə]

Noun

ломка • (lómka) f inan (genitive ло́мки, nominative plural ло́мки, genitive plural ло́мок)

  1. breaking
  2. (slang) withdrawal symptoms (as with drug addicts)

Pronunciation
