Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

    • как сообща́ется ― kak soobščájetsja ― as is reported according to reports it is reported that, как сообща́ется, захва́чено де́сять зало́жников ― kak soobščájetsja, zaxváčeno désjatʹ zalóžnikov ― ten persons are reported to have been taken hostage
  • to be communicated, to communicate
    • to be in communication (with), to communicate (with)
      • to be imparted (to)
        • passive of сообща́ть (soobščátʹ)
