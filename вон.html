вон (764)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [von]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -on

Etymology 1

Inherited from Old East Slavic вънъ (vŭnŭ). Cognate with dialectal вонки (vónki, “out”), Old Church Slavonic вънъ (vŭnŭ, “out”), Ukrainian вонка (vónka, “out”), Bulgarian вън (vǎn, “out”) (also вънка (vǎnka)), Serbo-Croatian ва̏н (“out”), Slovene vèn (“out”) (tonal orthography), Czech ven (“out”), Slovak von (“out”), Old Polish wen (“out”), Upper Sorbian won (“out”), Lower Sorbian wen (“out”). See вне (vne) for further discussion.

Adverb

вон • (von)

  1. (informal) there
    Вон там ― Von tám ― over there
  2. off, out (away), outside, away
    Он вы́шел вон.
    On výšel von.
    He went out (and away).

Interjection

вон! • (von!)

  1. out, get out!
    пошёл вон! ― pošól von! ― get out!; get away from me!
    вон (оно) что! ― von (onó) što! ― You don’t say!
Related terms
  • вне (vne)
