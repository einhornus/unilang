рю́мка -> wineglass
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: рю́мки
Genitive: рю́мки



Etymology: Borrowed from German Römer +‎ -ка (-ka).



Definitions (English):
  • any of several types of glasses primarily used for drinking liquor, such as a wine glass or shot glass
    • shot (a quantity of liquor)
