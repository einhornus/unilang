пособие -> financial assistance, textbook, educational aid
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лето)

Plural: пособия
Genitive: пособия



Etymology: According to Vasmer, ultimately a univerbation corresponding to по- (po-) +‎ себе́́ (sebé). See also посо́бник (posóbnik), подсо́бный (podsóbnyj).



Definitions (English):
  • allowance, benefit, pension, dole gratuity
    • handbook, manual, tutorial, textbook
