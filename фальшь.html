фальшь (17418)


Etymology

Borrowed from German falsch (“false”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [falʲʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

фальшь • (falʹšʹ) f inan (genitive фа́льши, nominative plural фа́льши, genitive plural фа́льшей)

  1. falsity, falsehood
  2. insincerity
  3. (music) false note(s); singing / playing out of tune
