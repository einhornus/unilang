шва́бра (14006)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch zwabber or dialectal German Schwabber.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʂvabrə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

шва́бра • (švábra) f inan (genitive шва́бры, nominative plural шва́бры, genitive plural шва́бр)
