French servir +‎ -овать (-ovátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪrvʲɪrɐˈvatʲ]

Verb

сервирова́ть • (servirovátʹ) impf or pf

  1. to set (the table)
  2. to serve (food, drinks, etc.)
Related terms
  • серви́з (servíz), сервизный (servíznyj)
