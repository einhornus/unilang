заигра́ть -> to start playing
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я заигра́ю
Ты заигра́ешь
Past: заигра́л/заигра́ла



Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ игра́ть (igrátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to begin to play
    • to begin to sparkle
      • to look to advantage, to stand out
        • (a record, tape recorder etc.) to spoil/wear out by playing
          • to run down, to exhaust, to overuse, to overwear (by repetition)
            • (colloquial) to appropriate, to help oneself (to), to hook
