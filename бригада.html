брига́да (1679)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Brigade.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brʲɪˈɡadə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

брига́да • (brigáda) f inan (genitive брига́ды, nominative plural брига́ды, genitive plural брига́д)

  1. crew, team, gang (group of people employed to work together on a common task)
  2. (military) brigade
  3. (criminal slang) gang, crew (group of criminals)

Synonyms

  • арте́ль (artélʹ)

Related terms

  • авиабригада (aviabrigáda), бригади́р (brigadír), бригадирша (brigadirša), бригадирство (brigadirstvo)
  • брига́дный (brigádnyj), бригадирский (brigadirskij)
  • бригадирить (brigadiritʹ)
