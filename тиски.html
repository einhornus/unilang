тиски́ (15459)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲɪˈskʲi]

Noun

тиски́ • (tiskí) m inan pl (genitive тиско́в, plural only)

  1. vise (two-jawed instrument for holding work)

Related terms

  • ти́скать impf (tískatʹ), ти́снуть pf (tísnutʹ)
