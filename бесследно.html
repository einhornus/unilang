бессле́дно (7900)


Etymology

бесследный (besslédnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲɪs⁽ʲ⁾ːˈlʲednə]

Adverb

бессле́дно • (besslédno)

  1. without́ a trace, completely, utterly (of a disappearance)
