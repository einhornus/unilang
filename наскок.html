наско́к (24217)


Etymology

Deverbal from наскочи́ть (naskočítʹ), with underlying final к (compare the imperfective наска́кивать (naskákivatʹ)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈskok]

Noun

наско́к • (naskók) m inan (genitive наско́ка, nominative plural наско́ки, genitive plural наско́ков)

  1. (colloquial) lunge, pounce, swoop
  2. (colloquial) attack

Related terms

  • наска́кивать impf (naskákivatʹ), наскочи́ть pf (naskočítʹ)
