ма́тка (5793)


Etymology

мать (matʹ, “mother”) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmatkə]

Noun

ма́тка • (mátka) f inan or f anim (genitive ма́тки, nominative plural ма́тки, genitive plural ма́ток, related adjective ма́точный)

  1. (animate, rare) mother
  2. (animate) stud female (in some domesticated animals), queen bee
  3. (inanimate) uterus, womb
