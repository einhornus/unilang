Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c/c

Past: гнл/гна́л



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *gъnati, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰénti, from *gʷʰen-, whence English fend defend offend.



Definitions (English):
  • to drive
    • to distill
      • to chase, to pursue
        • to speed along
          • (slang) to lie
            • (slang) to give (used in the imperative to make a forceful demand)
