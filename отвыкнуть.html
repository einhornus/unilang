отвы́кнуть (9137)


Etymology

от- (ot-) +‎ -выкнуть (-výknutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtˈvɨknʊtʲ]

Verb

отвы́кнуть • (otvýknutʹ) pf (imperfective отвыка́ть)

  1. to get out of the habit (of), to break oneself of the habit (of)
  2. to become estranged (from)
    отвы́кнуть от́ дома ― otvýknutʹ ot dóma ― to become a stranger in one's own house
