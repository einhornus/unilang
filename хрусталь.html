хрустль -> crystal, cut glass
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язк)

Plural: хрустал
Genitive: хрустал



Etymology: Borrowed from Byzantine Greek κρουστάλλιον (kroustállion), a diminutive form of κρούσταλλον (kroústallon), a variant of Byzantine Greek κρύσταλλον (krústallon), both ultimately from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos). Compare Greek κρούσταλλο (kroústallo). The starting х- was possibly influenced by хрустеть (xrustétʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • crystal (fine glassware, material used to make it)
    • mineral,
      • го́рный хруста́ль ― górnyj xrustálʹ ― rock crystal

      • разг. изде́лия из тако́го стекла
