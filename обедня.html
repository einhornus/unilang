обе́дня -> mass (church service)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: обе́дни
Genitive: обе́дни



Etymology: From обед (obéd, “lunch”) +‎ -ня (-nja), presumably due to its occurrence around lunchtime.



Definitions (English):
  • (religion) mass, liturgy
