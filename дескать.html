де́скать -> (indicating reported speech) ‘he said’, ‘they said’ etc. (coll)
Definitions (English):
  • (a particle to introduce direct speech, somebody else's words or thoughts - real or imaginary), allegedly.
    • Он, де́скать, не зна́л.Ón, déskatʹ, ne znál. He says he didn't know.
