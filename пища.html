пища -> food
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: пищи
Genitive: пищи



Etymology: Borrowing from Old Church Slavonic пишта (pišta), from Proto-Slavic *piťa.

  • present adverbial imperfective participle of пища́ть (piščátʹ)
