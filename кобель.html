кобе́ль -> male dog
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: кобели́
Genitive: кобеля́



Etymology: Unclear. No cognates in other Slavic languages. Possibly related to Ossetian къӕбула (k’æbula, “young dog”). Trubachyov hypothesizes that the term originally referred to fur color, and explains it as *ко-бѣль, ultimately from Proto-Slavic *bělъ (“white”).
