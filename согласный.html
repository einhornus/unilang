согла́сный -> in agreement, (as noun) consonant
Adjective



Etymology: From со- (so-, “with”) +‎ глас (glas, “voice”) +‎ -ный (-nyj, adjectival suffix). As a linguistics term, it is a semantic loan from Ancient Greek σῠμφωνον (súmphōnon).



Definitions (English):
  • ready (for) willing (to)
    • agreeable
      • harmonious, concordant
        • (linguistics) consonant, consonantal
          • (linguistics) consonant
