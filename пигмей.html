пигме́й (31085)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪɡˈmʲej]

Noun

пигме́й • (pigméj) m anim (genitive пигме́я, nominative plural пигме́и, genitive plural пигме́ев, feminine пигмейка)

  1. pygmy (a member of various African peoples)
