отрабтывать -> to work off (debt etc.), work (shift etc.), finish working
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я отрабтываю
Ты отрабтываешь
Past: отрабтывал/отрабтывала



Etymology: отрабо́тать (otrabótatʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to work off (usually a debt)
    • отраба́тывать кармический долг ― otrabátyvatʹ karmíčeskij dolg ― to work off the karmic debt
  • to work (for a certain period of time)
    • to improve, to polish up (through testing by identifying and eliminating flaws)
      • to complete, to perfect (a certain task)
