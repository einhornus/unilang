торг (13165)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic търгъ (tŭrgŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tъrgъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tork]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

торг • (torg) m inan (genitive то́рга, nominative plural торги́, genitive plural торго́в, related adjective торго́вый)

  1. haggling, bargaining
  2. (in the plural) auction, bidding
  3. (archaic) market, bazaar
