омле́т (25307)


Etymology

Borrowed from French omelette.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈmlʲet]
  • омле́т

  • Rhymes: -et

Noun

омле́т • (omlét) m inan (genitive омле́та, nominative plural омле́ты, genitive plural омле́тов)
