злой -> evil, wicked, bad-tempered, fierce
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *zъlъ.



Definitions (English):
  • wicked, evil, ill-natured,
    • злы́е дела ― zlýje delá ― evil deeds
  • malicious, malevolent, spiteful.
    • Его́ губы искривила зла́я улы́бка. ― Jevó gúby iskrivíla zlája ulýbka. ― A malevolent smile played on his lips.
  • angry, enraged.
    • Осторо́жно, зла́я соба́ка! ― Ostoróžno, zlája sobáka! ― Beware of angry dog.
  • rabid, irate, venomous
    • fierce, severe, bitter,
      • злой ветер ― zloj véter ― a bitter wind
    • ill, harmful, evil, bad,
      • зла́я судьба́ ― zlája sudʹbá ― an ill fate
    • (figuratively) pungent, stinging, painfully spicy,
      • зла́я приправа ― zlája pripráva ― a pungent seasoning
    • (medicine) malignant
