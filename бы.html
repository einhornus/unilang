бы -> would (conditional particle)

Etymology: The word was the third-person singular aorist of быть (bytʹ, “to be”), an otherwise obsolete Russian tense (see Old Church Slavonic бꙑти (byti)).



Definitions (English):
  • A verbal particle used to render conditional and subjunctive patterns.
    • Я говори́л. ― Ja govoríl. ― I spoke. Я бы говори́л. ― Ja by govoríl. ― I would (should) have spoken. I would speak. I might speak.
