непоня́тно -> incomprehensible/ly

Etymology: не- (ne-, “un-”) +‎ поня́тно (ponjátno, “understandably, comprehensibly”)



Definitions (English):
  • unintelligibly, incomprehensibly
    • strangely, oddly
      • short neuter singular of непоня́тный (neponjátnyj)
