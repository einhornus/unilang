инфля́ция (10841)


Etymology

Ultimately from Latin inflatio, through the intermediate of another language such as Polish inflacja.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪnˈflʲat͡sɨjə]
  		• (file)

Noun

инфля́ция • (infljácija) f inan (genitive инфля́ции, nominative plural инфля́ции, genitive plural инфля́ций, related adjective инфляцио́нный)

  1. inflation (increase in prices)

Related terms

  • инфляционность (infljacionnostʹ)
