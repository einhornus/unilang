па́лец -> finger, toe
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: па́льцы
Genitive: па́льца



Etymology: From Old East Slavic пальць (palĭcĭ), from Proto-Slavic *palьcь (“thumb, finger”).
