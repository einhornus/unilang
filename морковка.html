морковка (12438)


Etymology

морко́вь (morkóvʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐrˈkofkə]

Noun

морковка • (morkóvka) f inan (genitive морко́вки, nominative plural морко́вки, genitive plural морко́вок)

  1. (colloquial) carrot (root vegetable, usually orange)
