магази́н -> shop, store
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: магази́ны
Genitive: магази́на



Etymology: From German Magazin or Dutch magazijn, from Middle French magasin (“warehouse, store”), from Italian magazzino (“storehouse”), from Arabic مَخَازِن‎ (maḵāzin, “storerooms, storehouses”).



Definitions (English):
  • shop, store, market
    • magazine (ammunition holder enabling multiple rounds of ammunition to be fed to a gun)
      • honey storehouse/storeroom
        • hopper, dispenser, stacker
          • (dated) warehouse
