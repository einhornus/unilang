иску́сство -> art
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: иску́сства
Genitive: иску́сства



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic искусьство (iskusĭstvo), from искоусъ (iskusŭ, “test, experiment”) + -ство (-stvo), likely from Proto-Germanic *kustiz (“choice, trail”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵews-, whence English choice and gusto.



Definitions (English):
  • art
    • skill, craftsmanship, craft

          • само́ дело, требующее тако́го уме́ния
