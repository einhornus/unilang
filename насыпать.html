насыпа́ть (9783)


Etymology 1

на- (na-) +‎ сы́пать (sýpatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈsɨpətʲ]

Verb

насыпа́ть • (nasýpatʹ) pf (imperfective сы́пать or насыпа́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew, to pour (to distribute objects or pieces of something, of friable objects, not liquids)
  2. to sputter, to pelt, to spout

Etymology 2

насыпа́ть (nasýpatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəsɨˈpatʲ]

Verb

насыпа́ть • (nasypátʹ) impf (perfective насыпа́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew, to pour (to distribute objects or pieces of something, of friable objects, not liquids)
  2. to sputter, to pelt, to spout

Related terms

  • насыпь f (násypʹ)
