спу́тник -> travelling companion, satellite
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: спу́тники
Genitive: спу́тника



Etymology: From с- (s-, “with”) +‎ путник (pútnik, “traveller”).



Definitions (English):
  • a fellow traveler
    • satellite, moon (a natural satelite of a planet)
      • an artificial satellite of a planet or moon, sputnik
