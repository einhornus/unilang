запали́ть (15556)


Etymology

за- (za-) + пали́ть (palítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəpɐˈlʲitʲ]

Verb

запали́ть • (zapalítʹ) pf (imperfective пали́ть or запа́ливать)

  1. (low colloquial) to kindle, to set on fire
  2. (low colloquial) to drive (a horse) too hard
  3. (low colloquial) to water (an overheated horse)
  4. (low colloquial, perfective only, intransitive) to begin to shoot
  5. (low colloquial, intransitive) to throw (+ instrumental, a stone)

Related terms

  • пали́ть (palítʹ)
  • палёный (paljónyj)
  • паля́щий (paljáščij)
  • пальба́ (palʹbá)
