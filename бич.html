бич -> whip, scourge
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: бичи
Genitive: бича



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *bičь.
  • Probably from English beachcomber.


Definitions (English):
  • whip, scourge
    • ( Southern Russia) swipple
      • (colloquial) bum, hobo (homeless)
