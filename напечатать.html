напеча́тать (3857)


Etymology

на- (na-) +‎ печа́тать (pečátatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəpʲɪˈt͡ɕatətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

напеча́тать • (napečátatʹ) pf (imperfective печа́тать)

  1. to print (to copy something on a surface, especially by machine)
  2. to type (to use a typewriter or to enter text or commands intó a computer using a keyboard)

Related terms

  • печа́ть (pečátʹ)
  • печа́тный (pečátnyj)
