лнза -> lens
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: лнзы
Genitive: лнзы



Etymology: Borrowed from German Linse in 1870s (earlier attested once in 1847 with the sense 'lentiform shape'), from Middle High German linse (“lentil”), from Old High German linsa, likely a borrowing from an unidentified source.



Definitions (English):
  • lens (object focusing or defocusing the light passing through it)
