оджда -> clothes
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: оджды
Genitive: оджды



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic, from Proto-Slavic *odeďa. Doublet of одёжа (odjóža), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • clothes, clothing, garments
    • surfacing, top dressing
