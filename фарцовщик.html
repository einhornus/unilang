фарцо́вщик (22522)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fɐrˈt͡sofɕːɪk]

Noun

фарцо́вщик • (farcóvščik) masculine gender">m anim (genitive фарцо́вщика, nominative plural фарцо́вщики, genitive plural фарцо́вщиков, feminine фарцовщица)

  1. (historical, strongly disapproving) huckster, spiv (a person engaged in фарцовка (farcóvka). Фарцо́вщик was a Soviet era term for a person illegally selling something, a "private vendor", "private seller", as it was forbidden in the USSR to be self-employed)

Related terms

  • фарцовка (farcóvka), фарцовщица (farcovščica)
  • фарцевать (farcevatʹ)
