  • IPA(key): [ˈmʲiləvətʲ]

Verb

милова́ть • (mílovatʹ) impf (perfective поми́ловать)

  1. to show mercy (to), to pardon, to grant pardon (to)
    бог миловал ― box miloval ― God has been merciful (to somebody); the Lord has spared (somebody)
