блаже́нный -> blessed
Adjective



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic блаженъ (blaženŭ), passive participle of блажити (blažiti), from Proto-Slavic *bolžiti, from *bolgo or *bolgъ, of unknown origin.



Definitions (English):
  • blissful
    • (church) beatified, saint,
      • собо́р Василия Блаже́нного, sobór Vasilija Blažennovo. St. Basil's Cathedral (in Red Square, Moscow)
    • (as a noun) "God's fool", holy fool (idiot believed to possess the gift of prophecy)
