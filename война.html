во́йна -> war
Noun, declension 1d (pattern стра́на)

Plural: войны
Genitive: во́йны



Etymologies:
  • From Old East Slavic во́ина (voina), from Proto-Slavic *vojьna (“war”), from *vojьnъ, from *vojь (“soldier”) + *-ьnъ.
  • Borrowed from Russian война́́ (vojná).
