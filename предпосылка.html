предпосы́лка -> prerequisite, precondition
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: предпосы́лки
Genitive: предпосы́лки



Etymology: предпосылать (predposylátʹ, “to precede (as an explanation)”) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • prerequisite, precondition (something that must be gained in order to gain something else)
    • supposition, presupposition, premise, background
