жучо́к (13714)


Etymology

жук (žuk) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʐʊˈt͡ɕɵk]

Noun

жучо́к • (žučók) m anim or m inan (genitive жучка́, nominative plural жучки́, genitive plural жучко́в)

  1. (animate) diminutive of жук (žuk) (small) beetle, bug (insect)
  2. (inanimate) home-made electrical fuse
  3. (inanimate) bug, wiretap (an electronic intercept device)
