толчо́к (4493)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐlˈt͡ɕɵk]

Etymology 1

Noun

толчо́к • (tolčók) m inan (genitive толчка́, nominative plural толчки́, genitive plural толчко́в)

  1. push, impulse
    давать/дать толчо́к чему-либо ― davátʹ/datʹ tolčók čemú-libo ― to stimulate something off / to give impetus to something
  2. (colloquial) toilet (fixture), toilet bowl
  3. (colloquial) flea market
Related terms
  • толка́ть impf (tolkátʹ), толкну́ть pf (tolknútʹ)

Etymology 2

Calque of Danish stød.

Noun

толчо́к • (tolčók) m inan (genitive толчка́, nominative plural толчки́, genitive plural толчко́в)

  1. (phonology) stød
