рысь -> trot, lynx
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: ры́си
Genitive: ры́си



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *rysь, from Proto-Indo-European *lewk-, whence English lynx.
  • Related to ристать (ristátʹ).


Definitions (English):
  • lynx
    • trot (horse gait)
