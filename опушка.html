опу́шка (6613)


Etymology

From опуши́ть (opušítʹ, “to decorate/sheathe the edges of (something) with fur”) +‎ -ка (-ka), transferred from the action of decorating the edges of something to the edge itself, then narrowed specifically to a forest edge.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈpuʂkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

опу́шка • (opúška) f inan (genitive опу́шки, nominative plural опу́шки, genitive plural опу́шек)

  1. forest border, forest edge
  2. fur trimming, edging
