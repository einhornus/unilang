опшка -> edge (of a forest)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: опшки
Genitive: опшки



Etymology: From опушить (opušítʹ, “to decorate/sheathe the edges of (something) with fur”) +‎ -ка (-ka), transferred from the action of decorating the edges of something to the edge itself, then narrowed specifically to a forest edge.



Definitions (English):
  • forest border, forest edge
    • fur trimming, edging
