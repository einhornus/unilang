брюшко́ (16790)


Etymology 1

брю́хо (brjúxo) + -ко́ (-kó)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brʲʊʂˈko]

Noun

брюшко́ • (brjuškó) n inan (genitive брюшка́, nominative plural брюшки́, genitive plural брюшко́в)

  1. abdomen (the posterior section of an arthopod's body)

Etymology 2

брю́хо (brjúxo) + -ко (-ko)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbrʲuʂkə]

Noun

брю́шко • (brjúško) n inan (genitive брю́шка, nominative plural брю́шки, genitive plural брю́шек)

  1. (colloquial) endearing form of брю́хо (brjúxo): belly
