экзеку́ция (27005)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪɡzɨˈkut͡sɨjə] (phonetic respelling: экзэку́ция)
  • IPA(key): [ɪɡzʲɪˈkut͡sɨjə]

Noun

экзеку́ция • (ekzɛkúcija, ekzekúcija) f inan (genitive экзеку́ции, nominative plural экзеку́ции, genitive plural экзеку́ций)

  1. execution (the act of putting to death or being put to death as a penalty)
  2. (dated) flogging, corporal punishment
  3. (dated) execution of a judicial sentence

Synonyms

  • казнь (kaznʹ)
