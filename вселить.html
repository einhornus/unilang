всели́ть (24669)


Etymology

в- (v-) +‎ селить (selítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fsʲɪˈlʲitʲ]

Verb

всели́ть • (vselítʹ) pf (imperfective вселя́ть)

  1. (transitive) to make someone occupy some space; to settle someone or something in
  2. (transitive, figuratively) to imbue (confidence, fear, etc)
