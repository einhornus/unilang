сконфузить (24287)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ конфузить (konfúzitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [skɐnˈfuzʲɪtʲ]

Verb

сконфузить • (skonfúzitʹ) pf (imperfective конфузить)

  1. to disconcert, to discomfit, to embarrass, to put in an awkward or embarrassing position

Related terms

  • конфу́з (konfúz)
