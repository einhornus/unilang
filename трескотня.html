трескотня́ (23713)


Etymology

треск- (tresk-) +‎ -отня (-otnjá)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [trʲɪskɐtʲˈnʲa]

Noun

трескотня́ • (treskotnjá) f inan (genitive трескотни́, nominative plural трескотни́, genitive plural трескотней)

  1. (colloquial) crackle, crackling
  2. (colloquial) chatter, prattle
