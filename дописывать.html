допи́сывать (26562)


Etymology

допи́сать (dopisátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dɐˈpʲisɨvətʲ]

Verb

допи́сывать • (dopísyvatʹ) impf (perfective допи́сать)

  1. to finish (writing)
  2. to write (to)
  3. to write in addition, to add
  4. to finish (painting), to paint in addition
