дождь -> rain
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: дожди́
Genitive: дождя́



Etymology: From Old East Slavic дъждь (dŭždĭ), from Proto-Slavic *dъždžь, with spelling from Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • (meteorology) rain.
    • Дождь идёт. Doždʹ idjót. It’s raining.
