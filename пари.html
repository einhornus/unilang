пари (11736)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈrʲi]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from French pari.

Noun

пари́ • (parí) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. bet, wager (an agreement)
Synonyms
  • закла́д (zaklad)

Etymology 2

Verb

пари́ • (parí)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of пари́ть (parítʹ)

Etymology 3

Noun

па́ри • (pári) m anim or m anim pl

  1. genitive singular of па́ря (párja)
  2. nominative plural of па́ря (párja)
