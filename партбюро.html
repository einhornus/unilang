партбюро́ (25770)


Etymology

Acronym of парти́йное бюро́ (partíjnoje bjuró, “party bureau”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˌpardbʲʊˈro]

Noun

партбюро́ • (partbjuró) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. Party Bureau, "partbyuro" (the leading elective body of the primary party organization of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union)
  2. meeting of this body
  3. a similar organization in any institute

Related terms

  • парти́йный (partíjnyj)
  • па́ртия (pártija)
  • бюро́ (bjuró)
