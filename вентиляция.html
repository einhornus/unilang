вентиля́ция (14210)


Etymology

Ultimately from Late Latin ventilatio, borrowed through́ the intermediate of another language.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪnʲtʲɪˈlʲat͡sɨjə]

Noun

вентиля́ция • (ventiljácija) f inan (genitive вентиля́ции, nominative plural вентиля́ции, genitive plural вентиля́ций, related adjective вентиляцио́нный)
