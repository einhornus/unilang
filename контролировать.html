контроли́ровать (4956)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kəntrɐˈlʲirəvətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

контроли́ровать • (kontrolírovatʹ) impf (perfective проконтролировать)

  1. to control, to check
  2. to monitor, to supervise
  3. to rule, to superintend (no perfective form)

Related terms

  • контро́ль (kontrólʹ)
