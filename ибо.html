и́бо • (íbo)

  1. (literary) for, because, as, since

Usage notes

  • Though described as literary as of the 20th century, particularly since the 21st century colloquial usage also occurs, where it may be used humorously or as slang, "spicing up" the speech; compare also the Ukrainian бо (bo). Regardless of these trends, Russian и́бо (ibo) typically remains being prescribed as literary only.

Synonyms

  • потому́ что (potomú što)
  • так как (ták kak)
