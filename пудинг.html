пу́динг (28127)


Etymology

Borrowed from English pudding.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpudʲɪnk]

Noun

пу́динг • (púding) m inan (genitive пу́динга, nominative plural пу́динги, genitive plural пу́дингов)

  1. pudding
