бесе́дка -> summer-house
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: бесе́дки
Genitive: бесе́дки



Etymology: From беседа (beséda, “conversation”), meaning "a place to have a conversation".



Definitions (English):
  • arbor, gazebo, pavilion (a shady place for sitting)
