dyer (17197)


Etymology

dye +‎ -er

Pronunciation

  • (General American) IPA(key): /ˈdaɪɚ/
  • (Received Pronunciation) IPA(key): /ˈdaɪə/
  • Rhymes: -aɪə(ɹ)

Noun

dyer (plural dyers)

  1. One who dyes, especially one who dyes cloth etc. as an occupation.

Synonyms

  • litster (archaic, UK, Scotland)

Related terms

  • dyer's broom
  • dyer's rocket
  • dyer's weed
