Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: пу́хи
Genitive: пу́ха



Etymology: From Old East Slavic пухъ (puxŭ), from Proto-Slavic *puxъ



Definitions (English):
  • down (feathers)
    • fluff
      • short masculine singular past indicative imperfective of пухнуть (púxnutʹ)
