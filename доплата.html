допла́та (28661)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dɐˈplatə]

Noun

допла́та • (dopláta) f inan (genitive допла́ты, nominative plural допла́ты, genitive plural допла́т)

  1. additional payment, extra charge, surcharge
