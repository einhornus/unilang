Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Etymology: освобожда́ть (osvoboždátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to get free, to break free, to free oneself (from), to get loose
    • to become empty to become vacant
      • to become free, to get free time (e.g. after work)
        • passive of освобожда́ть (osvoboždátʹ)
