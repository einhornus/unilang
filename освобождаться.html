освобожда́ться (11245)


Etymology

освобожда́ть (osvoboždátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐsvəbɐʐˈdat͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

освобожда́ться • (osvoboždátʹsja) impf (perfective освободи́ться)

  1. to get free, to break free, to free oneself (from), to get loose
  2. to become empty; to become vacant
  3. to become free, to get free time (e.g. after work)
  4. passive of освобожда́ть (osvoboždátʹ)
