портки́ (19467)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic пъртъ (pŭrtŭ, “piece of cloth (sg)”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐrtˈkʲi]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

портки́ • (portkí) m inan pl or f inan pl (genitive портко́в or порто́к, plural only)

  1. (colloquial) pants, trousers
