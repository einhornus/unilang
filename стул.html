стул -> chair
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: сту́лья
Genitive: сту́ла



Etymologies:
  • Old East Slavic стулъ (stulŭ), borrowed from a Germanic language, probably Old Norse or Middle Low German, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *stōlaz.
  • Borrowed from English stool.


Definitions (English):
  • chair, seat
    • (medicine) stool, feces
