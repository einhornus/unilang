хо́лдинг (26693)


Etymology

Borrowed from English holding.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxoldʲɪnk]
  • Audio

Noun

хо́лдинг • (xólding) m inan (genitive хо́лдинга, nominative plural хо́лдинги, genitive plural хо́лдингов)
