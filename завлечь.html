завле́чь (30526)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ влечь (vlečʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈvlʲet͡ɕ]

Verb

завле́чь • (zavléčʹ) pf (imperfective завлекать)

  1. to allure, to lure, to entice (to attempt to draw)
