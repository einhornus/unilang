по́рочный -> vicious, defective
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic порочныи (poročnyi), from порокъ (porokŭ) +‎ -ныи (-nyi). Surface analysis: поро́к (porók) +‎ -ный (-nyj).



Definitions (English):
  • immoral, vicious, depraved, wanton
    • faulty, defective, flawed, fallacious
