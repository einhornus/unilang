разду́мывать (5847)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐzˈdumɨvətʲ]

Etymology 1

Verb

разду́мывать • (razdúmyvatʹ) impf (perfective разду́мать)

  1. to think (about), to muse (over), to ponder (over), to consider, to ruminate (on), to brood (over), to deliberate
  2. to hesitate
    не разду́мывая ― ne razdúmyvaja ― without́ hesitation; without́ a moment's thought

Related terms

  • разду́мье (razdúmʹje)

Etymology 2

Verb

разду́мывать • (razdúmyvatʹ) impf (perfective разду́мать)

  1. to change one's mind
