гумано́ид (26366)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡʊmɐˈnoɪt]

Noun

гумано́ид • (gumanóid) m anim (genitive гумано́ида, nominative plural гумано́иды, genitive plural гумано́идов)

  1. humanoid (a being having the appearance or characteristics of a human)
