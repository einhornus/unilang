кок (14973)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kok]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from Dutch kok ("cook")

Noun

кок • (kok) m anim or m inan (genitive ко́ка, nominative plural ко́ки, genitive plural ко́ков)

  1. ship cook
  2. quiff (hair style)

Etymology 2

Noun

кок • (kok) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of ко́ка (kóka)
