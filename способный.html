спосо́бный -> able, capable
Adjective



Etymology: From спо́соб (spósob) +‎ -ный (-nyj). Related to Serbo-Croatian sposoban.



Definitions (English):
  • able, apt, capable (able and efficient)
    • gifted (in), talented
      • capable of
