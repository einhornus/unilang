боров (30587)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *borvъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈborəf]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

боров • (bórov) m anim (genitive борова, nominative plural бо́ровы, genitive plural боровов)

  1. castrated male pig (usually fattened)
  2. (colloquial) obese man

Noun

боров • (bórov) m inan (genitive борова, nominative plural борова, genitive plural боровов)

  1. flue, breeching, chimney intake
