зени́тчик (26677)


Etymology

зени́т(ка) (zenít(ka)) +‎ -щик (-ščik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zʲɪˈnʲit͡ɕːɪk]

Noun

зени́тчик • (zenítčik) m anim (genitive зени́тчика, nominative plural зени́тчики, genitive plural зени́тчиков)

  1. (military) operator, or a crew member of a surface-to-air anti-aircraft missile system
