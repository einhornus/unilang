симули́ровать (26936)


Etymology

From German simulieren, from Latin simulō and German -ieren, with the Russian suffix -овать (-ovatʹ). Morphologically симул- + -ировать (-írovatʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪmʊˈlʲirəvətʲ]

Verb

симули́ровать • (simulírovatʹ) impf or pf

  1. to simulate
  2. to feign an illness or disability, especially in clinical or legaĺ context; to malinger

Related terms

  • симуля́нт (simuljánt)
  • симуляция (simuljácija)
