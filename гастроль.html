гастро́ль (9050)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Gastrolle.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡɐˈstrolʲ]
  • Rhymes: -olʲ

Noun

гастро́ль • (gastrólʹ) f inan (genitive гастро́ли, nominative plural гастро́ли, genitive plural гастро́лей)

  1. (usually in the plural) (concert) tour
