Verb[edit]

растеря́ться • (rasterjátʹsja) pf

  1. to get lost (of multiple objects, especially if they are many or they are lost one after another)
  2. to lose one's head; to become confused, to mixed up flustered; to be bewildered
  3. passive of растеря́ть (rasterjátʹ)

Related terms[edit]

  • расте́рянный (rastérjannyj)
