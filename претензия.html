прётензия -> claim
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: прётензии
Genitive: прётензии


Definitions (English):
  • claim, pretension, title (demand for one's rights, or for someone/something one thinks they have the right to).
    • complaint, dissatisfaction.
      • (law) formal complaint of a creditor/purchaser/customer expressing protest to a debtor/supplier/contractor on account of a contract violation.
        • pretense (an attempt to impress others by showing off some positive attributes).
