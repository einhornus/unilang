мундир -> uniform
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: мундиры
Genitive: мундира



Etymology: From Polish mundur, from German Montur, from French monture (see there for more) and perhaps influenced by “mondiren”, an earlier form of the German verb montieren



Definitions (English):
  • (military) uniform,
    • карто́фель/карто́шка в мунди́ре ― kartófelʹ/kartóška v mundíre ― (young) potatoes in their jackets / skin
