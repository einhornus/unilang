детль -> detail, component (of machine)
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жзнь)

Plural: детли
Genitive: детли



Etymology: Borrowed from French détail, from détailler (“to set apart”). Attested since the middle of 19th century.



Definitions (English):
  • detail, nicety
    • (in the plural) minutiae
      • (engineering) part, component

        • ме́лкая подро́бность
