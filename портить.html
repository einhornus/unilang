по́ртить -> to spoil, damage
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я по́рчу
Ты по́ртишь
Past: по́ртил/по́ртила



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic пъртити (pŭrtiti, “spend”), from *pъrtiti. Cognate to пороть (porótʹ), портно́й (portnój).



Definitions (English):
  • to spoil, to ruin, to mar, to damage
    • (colloquial) to corrupt, to spoil, to deflower (virgins)
