Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Etymology: Infinitive modified by analogy with 1sg. реву́ (revú), from Old East Slavic рюти (rjuti), from Proto-Slavic *řuti.



Definitions (English):
  • to roar, to bellow, to howl
    • (colloquial) to cry, to weep (loudly)
