грифо́н (22703)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡrʲɪˈfon]

Noun

грифо́н • (grifón) m anim (genitive грифо́на, nominative plural грифо́ны, genitive plural грифо́нов)

  1. (mythology) griffin (mythical beast having the body of a lion and the wings and head of an eagle)
