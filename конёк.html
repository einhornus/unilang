конёк -> small horse, hobby
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: коньки
Genitive: конька́



Etymology: конь (konʹ) +‎ -ёк (-jók)



Definitions (English):
  • (animate) diminutive of конь (konʹ): (little) horse, horsy,
    • морско́й конёк ― morskój konjók ― seahorse. Конёк-горбунок ― Konjók-Gorbunók ― Konyok-Gorbunok ("Little Humpbacked Horse" in the Slavic mythology)
  • (inanimate) skate (ice skate or roller skate)
    • однорядный роликовый конёк ― odnorjádnyj rólikovyj konjók ― in-line skate
  • (animate) hobby, fad, soapbox, favourite subject,
    • сесть на своего́ конька́ ― sestʹ na svojevó konʹká ― to pursue one's favorite subject, to mount one's hobby-horse
  • (animate) pipit (bird)
    • (inanimate) (roof) ridge, finial
