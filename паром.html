паром -> ferry
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: паро́мы
Genitive: паро́ма



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *pormъ. This is an instance in which the vowel reduction о (o) → а (a) is reflected in spelling: the expected spelling is пором (poróm).
