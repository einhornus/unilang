паром (7045)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *pormъ. This is an instance in which the vowel reduction о (o) → а (a) is reflected in spelling: the expected spelling is пором (poróm).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈrom]

Noun

паром • (paróm) m inan (genitive паро́ма, nominative plural паро́мы, genitive plural паро́мов)

  1. ferry

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈparəm]

Noun

паром • (párom) m inan

  1. instrumental singular of пар (par)
