ангело́чек (28247)


Etymology

ангело́к (angelók) + -ек (-ek)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐnɡʲɪˈlot͡ɕɪk]

Noun

ангело́чек • (angelóček) m anim (genitive ангело́чка, nominative plural ангело́чки, genitive plural ангело́чков)

  1. (colloquial) endearing form of ангело́к (angelók): dear little angel
  2. (colloquial, figuratively) little angel, little darling (child with a sweet disposition)
