мужи́цкий (18424)


Etymology

мужи́к (mužík) +‎ -цкий (-ckij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʊˈʐɨt͡skʲɪj]

Adjective

мужи́цкий • (mužíckij)

  1. (relational, dated) muzhik (Pre-Revolutionary Russian peasant); muzhik's
  2. (colloquial, figuratively) boorish, rustic

Related terms

  • мужи́к (mužík)
  • муж (muž), мужчи́на (mužčína)
