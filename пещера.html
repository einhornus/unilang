пещера -> cave
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: пещеры
Genitive: пещеры



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic пеще́ра (peštera, “cave”), from Proto-Slavic *peťera, from *peťь (“oven”) + *-era, from *peťi (“to bake”) + *-tь. Doublet of печо́ра (pečóra), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • cave,
    • он два́дцать ле́т в пеще́ре провёл ― on dvádcatʹ let v peščére provel ― he spent twenty years in a cave
