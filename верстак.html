верста́к (12992)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Werkstatt (“workshop”) + -ак (-ak)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪrˈstak]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

верста́к • (versták) m inan (genitive верстака́, nominative plural верстаки́, genitive plural верстако́в)

  1. workbench
