соне́т (20200)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian sonetto.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈnʲet]
  • IPA(key): [sɐˈnɛt] (phonetic respelling: сонэт)

Noun

соне́т • (sonét, sonɛt) m inan (genitive соне́та, nominative plural соне́ты, genitive plural соне́тов)

  1. sonnet
