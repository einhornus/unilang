кукуру́за -> maize
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: кукуру́зы
Genitive: кукуру́зы



Etymology: Uncertain. Cognate with Ukrainian кукуру(д)з (kukurú(d)z), кукуру(д)за (kukurú(d)za), Bulgarian кукуру́з (kukurúz), кукумара (kukumára), кукуратка (kukurátka), Serbo-Croatian куку̀руз, Slovene kukuruza, kukorica, koruza, Polish kukurudza, kukurydza. German Kukuruz was borrowed from Serbo-Croatian, but the West Slavic terms have in turn been considered borrowings from German.



Definitions (English):
  • corn, maize, Indian corn
