расправляться -> to deal with, straighten out
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я расправляюсь
Ты расправляешься
Past: расправлялся/расправлялась



Etymology: расправля́ть (raspravljátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to beat violently, to punish
    • to get rid of (someone) by destroying them or rendering them harmless
      • (figuratively, colloquial) to deal with (something), to handle (something), to manage (something)
        • расправля́ться без суда́́ ― raspravljátʹsja bez sudá ― to take the law into one's own hands
      • (figuratively, colloquial) to eat up (something) without leaving a trace
        • to get smoothed out, to even out
          • to become straight, to straighten out
            • passive of расправля́ть (raspravljátʹ)
