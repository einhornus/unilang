мя́гкий -> soft, gentle
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic мѧкъкъ (mękŭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *mękъkъ, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *minˀkakas, from Proto-Indo-European *mn̥k-o-kó-s, from *menk-, whence English mingle. Compare Rusyn мнягкый (mnjáhkŷj), Belarusian мяккі (mjákki), Ukrainian м'який (mʺjakýj), Slovene mêhek.
