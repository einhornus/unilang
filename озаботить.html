озабо́тить (16756)


Etymology

о- (o-) +‎ забо́тить (zabótitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐzɐˈbotʲɪtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

озабо́тить • (ozabótitʹ) pf (imperfective озабочивать)

  1. to cause anxiety, to disquiet, to worry
  2. to add someone (more trouble, work, responsibilities, etc.)
