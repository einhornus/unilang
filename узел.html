зел -> knot, junction, bundle
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язк)

Plural: узл
Genitive: узл



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *ǫzlъ (“knot”). Doublet of вензель (vénzelʹ), a borrowing from Polish węzeł. Cognate with Old Church Slavonic ѫзлъ (ǫzlŭ).



Definitions (English):
  • knot, bundle
    • (rail transport) junction
      • center, centre, hub
        • (anatomy) ganglion
          • (nautical) knot (unit of speed)
