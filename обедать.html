Etymology: From Old East Slavic обѣдати (obědati), from Proto-Slavic *obědati, from *obědъ +‎ *-ati, ultimately from *ěsti (“to eat”). Equivalent to обед (obéd, “dinner, lunch”) +‎ -ать (-atʹ, “infinitive suffix”). See also объедать (obʺjedátʹ).

  • (intransitive) to eat lunch
