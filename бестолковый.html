бестолко́вый -> stupid, incoherent, muddled
Adjective



Etymology: бес- (bes-) +‎ толк (tolk) +‎ -овый (-óvyj)



Definitions (English):
  • muddle-headed, stupid
    • confused, incoherent
      • pointless, useless
