шабло́н -> cliche, routine, pattern
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: шабло́ны
Genitive: шабло́на



Etymology: Borrowed from German Schablone (“pattern”).



Definitions (English):
  • template, pattern
    • stencil
      • mould
        • (figuratively) platitude, cliché
