зо́дчий (27722)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic зьдъчии (zĭdŭčii, “potter, builder, bricklayer, mason”), from зьдъ (zĭdŭ, “stone wall”), зьдати (zĭdati), from Proto-Slavic *zьdati (“to build”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈzot͡ɕːɪj]

Noun

зо́дчий • (zódčij) masculine gender">m anim (genitive зо́дчего, nominative plural зо́дчие, genitive plural зо́дчих)

  1. architect

Related terms

  • зо́дчество (zódčestvo)
