Noun[edit]

карти́нка • (kartínka) f inan (genitive карти́нки, nominative plural карти́нки, genitive plural карти́нок)

  1. Diminutive of карти́на (kartína): a small picture, photograph or illustration; icon

Usage notes[edit]

This term is typically used in a general fashion to refer to images, photographs etc., with a tendency of usage for creations that were made with the help of technology. In terms of refering to drawings, карти́на is mainly used for hand-made paintings and often carries a sense of referring to a perceived work of art.
