посере́ть (17812)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ се́реть (serétʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəsʲɪˈrʲetʲ]

Verb

посере́ть • (poserétʹ) pf (imperfective се́реть)

  1. to become gray
  2. (figuratively, colloquial) to become gloomy
  3. (figuratively, colloquial) to fade (e.g. of talent), to become commonplace

Related terms

  • се́рый (séryj), се́рость (sérostʹ)
