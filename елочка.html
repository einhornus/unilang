Noun[edit]

ёлочка • (jóločka) f inan (genitive ёлочки, nominative plural ёлочки, genitive plural ёлочек)

  1. Diminutive of ёлка (jólka); little fir tree, little spruce, little Christmas tree (New Year's tree)
  2. herringbone (pattern)
  3. (typography) guillemet (type of quotation mark; either « or »)

Related terms[edit]

  • (herringbone): ёлочкой (jóločkoj), в ёлочку (v jóločku)
