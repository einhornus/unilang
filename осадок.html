осдок -> sediment, deposition, after-taste
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: осдки
Genitive: осдка


Definitions (English):
  • sediment, deposition, deposit
    • (figuratively) after-taste, feeling of resentment
      • genitive plural of оса́дка (osádka)
