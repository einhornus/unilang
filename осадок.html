оса́док (11379)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈsadək]

Etymology 1

Noun

оса́док • (osádok) m inan (genitive оса́дка, nominative plural оса́дки, genitive plural оса́дков)

  1. sediment, deposition, deposit
  2. (figuratively) after-taste, feeling of resentment
Usage notes
  • Not to be confused with оса́дки (osádki, “precipitation, fallout”) or оса́дка (osádka, “draft, depth (of a ship)”).
