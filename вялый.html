вя́лый (6275)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʲalɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

вя́лый • (vjályj) (comparative (по)вялее or (по)вялей)

  1. withered, faded
  2. flabby, flaccid, limp
  3. sluggish, listless, languid, lethargic

Related terms

  • вя́нуть (vjánutʹ)
