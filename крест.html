крест -> cross
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: кресты́
Genitive: креста́



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic крьстъ (krĭstŭ), from Proto-Slavic *krьstъ. Doublet of крёст (krjost), the inherited East Slavic form.
