Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Etymology: о- (o-) +‎ пусти́ть (pustítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to let down, to lower, to sink (to allow to descend)
    • опусти́ть го́лову ― opustítʹ gólovu ― to hang one's head, опусти́ть руки ― opustítʹ rúki ― to lose heart to become disheartened, опусти́ть глаза ― opustítʹ glazá ― to cast down, to drop one's eyes, to lower one's gaze
  • to omit, to leave out
