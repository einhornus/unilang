кран -> tap, crane
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: краны
Genitive: крана



Etymology: Borrowed from Dutch kraan or from Low German kran. Cognate to German Kranich (“crane”)



Definitions (English):
  • (engineering) valve (a ball valve, a plug valve, or similar rotating device controlling flow through a pipe)
    • faucet, tap, spigot
      • crane (machinery)
