крыть -> to cover
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я крою
Ты кроешь
Past: крыл/крыла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *kryti.



Definitions (English):
  • to cover, to roof (a house) to thatch, to tile,
    • крыть ши́фером ― krytʹ šíferom ― to slate
  • to coat (with paint)
    • to cover, to trump (in card games)
      • ему́́ не́чем крыть (colloquial)jemú néčem krytʹhe hasn't a leg to stand on, there's nothing he can say to that(literally, “he has no (good) card to cover”)
    • (colloquial) to scold, to rail (at), to swear (at)
      • (agriculture) to mount (an animal to mate)
