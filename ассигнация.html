ассигна́ция (19461)


Etymology

Borrowed from Polish language">Polish asygnacja, from Latin assignātiō (“allocation, designation, appointment”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐsʲɪɡˈnat͡sɨjə]

Noun

ассигна́ция • (assignácija) f inan (genitive ассигнации, nominative plural ассигнации, genitive plural ассигна́ций)

  1. (dated or historical) banknote (particularly, paper money in Russia from 1769 to 1849 and in France during the French Revolution)
  2. (finance) payment order

Synonyms

  • (paper money during the French Revolution): ассигнат (assignát)

Related terms

  • ассигновать (assignovátʹ), ассигнова́ние (assignovánije)
