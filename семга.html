семга (29162)


Etymology

Uncertain; compare Bulgarian сьомга (sjómga).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲɵmɡə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

сёмга • (sjómga) f anim (genitive сёмги, nominative plural сёмги, genitive plural сёмг)

  1. salmon (the fish and the seafood)

Usage notes

сёмга refers specifically to the Atlantic salmon, which has a higher fat content and is considered superior to the Pacific salmon.

Synonyms

  • лосо́сь (losósʹ)
