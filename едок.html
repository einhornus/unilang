едок (21631)


Etymology 1

ед- (jed-) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [(j)ɪˈdok]

Noun

едок • (jedók) m anim (genitive едока́, nominative plural едоки́, genitive plural едоко́в)

  1. eater (person who eats, usually of a specified type, cf. "fast eater", "picky eater", etc.)
  2. (official) person to feed, mouth to feed

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈjedək]

Adjective

едок • (jédok)

  1. short masculine singular of е́дкий (jédkij)
