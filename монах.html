мона́х -> monk
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: мона́хи
Genitive: мона́ха



Etymology: Borrowed from Russian Church Slavonic монахъ (monaxŭ), from Ancient Greek μοναχός (monakhós, “monk, (originally) solitary”)
