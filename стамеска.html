стаме́ска (29097)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Stemmeisen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stɐˈmʲeskə]

Noun

стаме́ска • (staméska) f inan (genitive стаме́ски, nominative plural стаме́ски, genitive plural стаме́сок)

  1. (wood) chisel (tool consisting of a slim oblong block of metal)
    долбёжная стаме́ска ― dolbjóžnaja staméska ― carving chisel
