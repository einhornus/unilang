те́рем (14802)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *termъ. Cognate with Serbo-Croatian trijem/тријем, Old Polish trzem.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲerʲɪm]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

те́рем • (térem) m inan (genitive терема, nominative plural терема, genitive plural теремо́в, related adjective теремной, diminutive теремок)

  1. tower, tower-room; a living area in a building or upper part of a building in the shape of a tower, especially in ancient Rus.
