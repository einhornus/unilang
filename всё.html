всё -> all, all the time
Definitions (English):
  • always, all the time
    • only, just,
      • всё бо́льше и бо́льше ― vsjó bólʹše i bólʹše ― more and more, всё же ― vsjó že ― nevertheless yet
    • (colloquial) only exactly all,
      • э́то всё ты винова́т! ― éto vsjó tý vinovát! ― it's (all) your fault!
    • (colloquial, predicative) that's it done,
      • вот и всё! ― vot i vsjó! ― that's it!
    • nominative neuter singular of весь (vesʹ): all, the whole, the whole of, everything
      • accusative neuter singular of весь (vesʹ)
