вскрывть -> to unseal, disclose, dissect, open, reveal
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я вскрывю
Ты вскрывешь
Past: вскрывл/вскрывла



Etymology: вскрыть (vskrytʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to open, to unseal,
    • вскрыва́ть письмо́ ― vskryvátʹ pisʹmó ― to open a letter, to tear a letter open
  • to reveal, to bring to light, to disclose, to uncover, to expose,
    • вскрыва́ть недоста́тки ― vskryvátʹ nedostátki ― to expose shortcomings, to lay bare shortcomings
  • to dissect, to make a postmortem, to perform an autopsy on
    • to burst, to cut, to open, to lance,
      • вскрыва́ть нарыв ― vskryvátʹ narýv ― to cut an abscess
