поглоти́ть (7750)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəɡlɐˈtʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

поглоти́ть • (poglotítʹ) pf (imperfective поглоща́ть)

  1. to swallow up, to devour
  2. to absorb, to take up
