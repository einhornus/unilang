партнерша (15108)


Etymology

From партнёр (partnjór) +‎ -ша (-ša).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐrt⁽ʲ⁾ˈnʲɵrʂə]

Noun

партнёрша • (partnjórša) f anim (genitive партнёрши, nominative plural партнёрши, genitive plural партнёрш, masculine партнёр)

  1. female partner, associate (person united with another or others in an act, enterprise, or business; a partner or colleague)

Related terms

  • партнёрский (partnjórskij)
  • партнёрство (partnjórstvo)
