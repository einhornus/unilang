гауптва́хта (13805)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Hauptwache (“main guardhouse”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡəʊptˈvaxtə]

Noun

гауптва́хта • (gauptváxta) f inan (genitive гауптва́хты, nominative plural гауптва́хты, genitive plural гауптва́хт)

  1. (military) a stockade (prison)
  2. (military) detention in a stockade as a punishment
    сажа́ть or отправля́ть на гауптва́хту ― sažátʹ or otpravljátʹ na gauptváxtu ― to confine to stockade
  3. (military, dated) a guardhouse with an area for line-up of guards
  4. (military, dated) a chief guard of a city or a garrison

Synonyms

  • (stockade): губа́ (gubá)
  • (punishment): губа́ (gubá)
