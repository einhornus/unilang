неповтори́мый -> unique, exceptional
Adjective



Etymology: From не- (ne-) +‎ повторимый (povtorímyj, “repeatable”), present passive participle of повтори́ть (povtorítʹ, “to repeat”).



Definitions (English):
  • unique, unmatched, inimitable
    • unrepeatable
