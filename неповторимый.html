неповтори́мый (9437)


Etymology

From не- (ne-) +‎ повторимый (povtorímyj, “repeatable”), present passive participle of повтори́ть (povtorítʹ, “to repeat”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪpəftɐˈrʲimɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

неповтори́мый • (nepovtorímyj)

  1. unique, unmatched, inimitable
  2. unrepeatable
