власть -> power
Noun, declension 3e (pattern ночь)

Plural: власти
Genitive: власти



Etymology: Borrowing from Old Church Slavonic власть (vlastĭ), from Proto-Slavic *volstь. Doublet of во́лость (vólostʹ), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • authority, authorities, power
    • rule, regime
      • control
        • (law) force (legal validity)
