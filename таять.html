тять -> to melt
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я тю
Ты тешь
Past: тял/тяла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *tàjati.



Definitions (English):
  • to melt, to thaw,
    • воск та́ет ― vosk tájet ― the wax is melting, та́ять во рту ― tájatʹ vo rtu ― to melt in the mouth
  • to melt away, to wane, to dwindle,
    • его́ си́лы та́ют ― jevó síly tájut ― his strength is dwindling (is on the wane)зву́ки та́ют ― zvúki tájut ― sounds fade away, на́ши запа́сы та́ют ― náši zapásy tájut ― our stocks are dwindling
  • (imperfective only) to waste away, to pine (with), to languish (with)
    • to melt (with)
