висо́к -> temple (ear to forehead)
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: виски́
Genitive: виска́



Etymology: Per Vasmer, from висе́ть (visétʹ, “to hang”) +‎ -ок (-ók) original meaning (still found dialectally) "hair hanging at the temples". Compare Belarusian виската (viskáta, “slovenly woman with disheveled hair”).



Definitions (English):
  • (anatomy) temple
