березка (8559)


Etymology

берёза (berjóza) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲɪˈrʲɵskə]

Noun

берёзка • (berjózka) f inan (genitive берёзки, nominative plural берёзки, genitive plural берёзок)

  1. (colloquial) endearing diminutive of берёза (berjóza): (small) birch(-tree); young birch(-tree)
  2. (colloquial) endearing form of берёза (berjóza). Used for example of a tall, slender, pale-skinned Russian woman.
    • Э́то тебе́ не ру́сская то́нкая берёзка!
  3. field bindweed (Convolvulus arvensis)
