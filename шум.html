Noun[edit]

шум • (šum) m inan (genitive шу́ма, nominative plural шумы or шумы*, genitive plural шумов or шумов*) (* Professional usage.)

  1. noise, radio static
  2. sound, murmur
  3. uproar, din, blare, clamour, hubbub
  4. whirr
  5. sensation, stir, bustle
