тахта́ (8017)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Persian تخته‎ (taxte).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [tɐxˈta]

Noun[edit]

тахта́ • (taxtá) f inan (genitive тахты́, nominative plural тахты́, genitive plural тахт)

  1. ottoman, couch (furniture)
