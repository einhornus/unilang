рубха -> shirt
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: рубхи
Genitive: рубхи



Etymology: рубъ (rub, “a piece of cloth”) +‎ -аха (-axa), from Proto-Slavic *rǫbъ.



Definitions (English):
  • shirt,
    • рубаха-парень ― rubáxa-párenʹ ― plain/straightforward fellow
