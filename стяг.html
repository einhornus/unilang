стяг (27330)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic стѧгъ (stęgŭ), which is most probably from Old Norse stǫng (whence English stang and Danish stang). Compare шта́нга (štánga), from German Stange. Alternative reconstructions point towardś Proto-Slavic *stěgъ. Cognate with Belarusian сцяг (scjah) and Ukrainian стяг (stjah).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲtʲak]

Noun

стяг • (stjag) m inan (genitive стя́га, nominative plural стя́ги, genitive plural стя́гов)

  1. (poetic, literary) banner, flag

Synonyms

  • зна́мя (známja), флаг (flag), хоругвь (xorúgvʹ)
