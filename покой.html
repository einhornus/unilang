покй -> peace, quiet
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: поки
Genitive: покя



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *pokojь.



Definitions (English):
  • calm, rest, peace
    • quiet
      • old name of the letter
        • п in the early Cyrillic alphabet
      • apartment, room, chamber,
        • приёмный поко́й ― prijómnyj pokój ― admission department, admission room (in a hospital)
