това́рищ -> comrade
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: това́рищи
Genitive: това́рища



Etymology: From Old East Slavic товарищь (tovariščĭ), from Proto-Slavic *tovarišь, from *tovarъ +‎ *-iščь. Early borrowing from Turkic tavar (“property, goods”) and -iš (“friend”).Ώ]



Definitions (English):
  • comrade, friend (male or female)
    • mate, companion, pal (male or female)
      • colleague, assistant (male or female)
        • classmate, fellow student (male or female)
          • A word used to address compatriots, fellow citizens (male or female) in the Soviet era.
