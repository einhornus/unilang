огово́рка -> reservation, proviso, slip of the tongue
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: огово́рки
Genitive: огово́рки



Etymology: оговорить (ogovorítʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • reservation, proviso, stipulation, ifs,
    • с огово́ркой ― s ogovórkoj ― with reserve, без огово́рок ― bez ogovórok ― without reserve unreservedly
  • slip of the tongue,
    • огово́рка по Фрейду ― ogovórka po Frɛjdu ― Freudian slip
  • (colloquial) ifs, ands, or buts
    • caveat, clause, reservation, disclaimer
