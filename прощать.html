проща́ть -> to forgive (s.o. or sth), pardon
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Past: проща́л/проща́ла



Etymology: From прости́ть (prostítʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to forgive, to pardon, to excuse
    • to remit
      • to condone, to overlook
        • (imperative only) to farewell
