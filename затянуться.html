затяну́ться (4751)


Etymology

затяну́ть (zatjanútʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zətʲɪˈnut͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

затяну́ться • (zatjanútʹsja) pf (imperfective затя́гиваться)

  1. to be tightened, to jam
  2. to grow overcast
  3. (medicine) to skin over, to heal (of wounds)
    ра́на затяну́лась ― rána zatjanúlasʹ ― the wound has skinned over / the wound has closed
  4. to be delayed, to be dragged out
  5. to inhale (cigarette, cigar, smoking pipe)
  6. passive of затяну́ть (zatjanútʹ)
