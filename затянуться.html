затянуться -> to be covered (by), drag on, inhale (when smoking), heal (of wound), tighten one’s belt
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Я затянусь

Past: затянулся/затянулась



Etymology: затяну́́ть (zatjanútʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to be tightened, to jam
    • to grow overcast
      • (medicine) to skin over, to heal (of wounds)
        • ра́на затяну́́лась ― rána zatjanúlasʹ ― the wound has skinned over / the wound has closed
      • to be delayed, to be dragged out
        • to inhale (cigarette, cigar, smoking pipe)
          • passive of затяну́́ть (zatjanútʹ)
