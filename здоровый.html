здоро́вый -> healthy
Adjective



Etymology: Inherited from Proto-Slavic *sъdorvъ. Doublet of здравый (zdrávyj), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • healthy, sound
    • strong, robust
      • wholesome, salubrious
        • big, large
