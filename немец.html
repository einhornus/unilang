немец -> German (noun)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: не́мцы
Genitive: не́мца



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *němьcь (“foreigner, German”), from *němъ (“mute”) (Russian немой (nemój)).



Definitions (English):
  • German, German man
    • (obsolete) foreigner
