Verb[edit]

повлия́ть • (povlijátʹ) pf (imperfective влия́ть)

  1. to influence, to have an influence
  2. to affect, to govern
  3. to have effect, to have action

Related terms[edit]

  • влия́ние n (vlijánije)
  • влия́тельный (vlijátelʹnyj)
