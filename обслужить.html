обслужи́ть (18771)


Etymology

об- (ob-) +‎ служи́ть (služítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐpslʊˈʐɨtʲ]

Verb

обслужи́ть • (obslužítʹ) pf (imperfective обслу́живать)

  1. to serve, to attend to, to wait on
  2. to maintain, to service (e.g. equipment)
  3. to cater for, to supply (for)
