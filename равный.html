равный -> equal (adj)
Adjective



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic равьнъ (ravĭnŭ), from Proto-Slavic *orvьnъ. Doublet of ро́вный (róvnyj), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • equal (the same in all respects)
