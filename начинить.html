начини́ть (21386)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nət͡ɕɪˈnʲitʲ]

Etymology 1

на- (na-) +‎ чини́ть (činítʹ, “to cause, to administer”)

Verb

начини́ть • (načinítʹ) pf (imperfective начинять)

  1. to fill up the inside of (something), to stuff
  2. (figuratively, colloquial) to equip in abundance (e.g. with knowledge, instruments, etc.)
Related terms
  • чини́ть impf (činítʹ), учини́ть pf (učinítʹ)
  • чиниться (činítʹsja)

Etymology 2

на- (na-) +‎ чини́ть (činítʹ, “to repair”)

Verb

начини́ть • (načinítʹ) pf (imperfective начинивать)

  1. (colloquial) to sharpen (a usually large quantity of something)
  2. (colloquial) to repair (a usually large quantity of something)
Related terms
  • чинка (čínka)
  • чини́ть impf (činítʹ), почини́ть pf (počinítʹ)
  • чиниться (činítʹsja)
