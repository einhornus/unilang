ряби́ть (24036)


Etymology

рябь (rjabʹ) + -и́ть (-ítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪˈbʲitʲ]

Verb

ряби́ть • (rjabítʹ) impf

  1. (transitive or intr) to ripple
  2. to speckle
  3. (colloquial) to be speckled
  4. to pockmark
  5. (impersonal) to have spots in the eyes

Related terms

  • рябь (rjabʹ), рябо́й (rjabój)
  • ря́бина (rjábina), ряби́нка (rjabínka)
