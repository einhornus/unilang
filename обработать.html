обрабо́тать (9575)


Etymology[edit]

об- (ob-) +‎ рабо́тать (rabótatʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɐbrɐˈbotətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

обрабо́тать • (obrabótatʹ) pf (imperfective обраба́тывать)

  1. to process, to treat, to work on
  2. to cultivate (soil)
  3. to finish, to polish
  4. to cleanse (a wound), to apply antiseptic
  5. (colloquial) to indoctrinate, to influence

Related terms[edit]

  • обрабо́тка (obrabótka)
  • обрабатывание (obrabátyvanije)
