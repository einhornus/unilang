струя́ -> jet, stream
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: струи
Genitive: струи



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic струя́ (struja), from Proto-Slavic *struja.



Definitions (English):
  • jet, air blast (stream of fluid)
    • spurt, squirt, stream
      • current, efflux, flow
