сло́во -> word
Noun, declension 2c
Plural: слова
Genitive: слова



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *slȍvo (“word”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱléwos (“fame”).



Definitions (English):
  • word, term,
    • гру́бое сло́во ― grúboje slóvo ― harsh word, устаревшее сло́во ― ustarévšeje slóvo ― obsolete word
  • speech, address
    • promise,
      • наруша́ть сло́во ― narušátʹ slóvo ― to break one's word, to go back upon / on one's word (breaking promise or contract)
