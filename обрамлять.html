обрамля́ть (29979)


Etymology

From обрамить (obrámitʹ) +‎ -ять (-játʹ) or from обрамить (obramítʹ) +‎ -ять (-játʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐbrɐˈmlʲætʲ]

Verb

обрамля́ть • (obramljátʹ) impf (perfective обрамить or обрамить)

  1. to frame (a painting), to put inside a frame
    Perfectives: обрамить (obrámitʹ), обрамить (obramítʹ)
  2. (figuratively, literary) to encircle, to frame (e.g. of light around a face, trees around an alley, etc.)
    Perfective: обрамить (obramítʹ) (only)
