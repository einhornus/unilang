докла́дывать (3423)


Etymology

до- (do-) +‎ кладывать (kladyvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dɐˈkladɨvətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

докла́дывать • (dokládyvatʹ) impf (perfective доложи́ть)

  1. to report, to announce (to notify formally)
  2. to add more, to put more (e.g. intó a portion)

Related terms

  • докла́д (doklád)
