Noun[edit]

осложне́ние • (osložnénije) n inan (genitive осложне́ния, nominative plural осложне́ния, genitive plural осложне́ний)

  1. complication (the act or process of complicating; the state of being complicated; intricate or confused relation of parts; entanglement)
  2. (medicine) complication (a disease or diseases, or adventitious circumstances or conditions, coexistent with and modifying a primary disease, but not necessarily connected with it)
