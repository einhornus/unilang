у́зкий -> narrow
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *ǫzъkъ, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂énǵʰus. Cognates include Armenian անձուկ (anjuk, “narrow”), German eng (“narrow”), Irish cúng (“narrow”), Latin angustus (“narrow”), Sanskrit अंहु (aṃhu, “narrow”).



Definitions (English):
  • narrow
    • tight
      • (short form only) too narrow, too tight (of clothing and shoes)
        • мне э́ти штаны́ узки ― mne éti štaný úzki ― these trousers are too tight for me
