полови́к (19012)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəlɐˈvʲik]

Noun

полови́к • (polovík) m inan (genitive половика́, nominative plural половики́, genitive plural половико́в)

  1. floormat (a mat that is laid on the floor)
