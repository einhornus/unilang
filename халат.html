халат -> dressing-gown, overall, white coat
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: халаты
Genitive: халата́



Etymology: A Turkic borrowing. Compare Ottoman Turkish خلعت‎ (hilat, “kaftan”), Azerbaijani xələt (“dressing gown”), Uzbek xalat (“robe”). Ultimately from Arabic خِلْعَة‎ (ḵilʿa, “robe of honor”).



Definitions (English):
  • dressing gown, bathrobe (for men or women)
    • dress (any kind that is knee-length to full and that opens down the front)
      • smock
        • surgical gown
          • oriental robe
