Verb[edit]

вка́лывать • (vkályvatʹ) impf (perfective вколоть)

  1. to stick (in, into)
  2. (colloquial) to inject (to), to give a shot (of)

Verb[edit]

вка́лывать • (vkályvatʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to work hard, to sweat (one's guts out)

Synonyms[edit]

  • иша́чить (išačitʹ), мантулить (mantulitʹ)
