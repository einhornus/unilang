недопи́ть (16736)


Etymology[edit]

недо- (nedo-) +‎ пить (pítʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪdɐˈpʲitʲ]

Verb[edit]

недопи́ть • (nedopítʹ) pf (imperfective недопивать)

  1. to not drink completely
  2. to not drink enough, to not drink until satisfied
