выруба́ть (21621)


Etymology

вы́рубить (výrubitʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɨrʊˈbatʲ]

Verb

выруба́ть • (vyrubátʹ) impf (perfective вы́рубить)

  1. to cut down, to fell
  2. to cut out
  3. (hole, window etc) to make
  4. (ore, coal) to cut, to hew
  5. (colloquial) to turn off, to switch off, to cut off

Related terms

  • вырубка (výrubka)
  • руби́ть (rubítʹ)
  • ру́бка (rúbka)
  • руба́нок (rubánok)
  • рубище (rúbišče)
