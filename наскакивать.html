Verb[edit]

наска́кивать • (naskákivatʹ) impf (perfective наскочи́ть) (на (na) + accusative)

  1. to pounce on, to jump onto
  2. (colloquial, figuratively) to attack with unfounded accusations
  3. (colloquial) to collide with, to run into
  4. (colloquial) to stumble into (a bad situation)

Verb[edit]

наска́кивать • (naskákivatʹ) impf (perfective наскакать) (на (na) + accusative)

  1. to jump into (e.g. a fence) while on horseback
  2. to jump at from multiple sides (of a group)
