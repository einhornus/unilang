завязть -> to tie, start, get involved in
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я завяж
Ты завжешь
Past: завязл/завязла



Etymologies:
  • за- (za-) +‎ вяза́ть (vjazátʹ)
  • завязнуть (zavjáznutʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)


Definitions (English):
  • to tie up, to fasten
    • (figuratively) to start, to strike up,
      • завяза́ть знако́мство ― zavjazátʹ znakómstvo ― to strike up an acquaintance
    • (colloquial) to quit (a bad habit or activity)
      • to stick (in), to sink (in)
        • (colloquial) to get stuck (in)
