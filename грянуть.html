гря́нуть (7343)


Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *grędnǫti.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈɡrʲanʊtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

гря́нуть • (grjánutʹ) pf

  1. to break out, to burst out, to burst forth
    гром гря́нул ― grom grjánul ― there was a clap/crash/peal of thunder
    гря́нул вы́стрел ― grjánul výstrel ― a shot rang out
  2. (song, orchestra) to strike up

Related terms

  • грясти́ (grjastí)
