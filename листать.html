листа́ть (6197)


Etymology

лист (list) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈstatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

листа́ть • (listátʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to leaf through́, to flip through́ (a book, manuscript, etc.)
  2. (colloquial) to skim, to browse (a book, manuscript, etc.)

Related terms

  • лист (list)
  • листо́вка (listóvka)
  • листо́к (listók)
  • листово́й (listovój)
