велкий -> great
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *velikъ.



Definitions (English):
  • large, big
    • (short form only) too big larger than necessary.
      • Э́ти сапоги́ мне велики.Éti sapogí mne velikí. These boots are too big for me.
    • great, outstanding
