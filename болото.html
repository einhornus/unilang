болото -> marsh
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лето)

Plural: болота́
Genitive: болота́



Etymology: From Old East Slavic боло́то (boloto), from Proto-Slavic *bolto (“swamp mud”). Doublet of бла́то (bláto), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic. Cognates include Slovene bláto (“swamp mud”), Polish błoto (“mud”).



Definitions (English):
  • bog, swamp, marsh, quagmire, mire
