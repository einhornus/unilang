ганте́ль (24771)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Hantel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡɐnˈtɛlʲ] (phonetic respelling: гантэль)

Noun

ганте́ль • (gantɛlʹ) f inan (genitive ганте́ли, nominative plural ганте́ли, genitive plural ганте́лей)

  1. (sports) dumbbell
  2. (figuratively) anything shaped like a dumbbell
