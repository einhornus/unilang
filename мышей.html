мыше́й (8114)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɨˈʂɛj]

Etymology 1

Noun

мыше́й • (myšéj) f anim pl

  1. genitive plural of мышь (myšʹ)
  2. accusative plural of мышь (myšʹ)

Etymology 2

Cognate with synonymous Ukrainian мишій (myšíj), мишей (myšéj), мушій (mušíj), муший (mušýj), Belarusian мяшэй (mjašej), мяжэй (mjažej). All perhaps from the word for "fly (insect)" – Russian му́ха (múxa), Ukrainian му́ха (múxa), paralleled in Serbo-Croatian muhar / мухар, Slovak mušec. Some forms are influenced by the word for mouse — Russian мышь (myšʹ), Ukrainian миша (mýša).

Noun

мыше́й • (myšéj) m inan (genitive мыше́я, nominative plural мыше́и, genitive plural мыше́ев)

  1. foxtail, bristle grass, Setaria gen. et spp.
    Synonym: щетинник (ščetínnik)
