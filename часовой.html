часовй -> hour (adj), watch, clock (adj), (as noun) sentry
Adjective



Etymology: From час (čas) +‎ -овой (-ovój) (meaning "hour" (relational)) or from часы́ (časý) +‎ -овой (-ovój) (meaning "clock, watch" (relational)).



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) hour,
    • часово́й по́яс ― časovój pójas ― time zone (literally, “hour zone”)
  • by the hour
    • (relational) watch, clock,
      • часово́й ма́стер ― časovój máster ― watchmaker
    • sentry, sentinel
