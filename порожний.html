поро́жний -> empty (coll)
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic порожьнъ (porožĭnŭ), from earlier пороздьнъ (porozdĭnŭ), inherited from Proto-Slavic *porzdьnъ. Doublet of праздный (prázdnyj), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • empty (devoid of content)
