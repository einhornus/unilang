восто́к -> east
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: восто́ки
Genitive: восто́ка



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic въстокъ (vŭstokŭ, “rising, east”), a calque of Ancient Greek ἀνατολή (anatolḗ, “rising, east”). Related to встать (vstatʹ).
