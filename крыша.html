крша -> roof
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: крши
Genitive: крши



Etymology: From Old East Slavic [Term?], from Proto-Slavic *kryša, from *kryti (“to cover, hide”) + *-ša (< *-sja).



Definitions (English):
  • roof, подводи́ть/
    • подвести́ дом под кры́шу ― podvodítʹ/podvestí dom pod krýšu ― to roof the house
  • (figuratively) house, dwelling place
    • (colloquial, figuratively) head, mind,
      • у па́рня кры́ша не в поря́дке ― u párnja krýša ne v porjádke ― the guy's not right in the head
    • (slang, criminal) protection someone who provides protection
