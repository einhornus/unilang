казённый -> belonging to the State
Adjective



Etymology: From казна́ (kazná) +‎ -ный (-nyj) derived as if казна́ (kazná) had a reducible stem, with hypothetical unreduced genitive plural *казён (*kazjón) (in fact, the noun has no genitive plural).



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) state, government
    • official, public
      • formal, perfunctory
        • commonplace
          • (military) breech (of a rifle)
