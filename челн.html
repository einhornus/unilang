челн (29951)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *čьlnъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡ɕɵln]

Noun

чёлн • (čoln) masculine gender">m inan (genitive челна, nominative plural челны, genitive plural челнов)

  1. boat, canoe, dugout (usually hollowed out of a single log)

Related terms

  • челно́к (čelnók)
