позвони́ть (946)


Etymology

по- (po-) + звони́ть (zvonítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəzvɐˈnʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

позвони́ть • (pozvonítʹ) pf (imperfective звони́ть)

  1. to ring (to cause to ring) (в (v) + accusative, a bell, someone's house, etc.)
  2. to ring (to make a ringing sound) (of a bell, of the telephone, etc.)
  3. to call (to contact by telephone) (+ dative, someone)

Related terms

  • звон (zvon), звонок (zvonok)
