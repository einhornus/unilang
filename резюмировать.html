резюми́ровать (16349)


Etymology

Perhaps from German resümieren + -овать (-ovatʹ); see also -и́ровать (-írovatʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪzʲʉˈmʲirəvətʲ]

Verb

резюми́ровать • (rezjumírovatʹ) impf or pf

  1. to sum up, to summarize, to recapitulate

Related terms

  • резюме́ (rezjumɛ́)
