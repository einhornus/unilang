Verb[edit]

стя́гивать • (stjágivatʹ) impf (perfective стяну́ть)

  1. to tighten, to tie up, to strap down/up
  2. to gather, to draw up
    стя́гивать си́лы ― stjágivatʹ síly ― to draw up forces
  3. to pull off/away

Related terms[edit]

  • стяжка (stjážka)
