мано́к (22089)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈnok]

Noun

мано́к • (manók) m inan (genitive манка́, nominative plural манки́, genitive plural манко́в)

  1. hunter's whistle, call
  2. (figuratively) bait
Related terms
  • мани́ть (manítʹ)
