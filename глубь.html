глубь (6345)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *glǫb, from Proto-Indo-European *glewbʰ-, whence English cleave, cleft and glyph.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡlupʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

глубь • (glubʹ) f inan (genitive глуби, uncountable)

  1. depth
    в глубь страны ― v glubʹ straný ― (towardś) inland

Related terms

  • глубина́ (glubina)
