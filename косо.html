ко́со (8061)


Etymology

косо́й (kosój) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkosə]

Adverb

ко́со • (kóso)

  1. slantwise, obliquely, aslant, askew, asquint
    смотре́ть ко́со ― smotrétʹ kóso ― to look sideways, to look askance, to frown upoń smb., to scowl

Adjective

ко́со • (kóso)

  1. short neuter singular of косо́й (kosój)
