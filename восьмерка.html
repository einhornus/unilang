восьмерка (19593)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐsʲˈmʲɵrkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

восьмёрка • (vosʹmjórka) f inan (genitive восьмёрки, nominative plural восьмёрки, genitive plural восьмёрок)

  1. number eight, eight (e.g. cards), figure of eight
  2. (card games) eight
    козырна́я восьмёрка ― kozyrnája vosʹmjórka ― the eight of trumps
  3. No 8 bus, tram, etc
