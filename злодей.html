злодей -> villain
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: злодеи
Genitive: злодея



Etymology: From зло (zlo) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ -дей (-dej), combining form of де́ять (déjatʹ, “to do”).



Definitions (English):
  • malefactor, evildoer, villain, miscreant, scoundrel
