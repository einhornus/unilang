постоянно -> constantly, perpetually

Etymology: From the verb постоя́ть (postojátʹ), from по- (po-) + сто́я́ть (stojátʹ), from Proto-Slavic *stojati (“to stand”), from Proto-Indo-European *stoh₂-.



Definitions (English):
  • constantly, always
    • short neuter singular of постоя́нный (postojánnyj, “constant”)
