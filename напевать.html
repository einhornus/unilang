напева́ть (7676)


Etymology

напе́ть (napétʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəpʲɪˈvatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

напева́ть • (napevátʹ) impf (perfective напе́ть)

  1. to hum (a melody)
  2. (colloquial) to sing (demonstrating a melody)
  3. to have one's singing recorded (on)
    напева́ть диск ― napevátʹ disk ― to record a disk (of one's singing)
  4. (colloquial, derogatory) to slander

Synonyms

  • (hum): мурлы́кать (murlýkatʹ)
  • (sing): петь (petʹ)
  • (record): запи́сывать (zapísyvatʹ)
  • (slander): нагова́ривать (nagovárivatʹ)

Related terms

  • напе́в (napév)
  • напевный (napévnyj)
