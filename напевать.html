напе́вать -> to sing, sing quietly, hum
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я напе́ваю

Past: напе́вал/напе́вала



Etymology: напе́ть (napétʹ) +‎ -ва́ть (-vátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to hum (a melody)
    • (colloquial) to sing (demonstrating a melody)
      • to have one's singing recorded (on)
        • напева́ть диск ― napevátʹ disk ― to record a disk (of one's singing)
      • (colloquial, derogatory) to slander
