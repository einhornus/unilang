франт (22733)


Etymology

Borrowed from Polish language">Polish frant (“rogue, clown, comedian”), ultimately from a diminutive of Czech František (given name).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [frant]

Noun

франт • (frant) m inan (genitive фра́нта, nominative plural фра́нты, genitive plural фра́нтов)

  1. dandy, fop, beau
