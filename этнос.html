э́тнос (31598)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek ἔθνος (éthnos), from Proto-Indo-European *swedh-no-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈɛtnəs]

Noun

э́тнос • (étnos) m inan (genitive э́тноса, nominative plural э́тносы, genitive plural э́тносов)

  1. ethnicity, ethnic group, tribe, ethnos

Related terms

  • этни́ческий (etníčeskij)
