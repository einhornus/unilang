князь -> prince
Noun, declension 2c (pattern город)

Plural: князья
Genitive: князя



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *kъnędzь, borrowed from Germanic, from Proto-Germanic *kuningaz. The /dz/ is due to the progressive palatalization.Cognate to Old Church Slavonic кънѧзь (kŭnęzĭ).Russian княги́ня (knjagínja, “princess”) has preserved the original, Germanic g sound.
