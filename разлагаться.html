разлагаться -> to decompose, disintegrate
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Past: разлагался/разлагалась



Etymology: разлага́ть (razlagátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to decompose, (mathematics) to be factorized
    • to decompose, to rot, to decay
      • to get corrupted/demoralized
        • passive of разлага́ть (razlagátʹ)
