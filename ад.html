ад -> hell
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: а́ды
Genitive: а́да



Etymology: From Old East Slavic адъ (adŭ), from Old Church Slavonic адъ (adŭ), from Ancient Greek ᾍδης (Hāidēs).



Definitions (English):
  • Hades,
    • сошествие Христа в ад – Christ's Harrowing of Hell
  • hell (the abode of the damned also used figuratively)
