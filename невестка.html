неве́стка -> daughter-in-law, sister-in-law (brother’s wife)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: неве́стки
Genitive: неве́стки



Etymology: From невеста (nevésta) +‎ -ка (-ka). Cognate with Belarusian нявестка (njavjéstka).
