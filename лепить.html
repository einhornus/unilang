лепть -> to model, mould
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я лепл
Ты лпишь
Past: лепл/лепла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *lěpiti.



Definitions (English):
  • to model, to sculpt, to fashion, to shape. Perfective:
    • вы́лепить (výlepitʹ)
  • to build, to make (cells, a nest, etc.) Perfective:
    • слепить (slepítʹ)
  • (colloquial) to stick on. Perfective:
    • налепить (nalepítʹ)
  • (colloquial) to slap, to smack. Perfectives:
    • залепить (zalepítʹ), влепить (vlepítʹ)
  • (colloquial) to babble,
    • лепи́ть вздор ― lepítʹ vzdor ― to talk nonsense
