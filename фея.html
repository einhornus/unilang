фе́я -> fairy
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: фе́и
Genitive: фе́и



Etymology: Via German Fee or directly from French fée, from Vulgar Latin Fāta (“goddess of fate”).



Definitions (English):
  • fairy
    • (figuratively) enchantress (a good-hearted, charming woman)
