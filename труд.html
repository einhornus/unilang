труд -> labour
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: труды́
Genitive: труда́



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic трудъ (trudŭ), from Proto-Slavic *trudъ, from Proto-Indo-European *trewd-, whence English thrust.



Definitions (English):
  • work, labor/labour
    • effort, pains
      • handicrafts
