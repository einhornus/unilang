чека (9849)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡ɕɪˈka]

Noun

чека • (čeká) f inan (genitive чеки, nominative plural чеки, genitive plural *чек)

  1. linchpin
  2. safety pin

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈt͡ɕekə]

Noun

чека • (čéka) m inan or m anim

  1. genitive singular of чек (ček)
  2. animate accusative singular of чек (ček)
