шевельну́ться -> to stir
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Past: шевельну́лся/шевельну́лась



Etymology: шевельнуть (ševelʹnútʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to stir, to move,
    • он не шевельну́лся ― on ne ševelʹnúlsja ― he never stirred he did not even budge
  • (figuratively) to awaken, to show signs of life
    • (figuratively) (thoughts, feelings, etc.) to appear
      • passive of шевельнуть (ševelʹnútʹ)
