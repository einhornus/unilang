ххот -> loud laughter, guffaw
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: ххоты
Genitive: ххота



Etymology: According to Vasmer, from Proto-Slavic *xoxotъ, related to *xoxotati (“to laugh loudly”).



Definitions (English):
  • laughter, roar, guffaw
