метр -> metre (measurement)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ме́тры
Genitive: ме́тра



Etymology: Borrowed from French mètre.



Definitions (English):
  • meter/metre ( SI unit of measure)
    • meter stick (metre stick)
      • складной метр ― skladnój metr ― a folding meter stick
    • tape measure, measuring tape (at least 1 meter long)
