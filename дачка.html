да́чка (22087)


Etymology

Diminutive of Proto-Slavic *dъ̏ťi (cognate with Ukrainian дочка́ (dočká), Russian до́чка (dóčka)), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *duktē, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰugh₂tḗr.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dat͡ʂˈka]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

дачка́ • (dačká) f anim (genitive дачкі́, nominative plural до́чкі)

  1. daughter

Related terms

  • ха́та, та́чка, да́чка (xata, tačka, dačka)
