да́чка (22085)


Etymology

Diminutive of Proto-Slavic *dъ̏ťi (cognate with Ukrainian до́чка (dočká), Russian до́чка (dóčka)), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *duktē, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰugh₂tḗr.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dat͡ʂˈka]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

да́чка • (dačká) f anim (genitive дачкі, nominative plural дочкі)

  1. daughter

Related terms
