кося́к (6958)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈsʲak]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

кося́к • (kosják) m inan (genitive косяка́, nominative plural косяки́, genitive plural косяко́в, diminutive косячок)

  1. shoal, school (a group of fish)
  2. flock (a group of birds)
  3. herd (a group of horses)
  4. jamb, cant, cheek, door-post
  5. (slang) joint, a marijuana cigarette
  6. (slang) fault, mistake
