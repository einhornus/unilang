слу́шаться (4209)


Etymology

слу́шать (slúšatʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- whence English listen and slave.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsluʂət͡sə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

слу́шаться • (slúšatʹsja) impf (perfective послу́шаться)

  1. to obey, to take (someone's) advice, to listen to
    слу́шаться роди́телей
    to obey one's parents
  2. (imperfective only) to obey, to be under control (of a mechanism or body part)
  3. passive of слу́шать (slúšatʹ)
