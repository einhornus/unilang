нагря́нуть (11484)


Etymology

на- (na-) + гря́нуть (grjánutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈɡrʲanʊtʲ]

Verb

нагря́нуть • (nagrjánutʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial) to come unexpectedly (out of the blue)
    нагря́нули го́сти ― nagrjánuli gósti ― guests arrived unexpectedly
  2. (colloquial) to break out, (cold) to strike

Related terms

  • гря́нуть (grjánutʹ)
  • гряду́щий (grjadúščij)
