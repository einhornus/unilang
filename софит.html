софи́т (29105)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian soffitto (“ceiling”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈfʲit]

Noun

софи́т • (sofít) m inan (genitive софи́та, nominative plural софи́ты, genitive plural софи́тов)

  1. stage lighting instrument (e.g. spotlight, floodlight)
  2. metal rack for hanging light fixtures above a stage, raised and lowered by an electric actuator
  3. (architecture) soffit
