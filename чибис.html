чи́бис (20370)


Etymology[edit]

Onomatopoeic

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈt͡ɕibʲɪs]

Noun[edit]

чи́бис • (číbis) m anim (genitive чи́биса, nominative plural чи́бисы, genitive plural чи́бисов)

  1. lapwing, pewit (bird belonging to the subfamily Vanellinae)
