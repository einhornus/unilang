ква (26399)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kva]

Interjection

ква • (kva)

  1. croak, ribbit: an imitation of the sound made by a frog.
    ква ква ― kva kva ― ribbit ribbit

Synonyms

  • квак (kvak)

Related terms

  • квакать (kvákatʹ)
