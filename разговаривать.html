разговаривать -> to converse, talk
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я разговариваю
Ты разговариваешь
Past: разговаривал/разговаривала



Etymology: разговори́ть (razgovorítʹ) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • (intransitive) to speak, to talk, to converse (with)
    • (intransitive) to be on speaking terms (with)
      • (transitive) to dissuade (someone)
        • (transitive) to get (someone) talking
