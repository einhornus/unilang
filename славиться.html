слави́ться -> to be famous for

Etymology: сла́вить (slávitʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to be famous (for), to be famed (for), to be renowned (for), to have a reputation (for)
    • (imperative) to glory (to)
      • сла́вься, наш вели́́кий наро́д! ― slávʹsja, naš velíkij naród! ― glory to our great people!
    • passive of сла́вить (slávitʹ)
