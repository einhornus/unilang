зада́вать -> to set, assign (sth to s.o.)
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я за́даю
Ты задаёшь
Past: зада́вал/зада́вала



Etymology: за́да́ть (zadátʹ) +‎ -ва́ть (-vátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to set, to assign (an exercise, a lesson)
    • Э́то нам не задава́ли! ― Éto nam ne zadaváli! ― It was not our homework!
  • to ask (a question)
    • задава́ть вопро́с/вопро́сы ― zadavátʹ voprós/voprósy ― to ask a question/questions
  • to pose (a problem)
    • (colloquial) (a dinner, a banquet) to give, to throw
      • (a punishment) to give, to administer,
        • задава́ть трёпку ― zadavátʹ trjópku ― to give a thrashing, to give a dressing-down
      • to preset, to specify, to assign
