ня́нька (10537)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnʲænʲkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ня́нька • (njánʹka) f anim (genitive ня́ньки, nominative plural ня́ньки, genitive plural ня́нек)

  1. Diminutive of ня́ня (njánja)
    У семи́ ня́нек дитя́ без глаза. ― U semí njánek ditjá bez gláza. ― Too many cooks spoil the broth. / Everybody's business is nobody's business. (Russian proverb)
    Ня́нька забо́тится о дву́х младе́нцах. ― Njánʹka zabótitsja o dvux mladéncax. ― A (young) nanny looks after two infants.
