загранпаспорт (32010)


Etymology

Acronym of заграни́чный паспорт (zagraníčnyj pásport), literally "foreign passport" or "passport for abroad".

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˌɡranˈpaspərt]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

загранпаспорт • (zagranpásport) m inan (genitive загранпаспорта, nominative plural загранпаспорта, genitive plural загранпаспортов)

  1. passport (for traveling)

Usage notes

  • In Russia, there are two distinct types of passports: заграни́чный паспорт (zagraníčnyj pásport) (i.e. foreign passport) and вну́тренний паспорт (vnútrennij pásport) (i.e. internal or domestic passport). The former is used for traveling abroad and the latter is a standard Id.
