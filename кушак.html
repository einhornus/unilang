кушак (27239)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic кушакъ (kušakŭ), from Crimean Tatar кушак or Turkish kuşak

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kʊˈʂak]

Noun

кушак • (kušák) m inan (genitive кушака́, nominative plural кушаки́, genitive plural кушако́в)

  1. belt, girdle, sash
