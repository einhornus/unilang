соро́дич (19176)


Etymology

со- (so-) +‎ ро́дич (ródič)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈrodʲɪt͡ɕ]

Noun

соро́дич • (soródič) masculine gender">m anim (genitive соро́дича, nominative plural соро́дичи, genitive plural соро́дичей)

  1. relative, kinsman
  2. fellow countryman, compatriot
