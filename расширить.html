расши́рить (6035)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic расширити (rasširiti), asynchronically analyzable as рас- (ras-) +‎ ширить (šíritʹ, “to widen”). See ширь (širʹ, “breadth, width”) for more

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈʂːɨrʲɪtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

расши́рить • (rasšíritʹ) pf (imperfective расширя́ть)

  1. (transitive) to widen, to broaden
  2. (transitive) to increase
  3. (transitive) to broaden, to enlarge, to extend, to expand

Synonyms

  • (to increase): нарасти́ть (narastítʹ)
  • (to expand): разверну́ть (razvernútʹ)
