вершо́к (19097)


Etymology

Per Vasmer, from верх (verx, “top”) +‎ -ок (-ók), probably originally "length of the upper phalanx of the index finger".

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪrˈʂok]

Noun

вершо́к • (veršók) m inan (genitive вершка́, nominative plural вершки́, genitive plural вершко́в)

  1. an old Russian unit of length approximately equal to 4.445 centimeters or 1/16 of an arshin
