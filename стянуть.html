стяну́ть (6397)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ тяну́ть (tjanútʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲtʲɪˈnutʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

стяну́ть • (stjanútʹ) pf (imperfective стя́гивать)

  1. to tighten, to tie up, to strap down/up
  2. to gather, to draw up
    стяну́ть си́лы ― stjanútʹ síly ― to draw up forces
  3. to pull off/away
  4. (colloquial) to filch, to swipe, to pinch

Related terms

  • стягивание (stjagivánije)
  • стяжка (stjážka)
