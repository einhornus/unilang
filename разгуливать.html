разгу́ливать (12378)


Etymology

разгулять (razguljátʹ) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐzˈɡulʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

разгу́ливать • (razgúlivatʹ) impf (perfective разгулять)

  1. (colloquial, imperfective only) to stroll about/around, to walk about/around
  2. (colloquial, imperfective only) to walk around unhindered, to be on the loose
    по го́роду разгу́ливают престу́пники ― po górodu razgúlivajut prestúpniki ― criminals are on the loose in town
  3. (colloquial, imperfective only) to spread around, to circulate (a rumor, etc.)
  4. (colloquial, imperfective only) to blow
    по коридо́ру разгу́ливает сквозня́к ― po koridóru razgúlivajet skvoznják ― there is a strong draught/draft passing through́ the corridor
  5. to keep from falling asleep, to wake up
  6. to cast awaý, to lay aside, to put out of mind
    разгу́ливать грусть/тоску ― razgúlivatʹ grustʹ/toskú ― to cast one's gloom awaý

Related terms

  • разгу́л (razgúl), разгульный (razgúlʹnyj)
  • разгулье (razgúlʹje)
  • гуля́ть (guljátʹ)
  • гуля́нье (guljánʹje)
  • гуля́ка (guljáka)
