шпингале́т (29907)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Spaniolett, from French espagnolette (“Spanish latch”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂpʲɪnɡɐˈlʲet]

Noun

шпингале́т • (špingalét) masculine gender">m anim or masculine gender">m inan (genitive шпингале́та, nominative plural шпингале́ты, genitive plural шпингале́тов)

  1. (inanimate) bolt, catch, latch, fastener
  2. (animate, colloquial or slang) lad, youth, teenager
