кла́няться (5957)


Etymology

From dialectal кланять (klánjatʹ, “to make bow”) +‎ -ся (-sja), inherited from Proto-Slavic *klanjati. Iterative of клони́ть (klonítʹ), with unexpected stress shift and root vowel lengthening.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈklanʲɪt͡sə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

кла́няться • (klánjatʹsja) impf (perfective поклони́ться)

  1. to bow (to, beforé), to greet (to bend oneself as a gesture of respect or deference)
  2. to give/send regards to someone
    Кла́няйтесь ему́ от́ меня́.
    Klánjajtesʹ jemú ot menjá.
    Give him my regards.
  3. to cringe (beforé); to humiliate oneself (beforé); to humbly beg

Related terms

  • покло́н (poklón)
