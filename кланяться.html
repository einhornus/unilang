клняться -> to bow
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я клняюсь
Ты клняешься
Past: клнялся/клнялась



Etymology: From dialectal кланять (klánjatʹ, “to make bow”) +‎ -ся (-sja), inherited from Proto-Slavic *klanjati. Iterative of клонить (klonítʹ), with unexpected stress shift and root vowel lengthening.



Definitions (English):
  • to bow (to, before), to greet (to bend oneself as a gesture of respect or deference)
    • to give/send regards to someone.
      • Кла́няйтесь ему́ от меня. Klánjajtesʹ jemú ot menjá. Give him my regards.
    • to cringe (before) to humiliate oneself (before) to humbly beg
