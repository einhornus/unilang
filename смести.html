смести́ (24624)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [smʲɪˈsʲtʲi]

Etymology 1

Verb

смести́ • (smestí) pf (imperfective смета́ть)

  1. to brush off, to sweep off, to wipe off
  2. to carry awaý (by wind, etc.)
  3. (figuratively) to destroy, to dispel, to eliminate
  4. to collect intó one place (by raking or sweeping)
Related terms
  • мести impf (mestí)

Etymology 2

Verb

смести́ • (smestí)

  1. second-person singular imperative perfective of смести́ть (smestítʹ)
