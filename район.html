район -> region
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: райо́ны
Genitive: райо́на



Etymology: First attested in 1865. Borrowed from French rayon, from rai + -on, from Latin radius.



Definitions (English):
  • district
    • region, area
