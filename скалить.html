Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈskalʲɪtʲ]

Verb

ска́лить • (skálitʹ) impf

  1. to show (one's teeth)
    ска́лить зу́бы ― skálitʹ zúby ― (1) to bare one's teeth; (2) (colloquial) to grin; (3) (colloquial) to laugh

Synonyms

  • оскаливать (oskálivatʹ)
