наибо́льший (6274)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəɪˈbolʲʂɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

наибо́льший • (naibólʹšij)

  1. superlative degree of большо́й (bolʹšój):
    1. the greatest, the largest, the most
    2. the worst (in negative context)
    3. maximal, maximum
