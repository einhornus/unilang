ма́терный (14387)


Etymology

From матер- (máter-) +‎ -ный (-nyj), based off of the oblique stem of мать (matʹ, “mother”); compare мат (mat, “obscene language”), derived from the same word.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmatʲɪrnɨj]

Adjective

ма́терный • (máternyj)

  1. (colloquial) obscene, bad, dirty, foul

Related terms

  • мат m (mat)
  • матерщина f (materščína)
  • мать f (matʹ)
