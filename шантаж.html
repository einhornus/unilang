шанта́ж (14031)


Etymology

Borrowed from French chantage.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂɐnˈtaʂ]

Noun

шанта́ж • (šantáž) m inan (genitive шантажа́, nominative plural шантажи́, genitive plural шантаже́й)

  1. blackmail (payment of money exacted by means of intimidation)
