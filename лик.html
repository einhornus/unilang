лик (7377)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *likъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲik]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

лик • (lik) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ли́ка, nominative plural ли́ки, genitive plural ли́ков)

  1. (obsolete, poetic) face
  2. image, representation of face on the icon

Related terms

  • лицо́ (licó)
