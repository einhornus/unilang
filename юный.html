юный -> young
Adjective



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic юнъ (junŭ), from Proto-Slavic *junъ, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂yuh₁en-. Displaced the inherited Old East Slavic унъ (unŭ), оунъ (unŭ).



Definitions (English):
  • young, youthful (in the early part of life or growth)
    • juvenile
