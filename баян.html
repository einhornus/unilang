баян -> bayan, Russian accordion
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: баяны
Genitive: баяна



Etymology: The “old joke” sense originated from a joke (Internet meme) at http://www.anekdot.ru — "Хорони́ли тёщу — порва́ли два бая́на" (when we buried my mother-in-law, we broke two accordions).
