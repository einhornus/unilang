Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Definitions (English):
  • to be divorced, to divorce, to obtain a divorce (
    • с (s) + instrumental) Светла́́на разво́дится с Никола́ем. Svetlána razvóditsja s Nikolájem. Svetlana is divorcing Nikolay
  • to grow in number, to breed
    • passive of разводи́́ть (razvodítʹ)
