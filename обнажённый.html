обнажённый -> naked
Adjective


Definitions (English):
  • past passive perfective participle of обнажи́ть (obnažítʹ)
    • naked, bare (of a person)
      • (of plants) not having leaves
        • (figuratively) sincere, candid
          • naked (vice etc. inanimate)
