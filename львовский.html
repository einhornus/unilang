льво́вский (23970)


Etymology

Льво́в (Lʹvov) + -ский (-skij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲvofskʲɪj]

Adjective

льво́вский • (lʹvóvskij) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) Lviv, Lvov (city in Ukraine)
  2. (relational) Lvov (Russian surname, e.g. of Alexei Lvov, Russian composer)
  3. (relational) Lvovo, Lvovsky, Lvovskoye (name of several settlements in Russia)

Related terms

  • Льво́в (Lʹvov)
