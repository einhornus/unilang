тот -> that

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *tъ, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *tas, from Proto-Indo-European *só, *séh₂, *tód (“this, that”). Cognate with Latvian tas, Lithuanian tas, ta, Sanskrit स (sá), Old English se (whence English the).



Definitions (English):
  • that, those
    • the one
      • the other
