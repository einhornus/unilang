затеять -> to undertake, start, organize (coll)
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я затею
Ты затеешь
Past: затеял/затеяла



Etymology: Cognate with Belarusian заце́яць (zacjéjacʹ, “to undertake”) and (with a different prefix) Ukrainian ви́тіяти (výtijaty, “to undertake”). Per Vasmer, the base verb (Proto-Slavic *tějati) is possibly related to Latvian tievêt (“to strive for”), tĩtît (“to be stubborn, to be annoyed”) (1sg. tĩtĩju), tītinât (“to tease, to annoy”), Lithuanian titinoti (“to brag”).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) to undertake, to start,
    • зате́ять недо́брое ― zatéjatʹ nedóbroje ― to misbehave, to be up to no good, зате́ять дра́ку ― zatéjatʹ dráku ― to start a fight
  • (colloquial) to decide (to do something)
    • (colloquial) to venture
