я́щер (21177)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *aščerъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈjæɕːɪr]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

я́щер • (jáščer) m anim (genitive я́щера, nominative plural я́щеры, genitive plural я́щеров)

  1. (a large) lizard (reptilé)
  2. pangolin (mammal)

Synonyms

  • (pangolin): панголин (pangolín)

Related terms

  • я́щерица (jáščerica)
  • я́щерка (jáščerka)
