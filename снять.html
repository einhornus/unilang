снять -> to take off, photograph, rent
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c/c

Я сним
Ты снмешь
Past: снл/сня́л



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *sъnęti, from *sъ(n)- (see *sъ(n)) + *ęti. Reanalyzed as с- (s-) + -нять (-njatʹ), from which other words in -нять (-njatʹ) were created, such as наня́ть (nanjátʹ), обня́ть (obnjátʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to take off, to take away, to take down
    • to take, to make
      • to photograph, to film
        • to rent (e.g. an apartment)
