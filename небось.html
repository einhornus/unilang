небо́сь -> probably (coll)

Etymology: Contraction of не бо́йся (ne bójsja, literally “do not fear”).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) I suppose, most likely, probably, I dare say.
    • У вас там небо́сь снег идёт. U vas tam nebósʹ sneg idjót. I suppose it's snowing there at your place.
  • (colloquial) sure, no fear.
    • Небо́сь, не замёрзнешь! Nebósʹ, ne zamjórznešʹ! Don't worry, you won't get frostbite!
