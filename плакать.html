пла́кать -> to cry, weep, p: to begin to cry
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я плачу
Ты пла́чешь
Past: пла́кал/пла́кала



Etymology: From Old East Slavic плакати (plakati), from Proto-Slavic *plakati.



Definitions (English):
  • to cry, to weep
    • to mourn, to weep
      • in expressions.
        • Пла́кали денежки! ― Plákali dénežki! ― The money has simply vanished. / You can kiss your money good-bye. / You can whistle for your money. Па́лка по нему́ пла́чет. ― Pálka po nemú pláčet. ― He's asking for it. / What he needs is a thrashing.хоть пла́чь! ― xotʹ plačʹ! ― it is enough to make you weep!пла́кать в жиле́тку ― plákatʹ v žilétku ― to complain, to lament
