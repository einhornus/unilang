скорогово́рка -> tongue-twister, rapid speech
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: скорогово́рки
Genitive: скорогово́рки



Etymology: ско́рый (skóryj, “fast, quick”) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ говори́ть (govorítʹ, “to speak”) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • tongue-twister (phrase which is difficult to say)
    • rapid speech, sputter
