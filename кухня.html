кхня -> kitchen
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: кхни
Genitive: кхни



Etymology: Since the 18th century. Borrowed from Czech kuchyně (“kitchen”) via Polish kuchnia (“kitchen”), from Old High German chuhhina, kuchī̆na (“kitchen”), from Latin cocina (“kitchen”). Displaced native Russian поварня (povárnja, “kitchen”).



Definitions (English):
  • kitchen
    • suite of furniture for a kitchen
      • cuisine
        • (figuratively) behind-the-scenes dealings or operation,
          • полити́ческая ку́хня ― politíčeskaja kúxnja ― the nitty-gritty of politics
