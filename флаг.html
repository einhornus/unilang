флаг -> flag
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: фла́ги
Genitive: фла́га



Etymology: Borrowed from Dutch vlag. Influenced by English flag (as by spelling with ф instead of в) so as not to clash with влах, "Vlach".



Definitions (English):
  • flag, banner
