ака́ция (11088)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Akazie, from Latin acacia, from Ancient Greek ἀκακία (akakía)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈkat͡sɨjə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ака́ция • (akácija) f inan (genitive ака́ции, nominative plural ака́ции, genitive plural ака́ций)

  1. (botany) acacia (shrub or tree)
