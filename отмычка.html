отмы́чка (21087)


Etymology

отмыка́ть (otmykátʹ) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtˈmɨt͡ɕkə]

Noun

отмы́чка • (otmýčka) f inan (genitive отмы́чки, nominative plural отмы́чки, genitive plural отмы́чек)

  1. lock pick (tool)
  2. (figuratively) key (to understand something)
  3. (low colloquial) unlocking, unlatching
  4. (low colloquial) unfastening

Related terms

  • отмыка́ть impf (otmykátʹ), отомкну́ть pf (otomknútʹ)
  • -мыка́ть impf (-mykátʹ), -мкну́ть pf (-mknútʹ)
