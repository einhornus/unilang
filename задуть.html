заду́ть (22342)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ дуть (dutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈdutʲ]

Verb

заду́ть • (zadútʹ) pf (imperfective задува́ть)

  1. (colloquial, perfective only) to begin to blow (of the wind, etc.)
  2. to blow out (a candle, fire, etc.)
  3. (technical) to put (a blast furnace) intó action

Related terms

  • дуть impf (dutʹ), поду́ть pf (podútʹ)
  • ду́тый (dútyj)
  • дутьё (dutʹjó)
