бара́шек (16786)


Etymology

From бара́н (barán) +‎ -шек (-šek), with unexpected loss of н.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈraʂɨk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

бара́шек • (barášek) m anim (genitive бара́шка, nominative plural бара́шки, genitive plural бара́шков)

  1. (colloquial) endearing diminutive of бара́н (barán): little sheep, little ram, lamb
  2. lambskin
  3. (engineering) wing nut, thumbscrew
