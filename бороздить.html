борозди́ть (24915)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bərɐzʲˈdʲitʲ]

Verb

борозди́ть • (borozdítʹ) impf (perfective изборозди́ть)

  1. to furrow
  2. (intransitive) to leave ruts
  3. (figuratively) to travel around, to gallivant (by car, train or ship, etc.)

Related terms

  • борозда́ (borozdá)
  • вспа́хивать (vspáxivatʹ), вспаха́ть (vspaxátʹ)
