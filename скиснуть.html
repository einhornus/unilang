ски́снуть (19339)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ ки́снуть (kísnutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈskʲisnʊtʲ]

Verb

ски́снуть • (skísnutʹ) pf (imperfective ки́снуть or скисать)

  1. to turn sour (e.g. a milk product)
  2. (colloquial) to become sad or depressed
