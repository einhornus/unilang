Verb[edit]

произвести́ • (proizvestí) pf (imperfective производи́ть)

  1. to make, to carry out, to execute, to effect
    произвести́ ремо́нт ― proizvestí remónt ― to repair, to carry out repairs
  2. to give birth
    произвести́ на свет ― proizvestí na svet ― to bring into the world; to give birth (to)
  3. to produce
  4. to promote
  5. to cause, to produce, to create
