по́шлый -> vulgar, trivial, banal
Adjective



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic пошьлъ (pošĭlŭ, “ancient, primordial, former, usual”)



Definitions (English):
  • vulgar
    • commonplace, trivial, banal, trite
