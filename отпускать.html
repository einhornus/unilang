отпуска́ть -> to let go, release
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я отпуска́ю
Ты отпуска́ешь
Past: отпуска́л/отпуска́ла



Etymology: от- (ot-) +‎ пуска́ть (puskátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to let go, to let off,
    • не отпуска́ть ни на шаг ― ne otpuskátʹ ni na šag ― not let stray one step, not let stir a step from one's side
  • to release, to set free,
    • отпуска́ть на во́лю ― otpuskátʹ na vólju ― to set at liberty, to liberate, отпуска́ть на поруки ― otpuskátʹ na porúki ― to take bail
  • to dismiss, to give leave
    • to supply, to serve, to issue, to give out, to sell,
      • отпуска́ть шу́тку ― otpuskátʹ šútku ― to crack a joke, отпуска́ть комплименты ― otpuskátʹ kompliménty ― to make compliments, to pay compliments
    • to allot, to assign, to allow, to provide
      • to slacken, to relax, to turn loose, to loosen
        • (obsolete) to remit, to forgive,
          • отпуска́ть грехи́ ― otpuskátʹ grexí ― to give absolution
        • (hair or beard) to grow, to let grow
          • (metal) to temper, to draw
            • (pain) to lessen, to ease
