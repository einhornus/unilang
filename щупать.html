щу́пать -> to feel, touch (sth), probe
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я щу́паю
Ты щу́паешь
Past: щу́пал/щу́пала



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic щупати (ščupati) cognate with Russian щуп (ščup, “a probe, a dipstick”). Compare as well Sanskrit छुपति (chupati, “to touch”).



Definitions (English):
  • to feel, to touch to grope
    • to probe
