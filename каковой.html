каково́й (8752)


Etymology

как (kak) + -ово́й (-ovój)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kəkɐˈvoj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Determiner

каково́й • (kakovój)

  1. (obsolete) which; such as
  2. what sort of?

Usage notes

  • The long forms are obsolete but the short forms are in common use.

Synonyms

  • како́й (kakój)
  • кото́рый (kotóryj)

Related terms

  • как (kak)
  • каково́ (kakovó)
