шеде́вр (7907)


Etymology

Borrowed from French chef-d'œuvre.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂɨˈdɛvr] (phonetic respelling: шедэвр)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

шеде́вр • (šedɛvr) m inan (genitive шеде́вра, nominative plural шеде́вры, genitive plural шеде́вров)

  1. masterpiece (piece of work that has been given much critical praise)

Related terms

  • шедевральный (šedɛvrálʹnyj)
