привет -> greeting
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: приветы
Genitive: привета́



Etymology: From Old East Slavic привѣтъ (privětŭ, “greeting appeal dictum, word intention, will ?”), from Proto-Slavic *privětъ.
