касси́рша (12420)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈsʲirʂə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

касси́рша • (kassírša) f anim (genitive касси́рши, nominative plural касси́рши, genitive plural касси́рш, masculine касси́р)

  1. (colloquial) female cashier, teller

Usage notes

  • Some occupation names work for both males and females. The male form of the term may refer to a male or a female. This is a more colloquial term.
