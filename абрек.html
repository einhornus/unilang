абре́к (16294)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ossetian абрӕг (abræg, “robber”). Compare Armenian ավազակ (avazak, “robber”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈbrɛk] (phonetic respelling: абрэк)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

абре́к • (abrɛk) m anim (genitive абре́ка, nominative plural абре́ки, genitive plural абре́ков)

  1. Abrek
  2. (offensive, ethnic slur) a native of the Caucasus
