шнур -> cord, lace, flex, cable
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: шнуры́
Genitive: шнура́



Etymology: Borrowed from German Schnur.



Definitions (English):
  • cord (length of twisted strands)
    • (electric) line
