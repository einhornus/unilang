на́ры (3472)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnarɨ]

Etymology 1

Noun

на́ры • (náry) f inan plural number">pl (genitive на́р, plural only)

  1. plank bed (often used in prisons), bunk
  2. (idiomatic, colloquial) prison, jail
    На Канарах — лучше, чем на на́рах.
    Na Kanárax — lúčše, čem na nárax.
    On the Canary Islands is better than on the (prison) bunk.

Etymology 2

Noun

на́ры • (náry) m anim plural number">pl

  1. nominative plural of на́р (nar)
