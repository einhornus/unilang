ку́чер (10498)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Kutscher.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkut͡ɕɪr]

Noun

ку́чер • (kúčer) masculine gender">m anim (genitive кучера, nominative plural кучера, genitive plural кучеро́в)

  1. coachman (one who drives a coach)

Synonyms

  • изво́зчик (izvózčik), возни́ца (vozníca), ямщи́к (jamščík)
