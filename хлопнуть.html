хло́пнуть (2616)


Etymology

Perhaps хло́пать (xlópatʹ) +‎ -нуть (-nutʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxlopnʊtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

хло́пнуть • (xlópnutʹ) pf (imperfective хло́пать)

  1. to flap, to clap
  2. to slam (door)

Related terms

  • хлопок (xlopók)
