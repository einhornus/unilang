баржа (10138)


Etymology

Borrowed from French barge.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐrˈʐa]
  • IPA(key): [ˈbarʐə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

баржа or баржа • (bárža or baržá) f inan (genitive баржи or баржи, nominative plural баржи or баржи, genitive plural ба́рж or баржей)

  1. barge
