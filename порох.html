прох -> gunpowder
Noun, declension 2c (pattern грод)

Plural: по́рох
Genitive: проха



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic порохъ (poroxŭ, “dust”), Proto-Slavic *porxъ. Doublet of прах (prax).
