поддава́ть (27383)


Etymology

подда́ть (poddátʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pədːɐˈvatʲ]

Verb

поддава́ть • (poddavátʹ) impf (perfective подда́ть)

  1. to strike (from beloẃ), to kick
  2. (games) to give awaý (a piece or card)
  3. (colloquial) to add
    подда́ть жару ― poddátʹ žarú ― to add fuel to the fire
  4. (colloquial) to booze

Related terms

  • поддавки (poddavkí, “giveaway checkers”)
