осыпа́ться (10034)


Etymology 1

осыпа́ть (osýpatʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈsɨpət͡sə]

Verb

осыпа́ться • (osýpatʹsja) pf (imperfective осыпа́ться) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to crumblé
  2. (flowers, leaves) to fall (off), (crops) to shed its grain
  3. passive of осыпа́ть (osýpatʹ)

Etymology 2

осыпа́ть (osypátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐsɨˈpatʲ]

Verb

осыпа́ться • (osypátʹsja) impf (perfective осыпа́ться) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to crumblé
  2. (flowers, leaves) to fall (off), (crops) to shed its grain
  3. passive of осыпа́ть (osypátʹ)

Related terms

  • осыпа́ть impf (osypátʹ), осыпа́ть pf (osýpatʹ)
  • сы́пать impf (sýpatʹ), насыпа́ть pf (nasýpatʹ), посыпа́ть impf (posýpatʹ)
