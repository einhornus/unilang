мя́со -> meat
Noun, declension 2c
Plural: мяса
Genitive: мяса



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *męso, from Proto-Indo-European *mḗms. Compare Serbo-Croatian meso, Czech maso, Polish mięso, and Slovene meso. Compare Old Prussian mensa (“meat”).



Definitions (English):
  • (usually uncountable) meat
    • (usually uncountable) flesh, pulp
      • (biology, usually uncountable) tissue, flesh
        • (usually uncountable) (cannon) fodder,
          • пушечное мя́со ― púšečnoje mjáso ― cannon fodder
