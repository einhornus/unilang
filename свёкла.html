свёкла -> beet
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: свёклы
Genitive: свёклы



Etymology: From Old East Slavic сеѵклъ (seüklŭ), from Ancient Greek σεῦκλον (seûklon), as is Bulgarian цвекло (cvekló), Macedonian цвекло (cveklo), Serbo-Croatian ци̏кла / cȉkla, Slovak / Czech cvikla, Polish ćwikła, Hungarian cékla. Hither belongs also the taxonomic Beta vulgaris var. cicla and Arabic سِلْق‎ (silq).



Definitions (English):
  • (botany, usually uncountable) beet (the root plant Beta vulgaris)
