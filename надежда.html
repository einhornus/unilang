наде́жда -> hope
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: наде́жды
Genitive: наде́жды



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic наде́жда (nadežda), from Proto-Slavic *nadeďa. Doublet of надёжа (nadjóža), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • hope, expectation, prospect,
    • тщетные наде́жды ― tščétnyje nadéždy ― vain hopes
