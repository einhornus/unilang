глупый -> stupid, silly
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *glupъ. Cognate with Bulgarian глупав (glupav), Serbo-Croatian глу̏пав/glȕpav, Slovak hlúpy, Slovene glup. Non-Slavic cognates include Old Norse glópr (“idiot”) (confer Icelandic glópur). According to Erich Berneker and Uhlenbeck the Slavic words were borrowed from a Germanic source, which is contested by Vasmer.



Definitions (English):
  • silly, stupid, foolish
    • inane
      • blockhead, dolt, numskull
