бронх (13827)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek βρόγχος (brónkhos).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bronx]

Noun

бронх • (bronx) m inan (genitive бро́нха, nominative plural бро́нхи, genitive plural бро́нхов)

  1. bronchus

Related terms
