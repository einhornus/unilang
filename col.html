col (6917)


Etymology

Borrowed from French col, from Latin collum (“neck”). Doublet of collum.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kɒl/

Noun

col (plural cols)

  1. (geography) A dip on a mountain ridge betweeń two peaks.
