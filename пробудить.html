пробуди́ть -> to awaken

Etymology: про- (pro-) +‎ буди́ть (budítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • (literary, transitive, also figuratively) to arouse, to awaken
    • (colloquial) to spend some time trying to awaken (someone)
      • (literary) to engender (a feeling)
