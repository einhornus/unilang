Etymology: с- (s-) +‎ вяза́ть (vjazátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to tie, to bind. Perfectives:
    • свя́зывать (svjázyvatʹ), вяза́ть (vjazátʹ)
  • to tie together. Perfective:
    • свя́зывать (svjázyvatʹ)
  • to connect, to join. Perfective:
    • свя́зывать (svjázyvatʹ)
  • to knit, to crochet. Perfective:
    • вяза́ть (vjazátʹ)
