органи́зм -> organism
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: органи́змы
Genitive: органи́зма



Etymology: Borrowed from French organisme.



Definitions (English):
  • system of organs making up the body of a living thing.
    • Переедать вре́дно для на́шего органи́зма. Perejedátʹ vrédno dlja náševo organízma. Overeating is bad for our bodies.
  • organism (living thing, usually a small and simplistic one)
    • (figuratively) a complex structure or mechanism
