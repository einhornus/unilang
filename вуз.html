вуз (5964)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vus]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

вуз • (vuz) m inan (genitive ву́за, nominative plural ву́зы, genitive plural ву́зов)

  1. acronym of вы́сшее уче́бное заведе́ние (výsšeje učébnoje zavedénije, “higher/highest educational establishment”): university, college, institute

Related terms

  • вузовец (vuzovec), вузовка (vuzovka)
  • ву́зовский (vúzovskij)
