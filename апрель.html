апрель -> April
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: апрели
Genitive: апреля



Etymology: From, Old East Slavic апрѣль (aprělĭ), earlier априль (aprilĭ), from Byzantine Greek Απρίλιος (Aprílios), from Latin Aprīlis from (perhaps) Etruscan 𐌀𐌐𐌓𐌖 (apru) from Ancient Greek Ἀφροδίτη (Aphrodítē). The form with ѣ in the second syllable is explained by Sobolevsky as a loan from the Novgorodian written language.
