яра́нга (32368)


Etymology

From Chukchi яраӈы.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [(j)ɪˈranɡə]

Noun

яра́нга • (jaránga) f inan (genitive яра́нги, nominative plural яра́нги, genitive plural яра́нг)

  1. yaranga (a rounded or conical tent of reindeer hide, the traditional mobile home of some nomadic indigenous peoples of Russia.)
