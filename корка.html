корка -> crust, rind
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: корки
Genitive: корки



Etymologies:
  • кора́́ (korá) +‎ -ка (-ka)
  • An old compound. The second element is a Permic *kad, from Proto-Finno-Ugric *kota.


Definitions (English):
  • crust, scab
    • rind, peel
      • house
