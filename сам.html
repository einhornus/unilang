сам -> self

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *samъ, from Proto-Indo-European *somHós.



Definitions (English):
  • (definitive pronoun, emphatic) self, -self.
    • Я сам э́то ви́де́л. Ja sam éto vídel. I saw it myself. Само́́ госуда́рство так реши́́ло. Samó gosudárstvo tak rešílo. The State itself has so decided.
