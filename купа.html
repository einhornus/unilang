ку́па (28031)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic ку́па (kupa), from Proto-Slavic *kupa.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkupə]

Noun

ку́па • (kúpa) f inan (genitive ку́пы, nominative plural ку́пы, genitive plural ку́п)

  1. (dated) group, heap, pile
