поле́зть (1658)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ лезть (leztʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlʲesʲtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

поле́зть • (poléztʹ) pf (imperfective лезть or ла́зить)

  1. to climb (up, on to), to scramble
  2. to get (intó); to thrust the hand (intó)
    поле́зть в во́ду ― poléztʹ v vódu ― to get intó the water
  3. to thrust oneself (upoń); to intrude (upoń)
