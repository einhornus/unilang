мане́вр (6587)


Etymology 1

See манёвр (manjóvr).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈnʲevr]

Noun

мане́вр • (manévr) m inan (genitive мане́вра, nominative plural мане́вры, genitive plural мане́вров)

  1. maneuver (a movement, often one performed with difficulty)

Noun

мане́вр • (manjóvr) m inan

  1. Alternative spelling of манёвр (manjóvr)
