доск -> board

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *dъska, from Proto-Germanic *diskuz, borrowed from Latin discus. See there for details.



Definitions (English):
  • board, plank,
    • лы́жи из доски ― lýži iz doskí ― skis made from boards
  • blackboard,
    • кла́ссная доска́ ― klássnaja doská ― blackboard
  • plate,
    • грифельная доска́ ― grífelʹnaja doská ― slate, от доски до доски ― ot doskí do doskí ― from cover to cover
