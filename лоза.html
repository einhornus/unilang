лоза́ (16804)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *loza.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lɐˈza]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -a

Noun

лоза́ • (lozá) f inan (genitive лозы, nominative plural лозы, genitive plural лоз)

  1. vine
  2. rod, switch (thin, flexible stick)
