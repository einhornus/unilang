Adjective[edit]

уда́чный • (udáčnyj)

  1. successful
  2. fortunate
  3. lucky, prosperous
  4. felicitous, happy
  5. fortuitous
  6. chancy
  7. apposite (appropriate, relevant, well-suited)
  8. well-turned
