води́ть -> to lead, (p) to lead about
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я вожу́
Ты во́дишь
Past: води́л/води́ла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *voditi (“to lead, conduct”), iterative of *vesti (“to lead, conduct”), whence English wed and wage.



Definitions (English):
  • to lead, to conduct, to guide
    • to drive.
      • Я не вожу́ автома́т. ― Ja ne vožú avtomát. ― I don't drive automatic.
    • to move
      • to breed
