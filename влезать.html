влеза́ть (10279)


Etymology

влезть (vleztʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vlʲɪˈzatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

влеза́ть • (vlezátʹ) impf (perfective влезть)

  1. to get intó (something, also figurative), to climb intó, to scramble intó
    влеза́ть в долги ― vlezátʹ v dolgí ― get intó debts
  2. to fit in, to go in
  3. to meddle
    влеза́ть не в своё дело ― vlezátʹ ne v svojó délo ― to poke one's nose intó other people's affairs; to pry
    влеза́ть в душу ― vlezátʹ v dúšu ― to worm oneself intó smb.'s confidence (to get intó somebody's soul)
