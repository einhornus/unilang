вле́зать -> to climb in
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я вле́заю
Ты вле́заешь
Past: вле́зал/вле́зала



Etymology: влезть (vleztʹ) +‎ -а́ть (-átʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to get into (something, also figurative), to climb into, to scramble into,
    • влеза́ть в долги́ ― vlezátʹ v dolgí ― get into debts
  • to fit in, to go in
    • to meddle,
      • влеза́ть не в своё де́ло ― vlezátʹ ne v svojó délo ― to poke one's nose into other people's affairs to pry, влеза́ть в ду́шу ― vlezátʹ v dúšu ― to worm oneself into smb.'s confidence (to get into somebody's soul)
