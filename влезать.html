влеза́ть -> to climb in
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я влеза́ю

Past: влеза́л/влеза́ла



Etymology: влезть (vleztʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to get into (something, also figurative), to climb into, to scramble into,
    • влеза́ть в долги ― vlezátʹ v dolgí ― get into debts
  • to fit in, to go in
    • to meddle,
      • влеза́ть не в своё дело ― vlezátʹ ne v svojó délo ― to poke one's nose into other people's affairs to pry, влеза́ть в душу ― vlezátʹ v dúšu ― to worm oneself into smb.'s confidence (to get into somebody's soul)
