тын (26133)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic тꙑнъ (tynŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tynъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɨn]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

тын • (tyn) m inan (genitive ты́на, nominative plural тыны, genitive plural тынов)

  1. fence (especially one made of twigs)

Synonyms

  • забо́р (zabór), частоко́л (častokól)
