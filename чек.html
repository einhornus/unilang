чек -> cheque, receipt
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: че́ки
Genitive: че́ка



Etymology: Borrowed from English check.



Definitions (English):
  • cheque, check
    • receipt, bill
      • (colloquial) Contraction of челове́к (čelovék).
