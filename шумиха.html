шуми́ха (25389)


Etymology

шум (šum) +‎ -иха (-íxa)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂʊˈmʲixə]

Noun

шуми́ха • (šumíxa) f inan (genitive шуми́хи, nominative plural шуми́хи, genitive plural шуми́х)

  1. clamour/clamor, brouhaha, fuss, uproar, racket, ballyhoo, stir
