скрежета́ть (13436)


Etymology

Onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [skrʲɪʐɨˈtatʲ]

Verb

скрежета́ть • (skrežetátʹ) impf

  1. to grate, to grind
  2. to gnash (one's teeth) (+ instrumental)

Related terms

  • скре́жет (skréžet)
