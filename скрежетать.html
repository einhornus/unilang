скрежета́ть (13434)


Etymology[edit]

Onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [skrʲɪʐɨˈtatʲ]

Verb[edit]

скрежета́ть • (skrežetátʹ) impf

  1. to grate, to grind
  2. to gnash (one's teeth) (+ instrumental)

Related terms[edit]

  • скре́жет (skréžet)
