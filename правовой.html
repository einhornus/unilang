правово́й (7425)


Etymology

пра́во (právo) + -ово́й (-ovój)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəvɐˈvoj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

правово́й • (pravovój)

  1. legal, lawful, rightful
  2. based on law, constitutional
    правово́е госуда́рство ― pravovóje gosudárstvo ― constitutional state

Related terms

  • пра́во (právo)
