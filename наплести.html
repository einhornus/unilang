наплести́ (22401)


Etymology

на- (na-) +‎ плести́ (plestí)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəplʲɪˈsʲtʲi]

Verb

наплести́ • (naplestí) pf (imperfective наплетать)

  1. to weave (+ accusative or genitive, a quantity of things)
  2. (low colloquial, perfective only) to blather, to jabber (+ accusative, genitive or a clausé)

Related terms

  • плести́ impf (plestí), сплести́ pf (splestí)
  • плетёный (pletjónyj)
  • плетение (pleténije)
  • плеть (pletʹ)
  • плётка (pljótka)
  • плетёнка (pletjónka)
