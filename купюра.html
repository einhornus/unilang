купю́ра (8036)


Etymology

Borrowed from French coupure (“a cut”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kʊˈpʲurə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

купю́ра • (kupjúra) f inan (genitive купю́ры, nominative plural купю́ры, genitive plural купю́р)

  1. banknote
  2. cut, deletion (in a video or audio record, film, etc.)

Synonyms

  • (banknote): банкно́та (banknóta)
  • (cut): про́пуск (própusk), сокраще́ние (sokraščénije)
