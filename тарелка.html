та́релка -> plate
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: та́релки
Genitive: та́релки



Etymology: Polish talerz (“plate”) +‎ -ка (-ka), with metathesis (transposition) of l and r.



Definitions (English):
  • plate (a serving dish)
    • (plural) cymbals
      • (technical) plate, disc,
        • та́ре́лка бу́фера ― tarélka búfera ― buffer disc
