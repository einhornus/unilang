тетрдь -> exercise book
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жзнь)

Plural: тетрди
Genitive: тетрди



Etymology: Borrowed from Ancient Greek τετράδιον (tetrádion, “quaternion of parchment”), from τετράς (tetrás).



Definitions (English):
  • exercise book, notebook
