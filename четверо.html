че́тверо (2794)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈt͡ɕetvʲɪrə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Numeral

че́тверо • (čétvero)

  1. four (in a group together), four of them
    че́тверо дете́й ― čétvero detéj ― four children
    че́тверо часо́в ― čétvero časóv ― four watches
    че́тверо очко́в ― čétvero očkóv ― four pairs of glasses
    че́тверо из нас ― čétvero iz nas ― four of us
    нас бы́ло че́тверо ― nas býlo čétvero ― there were four of us

Usage notes

The nominative of a collective numeral governs the genitive plural of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. The other cases agree with that of the noun, pronoun, or adjective. Not used with feminine nouns. Used mostly when speaking of children and with a few nouns that are used in the plural only, such as часы́, очки́, or with pronouns.

Related terms

  • четвёрка (četvjórka)
  • четверной (četvernoj)
  • учетверить (učetveritʹ)
  • вче́тверо (včétvero)
