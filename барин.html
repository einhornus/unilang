ба́рин -> landowner, barin, gentleman
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ба́ры
Genitive: ба́рина


Definitions (English):
  • (historical) boyar, barin, nobleman, gentleman, landlord (a rank of aristocracy in Russia)
    • хозя́ин — ба́рин (idiom) ― xozjáin — bárin (idiom) ― it's up to you, your choice, you're the boss.
  • (archaic) a form of address to noble people, sir, master
    • a person who doesn't want to work,
      • сиде́ть ба́рином сиде́ть как ба́рин ― sidétʹ bárinom sidétʹ kak bárin ― to sit around like royalty
