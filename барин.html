барин (3012)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbarʲɪn]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

барин • (bárin) masculine gender">m anim (genitive ба́рина, nominative plural ба́ре, genitive plural бар, feminine ба́рыня)

  1. (historical) boyar, barin, nobleman, gentleman, landlord (a rank of aristocracy in Russia)
    хозя́ин — барин (idiom) ― xozjáin — bárin (idiom) ― it's up to you, your choice, you're the boss.
  2. (archaic) a form of address to noble people, sir, master
  3. a person who doesn't want to work
    сиде́ть ба́рином; сиде́ть как барин ― sidétʹ bárinom; sidétʹ kak bárin ― to sit around like royalty

Synonyms

  • боя́рин (bojárin) (boyar; an older term)
  • господи́н (gospodín), госуда́рь (gosudárʹ), су́дарь (súdarʹ) (archaic forms of address)
  • лорд (lord) (noble man)

Related terms

  • ба́рский (bárskij)
