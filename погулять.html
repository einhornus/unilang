погуля́ть -> to take a walk, (coll) take time off

Etymology: по- (po-) +‎ гуля́ть (guljátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to walk, to go for a walk, to stroll for a while
    • to have a time-off, to have a free time for a while
      • to make merry, to enjoy oneself, to carouse for a while
        • to go with, to go out with someone for a while


          Definitions (Russian):
          • соверши́ть что-нибудь (како́е-нибудь де́йствие) в соотве́тствии со знач. гл. гуля́ть
