  • to tear
    • to jerk, to tug
      • to start with a jerk, to get off with a jerk
        • (colloquial) to dash, to rush, to race, to scurry, to spurt (car) to speed, to tear off, to zip, to zoom
          • (colloquial) to take flight, to take to one's heels, to whip off, to whip away, to dash off
            • to blow up, to blast
