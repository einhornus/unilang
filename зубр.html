зубр (9150)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *zǫbrъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zubr]

Noun

зубр • (zubr) m anim (genitive зу́бра, nominative plural зу́бры, genitive plural зу́бров, related adjective зубровый)

  1. bison (European bison), wisent
  2. (colloquial) diehard, ace, pro

Related terms
