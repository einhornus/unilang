описть -> to describe
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я опиш
Ты опшешь
Past: описл/описла



Etymologies:
  • о- (o-) +‎ писа́ть (pisátʹ, “to write”)
  • о- (o-) +‎ писа́ть (písatʹ, “to pee, to piss”)


Definitions (English):
  • to describe, to depict, to portray (to represent in words)
    • to make / take an inventory (of), to inventory
      • (mathematics) to describe, to circumscribe
        • to urinate (on something)
