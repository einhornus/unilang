обста́вить (10293)


Etymology

об- (ob-) +‎ ста́вить (stávitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐpˈstavʲɪtʲ]

Verb

обста́вить • (obstávitʹ) pf (imperfective обставлять)

  1. to surround (with), to encircle (with)
  2. to furnish
    обста́вить кварти́ру ― obstávitʹ kvartíru ― to furnish a flat (an apartment)
  3. to arrange
  4. (colloquial) to trick, to cheat
  5. (colloquial) to get ahead (of)
  6. (colloquial) to beat, to defeat

Related terms

  • ста́вить impf (stávitʹ), поста́вить pf (postávitʹ)
  • обстано́вка f (obstanóvka)
