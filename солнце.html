со́лнце -> sun
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: со́лнца
Genitive: со́лнца



Etymology: From Old East Slavic сълньце (sŭlnĭce), from Proto-Slavic *sъlnьce, from Proto-Indo-European *sóh₂wl̥, whence English sun, solar and helium.



Definitions (English):
  • sun,
    • восхо́д со́лнца ― vosxód sólnca ― sunrise
