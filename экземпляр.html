экземпля́р -> copy (of book etc.)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: экземпля́ры
Genitive: экземпля́ра



Etymology: Borrowed from German Exemplar.



Definitions (English):
  • specimen, sample, instance, copy
