котёл -> boiler
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: котлы
Genitive: котла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *kotьlъ, from Gothic 𐌺𐌰𐍄𐌹𐌻𐍃 (katils, “kettle”), from Latin catillus.

        • (card games, slang) pot (money available to be won in a card hand)
