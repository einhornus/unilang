пароход -> steamer
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: пароходы
Genitive: парохода



Etymology: From пар (par, “steam”) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ *ход (*xod), a deverbal agentive form of ходи́́ть (xodítʹ, “to go”).



Definitions (English):
  • steamboat, steamer, steamship
