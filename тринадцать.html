трина́дцать (6323)


Etymology

три (tri) + на (na) + дцать (dcatʹ), or "three on ten". The final syllable дцать (dcatʹ) is an old short form of де́сять (désjatʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [trʲɪˈnat͡s(ː)ɨtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Numeral

трина́дцать • (trinádcatʹ)

  1. thirteen (13)

Usage notes

трина́дцать (trinadcatʹ) in the nominative case and accusative case governs the genitive plural of the noun. In other cases, it governs the corresponding plural case of the noun.

  • трина́дцать ма́льчиков ― trinádcatʹ málʹčikov ― thirteen boys
  • с трина́дцатью ма́льчиками ― s trinádcatʹju málʹčikami ― with thirteen boys
  • ма́льчиков трина́дцать ― málʹčikov trinádcatʹ ― approximately thirteen boys
