ле́пта (27079)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek λεπτόν (leptón), neuter form of λεπτός (leptós, “small”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲeptə]

Noun

ле́пта • (lépta) f inan (genitive ле́пты, nominative plural ле́пты, genitive plural ле́пт)

  1. lepton (coin)
  2. (idiomatic) mité, two cents, (small) contribution
