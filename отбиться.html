отби́ться (12412)


Etymology

отби́ть (otbítʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐdˈbʲit͡sːə]

Verb

отби́ться • (otbítʹsja) pf (imperfective отбива́ться)

  1. to fight off, to ward off, to beat off, to repulse, to defend oneself
  2. to come off, to fall off
  3. to fall behind from a group, to get lost
  4. passive of отби́ть (otbítʹ)
