мо́лодец -> fine fellow, well done!
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: молодцы
Genitive: молодца



Etymology: мо́лодо́й (molodój) +‎ -ец (-ec)



Definitions (English):
  • fine fellow, fine girl
    • attaboy, attagirl, well done, bravo, good for you, good for her
      • brave man, clever man (usually a young man)
        • attaboy!, attagirl!, well done!
