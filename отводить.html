отводи́ть (6071)


Etymology

от- (ot-) +‎ води́ть (vodítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtvɐˈdʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

отводи́ть • (otvodítʹ) impf (perfective отвести́)

  1. to lead awaý; to take / draw aside; to take awaý
  2. to divert
  3. to avert, to parry; to deflect; to draw off; to ward off
  4. to allocate, to assign
  5. to withdraw
    отводи́ть войска в два эта́па ― otvodítʹ vojská v dva etápa ― to withdraw troops in two stages
  6. (idiomatic)
    отводи́ть душу ― otvodítʹ dúšu ― to unburden one's heart, to pour out one's heart
  7. to recuse
Related terms
  • отво́д m (otvód)
