кувшин -> jug, pitcher

Etymology: Presumably borrowed from Lithuanian *kaušinas, derived from Lithuanian káušas (“ladle, dipper, big spoon”), itself derived from Lithuanian káuti (“hew, chop out”), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *káuˀtei, from Proto-Indo-European *kowh₂-, from the root *kewh₂-.



Definitions (English):
  • jug, pitcher
