зае́сть (11401)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ есть (jestʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈjesʲtʲ]

Verb

зае́сть • (zajéstʹ) pf (imperfective заеда́ть)

  1. to jam, to stick (e.g. of equipment or zippers) (alsó impersonal)
    ключ в замке зае́ст ― ključ v zamké zajést ― the key is sticking in the lock
  2. to eat after a drink of alcohol or medicine
    зае́сть лекарство са́харом ― zajéstʹ lekárstvo sáxarom ― to take sugar after the medicine
  3. to kill by eating to death
  4. (figuratively) to torment
