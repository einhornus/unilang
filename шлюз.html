шлюз -> lock, sluice
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: шлю́зы
Genitive: шлю́за



Etymology: Borrowed from Dutch sluis or German Schleuse



Definitions (English):
  • sluice, lock, gateway
    • airlock
