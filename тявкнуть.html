тя́вкнуть (28178)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲafknʊtʲ]

Verb

тя́вкнуть • (tjávknutʹ) pf (imperfective тя́вкать)

  1. to yap, to yelp (of a small dog, to bark in a non-growly manner)
