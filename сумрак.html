сумрак -> dusk
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: сумра́ки
Genitive: сумра́ка



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic сѫмракъ (sǫmrakŭ), from Proto-Slavic *sǫmorkъ. Displaced native Old East Slavic суморокъ (sumorokŭ).



Definitions (English):
  • twilight, dusk, duskiness
    • gloom
