ноя́брь -> November
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: ноябри́
Genitive: ноября́



Etymology: Old East Slavic, Old Church Slavonic ноѩбрь (nojębrĭ), from Byzantine Greek Νοέμβριος (Noémbrios), from Latin november, from novem, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁néwn̥ (“nine”) + Latin -ber from -bris, an adjectival suffix.
