характе́рный -> characteristic (adj)
Adjective



Etymology: хара́ктер (xarákter) +‎ -ный (-nyj)



Definitions (English):
  • possessing pronounced, distinctive features
    • characteristic, particular, typical, specific
      • (relational, theater) character (expressing a certain psychological type)
        • характе́рный актёр ― xarákternyj aktjór ― character actor
      • (colloquial) strong-willed, having a strong character
