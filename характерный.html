характерный (2878)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [xərɐkˈtʲernɨj]

Adjective

характерный • (xaraktérnyj) (comparative (по)характернее or (по)характерней)

  1. possessing pronounced, distinctive features
  2. characteristic, particular, typical, specific

Etymology 2

хара́ктер (xarákter) +‎ -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [xɐˈraktʲɪrnɨj]

Adjective

характерный • (xarákternyj) (comparative (по)характернее or (по)характерней)

  1. (relational, theater) character (expressing a certain psychological type)
    характерный актёр ― xarákternyj aktjór ― character actor
  2. (colloquial) strong-willed, having a strong character
Related terms
  • хара́ктер (xarákter)
