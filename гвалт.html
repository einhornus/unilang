гвалт (24609)


Etymology[edit]

Polish gwałt, from Middle High German gewalt (“strength, power”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɡvalt]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

гвалт • (gvalt) m inan (genitive гва́лта, uncountable)

  1. (colloquial) din, row, rumpus, hubbub, racket (loud noise, commotion)
