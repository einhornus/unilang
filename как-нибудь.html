как-нибудь -> somehow, anyhow, sometime (in the future)
Definitions (English):
  • N. B. — the particle -
    • нибудь imparts a sense of indefiniteness similar to English any. The form как-нибудь is often associated with questions or negatives (like anyhow), while как-то is associated with affirmatives (like somehow).
  • (indefinite pronoun) somehow, somehow or other, anyhow.
    • Я ка́к-нибудь спра́влюсь ― Ja kák-nibudʹ správljusʹ ― I’ll make out somehow (or other).
  • (indefinite adverb) sometime.
    • Зайди́́те́ ко мне ка́к-нибудь! ― Zajdíte ko mne kák-nibudʹ! ― Drop in and see me sometime!
