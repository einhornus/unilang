салфе́тка -> napkin, tissue, serviette
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: салфе́тки
Genitive: салфе́тки



Etymology: Borrowed from German Salvette, from Italian salvietta



Definitions (English):
  • napkin, serviette
    • doily
      • (wet) wipe
