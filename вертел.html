Noun[edit]

вертел • (vértel) m inan (genitive вертела, nominative plural вертела, genitive plural вертело́в)

  1. spit, skewer
Synonyms[edit]

Verb[edit]

вертел • (vertél)

  1. masculine singular past indicative imperfective of верте́ть (vertétʹ, “to turn, to twist”)
