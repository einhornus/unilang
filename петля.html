петля -> loop, hinge

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *petьľa, from an Old Germanic i-umlauted form of Proto-Germanic *fatilaz: compare Old Norse fetill, Old High German fezzil (German Fessel), Old English fetel (English fettle).



Definitions (English):
  • loop (length of thread, line or rope, a shape produced by a curve)
    • noose
      • buttonhole, eye
        • stitch
          • hinge
