боро́дка (7311)


Etymology

борода́ (borodá) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈrotkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

боро́дка • (boródka) f inan (genitive боро́дки, nominative plural боро́дки, genitive plural боро́док)

  1. protrusion at the end of a key
  2. (colloquial) Diminutive of борода́ (borodá): a (small) beard
  3. (colloquial) endearing form of борода́ (borodá)
