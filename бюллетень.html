бюллете́нь -> bulletin, ballot-paper, medical certificate
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: бюллете́ни
Genitive: бюллете́ня



Etymology: Borrowed from French bulletin, from Italian bullettino, from Latin bulla.



Definitions (English):
  • ballot paper
    • bulletin (brief report)
      • bulletin, journal
        • (colloquial) medical certificate
