местечко (4820)


Etymology[edit]

Diminutive of место (mésto, “place, (dialectal) city”), or calqued from another Slavic language. Compare Polish miasteczko.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈsʲtʲet͡ɕkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

местечко • (mestéčko) n inan (genitive месте́чка, nominative plural месте́чки, genitive plural месте́чек)

  1. Diminutive of место (mésto)
  2. A small village or town on the territory of the former Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth.
  3. A shtetl, small Jewish village.
