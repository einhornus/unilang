местечко -> place, small town
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лто)

Plural: местчки
Genitive: местчка



Etymology: Diminutive of место (mésto, “place, (dialectal) city”), or calqued from another Slavic language. Compare Polish miasteczko.



Definitions (English):
  • Diminutive of место (mésto)
    • A small village or town on the territory of the former Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth.
      • A shtetl, small Jewish village.
