местечко (4822)


Etymology

Diminutive of ме́сто (mésto, “place, (dialectal) city”), or calqued from another Slavic language. Compare Polish miasteczko.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈsʲtʲet͡ɕkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

месте́чко • (mestéčko) n inan (genitive месте́чка, nominative plural месте́чки, genitive plural месте́чек)

  1. Diminutive of ме́сто (mésto)
  2. A small village or town on the territory of the former Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth.
  3. A shtetl, small Jewish village.
