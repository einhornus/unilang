чре́сла (32414)


Etymology

From Old Church Slavonic чрѣсла (črěsla), from Proto-Slavic *čerslo. Displaced the native East Slavic form чересла (čéresla).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈt͡ɕrʲeslə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

чре́сла • (črésla) n inan pl (genitive чре́сл, plural only)

  1. (archaic, anatomy) reins, small of the back

Synonyms

  • поясни́ца (pojasníca), бёдра (bjódra)
  • чересла (čéresla, dialectal)
