чай -> tea
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: чаи́
Genitive: ча́я



Etymologies:
  • From Turkic (compare Turkish çay), from Chinese 茶 (chá).
  • From ча́ю (čáju), first-person singular present of чаять (čájatʹ).
