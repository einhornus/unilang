биографи́ческий (22928)


Etymology

From биогра́фия (biográfija, “biography”) +‎ -ический (-íčeskij, “adjectival suffix”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲɪəɡrɐˈfʲit͡ɕɪskʲɪj]

Adjective

биографи́ческий • (biografíčeskij)

  1. biographical (relating to an account of a person's life)
