тура́ (8720)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from French tour, from Latin turris (“tower; rook”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʊˈra]

Noun

тура́ • (turá) f inan (genitive туры́, nominative plural туры́, genitive plural тур)

  1. (chess, informal) rook, castle
  2. (obsolete) tower
Synonyms
  • ладья́ (ladʹjá) (formal)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈturə]

Noun

тура́ • (túra) m inan or m anim

  1. genitive singular of тур (tur)
  2. animate accusative singular of тур (tur)
