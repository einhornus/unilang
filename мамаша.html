мама́ша (6857)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈmaʂə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

мама́ша • (mamáša) f anim (genitive мама́ши, nominative plural мама́ши, genitive plural мама́ш)

  1. (colloquial) mother, mamma, mummy
  2. familiar term of address for an (elderly) woman
