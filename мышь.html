мышь -> mouse
Noun, declension 3e (pattern ночь)

Plural: мыши
Genitive: мыши



Etymology: From Old East Slavic мꙑшь (myšĭ), from Proto-Slavic *myšь (“mouse”), from Proto-Indo-European *muh₂s. The computing term is a semantic loan from English mouse.
