ага (1969)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈaɡə]

Noun

ага • (ága) f anim (genitive а́ги, nominative plural а́ги, genitive plural а́г)

  1. cane toad (Bufo marinus, now Rhinella marina)
Synonyms
  • жаба-ага (žába-ága)

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Ottoman Turkish language">Ottoman Turkish آغا‎ (ağa), Turkish ağa.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈɡa]

Noun

ага • (agá) f anim (genitive а́ги, nominative plural а́ги, genitive plural а́г)

  1. (historical, Turkey, Central Asia) agha, master, patron

Etymology 3

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈʕa]
  • IPA(key): [ɐˈɣa]

Interjection

ага • (ahá or agá)

  1. yep, yeah (colloquial form of yes)
  2. aha, uh-huh (indicates that the speaker is listening to you, but does not necessarily imply agreement)
Synonyms
  • да (da)
  • так (tak)
  • угу́ (ugú)
