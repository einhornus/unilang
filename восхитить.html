восхити́ть (26024)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic въсхытити (vŭsxytiti, “to fascinate, to snatch away”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vəsxʲɪˈtʲitʲ]

Verb

восхити́ть • (vosxitítʹ) pf (imperfective восхища́ть)

  1. to enrapture, to delight, to ravish

Etymology 2

Possibly an independent borrowing of the same Old Church Slavonic word as above. See also -хитить (-xítitʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐˈsxʲitʲɪtʲ]

Verb

восхити́ть • (vosxítitʹ) pf (imperfective восхища́ть)

  1. (dated, high-style) to lift up
