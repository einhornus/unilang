восхи́тить (26024)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic въсхытити (vŭsxytiti, “to fascinate, to snatch awaý”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vəsxʲɪˈtʲitʲ]

Verb

восхи́тить • (vosxitítʹ) pf (imperfective восхища́ть)

  1. to enrapture, to delight, to ravish

Etymology 2

Possibly an independent borrowing of the same Old Church Slavonic word as above. See also -хитить (-xítitʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐˈsxʲitʲɪtʲ]

Verb

восхи́тить • (vosxítitʹ) pf (imperfective восхища́ть)

  1. (dated, high-style) to lift up
