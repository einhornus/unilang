распределение -> distribution
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лето)

Plural: распределения
Genitive: распределения



Etymology: распредели́́ть (raspredelítʹ) +‎ -е́ние (-énije)



Definitions (English):
  • distribution (act of distribution or being distributed)
    • равноме́рное распределе́ние ― ravnomérnoje raspredelénije ― even / uniform distribution
  • allocation (e.g. of resources)
    • assignment (e.g. of young specialists by educational institutions)
      • она́́ к нам прие́хала по распределе́нию, oná k nam prijéxala po raspredelénijushe came to us as her assignment (i.e. she was assigned to work at some place after graduating)
