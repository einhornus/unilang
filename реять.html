ре́ять (21044)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic рѣяти (rějati). Compare Old Church Slavonic рѣѩти (rějęti), Ukrainian ріяти (ríjaty), Bulgarian ре́я се (réja se), Latvian raĩdît, Sanskrit रेतस् (rétas), रीतिस् (rītíṣ), रयते (rīуаte), रिणाति (riṇāti), Latin rīvus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈrʲe(j)ɪtʲ]

Verb

ре́ять • (réjatʹ) impf (perfective ринуть)

  1. to soar, to fly, to hover
  2. to flutter, to fly (of a flag)
