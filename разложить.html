разложить -> to put away, lay, set up, distribute, lay out, spread
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я разложу
Ты разложишь
Past: разложил/разложила



Etymology: раз- (raz-) +‎ ложи́ть (ložítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to put (in separate locations), to put away
    • to lay out, to display
      • to lay flat (e.g. a newspaper or cot)
        • to spread out (cards)
          • разложи́́ть ка́рты ― razložítʹ kárty ― (for a divination) to spread the cards (for a game) to deal the card
        • to distribute, to apportion, to allocate
          • to build and light (a fire)
            • (dated, figuratively) to unharness
              • (chemistry) to decompose,
                • разложи́́ть во́ду на кислоро́д и водоро́д ― razložítʹ vódu na kisloród i vodoród ― to decompose/break water into oxygen and hydrogen
              • (mathematics) to factorize,
                • разложи́́ть число́́ на мно́жи́тели ― razložítʹ čisló na mnóžiteli ― to factorize a number
              • (physics) (force) to resolve
                • to demoralize, to corrupt,
                  • разложи́́ть а́рмию проти́вника ― razložítʹ ármiju protívnika ― to demoralize/corrupt the enemy's army
