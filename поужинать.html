поу́жинать (10060)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ ужина́ть (úžinatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈuʐɨnətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

поу́жинать • (poúžinatʹ) pf (imperfective ужина́ть)

  1. to dine; to have dinner; to have supper (refers to the 3rd meal of the day, served around 7 to 8 p.m.)

Related terms

  • у́жин (úžin)
