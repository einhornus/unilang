Verb[edit]

цвести́ • (cvestí) impf (perfective расцвести́)

  1. to bloom, to blossom, to flower
  2. to flourish, to prosper
  3. water to become overgrown, to be covered with duckweed/mold

Related terms[edit]

  • цвете́ние (cveténije)
  • цветок (cvétok)
