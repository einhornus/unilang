пантало́ны (24694)


Etymology

Borrowed from French pantalons.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəntɐˈlonɨ]

Noun

пантало́ны • (pantalóny) f inan pl (genitive пантало́н, plural only)

  1. (plural only) long underwear, long johns, that is worn either under normaĺ clothing in cold weather, or as a night dress.
