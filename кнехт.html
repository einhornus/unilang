кнехт (28819)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Knecht.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [knʲext]
  • IPA(key): [knɛxt] (phonetic respelling: кнэхт)

Noun

кнехт • (knext, knɛxt) m inan or m anim (genitive кне́хта, nominative plural кне́хты, genitive plural кне́хтов)

  1. (nautical, inanimate) bollard
  2. (historical, animate) knight, servant
