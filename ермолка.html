ермо́лка (31176)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Yiddish יאַרמלקע‎ (yarmlke), from Polish jarmułka (“skullcap”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [(j)ɪrˈmolkə]

Noun[edit]

ермо́лка • (jermólka) f inan (genitive ермо́лки, nominative plural ермо́лки, genitive plural ермо́лок)

  1. yarmulke
