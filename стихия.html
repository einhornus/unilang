стихи́я -> element, elemental force, chaos
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: стихи́и
Genitive: стихи́и



Etymology: Borrowed from Ancient Greek στοιχεῖον (stoikheîon).



Definitions (English):
  • element (one of the four classical elements of Ancient Greek philosophy)
    • elemental force of nature, and their spheres of influence
      • (figuratively) force of societal life that does not succumb to control by people or society
        • (figuratively) one's familiar, comfortable, or usual surroundings
