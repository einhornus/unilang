толь (4757)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tolʲ]
  • Rhymes: -olʲ

Etymology 1

Borrowed from French taule.

Noun

толь • (tolʹ) m inan (genitive толя, nominative plural то́ли, genitive plural то́лей)

  1. roofing felt (roofing and waterproofing material made by impregnating roofing paper with coal tar or shale oil products)

Etymology 2

Adverb

толь • (tolʹ)

  1. (obsolete) only, whether, so, such
Synonyms
  • столь (stolʹ), насто́лько (nastólʹko)
