прое́хать (3430)


Etymology

про- (pro-) +‎ е́хать (jéxatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈjexətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

прое́хать • (projéxatʹ) pf (imperfective проезжа́ть)

  1. to go through/by (by horse or vehicle), to ride through/by, to drive through/by
  2. to drive past/along/across
  3. to travel (a certain distance)

Usage notes

прое́хать and е́хать are concrete verbs. Their counterpart, е́здить, is an abstract verb.
