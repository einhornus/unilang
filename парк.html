парк (1756)


Etymology[edit]

From German Park, English park or French parc.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [park]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

парк • (park) m inan (genitive парка, nominative plural парки, genitive plural парков)

  1. park (for recreation)
    Этот парк знамени́т свои́ми ста́туями. ― Étot párk znamenít svoími státujami. ― This park is famous for its statues.
  2. yard, depot
  3. fleet, stock
  4. depot
