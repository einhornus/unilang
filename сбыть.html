сбыть (19912)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ быть (bytʹ)

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Verb

сбыть • (sbytʹ) pf (imperfective сбыва́ть)

  1. to sell, to market
  2. (colloquial) to get rid of
