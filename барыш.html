бары́ш (22611)


Etymology

Per Vasmer, probably from Turkish barış (“peace”). An alternative hypothesis suggests an origin in Chuvash *парӑш (*parăš, “gift”). Or its source can be varış, from the verb varmak (to arrive, come).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈrɨʂ]

Noun

бары́ш • (barýš) m inan (genitive барыша́, nominative plural барыши́, genitive plural барыше́й)

  1. (dated or colloquial, usually in the plural) profit, income
