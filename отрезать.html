Verb[edit]

отре́зать • (otrézatʹ) pf (imperfective отре́зать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off
    семь раз отме́рь, оди́н отре́жь ― semʹ raz otmérʹ, odín otréžʹ ― look before you leap
    отре́зать пути́ ― otrezatʹ putí ― close the door, bar the way
  2. to snip off
  3. to divide, to apportion
  4. (speech) to cut short, to snap out
