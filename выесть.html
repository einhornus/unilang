вы́есть (24370)


Etymology

вы- (vý-) +‎ есть (jestʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨ(j)ɪsʲtʲ]

Verb

вы́есть • (výjestʹ) pf (imperfective выедать)

  1. to eat awaý (to erode or corrode gradually), to eat out (eat the inside)
    выеденного яйца не стоит (idiomatic) ― výjedennovo jajcá ne stóit ― not worth a hill of beans, not worth twopence, not worth of a brass farthing (literally, “not worth an eaten out egg”)
