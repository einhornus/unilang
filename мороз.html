моро́з -> frost
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: моро́зы
Genitive: моро́за



Etymology: From Old East Slavic морозъ (morozŭ), inherited from Proto-Slavic *morzъ. Doublet of мраз (mraz), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • freezing weather, frost
