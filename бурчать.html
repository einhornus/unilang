бурча́ть (16202)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʊrˈt͡ɕætʲ]

Verb

бурча́ть • (burčátʹ) impf (perfective пробурча́ть)

  1. to grumble, to rumble (to make a low growling or rumbling stomach noise)
  2. to grumble, to complain
  3. to mutter, to mumble
