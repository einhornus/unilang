се́йнер (19480)


Etymology

Borrowed from English seiner.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲejnʲɪr]

Noun

се́йнер • (séjner) m inan (genitive сейнера, nominative plural се́йнеры, genitive plural сейнеров) (normal usage)
се́йнер • (séjner) m inan (genitive сейнера, nominative plural сейнера, genitive plural сейнеров) (professional usage)

  1. seiner (type of fishing boat)
