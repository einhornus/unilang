мышка (3180)


Etymology

From мышь (myšʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka). The computing term is a Semantic loan">semantic loan from English mouse.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmɨʂkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: мыш‧ка

Noun

мышка • (mýška) f anim or f inan (genitive мышки, nominative plural мышки, genitive plural мы́шек)

  1. (animate) Diminutive of мышь (myšʹ).
  2. (inanimate, computing, diminutive) mouse
