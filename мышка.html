мы́шка -> armpit, mouse
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: мы́шки
Genitive: мы́шки



Etymology: From мышь (myšʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka). The computing term is a semantic loan from English mouse.



Definitions (English):
  • (animate) Diminutive of мышь (myšʹ).
    • (inanimate, computing, diminutive) mouse
