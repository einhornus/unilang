слезть -> to climb down, climb off

Etymology: с- (s-) +‎ лезть (leztʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to come down, to get down (from), to dismount (from)
    • (colloquial) to come down,
      • ша́пка слезла ему́ на глаза ― šápka slézla jemú na glazá ― the hat came down over his eyes
    • (colloquial) (from a train, bus etc) to get out (of), to get off
      • (colloquial) to peel off, to come off
