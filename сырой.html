сыро́й -> damp, raw
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *syrъ, from Proto-Indo-European *súHros (“sour, salty, bitter”), whence English sour.



Definitions (English):
  • damp, moist
    • raw, uncooked, unbaked
      • crude
        • green, unripe
