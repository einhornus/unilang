одио́зный (29165)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed during the second half of the 19th century from French odieux +‎ -ный (-nyj), itself a borrowing from Latin odiōsus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɐdʲɪˈoznɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

одио́зный • (odióznyj) (comparative (по)одиознее or (по)одиозней)

  1. odious, extremely unpleasant
