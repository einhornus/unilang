пушо́к (2407)


Etymology

пух (pux) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʊˈʂok]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

пушо́к • (pušók) m inan (genitive пушка, nominative plural пушки, genitive plural пушко́в)

  1. down
  2. fluff
  3. bloom (on fruit)

Related terms

  • пух (pux)
