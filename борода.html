борода́ -> beard
Noun, declension 1f
Plural: бороды
Genitive: бороды



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *bordà, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰardʰéh₂. Doublet of брада (bradá) borrowed from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • beard,
    • анекдо́т с бородо́й ― anekdót s borodój ― stale joke
  • (colloquial) chin,
    • у него́ на бороде́ ямка ― u nevó na borodé jámka ― he's got a cleft chin. Synonym: подборо́док (podboródok) (regular term)
