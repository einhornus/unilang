сгоряча -> in the heat of the moment

Etymology: From с (s) +‎ горяча́́ (gorjačá), apparently an obsolete short-form genitive of горя́чий (gorjáčij, “passionate, hot-tempered”).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) rashly, in the heat of the moment, without reflection
    • (colloquial) in a temper
