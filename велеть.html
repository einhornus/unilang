веле́ть -> to order (s.o. to do sth)
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я велю́
Ты вели́шь
Past: веле́л/веле́ла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *velě̀ti, from Proto-Indo-European *welh₁-.



Definitions (English):
  • to order (to do something), to enjoin, to command.
    • Сам бог веле́л. (expression) Sam box velél. God Himself has commanded (it). (i.e. it's natural or allowed to do so)
