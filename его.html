его́ -> his, its
Definitions (English):
  • (possessive) his.
    • Где его́ кни́га? ― Gde jevó kníga? ― Where is his book? Я уже́ ви́дел его́ кни́гу. ― Ja užé vídel jevó knígu. ― I already saw his book.
  • (possessive) its
    • accusative of он (on): him.
      • Она́ его́ ви́дит. ― Oná jevó vídit. ― She sees him.
    • genitive of он (on): of him
      • accusative of оно́ (onó): it
        • genitive of оно́ (onó): of it


          Definitions (Russian):
