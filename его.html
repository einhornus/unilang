Pronoun[edit]

его́ • (jevó)

  1. (possessive) his
    Где его́ кни́га? ― Gde jevó kníga? ― Where is his book?
    Я уже́ ви́дел его́ кни́гу. ― Ja užé vídel jevó knígu. ― I already saw his book.
  2. (possessive) its

Pronoun[edit]

его́ • (jevó)

  1. accusative of он (on): him
    Она его́ ви́дит. ― Oná jevó vídit. ― She sees him.
  2. genitive of он (on): of him
  3. accusative of оно (onó): it
  4. genitive of оно (onó): of it
