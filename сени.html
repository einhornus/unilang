се́ни -> entrance hall (of peasant hut)
Noun, declension 2e
Plural: се́ни
Genitive:  —



Etymology: Plural of сень (senʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • hall, vestibule, entrance hall (a room between the porch and the habitable part of a house where people leave their boots and overcoats especially in rustic homes)
    • (obsolete) railroad carriage platform, tambour
      • genitive singular of сень (senʹ)
        • dative singular of сень (senʹ)
          • prepositional singular of сень (senʹ)
            • locative singular of сень (senʹ)
