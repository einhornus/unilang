сени (9760)


Etymology 1

Plural of сень (senʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲenʲɪ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

се́ни • (séni) f inan pl (genitive сене́й, plural only)

  1. hall, vestibule, entrance hall (a room betweeń the porch and the habitable part of a house where people leave their boots and overcoats; especially in rustic homes)
  2. (obsolete) railroad carriage platform, tambour
