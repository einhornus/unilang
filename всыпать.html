всыпать (20930)


Etymology 1

в- (v-) +‎ сы́пать (sýpatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈfsɨpətʲ]

Verb

всыпать • (vsýpatʹ) pf (imperfective всыпать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to pour (в (v) + accusative, intó)
  2. (colloquial) to beat or scold strongly (+ dative, someone, за (za) + accusative, for something)

Etymology 2

всыпать (vsýpatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fsɨˈpatʲ]

Verb

всыпать • (vsypátʹ) impf (perfective всыпать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to pour (в (v) + accusative, intó)
  2. (colloquial) to beat or scold strongly (+ dative, someone, за (za) + accusative, for something)

Related terms

  • сы́пать impf (sýpatʹ), насыпа́ть pf (nasýpatʹ), посыпа́ть pf (posýpatʹ)
  • сы́паться impf (sýpatʹsja), насыпаться pf (nasýpatʹsja), посыпа́ться pf (posýpatʹsja)
  • сыпу́чий (sypúčij), сыпучесть (sypúčestʹ)
