Verb[edit]

проте́чь • (protéčʹ) pf (imperfective протека́ть)

  1. to flow, to run
  2. to leak, to ooze
  3. to be leaky
  4. to elapse, to fly

Related terms[edit]

  • протечка (protečka), протекание (protekánije)
