пече́нье (9959)


Etymology

From печь (péčʹ), from Proto-Indo-European *pekʷ-, whence English cook and biscuit (in part).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪˈt͡ɕenʲje]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

пече́нье • (pečénʹje) n inan (genitive пече́нья, nominative plural пече́нья, genitive plural пече́ний, diminutive печенька)

  1. pastry, cruller
  2. cookie (U.S.), biscuit (U.K.)
  3. cracker

Related terms

  • печь impf (pečʹ), испе́чь pf (ispéčʹ)
  • печь f (pečʹ)
