тормоши́ть (18198)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tərmɐˈʂɨtʲ]

Verb

тормоши́ть • (tormošítʹ) impf (perfective растормошить)

  1. to pull (at, about)
  2. to tug/shake in order to awake, to wake up by tugging/shaking
  3. to bother, to pester, to worry, to give no peace
  4. to stir up, to spur/awake to activity
