по́дданный -> subject, citizen
Noun, declension 0a
Plural: по́дданные
Genitive: по́дданного


Definitions (English):
  • past passive perfective participle of поддать (poddátʹ)
    • national (a person owing allegiance to a state)
      • citizen (a person who possesses citizenship of a state)
        • subject (legal member of a state)
