арома́т -> scent, aroma
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: арома́ты
Genitive: арома́та



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic ароматъ (aromatŭ), from Ancient Greek ἄρομα (ároma), genitive ἀρομάτος (ἀρομάτος).



Definitions (English):
  • aroma, fragrance, scent, perfume
