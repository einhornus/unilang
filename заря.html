зар -> dawn, sunset
Noun, declension 1d (pattern стра́н)

Plural: зри
Genitive: зар



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *zorja.



Definitions (English):
  • dawn, daybreak,
    • на заре́ ― na zaré ― at the earliest stage, at the beginning (literally, “at the dawn”)от зари до зари ― ot zarí do zarí ― from morning to night, from dawn to dusk (literally, “from dawn to dawn”)
  • dusk, nightfall
    • (military) reveille
      • (military) tattoo, retreat
        • outset, start
