кувалда (13704)


Etymology

With metathesis, from Ukrainian ковадло (kovádlo, “anvil”) or Belarusian кавадла (kavádla, “anvil”), from Polish language">Polish kowadło (“anvil”).

Alternatively, a derivative of вали́ть (valítʹ, “to fell, to knock down”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kʊˈvaldə]

Noun

кувалда • (kuválda) f inan (genitive кува́лды, nominative plural кува́лды, genitive plural кува́лд)

  1. sledgehammer (kind of hammer)
