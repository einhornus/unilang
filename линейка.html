линйка -> line (on paper), ruler (for drawing lines)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: линйки
Genitive: линйки


Definitions (English):
  • ruler (for measuring or drawing straight lines)
    • line (for writing)
      • писа́ть по лине́йкам ― pisátʹ po linéjkam ― to write on the lines
    • (polygraphy) rule,
      • наборная лине́йка ― nabórnaja linéjka ― setting rule
    • line (formation)
