простоя́ть (8950)


Etymology

про- (pro-) + стоя́ть (stojátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəstɐˈjætʲ]

Verb

простоя́ть • (prostojátʹ) pf (imperfective проста́ивать)

  1. to stand, to stay (for some time)
  2. to stand idle
  3. to stand, to remain (of buildings)

Related terms

  • See стоя́ть (stojátʹ) for related verbs.
  • просто́й (prostój)
