развернуться -> to unroll, unfold, expand, swing round (make a U-turn)
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я развернусь
Ты развернёшься
Past: развернулся/развернулась



Etymology: разверну́́ть (razvernútʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to turn around, to swing about / around to make a U-turn (vehicle) to slew (about)
    • to be deployed (of troops)
      • to come unrolled / unfolded / unwrapped
        • to spread, to expand
          • to display oneself
            • passive of разверну́́ть (razvernútʹ)
