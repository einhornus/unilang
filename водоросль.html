во́доросль -> seaweed
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: во́доросли
Genitive: во́доросли



Etymology: From вода́ (vodá) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ -росл (-rosl) +‎ -ь (-ʹ), based on a difficult-to-explain combining form of расти́ (rastí, “to grow”) (compare feminine singular past росла́ (roslá)). A parallel construction occurs in леторосль (létoroslʹ, “sprout”), also found as леторость (létorostʹ), with cognates Old Church Slavonic лѣторасль (lětoraslĭ), Czech letorost, Polish latorośl.



Definitions (English):
  • (usually in the plural) seaweed, algae
