суво́ровский (20684)


Etymology

Суворов (Suvórov) +‎ -ский (-skij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʊˈvorəfskʲɪj]

Adjective

суво́ровский • (suvórovskij) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) Suvorov (referring to anyone named Suvorov, especially Alexander Suvorov, Russian military hero)
    Суво́ровское вое́нное учи́лище ― Suvórovskoje vojénnoje učílišče ― Suvorov Military School (type of Russian military boarding school for teenage boys)

Related terms

  • Суворов (Suvórov)
