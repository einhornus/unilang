просто́й -> simple
Adjective



Etymologies:
  • Inherited from Old East Slavic простъ (prostŭ), from Proto-Slavic *prostъ.
  • From проста́ивать (prostáivatʹ).


Definitions (English):
  • simple, ordinary
    • basic
      • (mathematics) prime
        • standstill, stoppage, idle time, downtime
          • second-person singular imperative perfective of простоя́ть (prostojátʹ)
