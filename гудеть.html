гуде́ть -> to start hooting/humming
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я гужу́
Ты гуди́шь
Past: гуде́л/гуде́ла



Etymology: From a common Slavic root. Compare Ukrainian гудіти (hudíty), Belarusian гусці (huscí), Slovene gódem



Definitions (English):
  • to buzz to drone to hoot, to honk
    • (colloquial) to party, revel
