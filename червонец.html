черво́нец (9702)


Etymology

From черво́нный (červónnyj, “red (old)”). Old 10 rouble banknotes were red.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡ɕɪrˈvonʲɪt͡s]

Noun

черво́нец • (červónec) masculine gender">m inan (genitive черво́нца, nominative plural червонцы, genitive plural черво́нцев)

  1. (dated) chervonets, a ten rouble banknote or gold piece
  2. (colloquial) ten of any currency
