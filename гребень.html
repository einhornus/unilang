гре́бень -> comb, crest
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: гре́бни
Genitive: гре́бня



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *grebenь, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰrebʰ-, whence English grab and grasp.



Definitions (English):
  • comb
    • (of a bird) crest, comb
      • hackle, hatchel
        • ridge, crest
