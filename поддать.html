подда́ть (15435)


Etymology

под- (pod-) +‎ дать (datʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈdːatʲ]

Verb

подда́ть • (poddátʹ) pf (imperfective поддава́ть)

  1. to strike from beloẃ, to kick
  2. (games) to give awaý (a piece or card)
  3. (colloquial) to add
    подда́ть жару ― poddátʹ žarú ― to add fuel to the fire
  4. (colloquial) to booze, to bend the elbow

Related terms

  • поддавки (poddavkí, “giveaway checkers”)
