толкну́ть -> to push, shove
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я толкну́
Ты толкнёшь
Past: толкну́л/толкну́ла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic тълкнути (tŭlknuti, “to push to knock to attack”), from Proto-Slavic *tъlknǫti.



Definitions (English):
  • to push, to shove, to thrust
    • to incite, to instigate
      • (slang) to sell
