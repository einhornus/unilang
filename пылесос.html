пылесо́с (14050)


Etymology[edit]

From пыль (pylʹ, “dust”) +‎ -е- (-e-) +‎ -сос (-sos), combining form of соса́ть (sosátʹ, “to suck”). Calque of German Staubsauger.[1] First attested in 1907. Calqued also as пылевсасыватель (pylevsasyvatelʹ) (1914).[1]
