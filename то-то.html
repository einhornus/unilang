то-то -> that’s it, that’s right
Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) Aha!, That's it!, There we go! What did I tell you?
    • Ну согласи́сь же, я права! — Ла́дно, права. — Вот то-то же! Nu soglasísʹ že, ja pravá! — Ládno, pravá. — Vot tó-to že! Come on, just say I'm right. — Fine, you are. — There we go!
  • (colloquial) how (emphatic, limited usage)
    • То-то он удиви́тся! Tó-to on udivítsja! How surprised he will be!, Won't he be surprised?
  • nominative neuter singular of тот-
    • то (tót-to)
  • accusative neuter singular of тот-
    • то (tót-to)
