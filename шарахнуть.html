шара́хнуть (16941)


Etymology

Perhaps from шарахать (šaráxatʹ) +‎ -нуть (-nutʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂɐˈraxnʊtʲ]

Verb

шара́хнуть • (šaráxnutʹ) pf (imperfective шарахать)

  1. (colloquial) to hit, to strike, to land a blow (on), to sock, to paste one
  2. (colloquial) to boom, to shoot with a boom
  3. (colloquial) to drink, to gulp down
