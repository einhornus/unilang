заде́л (10303)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈdʲel]

Etymology 1

за- (za-) + a deverbal noun derived from де́лать (délatʹ)

Noun

заде́л • (zadél) m inan (genitive заде́ла, nominative plural заде́лы, genitive plural заде́лов)

  1. reserve, stock, margin
  2. (computing) backlog
Related terms
  • де́лать (délatʹ)
  • дело (délo)

Etymology 2

Verb

заде́л • (zadél)

  1. masculine singular past indicative perfective of заде́ть (zadétʹ)
