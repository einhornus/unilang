упрёк -> reproach
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: упрёки
Genitive: упрёка



Etymology: From у- (u-) +‎ *прёк (*prjok, “crosswise”), from *перёк (*perjók) see поперёк (poperjók). Original meaning "objection".



Definitions (English):
  • reproach, reproof, rebuke (mild rebuke, or an implied criticism)
