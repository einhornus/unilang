котёлок -> pot, bowler hat
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: котелки
Genitive: котелка



Etymology: котёл (kotjól) +‎ -о́к (-ók)



Definitions (English):
  • dixie (a large iron pot, used in the army), kettle, pot
    • bowler hat, derby
      • (colloquial) bean, upper storey, dome,
        • у него́́ котело́к не ва́рит, u nevó kotelók ne várithe is out of his wits he is weak in the head (lit. 'he doesn't boil his dome')
