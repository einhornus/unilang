оплы́ть (27528)


Etymology[edit]

о- (o-) +‎ плыть (plytʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈplɨtʲ]

Verb[edit]

оплы́ть • (oplýtʹ) pf (imperfective оплывать)

  1. (transitive) to swim around
  2. (intransitive) to swell up; to become bloated; to gutter (of a candle)
