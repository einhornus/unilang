дюйм (19914)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch duim, cognate with English thumb.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲʉjm]

Noun

дюйм • (djujm) m inan (genitive дю́йма, nominative plural дю́ймы, genitive plural дю́ймов, related adjective дюймовый)

  1. inch
