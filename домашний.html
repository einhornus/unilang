дома́шний -> domestic
Adjective



Etymology: From дом (dom) +‎ -шний (-šnij).



Definitions (English):
  • home, household, house
    • private
      • domestic (relational) family,
        • дома́шнее живо́тное ― domášneje živótnoje ― domestic animal pet
      • home-made, homespun
        • (plural) (own) folks, family
