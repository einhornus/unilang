знава́ть (23190)


Etymology

знать (znatʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [znɐˈvatʲ]

Verb

знава́ть • (znavátʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial, usually negative) to experience (joy, grief, defeat, rest, etc.) (repeatedly or frequently); iterative aspect">iterative of знать (znatʹ)

Usage notes

This is an iterative verb, and in standard language is conjugated only in the infinitive and the past tense.

Related terms

  • знать impf (znatʹ), узна́ть pf (uznátʹ)
  • зна́ние (znánije)
