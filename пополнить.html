попо́лнить (14271)


Etymology

по- (po-) + -по́лнить (-pólnitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈpolnʲɪtʲ]

Verb

попо́лнить • (popólnitʹ) pf (imperfective пополня́ть)

  1. to replenish, to fill up, to supplement, to enrich, to widen, to enlarge
  2. (military) to reman, to reinforce, to replace, to beef up

Related terms

  • пополне́ние (popolnénije)
