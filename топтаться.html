Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Past: топтался/топталась



Etymology: топта́ть (toptátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to shift one's feet,
    • топта́ться на ме́сте ― toptátʹsja na méste ― to mark time, make no headway
  • (colloquial) to hang about, to hang around, to loiter, to wait
    • (colloquial) to dawdle, to dillydally
      • passive of топта́ть (toptátʹ)
