ребёнок -> child
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: де́ти
Genitive: ребёнка



Etymology: From Old East Slavic робя (robja), from Proto-Slavic *orbę.



Definitions (English):
  • (plural
    • де́ти, less commonly ребя́та) child, kid, baby: children, kids, babies
  • (plural only) (plural
    • ребя́та) guys, fellows, friends, comrades
