диван -> divan, couch
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: диваны
Genitive: дивана



Etymology: From Ottoman Turkish دیوان‎ (dîvân), from Persian دیوان‎ (divân) ultimately from a combination of Sumerian 𒁾 (dub, “clay tablet”)) + Old Persian *vahana- (“house”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂wes- (“to dwell”), whence English was and vestal.



Definitions (English):
  • (furniture) sofa, couch, divan
    • (historical) divan, diwan (the council of state in a Muslim country)
      • (poetry) divan, diwan (a collection of poems)
