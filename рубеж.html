рубеж -> boundary
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: рубе́жи
Genitive: рубе́жа



Etymology: Borrowed from Church Slavonic рубежь (rubežĭ), from Proto-Slavic *rǫbežь, from *rǫbiti (“to chop, hem”) + *-ežь. Doublet of рубёж (rubjóž).



Definitions (English):
  • boundary, border, borderline, frontier (the line or frontier area separating regions)
    • line
