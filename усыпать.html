усыпа́ть (12004)


Etymology 1

у- (u-) + сы́пать (sýpatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈsɨpətʲ]

Verb

усы́пать • (usýpatʹ) pf (imperfective усыпа́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew (with), to bestrew (with)
    не́бо усы́пано звёздами ― nébo usýpano zvjózdami ― the sky is studded with stars

Etymology 2

усы́пать (usýpatʹ) + -а́ть (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊsɨˈpatʲ]

Verb

усыпа́ть • (usypátʹ) impf (perfective усы́пать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew (with), to bestrew (with)

Related terms

  • сы́пать impf (sýpatʹ), насы́пать pf (nasýpatʹ), посы́пать impf (posýpatʹ)
