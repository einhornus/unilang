а́рфа (24813)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Harfe.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈarfə]

Noun

а́рфа • (árfa) f inan (genitive а́рфы, nominative plural а́рфы, genitive plural а́рф)

  1. harp

Related terms

  • арфист (arfist)
  • арфистка (arfistka)
