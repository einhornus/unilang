ту́ндра -> tundra (treeless northern wastes)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ту́ндры
Genitive: ту́ндры



Etymology: Borrowed from Kildin Sami тӯннтрэ (tūnntre), the accusative/genitive form of тӯнтар (tūntar).
