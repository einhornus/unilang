Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ носи́ть (nosítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to bring, to carry (somewhere) (in passing)
    • to note down, to enter (data, records)
      • (impersonal) to cause to skid,
        • маши́ну зано́сит ― mašínu zanósit ― the car is skidding
      • (impersonal) to cause to become covered (with) (e.g. snow, dust, etc.)
        • to lift, to raise (arm, leg, etc.)
          • to dirty by wearing a long time
