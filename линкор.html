Noun[edit]

линко́р • (linkór) m inan (genitive линко́ра, nominative plural линко́ры, genitive plural линко́ров)

  1. (nautical) battleship (20th century, dreadnought and later designs)

Usage notes[edit]

  • The term линко́р usually only refers to dreadnoughts and later all-big-gun battleship designs; pre-dreadnought steam-powered battleships are referred to as бронено́сец (bronenósec) or (formally) эскадренный бронено́сец (eskádrennyj bronenósec).

Synonyms[edit]

  • лине́йный кора́бль (linéjnyj koráblʹ)
