смечко -> (small) seed
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лто)

Plural: смечки
Genitive: смечка



Etymology: семя (sémja, “seed”) +‎ -ечко (-ečko, “(usually endearing diminutive suffix)”).



Definitions (English):
  • Diminutive of семя (sémja): seed
    • (usually in the plural) sunflower seeds
      • (usually in the plural) child's play, something very easy to do
