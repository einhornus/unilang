ли́хо -> dashingly, with spirit (coll)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: лиха
Genitive: лиха



Etymology: лихо́й (lixój) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • (poetic) evil, ill, misfortune, trouble,
    • не буди лиха пока́ ли́хо спи́т (ти́хо)ne budí líxa poká líxo spit (tíxo)don't trouble until trouble troubles you, не помина́йте лихом (colloquial)ne pominájte líxomremember me kindly, think kindly of me don't hold the grudge (said by a person leaving or about to do something risky)узна́ть, почём фунт лиха, uznátʹ, počóm funt líxato fall on hard times, to plumb the depths of misfortune(literally, “to find out the price of evil per pound”)
  • An odd number, see the Chetno i licho game.
    • dashingly, jauntily
      • short neuter singular of лихо́й (lixój)
