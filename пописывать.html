попи́сывать (29643)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ пи́сать (pisátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈpʲisɨvətʲ]

Verb

попи́сывать • (popísyvatʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to write (repeatedly or frequently); iterative of пи́сать (pisátʹ)
    Он попи́сывал стате́йки в ра́зных бульварных газетёнках.
    On popísyval statéjki v ráznyx bulʹvárnyx gazetjónkax.
    He used to write some little articles for some tabloids.

Usage notes

  • This is semantically an iterative verb, but unliké many such verbs it has a full conjugation in the standard language.
