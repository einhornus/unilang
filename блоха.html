блоха́ -> flea
Noun, declension 1f
Plural: блохи
Genitive: блохи



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *blъxa (“flea”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰluseh₂ (“flea”).



Definitions (English):
  • flea.
    • У меня в карма́не блоха́ на аркане ― U menjá v karmáne bloxá na arkáne ― I’m flat broke. (literally, “I have a flea on a lasso in my pocket.”)
