поле́чь (21065)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ лечь (lečʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlʲet͡ɕ]

Verb

поле́чь • (poléčʹ) pf (imperfective полегать)

  1. (perfective only) to die or be killed in battle (of all or many people)
  2. (perfective only, colloquial) to lie down (of all or many people)
  3. to bend to the ground (of cereal grains or trees)

Related terms

  • ложи́ться impf (ložítʹsja), лечь pf (lečʹ)
  • ложить (ložítʹ)
  • ло́же (lóže)
