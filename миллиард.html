миллиард -> 1,000,000,000 (American billion)

Etymologies:
  • From French milliard.
  • Borrowed from Russian миллиа́рд (milliárd).


Definitions (English):
  • billion (1,000,000,000, 109)
    • A milliard (a short scale billion).
