валять -> to drag, to roll
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я валяю
Ты валяешь
Past: валял/валяла


Definitions (English):
  • to lay or drag on the floor or ground,
    • валя́́ть по́ полу ― valjátʹ pó polu ― to drag along the floor, валя́́ть в гря́зи ― valjátʹ v grjázi ― to drag in the mire. Perfective: вы́валя́ть (vývaljatʹ)
  • to roll (something) (
    • в (v) + prepositional, in flour, bread crumbs, etc., thereby covering the object) Perfectives: вы́валя́ть (vývaljatʹ), обваля́ть (obvaljátʹ)
  • to roll (something) (into some form), to shape by rolling. Perfective:
    • сваля́ть (svaljátʹ)
  • to knead. Perfective:
    • сваля́ть (svaljátʹ)
  • to felt, to make out of felt,
    • валя́́ть шля́́пы ― valjátʹ šljápy ― to make felt hats. Perfective: сваля́ть (svaljátʹ)
  • (low colloquial) to do (something) quickly, to carelessly and haphazardly to churn out (a book), to dash off (a painting), to slap together (a proposal), etc. Perfective:
    • наваля́ть (navaljátʹ)
  • (colloquial) (only imperative)
    • валя́́й(те́) ― valjáj(te) ― go!, go ahead!, go on!, carry on!
