валя́ть (6545)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐˈlʲætʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

валя́ть • (valjátʹ) impf (perfective вы́валять or сваля́ть)

  1. to lay or drag on the floor or ground
    валя́ть по́ полу ― valjátʹ pó polu ― to drag alonǵ the floor
    валя́ть в гря́зи ― valjátʹ v grjázi ― to drag in the mire
    Perfective: вы́валять (vývaljatʹ)
  2. to roll (something) (в (v) + prepositional, in flour, bread crumbs, etc., thereby covering the object)
    Perfectives: вы́валять (vývaljatʹ), обваля́ть (obvaljátʹ)
  3. to roll (something) (into some form), to shape by rolling
    Perfective: сваля́ть (svaljátʹ)
  4. to knead
    Perfective: сваля́ть (svaljátʹ)
  5. to felt, to make out of felt
    валя́ть шля́пы ― valjátʹ šljápy ― to make felt hats
    Perfective: сваля́ть (svaljátʹ)
  6. (low colloquial) to do (something) quickly, to carelessly and haphazardly; to churn out (a book), to dash off (a painting), to slap together (a proposal), etc.
    Perfective: наваля́ть (navaljátʹ)
  7. (colloquial) (only imperative)
    валя́й(те́) ― valjáj(te) ― go!, go ahead!, go on!, carry on!
