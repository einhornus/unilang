затере́ть (19928)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ тере́ть (terétʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zətʲɪˈrʲetʲ]

Verb

затере́ть • (zaterétʹ) pf (imperfective затирать)

  1. to erase, to wipe off
  2. to paint over, to overwrite
  3. to hold fast, to trap
    затёртый льда́ми ― zatjórtyj lʹdámi ― icebound
  4. (colloquial, figuratively) to ruin the career of (someone) by scheming
  5. (colloquial) to wear out through́ constant use
  6. (perfective only) to begin to rub

Related terms

  • тере́ть impf (terétʹ), потере́ть pf (poterétʹ)
  • тёртый (tjórtyj)
  • тёрка (tjórka)
