подоба́ть (12382)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pədɐˈbatʲ]

Verb

подоба́ть • (podobátʹ) impf

  1. to become, to befit
    не подоба́ет так поступа́ть
    ne podobájet tak postupátʹ
    it doesn't become one to behave like that; such behaviour/conduct is unbecoming
    как подоба́ет ― kak podobájet ― as it becomes one; properly
