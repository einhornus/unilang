водоле́й (21282)


Etymology

From вода́ (vodá) + -о- (-o-) + -лей (-lej), combining form of лить (litʹ, “to pour”) (compare imperative singular ле́й (lej)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vədɐˈlʲej]

Noun

водоле́й • (vodoléj) m anim (genitive водоле́я, nominative plural водоле́и, genitive plural водоле́ев)

  1. (dated) someone who pours water, water pourer
  2. Aquarius (someone with a Aquarius star sign)
