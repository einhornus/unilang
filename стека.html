сте́ка (26896)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈstɛkə] (phonetic respelling: стэка)

Etymology 1

Borrowed from Italian stecca.

Noun

сте́ка • (stɛka) f inan (genitive сте́ки, nominative plural сте́ки, genitive plural стек)

  1. (sculpting) modeling tool (wooden, bone or metal tool for sculpting clay or similar soft material, with a wide, blade-like end)

Etymology 2

Noun

сте́ка • (stɛka) m inan

  1. genitive singular of стек (stɛk)
