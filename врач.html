врач -> doctor
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: врачи́
Genitive: врача́



Etymology: врать (vratʹ) +‎ -ач (-ač). Originally meant "witch-doctor, enchanter".



Definitions (English):
  • (medicine, formal) therapist, not surgeon, not hospital nurse
    • doc, vet, medic, physician, doctor
