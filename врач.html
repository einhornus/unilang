врач (527)


Etymology

врать (vratʹ) +‎ -ач (-ač). Originally meant "witch-doctor, enchanter".

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vrat͡ɕ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

врач • (vrač) m anim (genitive врача́, nominative plural врачи́, genitive plural враче́й)

  1. (medicine, formal) therapist, not surgeon, not hospital nurse
  2. doc, vet, medic, physician, doctor

Usage notes

A female medical doctor is referred to as врач, or женщина-врач, or, colloquially only, врачи́ха.

The archaic vocative form врачу́ (vráču) is currently found only in the set phrase "Врачу́, исцелися сам" (Physician, heal thyself).

Synonyms

  • терапе́вт (terapévt, tɛrapévt)
  • до́ктор (dóktor), зна́харь (znáxarʹ), ле́карь (lékarʹ), цели́тель (celítelʹ)
