нагоня́ть (17857)


Etymology

на- (na-) +‎ гоня́ть (gonjátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəɡɐˈnʲætʲ]

Verb

нагоня́ть • (nagonjátʹ) impf (perfective нагна́ть)

  1. to catch up to
  2. to equal (someone) in success
  3. to make up for (lost time)
  4. (colloquial) to cause (an emotion) (на (na) someone)

Related terms

  • гнать (gnatʹ)
