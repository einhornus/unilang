пла́мя -> flame
Noun, declension 3c
Plural: пламена́
Genitive: пла́мени



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic пламꙑ (plamy, “flame”), from Proto-Slavic *polmy (“flame”). Doublet of полымя (pólymja) and поломя (pólomja), the inherited East Slavic forms.
