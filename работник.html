работник -> worker, s.o. who works
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: работники
Genitive: работника



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *orbotьnikъ, from *orbotьnъ + *-ikъ.



Definitions (English):
  • worker, blue-collar worker, employee
