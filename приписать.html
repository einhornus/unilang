приписа́ть (9600)


Etymology

при- (pri-) +‎ пи́сать (pisátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪpʲɪˈsatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

приписа́ть • (pripisátʹ) pf (imperfective припи́сывать)

  1. to add (by writing)
  2. to attach, to register
  3. to arrogate, to ascribe, to attribute, to impute
