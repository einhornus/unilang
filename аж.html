аж -> even (coll) (= да́же)
Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) even,
    • мне аж стра́шно ста́ло, mne až strášno stálo. I even got scared, you know.
  • (colloquial) as many as, as much as, all the way to (a word used for emphasis)
    • мне сего́дня аж пять пи́сем пришло́mne sevódnja až pjatʹ písem prišlóI received as many as five letters today.


Definitions (Russian):

      • Мне сего́дня аж пять пи́сем пришло́.
