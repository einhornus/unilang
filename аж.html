аж (3331)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [aʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -aʂ

Adverb

аж • (až)

  1. (colloquial) even
    • мне аж стра́шно ста́ло
      mne až strášno stálo
      I even got scared, you know.
  2. (colloquial) as many as, as much as, all the way to (a word used for emphasis)
    • мне сего́дня аж пять пи́сем пришло́
      mne sevódnja až pjatʹ písem prišló
      I received as many as five letters today.
