носоро́г (12722)


Etymology

From нос (nos, “nose”) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ рог (rog, “horn”), a calque of Ancient Greek ῥινόκερως (rhinókerōs). Cognates include Ukrainian носоріг (nosoríh), Belarusian насарог (nasaróh).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəsɐˈrok]
  • Audio
