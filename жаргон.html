жаргон (11800)


Etymology

Borrowed from French jargon.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʐɐrˈɡon]

Noun

жаргон • (žargón) masculine gender">m inan (genitive жарго́на, nominative plural жарго́ны, genitive plural жарго́нов)

  1. jargon
  2. slang, cant

Related terms
