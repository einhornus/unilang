газе́ль (29854)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡɐˈzʲelʲ]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from French gazelle, from Arabic غَزَال‎ (ḡazāl).

Noun

газе́ль • (gazélʹ) f anim (genitive газе́ли, nominative plural газе́ли, genitive plural газе́лей)

  1. gazelle

Etymology 2

Generic capitalization of Газе́ль (GAZelʹ), the name of a series of light commercial vehicles, itself a blend of Газ (GAZ) (the manufacturer) and газе́ль (gazélʹ).

Noun

газе́ль • (gazélʹ) f inan (genitive газе́ли, nominative plural газе́ли, genitive plural газе́лей)

  1. minibus, minivan

Etymology 3

Borrowed from Arabic غَزَل‎ (ḡazal).

Noun

газе́ль • (gazélʹ) f inan (genitive газе́ли, nominative plural газе́ли, genitive plural газе́лей)

  1. (poetry) ghazal
