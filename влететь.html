влете́ть (6230)


Etymology

в- (v-) +‎ лететь (letétʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vlʲɪˈtʲetʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

влете́ть • (vletétʹ) pf (imperfective влета́ть)

  1. to fly intó
  2. (colloquial) to run intó (to enter while running), to burst intó
  3. (colloquial, impersonal) to get intó trouble
    ему́ влете́ло ― jemú vletélo ― he got intó trouble, he is in trouble

Synonyms

  • (run intó): вбежа́ть (vbežátʹ)
  • (get intó trouble): доста́ться (dostátʹsja), попа́сть (popástʹ), нагореть (nagorétʹ)
