поп -> priest (coll)
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: попы́
Genitive: попа́



Etymologies:
  • Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic попъ (popŭ), from Ancient Greek παπάς (papás), variant of πάππας (páppas, “daddy, papa”).
  • Borrowed from English pop.


Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial or derogatory) priest, cassock
    • pop, popular music
      • pop art
        • genitive plural of попа́ (pópa)
