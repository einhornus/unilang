дробь (9903)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *drobь. Compare Gothic language">Gothic got">𐌲𐌰𐌳𐍂𐌰𐌱𐌰𐌽 (gadraban, “to chisel, to hew out”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dropʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

дробь • (drobʹ) f inan (genitive дроби, nominative plural дроби, genitive plural дробе́й)

  1. (mathematics) fraction, broken number
  2. (weaponry) small-shot, pellets, shot
  3. (music) drum roll
  4. (symbol) slash
