я́ство (18070)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈjastvə]

Noun[edit]

я́ство • (jástvo) n inan (genitive я́ства, nominative plural я́ства, genitive plural яств)

  1. (dated or ironic) viand (a choice dish)
