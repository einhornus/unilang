то́нна (3402)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German Tonne.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈtonːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

то́нна • (tónna) f inan (genitive то́нны, nominative plural то́нны, genitive plural то́нн)

  1. metric ton, tonne (1000 kilograms)
  2. large number, large amount, large weight
  3. ton
  4. (colloquial) a thousand units
    у него́ то́нна баксов ― u nevó tónna báksov ― he’s got a thousand bucks.
