свод -> arch, vault (roof), collection of texts
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: свды
Genitive: свда



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *sъvodъ. Synchronically analyzable as deverbal of своди́ть (svodítʹ).
