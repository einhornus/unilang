берег -> bank, shore
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: берега
Genitive: берега



Etymology: From Old East Slavic берегъ (beregŭ), from Proto-Slavic *bergъ. Doublet of брег (breg), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • bank, shore, coast, beach
    • land
      • Alternative spelling of берё
        • г (berjóg)
