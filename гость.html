гость -> guest
Noun, declension 2e
Plural: го́сти
Genitive: го́стя



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *gostь, Proto-Indo-European *gʰóstis.



Definitions (English):
  • guest, visitor,
    • идти́ в го́сти ― idtí v gósti ― to go visiting, быть в гостя́х у кого́-либо ― bytʹ v gostjáx u kovó-libo ― to be a guest of someone
  • (obsolete) merchant, especially one that trades overseas.
