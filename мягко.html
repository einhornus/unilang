мгко -> softly

Etymology: мя́гкий (mjáxkij) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • softly, mildly, gently,
    • мя́гко говоря́ ― mjáxko govorjá ― to put it mildly, мя́гко выража́ясь ― mjáxko vyražájasʹ ― to put it mildly
  • Predicative
    • мя́гко
      • mjáxko
        • it is comfortable, it is soft (e.g. for someone to sit)
          • short neuter singular of мя́гкий (mjáxkij)
