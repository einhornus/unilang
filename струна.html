стру́на -> string (of instrument)
Noun, declension 1d (pattern стра́на)

Plural: струны
Genitive: стру́ны



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *struna.



Definitions (English):
  • string (of a musical instrument or tennis racket)
    • гита́рные стру́ны ― gitárnyje strúny ― guitar strings
  • long line (e.g. of a road)
    • side, feature (of human nature)
