изныва́ть (17212)


Etymology

изныть (iznýtʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪznɨˈvatʲ]

Verb

изныва́ть • (iznyvátʹ) impf (perfective изныть)

  1. to pine awaý, to pine for, to languish
  2. to be bored to death
