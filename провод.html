провод -> wire
Noun, declension 2c (pattern город)

Plural: про́вода
Genitive: провода́



Etymologies:
  • Ultimately deverbal of проводи́́ть (provodítʹ), with stress shift betraying an early derivation.
  • Deverbal of проводи́́ть (provodítʹ).


Definitions (English):
  • wire, lead, conductor
    • leading, conducting, steering
      • denoting, marking, indicating (by means of a long line or track)
        • construction (of canals, roads, etc.)
