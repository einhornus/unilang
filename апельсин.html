апельсн -> orange
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: апельсны
Genitive: апельсна



Etymology: Borrowed from Dutch appelsien, from French pomme de Sine (“Chinese apple”).

  • orange (fruit)
    • долька апельси́на ― dólʹka apelʹsína ― orange segment(понима́ть) как свинья́ в апельси́нах (saying) ― (ponimátʹ) kak svinʹjá v apelʹsínax ― (to know as much about something) as a pig about pineapples (literally, “(to understand) like a pig in oranges”)
