письмо́ -> letter
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: письма
Genitive: письма



Etymology: Inherited from Proto-Slavic *pisьmo. Cognate with Old Church Slavonic писмѧ (pismę, “letter (of the alphabet) writing”) (genitive писмене (pismene)).



Definitions (English):
  • letter (written communication)
    • writing
