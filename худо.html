ху́до (4976)


Etymology

худо́й (xudój) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxudə]

Noun

ху́до • (xúdo) n inan (genitive худа, uncountable)

  1. evil, harm
    нет худа без добра́
    net xúda bez dobrá
    every cloud has a silver lining

Adverb

ху́до • (xúdo) (comparative (по)хуже)

  1. ill, badly

Adjective

ху́до • (xúdo)

  1. short neuter singular of худо́й (xudój)
