лдка -> boat, rowing boat
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: лдки
Genitive: лдки



Etymology: From Old East Slavic лодъка (lodŭka), diminutive of лодья (lodĭja), from Proto-Slavic *oldī.



Definitions (English):
  • boat, dinghy, gig, yawl
