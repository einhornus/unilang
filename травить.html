тра́вить -> to exterminate, poison, damage, hunt, persecute
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Я травлю
Ты травишь
Past: тра́вил/тра́вила



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *traviti. Cognate with Old Church Slavonic травити (traviti), Ukrainian трави́́ти (travýty), Serbo-Croatian тра́вити (first singular тра̑ви̑м), Czech tráviti, Slovak trávit᾽, Polish trawić (first singular trawię). Further cognate with Ancient Greek τρώω (trṓō, “to hurt”), τιτρώσκω (titrṓskō), τρύ̄ω (trú̄ō), Old High German drawa (“threat”), drōа (“threat”), Old English þrēа (“threat, punishment”). More distantly related to тере́ть (terétʹ, “to rub”).
