по́рог -> threshold
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: по́роги
Genitive: по́рога



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *porgъ (“threshold”), Proto-Balto-Slavic *porʔgos, from Proto-Indo-European *porg-o-.



Definitions (English):
  • threshold, doorstep
    • rapid (turbulent flow section in a river)
