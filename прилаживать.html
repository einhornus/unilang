прила́живать (23678)


Etymology[edit]

прила́дить (priláditʹ) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪˈlaʐɨvətʲ]

Verb[edit]

прила́живать • (priláživatʹ) impf (perfective прила́дить)

  1. (colloquial) to attach, to fasten (к (k) + dative, to something)
  2. (colloquial, transitive) to fit (clothing)

  • нелады́ (neladý)
