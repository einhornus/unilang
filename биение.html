бие́ние (24476)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲɪˈjenʲɪje]

Noun

бие́ние • (bijénije) n inan (genitive бие́ния, nominative plural бие́ния, genitive plural бие́ний)

  1. beating, beat, throb, pulse

Related terms

  • бить (bitʹ)
