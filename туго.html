ту́го -> tight(ly), difficult, hardly

Etymology: туго́й (tugój) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • tightly
    • slowly
      • Predicative
        • ту́го
          • túgo
            • (colloquial) it is bad, hard, difficult,
              • им ту́го прихо́дится ― im túgo prixóditsja ― they are having a hard time, life is hard for them
            • (impersonal) it is tight, short, lacking,
              • ту́го с деньга́ми ― túgo s denʹgámi ― money is tight (literally, “it is tight with money”)
            • short neuter singular of туго́й (tugój)
