Verb[edit]

сбра́сывать • (sbrásyvatʹ) impf (perfective сбро́сить)

  1. to throw down, to drop
  2. (a government) to throw off
  3. (skin, leaves) to shed
  4. (clothes, shoes) to throw off

Related terms[edit]

  • броса́ть (brosátʹ)
  • сброс (sbros)
