каратэ (24953)


Etymology

Borrowed from Japanese 空手, from 唐手 (karate), from Okinawan 唐手 (tūdī, “empty hand”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kərɐˈtɛ]

Noun

каратэ • (karatɛ) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. karate
