венец -> crown, wreath, halo
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: венцы́
Genitive: венца́



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic вѣньць (věnĭcĭ), from Proto-Slavic *vĕnьcь, from *vĕnъ (“wreath”) + *-ьcь, details of further origin unclear (possibly from Proto-Indo-European *woh₁y-nos or derived later) but ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weh₁y- (“to twist, wind, weave”).

      • вести под венец, vestí pod venéclead to the altar
    • (astronomy) corona
      • (religion) halo
