playboy (15106)


Etymology

From play +‎ boy.

Pronunciation

  • (Received Pronunciation) IPA(key): /ˈpleɪbɔɪ/
  • (General American) IPA(key): /ˈpleɪˌbɔɪ/
  • Hyphenation: play‧boy

Noun

playboy (plural playboys)

  1. A single man, especially a wealthy one, who devotes himself to a life of leisure and pleasure, often sexual, without́ commitments or responsibilities.

Synonyms

  • hedonist
