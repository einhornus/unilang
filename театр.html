теа́тр -> theatre
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: теа́тры
Genitive: теа́тра



Etymologies:
  • Borrowed from French théâtre, from Latin theātrum, from Ancient Greek θέατρον (théatron), replacing earlier ѳеатръ (feátr) (феатр in modern orthography), which had been borrowed from the Byzantine Greek.
  • From Russian теа́тр (teátr).
