тысяча -> thousand

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *tysǫti, *tysęti, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *tūˀsantis, from Proto-Indo-European *tuHsont-, *tuHsenti-. Cognate with Ukrainian тисяча (tysjača), Serbo-Croatian тисућа, tisuća, Slovene tisoč, Czech and Slovak tisíc, Polish tysiąc. Akin to Lithuanian tūkstantis, Latvian tūkstotis, Gothic 𐌸𐌿𐍃𐌿𐌽𐌳𐌹 (þusundi).
