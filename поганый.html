пога́ный -> foul, vile
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic поганъ (poganŭ, “pagan”), from Latin paganus (“pagan”).



Definitions (English):
  • (archaic) heathen
    • foul, unclean
      • lousy, nasty, rotten
        • (mushroom) poisonous
