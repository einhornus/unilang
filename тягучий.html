тягу́чий (10640)


Etymology

тяг- (tjag-) +‎ -учий (-účij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲɪˈɡut͡ɕɪj]

Adjective

тягу́чий • (tjagúčij)

  1. viscous, viscid, syrupy, malleable, ductile
  2. (song etc) slow, leisured, leisurely
