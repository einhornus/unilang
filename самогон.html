самогн -> home-made vodka
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: самогны
Genitive: самогна



Etymology: сам (sam, “self”) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ гон (gon, “distill”), from гнать (gnatʹ, “to distill”).



Definitions (English):
  • homebrew, samogon, moonshine
    • any home-made distilled alcoholic beverage
