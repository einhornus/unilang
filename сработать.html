срабо́тать (5582)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ рабо́тать (rabótatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [srɐˈbotətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

срабо́тать • (srabótatʹ) pf (imperfective сраба́тывать)

  1. to work, to function (of a machine)
  2. (colloquial) to succeed, to work (of an attempt)
    срабо́тало! ― srabótalo! ― it worked!
  3. (dated or high style) to make, to produce
