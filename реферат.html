рефера́т (28035)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Referat.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪfʲɪˈrat]

Noun

рефера́т • (referát) m inan (genitive рефера́та, nominative plural рефера́ты, genitive plural рефера́тов)

  1. abstract, synopsis, summary (an abrigement or summary)
  2. report, paper, essay
