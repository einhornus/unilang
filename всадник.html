всдник -> horseman
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: всдники
Genitive: всдника



Etymology: Per Vasmer, most likely formed within Slavic from в- (v-, “in”) +‎ садить (sadítʹ, “to seat”) +‎ -ник (-nik) less likely related to Sanskrit सादिन् (sādin, “riding, rider”).



Definitions (English):
  • horseman, equestrian, rider
