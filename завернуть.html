заверну́ть -> to wrap, (за +а) turn (a corner)
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я заверну́
Ты завернёшь
Past: заверну́л/заверну́ла



Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ верну́ть (vernútʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to wrap, to roll
    • to screw tight, to tighten (a nut)
      • to tuck up, to roll up
        • to turn,
          • заверну́ть за у́гол ― zavernútʹ za úgol ― turn round the corner
        • (colloquial) (takes preposition
          • в) to drop in, to call (at a place)
        • (colloquial) to turn down, to reject,
          • моё заявле́ние заверну́ли ― mojó zajavlénije zavernúli ― my application was turned down
        • (colloquial) to deny sb. entry/access
