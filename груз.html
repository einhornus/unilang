груз -> load, cargo
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)





Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *grǫzъ, itself possibly deverbal of *gręzti (“to sink”). Compare Ukrainian грузь (hruzʹ) and Polish grąz.



Definitions (English):
  • weight, burden, load
    • cargo, freight, lading
