gat (15060)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *gatь (“dike”). Cognate with Slovak hať (“dam”), Upper Sorbian language">Upper Sorbian hat, Polish language">Polish gać, Lower Sorbian gat (“pond, dam”), and Russian гать (gatʹ, “causeway”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɡât/

Noun

gȁt m (Cyrillic spelling га̏т)

  1. ditch
  2. dam
