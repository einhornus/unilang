Noun[edit]

буга́й • (bugáj) m anim (genitive бугая or бугая, nominative plural бугаи or бугаи, genitive plural бугаёв or бугаев)

  1. (dialectal) uncastrated bull
  2. (low colloquial, derogatory) bull (a large, strong man)
