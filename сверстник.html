све́рстник -> person of the same age, contemporary
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: све́рстники
Genitive: све́рстника



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic съвьрстьникъ (sŭvĭrstĭnikŭ). Compare Old East Slavic съвьрстьнъ (sŭvĭrstĭnŭ, “of the same age”), съвьрсть (sŭvĭrstĭ, “of the same age coeval, age-mate”), from вьрста (vĭrsta, “age”). Compare Slovene vȓst (“row, type, age”) (tonal orthography) (genitive vrstȋ (tonal orthography)), Sanskrit वृत्ति f (vṛttí, “method, essence, nature, gender”). Morphologically с- (s-) +‎ верста́ (verstá) +‎ -ник (-nik).



Definitions (English):
  • contemporary
    • coeval
      • age-mate (one who is the same age as another)
