впи́тывать (15013)


Etymology

впита́ть (vpitátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈf⁽ʲ⁾pʲitɨvətʲ]

Verb

впи́тывать • (vpítyvatʹ) impf (perfective впита́ть)

  1. to soak up, to soak in, to drink in, to absorb, to imbibe, to take in, to sop
    впи́тывать с молоко́м матери ― vpítyvatʹ s molokóm máteri ― to imbibe with one's mother's milk/at one's mother's knees

Related terms

  • впитывание (vpítyvanije)
