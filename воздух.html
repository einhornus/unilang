вздух -> air
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: вздухи
Genitive: вздуха



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic въздоухъ (vŭzduxŭ).



Definitions (English):
  • (usually uncountable) air
    • (figuratively) air, atmosphere
      • (criminal slang) money
        • (religion) the veil for church communion vessels
