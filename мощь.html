мощь -> power, might
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: мощи
Genitive: мощи



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic мощь (moštĭ). Compare native мочь (močʹ) and Ukrainian міч (mič) (both inherited from Old East Slavic мочь (močĭ)) and Ukrainian міць (micʹ) and Belarusian моц (moc) (both borrowed from Polish moc).



Definitions (English):
  • might, force, power, strength
