ту́фля -> shoe, house shoe, slipper
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ту́фли
Genitive: ту́фли



Etymology: From the Middle Low German tuffele (compare Low German Tuffel (“shoe”)), from older pantuffel, from French pantoufle (“slipper”). Compare Norwegian and Danish tøffel (“slipper”), Swedish toffel (“slipper”), Latvian tupele.
