накры́ть -> to cover, lay

Etymology: на- (na-) +‎ крыть (krytʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to cover
    • to hit, to trap (with gunfire)
      • (intransitive) to lay (a table)
        • накры́ть на стол ― nakrýtʹ na stol ― to lay the table
      • (colloquial) to catch (in the act)
