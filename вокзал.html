вокзал -> (main) station
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: вокзалы
Genitive: вокзала



Etymology: Borrowed from English Vauxhall (former site of a pleasure garden). The ending was influenced by зал (zal, “hall”). The spelling фоксал (foksal) attested in 1777 is from French facs-hall, from the same English source. The transportation sense derives from an epithet for the Pavlovsk train station building, which at one time served as both a passenger lounge and a place of entertainment.
