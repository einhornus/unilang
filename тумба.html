ту́мба -> pedestal, bollard
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ту́мбы
Genitive: ту́мбы



Etymology: Borrowed from German Tumbe (“grave, coffin”), from Vulgar Latin tumba (“grave”).



Definitions (English):
  • cupboard, cabinet, pedestal,
    • кухонная ту́мба ― kúxonnaja túmba ― base unit, ту́мба пи́сьменного стол ― túmba písʹmennovo stol ― desk cupboard, прикроватная ту́мба ― prikrovatnaja túmba ― bedside table
  • advertising pillar
    • (historical) stone (to bind horses)
      • (colloquial) fat/rotund person, tub
