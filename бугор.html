бугор -> mound
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: бугры
Genitive: бугра



Etymology: Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewgʰ- (“to bend”). Cognates include Ukrainian бугі́р (buhír), Lithuanian baũgurs.



Definitions (English):
  • (inanimate) hill, hillock. Synonyms:
    • курга́н (kurgán), холм (xolm)
  • (inanimate) bump. Synonyms:
    • ко́чка (kóčka), ши́шка (šíška)
  • (animate, colloquial) big cheese, boss
