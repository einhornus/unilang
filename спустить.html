спусти́ть -> to lower, release
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я спущу́
Ты спустишь
Past: спусти́л/спусти́ла



Etymology: с- (s-) +‎ пусти́ть (pustítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to let down, to lower, to sink (to allow to descend) Synonym:
    • опусти́ть (opustítʹ)
  • to launch, to lower (a boat)
    • to unchain, to unleash, to let loose
      • (colloquial) to ejaculate (sperm)
