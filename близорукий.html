близору́кий (13222)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic близозоръкъ (blizozorŭkŭ), with haplology.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [blʲɪzɐˈrukʲɪj]

Adjective

близору́кий • (blizorúkij) (no comparative)

  1. nearsighted, myopic
  2. shortsighted, lacking in foresight

Synonyms

  • (lacking in foresight): недальновидный (nedalʹnovidnyj)
