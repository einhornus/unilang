садово́д (17319)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sədɐˈvot]

Noun

садово́д • (sadovód) m anim (genitive садово́да, nominative plural садово́ды, genitive plural садово́дов)

  1. horticulturist (a gardener, a person interested or practising horticulture)

Related terms

  • садово́дство (sadovódstvo)
