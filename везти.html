везти́ -> to transport, (+d) be lucky
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: b/b

Я везу́
Ты везёшь
Past: вёз/везла́



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *vezti, from Proto-Indo-European *weǵʰ-. Cognate with Latin veho.



Definitions (English):
  • to convey, to carry (by vehicle), to deliver, to transport
    • (impersonal verb) to be lucky, to have luck, to work out for, to succeed, to be successful (+ dative case)
      • Паше и Саше не везёт сего́дня ― Páše i Sáše ne vezjót sevódnja ― Pasha and Sasha are having no luck today.дурака́м всегда́ везёт ― durakám vsegdá vezjót ― fools are always lucky. Perfective: повезти́ (poveztí)
