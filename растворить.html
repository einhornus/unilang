растворть -> to open, dissolve
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я раствор
Ты раствришь
Past: растворл/растворла



Etymologies:
  • рас- (ras-) +‎ -творить (-tvorítʹ)
  • рас- (ras-) +‎ твори́ть (tvorítʹ)


Definitions (English):
  • (transitive) to open suddenly (e.g. a window)
    • (transitive) to spread (e.g. legs of a compass or person)
      • (transitive) to dissolve
        • (colloquial) to knead (dough or clay)
