заеда́ть (18611)


Etymology

заед- (zajed-) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zə(j)ɪˈdatʲ]

Verb

заеда́ть • (zajedátʹ) impf (perfective зае́сть)

  1. to jam, to stick (e.g. of equipment or zippers) (also impersonal)
    ключ в замке зае́ло ― ključ v zamké zajélo ― the key stuck in the lock
  2. to eat after a drink of alcohol or medicine
    заеда́ть лекарство са́харом ― zajedátʹ lekárstvo sáxarom ― to take sugar after the medicine
  3. to kill by eating to death
  4. (figuratively) to torment
