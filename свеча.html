све́ча -> candle

Etymology: From Old East Slavic свѣча (svěča, “candle”), from Proto-Slavic *svěťa (“candle”), from *světъ (“light”) + *-ja.



Definitions (English):
  • candle (a light source, usually of wax)
    • Свеча́́ горе́ла на столе́́. ― Svečá goréla na stolé. ― A candle was burning on the table. Све́чи - иску́сственные исто́чники све́та́. ― Svéči - iskússtvennyje istóčniki svéta. ― Candles are artificial light sources.
  • (physics) candela (unit of luminous intensity)
    • (automotive) spark plug / sparking plug
      • (medicine) suppository
