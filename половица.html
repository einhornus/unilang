полови́ца (14086)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəlɐˈvʲit͡sə]

Noun

полови́ца • (polovíca) f inan (genitive полови́цы, nominative plural полови́цы, genitive plural полови́ц)

  1. floor plank, flooring strip

Related terms
