Noun[edit]

казачо́к • (kazačók) m inan or m anim (genitive казачка́, nominative plural казачки́, genitive plural казачко́в)

  1. kazachok, kozachok (dance)
  2. endearing diminutive of каза́к (kazák): (young, little) Cossack (can also be condescending)
  3. (obsolete) boy servant
