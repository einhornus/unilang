заводи́ть (6745)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ води́ть (vodítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəvɐˈdʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

заводи́ть • (zavodítʹ) impf (perfective завести́)

  1. to take, to bring, to lead
    заводи́ть в тупик ― zavodítʹ v tupík ― to lead up a blind alley
  2. to get, to procure, to acquire, to buy
  3. to establish, to set up, to found
  4. to start
    заводи́ть разгово́р ― zavodítʹ razgovór ― to start a conversation
  5. (a habit, friendship) to form, to contract
  6. to start (a motor), to wind up (clock)
  7. to get somebody worked up

Related terms

  • заводи́ться (zavodítʹsja), завести́сь (zavestísʹ)
  • води́ть (vodítʹ), вести (vesti)
  • заведе́ние (zavedénije)
  • заво́д (zavód)
