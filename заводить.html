заводи́ть -> to take somewhere, start, start up, wind up
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я завожу́
Ты заво́дишь
Past: заводи́л/заводи́ла



Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ води́ть (vodítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to take, to bring, to lead,
    • заводи́ть в ту́пик ― zavodítʹ v tupík ― to lead up a blind alley
  • to get, to procure, to acquire, to buy
    • to establish, to set up, to found
      • to start,
        • заводи́ть разгово́р ― zavodítʹ razgovór ― to start a conversation
      • (a habit, friendship) to form, to contract
        • to start (a motor), to wind up (clock)
          • to get somebody worked up
