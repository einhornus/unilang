Adverb[edit]

то́нко • (tónko) (comparative (по)тоньше)

  1. thinly (not thickly)
  2. subtly
  3. delicately, finely (e.g. of produced workmanship)

Adjective[edit]

то́нко • (tónko)

  1. short neuter singular of то́нкий (tónkij)
