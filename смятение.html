смяте́ние -> confusion, disarray, panic
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: смяте́ния
Genitive: смяте́ния


Definitions (English):
  • confusion, disarray, tizzy
    • turmoil, agitation
      • tumult
        • commotion, whirl
          • perturbation
            • dither
              • alarm (sudden surprise with fear or terror)
                • distraction
