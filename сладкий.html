слдкий -> sweet
Adjective



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic сладъкъ (sladŭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *soldъkъ. See also the dialectal Russian солодкий (solódkij), a doublet.



Definitions (English):
  • sweet (having a pleasant taste)
    • sugary
