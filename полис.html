полис (27434)


Etymology 1

Probably derived from Middle French policie.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpolʲɪs]
  		• (file)

Noun

полис • (pólis) m inan (genitive полиса, nominative plural по́лисы, genitive plural по́лисов)

  1. policy
    страхово́й полис ― straxovój pólis ― insurance policy

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Ancient Greek πόλις (pólis)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpolʲɪs]

Noun

полис • (pólis) m inan (genitive полиса, nominative plural по́лисы, genitive plural по́лисов)

  1. (historical) polis (a Greek city-state)

Etymology 3

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlʲis]

Noun

полис • (polís) m inan (genitive полиса, uncountable)

  1. police
