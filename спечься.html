Verb[edit]

спе́чься • (spéčʹsja) pf (imperfective спекаться)

  1. (blood) to coagulate, curdle
  2. to cake, sinter
  3. (colloquial) to be exhausted, be burned out
    он уже́ спёкся ― on uže spjóksja ― he's had it
  4. (colloquial) to fail, come a cropper

Related terms[edit]

  • спекание (spekánije)
