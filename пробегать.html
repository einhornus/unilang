пробега́ть (7442)


Etymology 1

про- (pro-) +‎ бегать (bégatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəbʲɪˈɡatʲ]

Verb

пробега́ть • (probegátʹ) impf (perfective пробежа́ть)

  1. to run past, to run through́
  2. to run a certain distance or for some time
  3. to skim, to scan, to look through́

Etymology 2

про- (pro-) +‎ бегать (bégatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈbʲeɡətʲ]

Verb

пробега́ть • (probégatʹ) pf (imperfective бегать)

  1. to spend some time running
