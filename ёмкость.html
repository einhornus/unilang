ёмкость -> capacity
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: ёмкости
Genitive: ёмкости



Etymology: ёмкий (jómkij) +‎ -ость (-ostʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • capacitance, capacity, volume
    • bottle, or anything that can hold liquid, usually of higher capacity.
      • В пусту́ю ёмкость нали́ли 40 (сорок) ли́тров воды. ― V pustúju jómkostʹ nalíli 40 (sórok) lítrov vodý. ― ( Someone) poured 40 liters of water into the empty bottle.
    • (electronics) capacitor
