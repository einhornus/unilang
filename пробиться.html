проби́ться (5806)


Etymology

проби́ть (probítʹ) + -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈbʲit͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

проби́ться • (probítʹsja) pf (imperfective пробива́ться)

  1. to fight/force/make one's way through, to break/win/strike through
  2. (of plants) to shoot, to show, to push up
  3. passive of проби́ть (probítʹ)
