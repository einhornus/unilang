auditorium (9899)


Etymology

From audiō (“I hear”) +‎ -tōrium.

Pronunciation

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /au̯.diːˈtoː.ri.um/, [au̯.d̪iːˈt̪oː.ri.ʊ̃ˑ]
  • (Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /au̯.diˈto.ri.um/, [au̯.d̪iˈt̪ɔː.ri.um]

Noun

audītōrium n (genitive audītōriī or audītōrī); second declension

  1. (law) A hearing.
  2. A hall or lecturé room.
  3. A school.
  4. An audience; an assembled group of listeners.
