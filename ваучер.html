ва́учер (16397)


Etymology

Borrowed from English voucher.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvaʊt͡ɕɪr]

Noun

ва́учер • (váučer) m inan (genitive ва́учера, nominative plural ва́учеры, genitive plural ва́учеров)

  1. voucher (a piece of paper that entitles the holder to a discount, or that can be exchanged for goods and services)
