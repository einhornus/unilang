ка́торга -> hard labour, penal servitude
Noun, declension 1b
Plural: каторги
Genitive: каторги



Etymology: From Byzantine Greek κάτεργον (kátergon, “galley penal labor”), from Ancient Greek κάτεργος (kátergos).



Definitions (English):
  • (type of punishment) penal servitude, hard labour
    • (figuratively) misery, hard life
