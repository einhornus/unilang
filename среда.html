среда́ -> Wednesday, environment, milieu
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: среды
Genitive: среды



Etymologies:
  • Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic срѣда (srěda), from Proto-Slavic *serda. Displaced native Old East Slavic середа (sereda, “middle Wednesday”) (cf. середа (seredá)), третииникъ (tretiinikŭ, “Wednesday”, literally “third (day)”).
  • Same as Etymology 1


Definitions (English):
  • Wednesday,
    • в среду́ ― v srédu ― on Wednesday, по сре́дам ― po sredám ― on Wednesdays, к среде́ ― k sredé ― by Wednesday, со среды ― so sredý ― from Wednesday, со среды до пя́тницы ― so sredý do pjátnicy ― from Wednesday to Friday, в сле́дующую среду́ ― v slédujuščuju srédu ― next Wednesday
  • environment, surroundings, sphere
    • midst
      • medium
