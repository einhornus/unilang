седло́ -> saddle
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: сёдла
Genitive: седла



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic седъло (sedŭlo), from Proto-Slavic *sedъlo, cognate to сиде́ть (sidétʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • saddle (seat on an animal)
    • вьючное седло́ ― vʹjúčnoje sedló ― pack-saddle
