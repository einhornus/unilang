вы́порхнуть (30699)


Etymology

вы- (vý-) +‎ порхнуть (porxnútʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨpərxnʊtʲ]

Verb

вы́порхнуть • (výporxnutʹ) pf (imperfective выпархивать)

  1. to fly out, to flutter out, to flit out (quickly and easily of birds, butterflies, etc.)
  2. (colloquial, figuratively) to flit out

Related terms

  • порха́ть impf (porxátʹ), порхнуть pf (porxnútʹ)
