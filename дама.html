да́ма -> lady
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: да́мы
Genitive: да́мы



Etymology: Borrowed from French dame, from Latin domina (“lady”), from domus (“house”), from Proto-Indo-European *dṓm, from root Proto-Indo-European *dem- (“to build”)
