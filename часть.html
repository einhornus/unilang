часть -> part
Noun, declension 3e (pattern ночь)

Plural: ча́сти
Genitive: ча́сти



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic часть (častĭ), from Proto-Slavic *čęstь.



Definitions (English):
  • part,
    • бо́льшей ча́стью ― bólʹšej částʹju ― mostly, по бо́льшей ча́сти ― po bólʹšej části ― for the most part
  • share
    • piece,
      • по частя́м ― po častjám ― in pieces
    • department, section
      • (military) unit (regimental or smaller sized and administratively self-contained)
        • (colloquial) line, branch
          • (obsolete) police station
