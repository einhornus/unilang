скамь -> bench
Noun, declension 1d (pattern стра́н)

Plural: скмьи
Genitive: скамь



Etymology: From Old East Slavic скамиꙗ (skamija), from Byzantine Greek σκαμνία (skamnía), plural of σκαμνίον (skamníon).



Definitions (English):
  • bench (long seat in a park or garden)
