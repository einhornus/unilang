Verb[edit]

отойти́ • (otojtí) pf (imperfective отходи́ть)

  1. to go away, to go aside, to move away, to move aside
  2. (military) to withdraw, to fall back
  3. (colloquial) to be out for a while, to go away temporarily (with the attention to come back soon)
    Он отошёл на де́сять минут. ― On otošól na désjatʹ minút. ― He'll be back in ten minutes.
  4. (отойти́ от те́мы) to digress
  5. (colloquial) to come around, to recover, to come back to normal (e.g. after a shock or disease)
  6. (to change the owner) to pass to
