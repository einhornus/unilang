мёртвый -> dead
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *mьrtvъ, from Proto-Indo-European *mr̥twós, *mr̥tós, ultimately from the root *mer- (“to die”).



Definitions (English):
  • dead,
    • мёртвая то́чка ― mjórtvaja tóčka ― dead center, мёртвый час ― mjórtvyj čas ― after-dinner rest
  • lifeless
    • dead man
