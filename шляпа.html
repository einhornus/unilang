шляпа (2042)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic шляпа (šljapa), from Bavarian German Schlappe

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʂlʲapə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

шляпа • (šljápa) f inan (genitive шля́пы, nominative plural шля́пы, genitive plural шля́п)

  1. hat
  2. milksop
  3. (colloquial) unpleasant, difficult situation
