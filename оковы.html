око́вы -> fetters
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: око́вы
Genitive:  —



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *okovъ.



Definitions (English):
  • shackles, manacles, gyves, fetters, irons (paired wrist or ankle restraints)
    • (figuratively) bondage
