моло́ть (13486)


Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *melti.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈlotʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

моло́ть • (molótʹ) impf (perfective смолоть)

  1. to grind, to mill (to make smaller by breaking with a device)
  2. (colloquial) to talk nonsense

Related terms

  • кофемолка (kofemólka)
  • мельник (mélʹnik)
  • ме́льница (mélʹnica)
  • мельничиха (melʹničixa)
  • мололка (mololka)
  • помол (pomol)
