ве́ять -> to blow (wind), flutter
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я ве́ю
Ты ве́ешь
Past: ве́ял/ве́яла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic вѣꙗти (vějati), from Proto-Slavic *vějati, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *wḗˀtei, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂weh₁-.



Definitions (English):
  • (intransitive) to blow gently
    • (intransitive) to wave, to flutter
      • (transitive) to winnow (grain)
