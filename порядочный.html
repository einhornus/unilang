поря́дочный -> decent, respectable
Adjective



Etymology: поря́док (porjádok) +‎ -ный (-nyj)



Definitions (English):
  • decent, honest, respectable
    • (figuratively) decent, sizable, considerable
      • (colloquial) utter, out-and-out.
        • Он поря́дочный подле́ц. On porjádošnyj podléc. He is an utter wretch.
