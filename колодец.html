коло́дец -> well
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: коло́дцы
Genitive: коло́дца



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *koldьcь, from earlier *koldędzь (whence колодезь (kolódezʹ)) with change of suffix.



Definitions (English):
  • well (hole sunk into the ground)


    Definitions (Russian):
