вежливый -> polite
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic вѣжливои (věžlivoi, “experienced, versed”), itself from вѣжливои (věžlivoi, “knower, competent”), from Proto-Slavic *věďa, from *věděti (“to know”). Compare Old Church Slavonic невѣжда (nevěžda, “ignorant”). See also -ливый (-livyj).



Definitions (English):
  • courteous, civil, polite
