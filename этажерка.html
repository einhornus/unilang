этаже́рка -> bookcase
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: этаже́рки
Genitive: этаже́рки



Etymology: Borrowed from French étagère.



Definitions (English):
  • bookcase, bookshelf (furniture displaying books)
    • whatnot, a piece of furniture with open shelves for displaying ornaments.
