секи́ра (13912)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *sekyra, related to сечь (sečʹ, “to cut”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪˈkʲirə]

Noun

секи́ра • (sekíra) f inan (genitive секи́ры, nominative plural секи́ры, genitive plural секи́р)

  1. pole-axe
