мани́ть (7025)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *maniti (“to deceive”), from Proto-Indo-European *meh₂- (“good, timely”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈnʲitʲ]

Verb

мани́ть • (manítʹ) impf (perfective помани́ть)

  1. to beckon, to wave (to)
  2. to attract, to lure, to entice, to allure

Related terms

  • мано́к m (manók)
