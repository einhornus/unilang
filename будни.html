будни -> weekdays
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: будни
Genitive: будня



Etymology: From the non-attested contraction of *будьнъ дьнь (*budĭnŭ dĭnĭ). Compare Bulgarian бу́ддень (búddenʹ), Polish budny dzień. See буди́ть (budítʹ) and день (denʹ) for more.



Definitions (English):
  • weekday(s), working days
    • humdrum, colorless existence
