бу́дни -> weekdays
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: бу́дни
Genitive: бу́дня



Etymology: From the non-attested contraction of *будьнъ дьнь (*budĭnŭ dĭnĭ). Compare Bulgarian буддень (búddenʹ), Polish budny dzień. See буди́ть (budítʹ) and день (denʹ) for more.



Definitions (English):
  • weekday(s), working days
    • humdrum, colorless existence
