владыка -> master, ruler
Noun, declension 1a
Plural: владыки
Genitive: владыки



Etymology: From Old Church Slavonic владꙑка (vladyka). Cognates include Bulgarian влади́ка (vladíka), Macedonian владика (vladika).



Definitions (English):
  • sovereign, lord, arbiter, overlord (judge without control)
    • ( Eastern Orthodoxy, respectful) bishop
