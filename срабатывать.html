сраба́тывать (13384)


Etymology

срабо́тать (srabótatʹ) + -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [srɐˈbatɨvətʲ]

Verb

сраба́тывать • (srabátyvatʹ) impf (perfective срабо́тать)

  1. to work, to function (of a machine)
  2. to fire, to go off (of a weapon, alarm, etc.)
  3. (colloquial) to succeed, to work (of an attempt)
  4. (dated) to make, to produce
