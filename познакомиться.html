познако́миться -> to become acquainted with, meet
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Etymology: по- (po-) +‎ знако́миться (znakómitʹsja)



Definitions (English):
  • to meet, to make the acquaintance, to make somebody’s acquaintance, to get acquainted.
    • Прия́тно познако́миться. Prijátno poznakómitʹsja. Nice to meet you.
  • to familiarize oneself, to go into
    • to visit, to see
