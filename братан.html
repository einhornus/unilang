брата́н (15604)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brɐˈtan]

Noun

брата́н • (bratán) masculine gender">m anim (genitive братана́, nominative plural братаны́, genitive plural братано́в)

  1. (colloquial) older brother
  2. (colloquial) brother
  3. (colloquial) bro, a form of address to a friend
  4. (slang) a member of a братва́ (bratvá) (a criminal group)

Synonyms

  • брато́к (bratók)

Related terms

  • брат (brat)
