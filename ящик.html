щик -> box, crate
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: щики
Genitive: щика



Etymology: From Old East Slavic аскъ (askŭ) or ꙗскъ (jaskŭ), from Old Norse askr (“wooden vessel”) or eski (“ashen box”). See *askaz for cognates



Definitions (English):
  • box, case, chest
    • drawer (in a table)
      • (colloquial, derogatory) idiot box, boob tube ( TV set)
