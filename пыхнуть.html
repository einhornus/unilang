Verb[edit]

пыхну́ть • (pyxnútʹ) pf (imperfective пы́хать)

  1. (colloquial) to blaze once, to burn brightly (once) (of a fire)
  2. (colloquial) to flash (of a fire)
  3. (colloquial) to puff once

Verb[edit]

пыхну́ть • (pýxnutʹ) pf (imperfective пы́хать)

  1. (slang) to smoke a spliff, to smoke a joint
Synonyms[edit]
  • накуриться (nakurítʹsja)
