свети́льник (10552)


Etymology

From свети́ть (svetítʹ, “to shine, lamp, beam, light”) +‎ -льник (-lʹnik).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [svʲɪˈtʲilʲnʲɪk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

свети́льник • (svetílʹnik) masculine gender">m inan (genitive свети́льника, nominative plural свети́льники, genitive plural свети́льников)

  1. lamp, light fixture

Related terms

  • свет masculine gender">m (svet)
