мгла -> haze, gloom
Noun, declension 1b
Plural: мглы́
Genitive: мглы́



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *mьgla, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃migʰleh₂ (“mist, cloud”).



Definitions (English):
  • darkness
    • (meteorology) mist, haze
