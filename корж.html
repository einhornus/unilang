корж (10745)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [korʂ]

Noun

корж • (korž) m inan (genitive коржа́, nominative plural коржи́, genitive plural корже́й, diminutive коржик)

  1. galette, flatbread (a type of flat, round cake)
  2. base for a (large) cake
