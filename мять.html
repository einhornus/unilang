мять (15362)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *męti; compare Old Church Slavonic мѧти (męti).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲætʲ]
  • Rhymes: -ætʲ

Verb

мять • (mjatʹ) impf (perfective помя́ть or измя́ть or смять)

  1. to rumple, to crumple, to wrinkle, to fumble, to crease
  2. to knead
    Perfective: размя́ть (razmjátʹ)

Related terms

  • вмя́тина (vmjátina)
  • зами́нка (zamínka)
  • всмя́тку (vsmjátku)
