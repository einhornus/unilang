хребт -> spine, ridge, mountain range
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язк)

Plural: хребт
Genitive: хребт



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *xrьbьtъ. Compare Ukrainian хребе́т (xrebét), Belarusian хрыбет (xrybjét), Bulgarian хръбет (hrǎbét), Polish grzbiet



Definitions (English):
  • spine, spinal column, backbone (also figuratively)
    • crest (of a wave or hill), ridge, range (a chain of mountains)
      • Аляскинский хребе́т ― Aljáskinskij xrebét ― Alaska Range
