волос -> (a) hair
Noun, declension 2e
Plural: воло́сья
Genitive: во́лоса



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *volsъ. Doublet of влас (vlas), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic. From *wel- (“hair wool”)



Definitions (English):
  • (anatomy) a hair
    • genitive of во́лосы (vólosy)
