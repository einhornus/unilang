на́вык (8070)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnavɨk]

Noun

на́вык • (návyk) m inan (genitive на́выка, nominative plural на́выки, genitive plural на́выков)

  1. skill, experience
  2. acquired habit, practice
Related terms
  • навы́кнуть pf (navýknutʹ)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈvɨk]

Verb

навы́к • (navýk)

  1. short masculine singular past indicative perfective of навы́кнуть (navýknutʹ)
