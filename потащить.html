потащи́ть (5134)


Etymology[edit]

по- (po-) +‎ тащи́ть (taščítʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [pətɐˈɕːitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

потащи́ть • (potaščítʹ) pf (imperfective тащи́ть)

  1. to start pulling
  2. (colloquial) to start carrying (unwillingly or something heavy)
  3. to start stealing
