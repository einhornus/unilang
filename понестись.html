понести́сь (6969)


Etymology

понести́ (ponestí) +‎ -сь (-sʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pənʲɪˈsʲtʲisʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

понести́сь • (ponestísʹ) pf (imperfective нести́сь)

  1. to rush off (after), to dash off (after), to tear alonǵ (after)
    он понёсся за ни́ми ― on ponjóssja za ními ― he rushed/dashed off after them; he tore after them
  2. passive of понести́ (ponestí)
