бюдже́тник (25753)


Etymology

First attested in 1989. From бюдже́т (bjudžét, “budget”) +‎ -ник (-nik).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲʊd͡ʐˈʐɛtʲnʲɪk]

Noun

бюдже́тник • (bjudžétnik) m anim (genitive бюдже́тника, nominative plural бюдже́тники, genitive plural бюдже́тников)

  1. (colloquial) government employee (that is paid from the government budget)
