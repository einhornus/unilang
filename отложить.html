отложи́ть (3512)


Etymology

от- (ot-) +‎ ложить (ložítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtlɐˈʐɨtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

отложи́ть • (otložítʹ) pf (imperfective откла́дывать or отлагать)

  1. to set aside
  2. to lay by, to save (money for some purchase)
  3. to put off, to delay, to adjourn, to postpone
  4. (biology) to lay (eggs, spawn etc.)
  5. to fold/turn back

Related terms

  • отложе́ние (otložénije)
