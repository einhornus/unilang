жена́ -> wife
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: жёны
Genitive: жены



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *žena, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *génāˀ, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷḗn, whence English queen and gyno-.



Definitions (English):
  • wife
    • (obsolete) woman
