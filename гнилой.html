гнило́й -> rotten, decayed
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *gňilъ.



Definitions (English):
  • rotten, putrid
    • (figuratively) damp (of the weather or climate)
      • (colloquial, figuratively) weak, infirm
