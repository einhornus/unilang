са́ни -> sledge
Noun, declension 2e
Plural: са́ни
Genitive:  —



Etymology: From Old East Slavic са́ни (sani), plural of сань (sanĭ), from Proto-Slavic *sanь.



Definitions (English):
  • sledge, sleigh, sled (a vehicle on runners, used for conveying loads over the snow or ice)
    • (sports) luge (a racing sled)
