жентый -> married (of man)
Adjective



Etymology: жена́ (žená) +‎ -атый (-átyj)



Definitions (English):
  • (of a man) married, having a wife
    • (by extension, of both man and woman together, of a couple) married, having a spouse.
      • Они давно́ жена́ты. ― Oní davnó ženáty. ― they have been married for a long time
