жена́тый (5382)


Etymology[edit]

жена́ (žená) +‎ -атый (-átyj)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ʐɨˈnatɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes:

Adjective[edit]

жена́тый • (ženátyj)

  1. (of a man) married, having a wife
  2. (by extension, of both man and woman together, of a couple) married, having a spouse
    Они давно́ жена́ты. ― Oní davnó ženáty. ― they have been married for a long time

Usage notes[edit]

  • It's incorrect to use the term when referring to women, за́мужем (zámužem) or заму́жняя (zamúžnjaja) must be used instead.
  • It's more common and standard to use short forms predicatively, e.g.:
    он жена́т ― on ženát ― he is married

Related terms[edit]

  • жени́ть (ženítʹ)
  • жени́ться (ženítʹsja)
  • жени́х (ženíx)
