стрец -> elder
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: стрцы
Genitive: стрца



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *starьcь. Equivalent to ста́рый (stáryj) +‎ -ец (-ec).



Definitions (English):
  • (literary) old man, whitebeard
