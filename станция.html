ста́нция -> station
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ста́нции
Genitive: ста́нции



Etymology: A conflation of two sources — Russian archaic стан (stan, “postal station, stage”), стани́ца (staníca, “stanitsa”) and the European words for “station” descended from Latin statio, such as Polish stacja, French station, Italian stazione, etc. Both sources ultimately derive from Proto-Indo-European *steh₂-.
