тика́ть (12174)


Etymology 1

Onomatopoeic. From the tick-tack sound of a clock.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲikətʲ]

Verb

ти́кать • (tíkatʹ) impf (perfective ти́кнуть)

  1. to tick (of a clock)

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Ukrainian тіка́ти (tikáty).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲɪˈkatʲ]

Verb

тика́ть • (tikátʹ) impf

  1. (regional, Ukraine) to run away
