тика́ть (12172)


Etymology 1

Onomatopoeic. From the tick-tack sound of a clock.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲikətʲ]

Verb

тика́ть • (tíkatʹ) impf (perfective тикнуть)

  1. to tick (of a clock)

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Ukrainian тікати (tikáty).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲɪˈkatʲ]

Verb

тика́ть • (tikátʹ) impf
