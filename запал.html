запал (16207)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈpal]

Etymology 1

Deverbal from запали́ть (zapalítʹ).

Noun

запал • (zapál) m inan (genitive запала, nominative plural запа́лы, genitive plural запа́лов)

  1. fuse, primer
  2. (colloquial) ardor, enthusiasm
  3. heaves (disease of horses and other domesticated animals)
  4. spoilage of grain still on the stalk due to drought
  5. (slang) dangerous situation where failure may lead to the participants getting caught
Related terms
  • запали́ть (zapalítʹ)
  • запальчивый (zapálʹčivyj)
  • запальчивость (zapálʹčivostʹ)

Etymology 2

Verb

запал • (zapál)

  1. masculine singular past indicative perfective of запа́сть (zapástʹ)
