сми́рно (7583)


Etymology[edit]

сми́рный (smírnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈsmʲirnə]

Adverb[edit]

сми́рно • (smírno) (comparative (по)смирнее or (по)смирней)

  1. quietly (in a quiet manner)

Interjection[edit]

сми́рно! • (smírno!)

  1. (military) attention! Used as a command to bring soldiers to the attention position.
