сожрать -> to eat (very coll), gobble up
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b/c

Я сожру
Ты сожрёшь
Past: сожрал/сожра́ла



Etymology: со- (so-) +‎ жрать (žratʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • (low colloquial) to eat.
    • Ты сожра́л весь торт?! Ty sožrál vesʹ tort?! Have you eaten all the cake?!
  • (low colloquial) to gorge, to scarf down (overeat or eat greedily, immodestly)
    • Он не съе́л, а сожра́л. On ne sʺjel, a sožrál. He didn't eat—he made a pig of himself.
  • (colloquial, of an insect) to sting, to bite.
    • Комары́́ сожра́ли. Komarý sožráli. Mosquitos have stung me.
