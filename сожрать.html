сожра́ть (7759)


Etymology

со- (so-) +‎ жрать (žratʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐʐˈratʲ]

Verb

сожра́ть • (sožrátʹ) pf (imperfective жрать)

  1. (low colloquial) to eat
    Ты сожра́л весь торт?!
    Ty sožrál vesʹ tort?!
    Have you eaten all the cake?!
  2. (low colloquial) to gorge, to scarf down (overeat or eat greedily, immodestly)
    Он не съе́л, а сожра́л.
    On ne sʺjel, a sožrál.
    He didn't eat—he made a pig of himself.
  3. (colloquial, of an insect) to sting, to bite
    Комары́ сожра́ли.
    Komarý sožráli.
    Mosquitos have stung me.
