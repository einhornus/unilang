попива́ть (15467)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ пить (pitʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəpʲɪˈvatʲ]

Verb

попива́ть • (popivátʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to drink slowly, to sip
    он там сиди́т и чаёк попива́ет ― on tam sidít i čajók popivájet ― he sits there sipping his tea
  2. (colloquial) to take to drink, to drink occasionally, to take to the bottle
