вложи́ть (3741)


Etymology

в- (v-) + ложи́ть (ložítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vlɐˈʐɨtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

вложи́ть • (vložítʹ) pf (imperfective вкла́дывать or влага́ть)

  1. to enclose, to insert, to put in; to sheathe; to embed
  2. to invest, to deposit
  3. to contribute

Usage notes

The alternative imperfective влага́ть (vlagátʹ) is dated.

Related terms

  • вклад (vklad)
