кра́ля (24743)


Etymology[edit]

Possibly from Polish krala (“queen (card games)”), from Czech král (“king”). Cognates include Ukrainian кра́ля (králja)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈkralʲə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

кра́ля • (králja) f anim (genitive кра́ли, nominative plural кра́ли, genitive plural кра́ль, masculine кра́ль)

  1. (dated) a wealthy stately lady
  2. (dated, card games) queen
    Synonym: да́ма (dáma)
  3. (low colloquial or folklore) beauty, a beautiful woman; someone's female lover/girlfriend
