ткань (2123)


Etymology

From ткать (tkatʹ, “to weave”), from Proto-Slavic *tъkati, from Proto-Indo-European *trūk-, *trūg- (“to press, beat”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tkanʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ткань • (tkanʹ) f inan (genitive тка́ни, nominative plural тка́ни, genitive plural тканей)

  1. fabric, cloth
  2. (biology) tissue

Related terms

  • ткать (tkatʹ)
  • тка́цкий (tkáckij)
  • ткач (tkač)
  • ткачиха (tkačixa)
