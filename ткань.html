ткань -> cloth
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: тка́ни
Genitive: тка́ни



Etymology: From ткать (tkatʹ, “to weave”), from Proto-Slavic *tъkati, from Proto-Indo-European *trūk-, *trūg- (“to press, beat”).



Definitions (English):
  • fabric, cloth
    • (biology) tissue
