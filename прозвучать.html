Verb[edit]

прозвуча́ть • (prozvučátʹ) pf (imperfective звуча́ть)

  1. to sound
  2. to be heard (of sound, voice)
  3. to be discernible (of emotion, mood in a voice or words)
  4. to sound (of impression)
  5. to be known widely

Related terms[edit]

  • звук m (zvuk)
