ярость -> rage
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: ярости
Genitive: ярости



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *jarostь (“fury”) (compare Serbo-Croatian járȏst), from *jarъ (“furious”) + *-ostь (abstract nominalizer).
