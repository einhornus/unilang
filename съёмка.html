съёмка -> shooting (of film), surveying, taking away
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: съёмки
Genitive: съёмки



Etymology: Deverbal of снять (snjatʹ) + -ка (-ka), based on the nominal combining stem съём- (sʺjom-), derived from the obsolete present tense stem съем- (sʺjem-).



Definitions (English):
  • removal
    • photo or video shoot, instance of filming
      • rental
        • (geography, geodesy) survey
