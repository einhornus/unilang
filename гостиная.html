гости́ная -> sitting-room
Noun, declension 0a
Plural: гости́ные
Genitive: гости́ной



Etymology: Ellipsis of гости́ная ко́мната (gostínaja kómnata, literally “guest room”).



Definitions (English):
  • living room
    • suite of furniture for a living room


      Definitions (Russian):
