уша́нка (9430)


Etymology

From у́ши (uši, “ears”) +‎ -анка (-anka).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈʂankə]

Noun

уша́нка • (ušánka) f inan (genitive уша́нки, nominative plural уша́нки, genitive plural уша́нок)

  1. ushanka (traditional Russian fur cap with ear flaps)
