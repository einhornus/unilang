монастырь -> monastery, convent
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: монастыри
Genitive: монастыря



Etymology: Ultimately from Ancient Greek μοναστήριον (monastḗrion), converged with words ending with "-ырь" (cf. пусты́рь).

  • monastery, convent
