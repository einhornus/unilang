глу́хой -> deaf, indistinct, muffled, remote (of place)
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *gluxъ.



Definitions (English):
  • deaf
    • dull, vague, muffled (sound)
      • (phonology) unvoiced, voiceless (consonant) Antonym:
        • зво́нкий (zvónkij)
      • out-of-the-way, remote, god-forsaken (place)
        • overgrown, wild (forest)
          • dead-end
            • deaf person
              • (plural) the deaf
