евре́йка (10973)


Etymology

евре́й (jevréj) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [(j)ɪˈvrʲejkə]

Noun

евре́йка • (jevréjka) f anim (genitive евре́йки, nominative plural евре́йки, genitive plural евре́ек, masculine евре́й)

  1. a female Jew, Jewess, Hebrew woman

Usage notes

  • The word евре́йка is used to refer to any female of Jewish ancestry, regardless of religious beliefs. When specifically referring to a follower of Judaism, the word иудейка (iudéjka) is used.

Related terms

  • евре́й (jevréj)
  • еврейский (jevréjskij)
  • евре́йство (jevréjstvo)
  • еврейчик (jevréjčik)
  • евреечка (jevréječka)
