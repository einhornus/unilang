речь -> speech
Noun, declension 3e (pattern ночь)

Plural: ре́чи
Genitive: ре́чи



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *rěčь (“speech”), Old Church Slavonic рѣчь (rěčĭ, “speech”).



Definitions (English):
  • speech, language (spoken)
    • пряма́я речь ― prjamája rečʹ ― direct speech (grammar)ко́свенная речь ― kósvennaja rečʹ ― indirect speech (grammar)
  • discourse, talk, conversation
    • word
