ублю́док (11901)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈblʲudək]

Noun

ублю́док • (ubljúdok) m anim (genitive ублю́дка, nominative plural ублю́дки, genitive plural ублю́дков)

  1. bastard (person born to unmarried parents)
  2. (vulgar, swear word) bastard

Synonyms

  • (bastard, person born to unmarried parents): (somewhat) по́месь (pómesʹ), (somewhat) полукровка (polukróvka)

Related terms

  • ублюдочный (ubljudočnyj)
