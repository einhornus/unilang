инди́йский (7177)


Etymology

и́ндия (Índija) + -и́йский (-íjskij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪnʲˈdʲijskʲɪj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

инди́йский • (indíjskij)

  1. Indian, Hindu, East Indian (relating to India or the East Indians)

Usage notes

  • Not to be confused with инде́йский (indéjskij, “Indian (relating to the native inhabitants of the Americas)”).
