застыва́ть -> to harden, thicken, congeal, (coll) become stiff
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я застыва́ю

Past: застыва́л/застыва́ла



Etymology: From засты́ть (zastýtʹ) +‎ -вать (-vatʹ) or from застынуть (zastýnutʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to thicken, to harden, to solidify
    • to cool down
      • (colloquial) to freeze, to feel cold to catch cold
        • to stiffen, to freeze, to be / become / stand stockstill
