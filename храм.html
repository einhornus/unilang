храм -> temple, church
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: хра́мы
Genitive: хра́ма



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic храмъ (xramŭ), from Proto-Slavic *xormъ. Doublet of хоромы (xorómy).



Definitions (English):
  • temple,
    • храм Артемиды Эфесской ― xram Artemídy Efésskoj ― the Temple of Artemis at Ephesus
  • ( Christianity) church, cathedral (a place of Christian worship that has an altar)
    • храм Василия Блаже́нного ― xram Vasílija Blažénnovo ― Saint Basil's Cathedral
  • (figuratively, poetic) shrine
