старшинство (27850)


Etymology 1

ста́рший (stáršij) +‎ -инство (-instvó)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stərʂɨnstˈvo]

Noun

старшинство • (staršinstvó) n inan (genitive старшинства, uncountable)

  1. seniority
  2. (programming) precedence (of operators)

Etymology 2

старшина (staršiná) +‎ -ство (-stvo)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stɐrˈʂɨnstvə]

Noun

старшинство • (staršínstvo) n inan (genitive старшинства, nominative plural старшинства, genitive plural старши́нств)

  1. (literary, military) position or rank of a sergeant major
  2. (literary, prerevolutionary Russia) position or rank of a foreman
