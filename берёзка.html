берёзка -> little birch tree
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: берёзки
Genitive: берёзки



Etymology: берёза (berjóza) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) Diminutive of берё
    • за (berjóza): (small) birch(-tree) young birch(-tree)
  • (colloquial) endearing form of берё
    • за (berjóza). Used for example of a tall, slender, pale-skinned Russian woman. Э́то тебе́ не ру́сская то́нкая берёзка!
  • field bindweed ( Convolvulus arvensis)
