ндпись -> inscription
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жзнь)

Plural: ндписи
Genitive: ндписи



Etymology: From надписать (nadpisátʹ, “to caption, to write an inscription on”) +‎ -ь (-ʹ), with typical stress shift (compare по́дпись (pódpisʹ), за́пись (zápisʹ), etc.).



Definitions (English):
  • inscription, writing
    • dedication (on a book)
      • address (on an envelope)
        • legend (on a coin, medal, map)
