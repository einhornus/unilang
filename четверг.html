четверг -> Thursday
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: четве́рги
Genitive: четве́рга



Etymology: Derivation from Proto-Slavic *četvьrtъkъ, as in other Slavic languages, formally impossible - but c.f. Samogitian ketvergs (“Thursday”).



Definitions (English):
  • Thursday,
    • в четве́рг ― v četvérg ― on Thursday, по четверга́́м ― po četvergám ― on Thursdays, к четвергу́́ ― k četvergú ― by Thursday, с четверга́́ ― s četvergá ― from Thursday, с четверга́́ до пя́тницы ― s četvergá do pjátnicy ― from Thursday to Friday, в сле́ду́ющий четве́рг ― v slédujuščij četvérg ― next Thursday
