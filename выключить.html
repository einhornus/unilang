выключить -> to switch off
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я выключу́
Ты выключишь
Past: выключил/выключила



Etymology: вы- (vy-) +‎ ключ (ključ) +‎ -ить (-itʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • (transitive) to shut down, to turn off, to disable (to put a mechanism, device or system out of operation)
    • вы́ключить свет ― výključitʹ svet ― to switch the lights off
  • (transitive) to exclude (to omit from consideration)
