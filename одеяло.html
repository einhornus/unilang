одеяло -> blanket
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: одея́ла
Genitive: одея́ла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic одѣꙝло (oděꙝlo, “garment, blanket”), from Old Church Slavonic одѣꙗло (odějalo, “outerwear, cloak”), from Old Church Slavonic дѣти (děti, “to set, to place”), from Proto-Slavic *děti, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁-, whence English face and do.



Definitions (English):
  • blanket, counterpane
    • quilt


      Definitions (Russian):

        • Плюшевое одеяло.
