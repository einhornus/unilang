относться -> to relate to, to regard, treat
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Я отношсь
Ты отнсишься

    • (imperfective only) to apply, to appertain (to belong to or be a part of, whether by right, nature, appointment, or custom to relate to)
