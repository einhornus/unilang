прохла́да (7563)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈxladə]

Noun

прохла́да • (proxláda) f inan (genitive прохла́ды, nominative plural прохла́ды, genitive plural прохла́д)

  1. coolness (the state of being cool)
