нарва́л (29597)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐrˈval]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from English narwhal.

Noun

нарва́л • (narvál) m anim (genitive нарвала, nominative plural нарва́лы, genitive plural нарва́лов)

  1. narwhal

Etymology 2

Verb

нарва́л • (narvál)

  1. masculine singular past indicative perfective of нарва́ть (narvátʹ)
