буха́ть (16424)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *buxati, originally onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbuxətʲ]

Verb

буха́ть • (búxatʹ) impf (perfective бухну́ть)

  1. to thump, to bang
  2. cannons to thunder, to thud
  3. to let fall with a thud
  4. to blurt out
Related terms
  • бух (bux)

Etymology 2

According to Vladimir Dal it is related to the homograph буха́ть (búxatʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʊˈxatʲ]

Verb

буха́ть • (buxátʹ) impf (perfective бухну́ть)

  1. (colloquial) to drink, to booze, to tipple
Synonyms
  • кирять (kirjátʹ)
Related terms
  • бухой (buxój)
  • бу́хло (buxló)
