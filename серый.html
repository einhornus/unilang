се́рый (526)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *śěrъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲerɨj]
  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

се́рый • (séryj) (comparative (по)серее or (по)сере́й)

  1. grey, gray
    gray/grey color:
  2. (figuratively) dull, dreary
  3. (figuratively) unremarkable, mundane, mediocre
  4. (figuratively) dim, ignorant, uneducated
  5. (figuratively) not quite legal
    получа́ть се́рую зарпла́ту ― polučátʹ séruju zarplátu ― to get paid under the table

Noun

се́рый • (séryj) masculine gender">m anim (genitive се́рого, nominative plural се́рые, genitive plural се́рых)

  1. (euphemistic) wolf
  2. (criminal slang) cop
