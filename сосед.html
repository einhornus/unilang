сосе́д -> neighbour
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: сосе́ди
Genitive: сосе́да



Etymology: From Old East Slavic сусѣдъ (susědŭ, “neighbor”), from Proto-Slavic *sǫsědъ, deverbative of *sъsěděti (“sit together”). The nominal prefix су- (su-) was replaced with productive verbal со- (so-) compare dialectal сусед (suséd). Equivalent to со- (so-) +‎ -сед (-sed).
