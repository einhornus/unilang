блеснть -> to flash
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я блесн
Ты блеснёшь
Past: блеснл/блеснла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *blьsknǫti.



Definitions (English):
  • to shine, to glitter, to sparkle, to flash
    • (figuratively) to be gifted with, to shine with, to sparkle with (+ instrumental)
      • to flash across one's mind (perfective only)
        • у меня блесну́ла мысль, u menjá blesnúla myslʹthe thought flashed across my mind
