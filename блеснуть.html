блесну́ть (5354)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *blьsknǫti.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [blʲɪsˈnutʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

блесну́ть • (blesnútʹ) pf (imperfective блесте́ть or блиста́ть)

  1. to shine, to glitter, to sparkle, to flash
  2. (figuratively) to be gifted with, to shine with, to sparkle with (+ instrumental)
  3. to flash across one's mind (perfective only)
    у меня блесну́ла мысль
    u menjá blesnúla myslʹ
    the thought flashed across my mind

Related terms[edit]

  • блеск (blesk)
  • блёстка (bljóstka)
  • блестя́щий (blestjáščij)
  • блестяще (blestjášče)
