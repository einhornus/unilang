выпи́сывать (8300)


Etymology

вы́писать (výpisatʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɨˈpʲisɨvətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

выпи́сывать • (vypísyvatʹ) impf (perfective вы́писать)

  1. to sign out
  2. to subscribe (e.g. to a magazine)
  3. to write, to write out, to write down
  4. to copy
  5. to discharge (from hospital)
  6. to take out
  7. to draw out, to draw
  8. to draw up
