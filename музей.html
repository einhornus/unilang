музе́й -> museum
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: музе́и
Genitive: музе́я



Etymologies:
  • From Polish muzeum or German Museum, from Latin mūsēum, from Ancient Greek μουσεῖον (mouseîon).
  • Borrowed from Russian музе́й (muzéj).
