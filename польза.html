польза -> use, benefit
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: пользы
Genitive: пользы



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic по́льза (polĭza), from Proto-Slavic *polьdza, from *po + *lьga. Doublet of dialectal польга (polʹga). Related to нельзя́́ (nelʹzjá), лёгкий (ljóxkij).



Definitions (English):
  • use, benefit, advantage, profit, usefulness
