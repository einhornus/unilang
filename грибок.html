грибок (12822)


Etymology

гриб (grib) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡrʲɪˈbok]

Noun

грибок • (gribók) masculine gender">m inan (genitive грибка́, nominative plural грибки́, genitive plural грибко́в)

  1. Diminutive of гриб (grib): small mushroom
  2. fungus (any member of the kingdom Fungi)
