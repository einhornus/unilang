зноби́ть (19963)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [znɐˈbʲitʲ]

Verb

зноби́ть • (znobítʹ) impf

  1. (impersonal) to hit with a fever, chills, or shivers
    меня́ зноби́т. ― Menjá znobít. ― I have the chills.
  2. (regional) to chill
