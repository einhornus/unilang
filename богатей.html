богате́й (29143)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bəɡɐˈtʲej]

Noun

богате́й • (bogatéj) m anim (genitive богате́я, nominative plural богате́и, genitive plural богате́ев)

  1. (colloquial) rich man

Verb

богате́й • (bogatéj)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of богате́ть (bogatétʹ)
