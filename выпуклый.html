вы́пуклый -> prominent, bulging, convex
Adjective



Etymology: From вы- (vy-) +‎ пуклый (puklyj, “arched”), from пук (puk, “bunch, bundle”).



Definitions (English):
  • convex (curved or bowed outward like the outside of a bowl or sphere or circle)
    • gibbous
