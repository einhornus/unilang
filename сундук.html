сунду́к -> trunk, chest
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: сундуки́
Genitive: сундука́



Etymology: From Turkish صندوق‎ (sandûk), from Persian صندوق‎ (sanduq), from Arabic صُنْدُوق‎ (ṣundūq). Compare Bulgarian сандък (sandǎk, “box”), Serbo-Croatian sanduk/сандук (“box, chest”).



Definitions (English):
  • trunk, chest, coffer, strongbox
