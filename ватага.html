ва́тага -> band, gang
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: ва́таги
Genitive: ва́таги



Etymology: A Turkic borrowing with a sense development “tent, family” → “group of people”. Compare Turkish otağ (“tent”).



Definitions (English):
  • (slang) gang (big, noisy group of people)
