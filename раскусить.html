раскуси́ть -> to bite through, (p only) (coll) suss out, get to the heart of sth
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я раскушу́

Past: раскуси́л/раскуси́ла



Etymology: рас- (ras-) +‎ кусить (kusítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to bite through
    • (colloquial) to guess, to get to the core, to get to the heart, to get to the bottom (of), to see through (someone), to get (someone's) measure,
      • раскуси́ть, в чём дело ― raskusítʹ, v čóm délo ― to get to the core/heart of the matter, тепе́рь я вас раскуси́л ― tepérʹ ja vas raskusíl ― I've got to the bottom of you now, он раскуси́л её ― on raskusíl jejó ― he saw through her
