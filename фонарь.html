фона́рь -> lamp
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: фонари́
Genitive: фонаря́



Etymology: Borrowed from Byzantine Greek φανάριον (phanárion, “lamp, torch”). Suffix influenced by -арь (-árʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • lantern, streetlight, streetlamp
    • electric torch, pocket lamp, flashlight
      • taillight, taillamp
        • (colloquial) shiner, black eye
          • (aviation) canopy
