жуть (13837)


Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʰed- (“take, seize, grasp”). Compare Old English ġietan, English get, Ancient Greek χᾰνδᾰνω (khandánō, “to hold, contain”), Latin hedera (“ivy”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʐutʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

жуть • (žutʹ) f inan (genitive жу́ти, nominative plural жу́ти, genitive plural жу́тей)

  1. dismay, dread, horror

Related terms

  • жу́ткий (žútkij)
