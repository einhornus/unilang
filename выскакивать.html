выска́кивать (6269)


Etymology

выскок- (výskok-) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɨˈskakʲɪvətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

выска́кивать • (vyskákivatʹ) impf (perfective вы́скочить)

  1. to jump out, to leap out
  2. to appear, to come up (of a sore, pimple, etc.)
  3. to drop out, to fall out
