врыва́ться -> to burst in
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Past: врыва́лся/врыва́лась


Definitions (English):
  • to rush into, to burst, to enter by force, to violently or hurriedly, to intrude
    • to dig oneself into
      • passive of врывать (vryvátʹ)
