Noun

  1. chock, block of wood

Noun

чурка • (čúrka) m anim or f anim (genitive чу́рки, nominative plural чу́рки, genitive plural чу́рок)

  1. (colloquial) a dumb, uneducated person, a dullard, a booby
  2. (offensive, ethnic slur) an immigrant from Central Asia or the Caucasus, a wog

Synonyms

  • (an immigrant from Central Asia or the Caucasus, a dumb, uneducated person): чурбан (čurbán)
