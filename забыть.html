забыть -> to forget
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: забыти
Genitive: забыти



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *zaby̋ti, the composite of за- (za-) +‎ быть (bytʹ). Cognate with Ukrainian забути (zabuty).



Definitions (English):
  • to forget
    • to neglect
      • to leave, to forget to bring, to forget to take
        • to let it go, to let it slip
