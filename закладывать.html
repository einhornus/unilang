закладывать -> to put behind, lay down, found, (+inst) block (with), pawn, mortgage
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я закладываю
Ты закладываешь
Past: закладывал/закладывала



Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ -кла́дывать (-kládyvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to put, to lay, to place,
    • закла́дывать страни́цу ― zakládyvatʹ stranícu ― to mark a page, to put in a bookmark, закла́дывать за воротни́к ― zakládyvatʹ za vorotník ― to put away a lot of booze
  • to lay the foundation (of), to lay
    • to heap (with), to pile (with), to block up (with)
      • to pawn, to pledge, to mortgage
        • to harness, to put a horse, to get ready a carriage
          • (slang) to betray, to give up (to police)
