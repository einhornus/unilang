достове́рность -> authenticity, reliability
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: достове́рности
Genitive: достове́рности



Etymology: достове́рный (dostovérnyj) +‎ -ость (-ostʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • authenticity (the quality of being genuine or not corrupted from the original)
