та́лый (13801)


Etymology

Cognate to Russian та́ять (tájatʹ, “to melt, thaw”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtalɨj]

Adjective

та́лый • (tályj)

  1. thawed; melted; partly melted; slushy (e.g. of snow)

Related terms

  • та́ять impf (tájatʹ)
