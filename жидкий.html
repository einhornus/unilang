ждкий -> liquid, thin, feeble
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *židъkъ (“liquid, watery”). Cognate with Old Armenian գէջ (gēǰ, “moist”).



Definitions (English):
  • liquid, fluid (existing in the physical state of a liquid)
    • watery, weak, thin
      • sparse, scanty
