малева́ть (28863)


Etymology[edit]

From Polish malować, ultimately from Middle High German mâlên.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [məlʲɪˈvatʲ]

Verb[edit]

малева́ть • (malevátʹ) impf (perfective намалевать)

  1. (colloquial, somewhat derogatory) to draw (pictures), to paint
