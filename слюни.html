слю́ни (14546)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *slina, from Proto-Indo-European *H₂leyH- (“to smear”). Cognates include Old Church Slavonic слина (slina), Bulgarian слю́на (sljúna), Czech slina, Polish ślina.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈs⁽ʲ⁾lʲʉnʲɪ]

Noun

слю́ни • (sljúni) m inan pl (genitive слюне́й, plural only, diminutive слю́нки)

  1. (colloquial) saliva, drool
