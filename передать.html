Verb[edit]

переда́ть • (peredátʹ) pf (imperfective передава́ть)

  1. to hand over, to deliver, to give
  2. to broadcast, to transmit
  3. to reproduce, to render
  4. to tell, to take a message

Related terms[edit]

  • See дать (datʹ) for related terms.
