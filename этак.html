этак -> this way, thus, approximately (coll)

Etymology: э- (e-) +‎ так (tak)



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) so, in this manner, thus,
    • и так и э́так ― i tak i étak ― this way and that
  • (colloquial) some, about,
    • киломе́тров э́так 20 ― kilométrov étak 20 ― about/some 20 kilometres
