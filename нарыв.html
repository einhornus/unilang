нары́в (23674)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈrɨf]

Etymology 1

Deverbal of нарывать (naryvátʹ).

Noun

нары́в • (narýv) m inan (genitive нары́ва, nominative plural нары́вы, genitive plural нары́вов)

  1. abscess, boil
Related terms
  • нарывать (naryvátʹ)
  • нарывник (naryvnik)
  • нарывной (naryvnoj)

Etymology 2

Participle

нары́в • (narýv)

  1. short past adverbial perfective participle of нарыть (narýtʹ)
