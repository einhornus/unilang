наро́чно (3464)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *паrоčьnъ(jь). Asynchronically analyzable as на (na) + рок (rok, “fate, destiny”). Compare Old Church Slavonic нарочьнъ (naročĭnŭ), Bulgarian наро́чно (naročno), Polish naroczny.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈroʂnə] (phonetic respelling: наро́шно)
  • IPA(key): [nɐˈrot͡ɕnə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

наро́чно • (naróšno, naróčno)

  1. on purpose, deliberately
