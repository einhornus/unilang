доби́ть (10778)


Etymology

до- (do-) + бить (bitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dɐˈbʲitʲ]

Verb

доби́ть • (dobítʹ) pf (imperfective добива́ть)

  1. to kill off, to finish off
  2. to crush completely, to destroy completely
  3. (colloquial) to finish
  4. to beat (до (do) to a particular state)
  5. to finish chiming (of a clock)
