уны́ло (6944)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈnɨlə]

Etymology 1

уны́лый (unýlyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Adverb

уны́ло • (unýlo)

  1. dejectedly, despondently
  2. dismally, cheerlessly

Adjective

уны́ло • (unýlo)

  1. short neuter singular of уны́лый (unýlyj)

Related terms

  • уны́лый (unýlyj), уны́ние (unýnije)
  • уныва́ть (unyvátʹ)

Etymology 2

Verb

уны́ло • (unýlo)

  1. neuter singular past indicative perfective of уныть (unýtʹ)
