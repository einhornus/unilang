спи́сок -> list
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: спи́ски
Genitive: спи́ска



Etymology: с- (s-) +‎ пис- (pis-, “to write”) +‎ -ок (-ok, “an object that remains after process is done”).



Definitions (English):
  • list, register, roll (register or roll of paper consisting of an enumeration or compilation of a set of possible items)
    • вычёркивать/вычеркнуть кого́-либо из спи́ска ― vyčorkivatʹ/výčerknutʹ kovó-libo iz spíska ― to strike someone off the list
  • copy (especially of a picture or an icon)
