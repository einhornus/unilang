мута́ция (21253)


Etymology

Ultimately borrowed from Latin mutatio; cf. Polish mutacja.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʊˈtat͡sɨjə]

Noun

мута́ция • (mutácija) f inan (genitive мута́ции, nominative plural мута́ции, genitive plural мута́ций)

  1. mutation
