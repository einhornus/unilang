таскать -> to drag
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я таскаю
Ты таскаешь
Past: таскал/таскала


Definitions (English):
  • to carry, to lug, to drag,
    • ежедне́вно таска́ть тяжёлые су́мки ― ježednévno taskátʹ tjažólyje súmki ― to carry heavy bags every day, е́ле но́ги таска́ть ― jéle nógi taskátʹ ― to be barely able to drag one's feet
  • (colloquial, transitive) to pull along, to drag along,
    • таска́ть кого́́-либо всю́́ду с собо́й ― taskátʹ kovó-libo vsjúdu s sobój ― to take someone along everywhere
  • (colloquial) to wear (clothes or shoes, long or carelessly)
    • не таска́й э́ти ту́́фли в дождь ― ne taskáj éti túfli v doždʹ ― don't wear [put on] these shoes when it rains
  • (colloquial) to pull (someone, causing pain
    • за (za) + accusative, by some body part)таска́ть кого́́-либо за во́лосы ― taskátʹ kovó-libo za vólosy ― to pull someone's hair (literally, “to pull somebody by the hair”)
  • (colloquial) to pinch, to swipe, to pilfer, to filch
