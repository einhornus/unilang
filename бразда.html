бразда́ (29230)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brɐzˈda]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic бразда́ (brazda), from Proto-Slavic *borzda. Doublet of борозда́ (borozdá), the inherited East Slavic form.

Noun

бразда́ • (brazdá) f inan (genitive бразды́, nominative plural бразды́, genitive plural бразд)

  1. (archaic, poetic) Synonym of борозда́ (borozdá)

Etymology 2

From Old East Slavic бръзда (brŭzda), from Proto-Slavic *brъzda.

Noun

бразда́ • (brazdá) f inan (genitive бразды́, nominative plural бразды́, genitive plural бразд)

  1. (archaic, usually in the plural) reins
    бразды́ правле́ния ― brazdý pravlénija ― the reins of government
