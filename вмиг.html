вмиг (10539)


Etymology

в (v) +‎ миг (mig)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vmʲik]

Adverb

вмиг • (vmig)

  1. (colloquial) in an instant, in a moment, in a flash, in a twinkling, in no time
