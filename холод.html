хо́лод -> cold
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: холода
Genitive: холода



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *xoldъ (“coolness, cold”). Doublet of хлад (xlad), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • cold (low temperature)
