зада́ть (3021)


Etymology[edit]

за- (za-) +‎ дать (datʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈdatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

зада́ть • (zadátʹ) pf (imperfective задава́ть)

  1. to set, to assign (an exercise or lesson)
    Нам задали три упражне́ния по а́лгебре.
    Nam zádali tri upražnénija po álgebre.
    We were given (assigned) three exercises in algebra (as homework).
  2. to ask (a question)
    зада́ть вопрос/вопросы ― zadátʹ voprós/voprósy ― to ask a question/questions
  3. to pose (a problem)
  4. (colloquial) to give, to throw (a dinner or banquet)
  5. to give, to administer (a punishment)
    зада́ть трёпку ― zadátʹ trjópku ― to give a thrashing, to give a dressing-down
  6. to preset, to specify, to assign

Related terms[edit]

  • зада́ние (zadánije)
  • зада́ча (zadáča)
