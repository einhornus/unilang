задть -> to set, assign (sth to s.o.)
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b/c

Я задм
Ты задшь
Past: задал/задал



Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ дать (datʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to set, to assign (an exercise or lesson)
    • Нам задали три упражне́ния по алгебре. Nam zádali tri upražnénija po álgebre. We were given (assigned) three exercises in algebra (as homework).
  • to ask (a question)
    • зада́ть вопро́с/вопро́сы ― zadátʹ voprós/voprósy ― to ask a question/questions
  • to pose (a problem)
    • (colloquial) to give, to throw (a dinner or banquet)
      • to give, to administer (a punishment)
        • зада́ть трёпку ― zadátʹ trjópku ― to give a thrashing, to give a dressing-down
      • to preset, to specify, to assign
