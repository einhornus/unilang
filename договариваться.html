договриваться -> to reach agreement (with)
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я договриваюсь
Ты договриваешься
Past: договривался/договривалась



Etymology: договаривать (dogovárivatʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to agree (mutually), to arrange (about, for), to come to an agreement / understanding (about)
    • to come (to), to talk (to the point of)
      • догова́риваться до абсурда ― dogovárivatʹsja do absúrda ― to come to the point of uttering an absurdity, догова́риваться до хрипоты ― dogovárivatʹsja do xripotý ― to talk oneself hoarse
    • to negotiate (about) (no perfective)
      • договаривающиеся стороны ― dogovárivajuščijesja stórony ― contracting parties


    Definitions (Russian):
