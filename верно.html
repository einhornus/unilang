ве́рно -> faithfully, truly, it is true

Etymology: ве́рный (vérnyj) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • right, correctly, faithfully, loyally
    • devotedly, constantly
      • (colloquial) probably, I suppose, most likely.
        • Он, ве́рно, уже́ ушёл. ― On, vérno, užé ušól. ― He probably already left.
      • (colloquial) indeed, that's right
        • short neuter singular of ве́рный (vérnyj)
