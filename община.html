община (6339)


Etymology

From о́бщий (óbščij) +‎ -ина (-ína) (suffix-stressed) or from о́бщий (óbščij) +‎ -ина (-ina) (stem-stressed).

Pronunciation

  • община: IPA(key): [ɐpˈɕːinə]
  • община: IPA(key): [ˈopɕːɪnə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

община • (obščína) f inan (genitive общины, nominative plural общины, genitive plural общин)

  1. commune, collective society (primitive, often clan-based, society with collective ownership of the means of production)
  2. (religion) community of believers

Noun

община or община • (obščína or óbščina) f inan (genitive общины or общины, nominative plural общины or общины, genitive plural общин or общин)

  1. community
