пресле́довать (4243)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪs⁽ʲ⁾ˈlʲedəvətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

пресле́довать • (preslédovatʹ) impf

  1. to pursue, to chase
  2. to persecute
  3. to torment, to victimize
  4. (law) to prosecute
  5. to strive (for); to pursue
  6. to haunt
