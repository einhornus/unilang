Noun[edit]

пробе́г • (probég) m inan (genitive пробе́га, nominative plural пробеги, genitive plural пробе́гов)

  1. run (distance)
    пробе́г при поса́дке ― probég pri posádke ― landing run
  2. (sports) run, race
    лы́жный пробе́г ― lýžnyj probég ― ski run; ski race
  3. total distance passed
    пробе́г автомоби́ля ― probég avtomobílja ― mileage; number of kilometres travelled
    како́й пробе́г у ва́шего автомоби́ля?
    kakój probég u váševo avtomobílja?
    what mileage has your car done?; how many kilometres has your car travelled/done?
