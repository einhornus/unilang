торе́ц (13710)


Etymology

[19th century] From Italian tor(so) + -е́ц (-éc).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐˈrʲet͡s]

Noun

торе́ц • (toréc) m inan (genitive торца́, nominative plural торцы́, genitive plural торцо́в)

  1. butt end
    торе́ц шпа́лы ― toréc špály ― sleeper butt [head end]
    торе́ц дома ― toréc doma ― the (shorter) side of a building
  2. wooden paving block
