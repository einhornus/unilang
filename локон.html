ло́кон (20795)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Locken, plural for Locke

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlokən]

Noun

ло́кон • (lókon) m inan (genitive ло́кона, nominative plural ло́коны, genitive plural ло́конов)

  1. lock, curl, ringlet
