ка́ли (29614)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkalʲɪ]

Noun

ка́ли • (káli) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. (obsolete) potash
Usage notes
  • The word ка́ли is used nowadays only as the part of term е́дкое ка́ли (jédkoje káli).

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈlʲi]

Verb

ка́ли • (kalí)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of калить (kalítʹ)
