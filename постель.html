постль -> bedding, bed
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жзнь)

Plural: постли
Genitive: постли



Etymology: Analyzable as deverbal from постелить (postelítʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • bed (a piece of furniture to sleep on)
    • (no plural) bedding (bedclothes)
