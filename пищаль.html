пища́ль (24891)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *piščalь. Equivalent to пища́ть (piščátʹ) +‎ -аль (-alʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪˈɕːælʲ]

Noun

пища́ль • (piščálʹ) f inan (genitive пища́ли, nominative plural пища́ли, genitive plural пища́лей)

  1. (music, dated) shepherd's pipe
  2. (historical) harquebus
  3. (historical) hand cannon, hand bombard
