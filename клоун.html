клоун -> clown
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: клоуны
Genitive: клоуна



Etymology: From English clown.



Definitions (English):
  • clown.
    • Вааг бои́́тся кло́унов. ― Vaag boítsja klóunov. ― Vahag is scared of clowns.
  • (derogatory) irresponsible person, who states unfounded claims, often thoughtless or careless
