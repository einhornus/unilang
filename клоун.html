кло́ун -> clown
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: кло́уны
Genitive: кло́уна



Etymology: From English clown.



Definitions (English):
  • clown.
    • Вааг бои́тся кло́унов. ― Vaag boítsja klóunov. ― Vahag is scared of clowns.
  • (derogatory) irresponsible person, who states unfounded claims, often thoughtless or careless
