пове́дать (6164)


Etymology

From по- (po-) +‎ ведать (védatʹ). Cognates include Polish powiedzieć, powiadać, Czech povědět, Slovak povedať, Serbo-Croatian povijedati, Ukrainian повідати, Belarusian распавядаць, Slovenian povedati, Bulgarian изповя̀дам.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈvʲedətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

пове́дать • (povédatʹ) pf (imperfective поведывать)

  1. (dated or literary) to tell, to retell, to recount, to say

Synonyms

  • рассказа́ть (rasskazátʹ)

Related terms

  • ведать (védatʹ)
