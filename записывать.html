запи́сывать (3275)


Etymology[edit]

записа́ть (zapisátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈpʲisɨvətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

запи́сывать • (zapísyvatʹ) impf (perfective записа́ть)

  1. to write down, to enter (to set something down in writing)
  2. to record (to make a record of information; to make an audio or video recording of)
  3. to enroll, to register (to enlist (someone) or make (someone) a member of)
  4. (computing, rare) to save (to write a file to a disk)
  5. (computing) to burn (to write a file to an optical disc)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (register): регистри́ровать (registrírovatʹ)
  • (save): сохраня́ть (soxranjátʹ)
Related terms[edit]
  • за́пись (zápisʹ)
  • запи́ска (zapíska)
  • запи́сываться (zapísyvatʹsja), записа́ться (zapisátʹsja)
