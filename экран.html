экра́н -> screen
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: экра́ны
Genitive: экра́на



Etymology: Borrowed from French écran.



Definitions (English):
  • screen (a physical divider)
    • screen (theater screen, TV screen)
      • (colloquial) cinematography
