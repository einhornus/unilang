пра́вый -> right, right-wing

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *pravъ, from Proto-Indo-European *proHwo- (“right judge, master”), ultimately from *per-. Compare пра́вда (právda). Cognate with Latin prōvincia (“territory, dominion, office, duty, province”), Proto-Germanic *frawjô (“lord”).
