нажа́ть -> to press
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я нажму́
Ты нажмёшь
Past: нажа́л/нажа́ла



Etymologies:
  • на- (na-) +‎ жать (žatʹ, “to press, to squeeze”)
  • на- (na-) +‎ жать (žatʹ, “to reap, to harvest”)


Definitions (English):
  • to press, to push.
    • Нажми́те любу́ю клавишу. ― Nažmíte ljubúju klávišu. ― Press any key. (in computer games)
  • to urge, to impel, to influence, to put pressure on
    • to press on, to press ahead
      • to make by pressing
        • to reap, to harvest (a specified quantity of something)
