Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈjɵmkʲɪj]

Adjective

ёмкий • (jómkij) (rare/awkward comparative (по)ёмче)

  1. capacious
  2. pithy, succinct, laconic, concise (of a speech or written work)
