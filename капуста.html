капу́ста -> cabbage
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: капу́сты
Genitive: капу́сты



Etymology: From Old East Slavic капу́ста (kapusta), possibly a contaminated form of Latin composta or composita.



Definitions (English):
  • (botany, usually uncountable) cabbage
    • (uncountable, slang) money, in particular United States dollars.
