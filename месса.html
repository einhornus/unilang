ме́сса (23565)


Etymology

Probably borrowed from French messe. Compare also Italian messa, German Messe. All from Latin missa (“dismissed”), from ite, missa est (“go, (the assembly) is dismissed”), last words of the Roman Rite of the Catholic Church.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmʲesːə]

Noun

ме́сса • (méssa) f inan (genitive ме́ссы, nominative plural ме́ссы, genitive plural ме́сс)
