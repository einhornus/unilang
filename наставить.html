наста́вить (13150)


Etymology

на- (na-) + ста́вить (stávitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈstavʲɪtʲ]

Verb

наста́вить • (nastávitʹ) pf (imperfective наставля́ть)

  1. to put, to place, to set (many)
  2. to piece (on), to add, to add a piece, to put on
    наста́вить рога́ ― nastávitʹ rogá ― to cuckold.
  3. to lengthen
  4. to aim, to level, to point
  5. to instruct, to admonish, to edify, to exhort
  6. to teach

Related terms

  • наставле́ние (nastavlénije)
  • наста́вник (nastávnik)
