болван -> blockhead, idiot
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: болваны
Genitive: болвана



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *bъlvanъ.



Definitions (English):
  • blockhead, dimwit (stupid person)
    • Э́тот болва́н так и не по́нял что́ от него́́ тре́бовалось. ― Étot bolván tak i ne pónjal štó ot nevó trébovalosʹ. ― This idiot couldn't understand what was required from him.
  • dummy (in card games)
    • block (for hats or wigs)
