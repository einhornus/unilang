велосипе́д -> bicycle
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: велосипе́ды
Genitive: велосипе́да



Etymology: Borrowed from French vélocipède, from Latin vēlōx (“fast”) + pedēs (“feet”). Compare English velocipede.



Definitions (English):
  • bicycle, bike. Synonym:
    • вели́к (vélik) (colloquial)
