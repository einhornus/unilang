Verb[edit]

вспы́хнуть • (vspýxnutʹ) pf (imperfective вспы́хивать)

  1. to flash (to briefly illuminate a scene)
  2. to blaze up, to break out (of fire), to flare up
  3. to blush, to flush
    • 1886, Anton Chekhov, “The Chorus Girl”, in (Please provide the book title or journal name):
      Ба́рыня вспы́хнула, и лицо́ её задрожало.
      Bárynja vspýxnula, i licó jejó zadrožálo.
      The lady flushed and her face quivered.
