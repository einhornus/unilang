некогда -> there’s no time, once
Definitions (English):
  • there is no time to (negative of когда́́ (kogdá))
    • Мне не́когда́ ― Mne nékogda ― I have no time.
  • in the old days, in former times, formerly, once,
    • не́когда́ зна́мени́тое уче́ние ― nékogda znamenítoje učénije ― a once-famous doctrine
