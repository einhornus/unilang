па́чка -> packet, pack
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: па́чки
Genitive: па́чки



Etymology: From пак (pak) +‎ -ка (-ka), and the former from German Pack. See also паковать (pakovátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • bundle, pack, packet, batch, sheaf, package, parcel
    • tutu
      • (military) clip (ammunition)
