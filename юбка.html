бка -> skirt
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: бки
Genitive: бки



Etymology: Related to the Old French jupe and the Old Italian jupa.



Definitions (English):
  • skirt (part of clothing, especially woman's)
    • (disparagingly, animate) woman (as an object of a man's interest)
      • Он волочится за ка́ждой ю́бкой. On voločítsja za káždoj júbkoj. He follows every skirt (woman).
