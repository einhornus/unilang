пятиле́тка -> five-year plan
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: пятиле́тки
Genitive: пятиле́тки



Etymology: From пять (pjatʹ, “five”) + ле́т (let, “year”)



Definitions (English):
  • period of five years
    • five-year plan (for the national economy of the USSR)
