заходи́ть -> (к +d) to call on s.o., (за +a) go behind, set (of sun)
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Я захожу́
Ты захо́дишь
Past: заходи́л/заходи́ла



Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ ходи́ть (xodítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to go (to see), to come in (to see, etc.), to call, to drop in
    • to pick up, to fetch
      • (nautical) to call at, to enter (a port)
        • (nautical) to get, to advance
          • (nautical) to pass, to draw out
            • (aviation) to approach
              • (military) to outflank
                • (military) to turn, to disappear, to go behind
                  • (astronomy) to set.
                    • В Хельсинки со́лнце захо́дит в по́лночь. ― V Xélʹsinki sólnce zaxódit v pólnočʹ. ― The sun sets at midnight in Helsinki.
