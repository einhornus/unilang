рукави́ца -> mitten, glove (without fingers)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: рукави́цы
Genitive: рукави́цы


Definitions (English):
  • glove (covering four fingers together), mitten, mitt,
    • держа́ть в ежовых рукави́цах ― deržátʹ v ježóvyx rukavícax ― to keep a tight rein, to rule with a rod of iron
  • gauntlet
