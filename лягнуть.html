лягну́ть (28003)


Etymology

лягать (ljagátʹ) +‎ -нуть (-nútʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪɡˈnutʲ]

Verb

лягну́ть • (ljagnútʹ) pf (imperfective лягать)

  1. to kick
  2. (colloquial) to knock, to let out (at), to jump (on) (to tell something offensive of someone)

Synonyms

  • пнуть (pnutʹ)

Related terms

  • лягаться (ljagátʹsja)
