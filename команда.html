кома́нда -> team, crew, command
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: кома́нды
Genitive: кома́нды



Etymology: Borrowed from French commande or German Kommando.



Definitions (English):
  • command, order
    • team, crew, party
      • (military) detachment
        • (nautical) crew, ship's company
          • (sports) team
