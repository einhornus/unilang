экспона́т (9441)


Etymology

Perhaps borrowed from a Pseudo-Latin construction Latin *expōnātus (“(something) exhibited, displayed”), from expōnō (the correct past passive participle is expositus).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪkspɐˈnat]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

экспона́т • (eksponát) m inan (genitive экспона́та, nominative plural экспона́ты, genitive plural экспона́тов)

  1. exhibit (something exhibited)
