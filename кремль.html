кремль (3133)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic кремль (kremlĭ), from Proto-Slavic *kremljь.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [krʲemlʲ]

Noun

кремль • (kremlʹ) m inan (genitive кремля́, nominative plural кремли́, genitive plural кремле́й)

  1. kremlin (a central fortress in medieval Russian cities)
  2. (capitalized) The Kremlin (in Moscow)
