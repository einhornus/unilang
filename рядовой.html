рядо́вой -> ordinary, rank and file, (as noun) private soldier
Adjective



Etymology: ряд (rjad) +‎ -ово́й (-ovój)



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) row, line,
    • рядовы́́е ку́льту́ры ― rjadovýje kulʹtúry ― row crops
  • ordinary, common, average, routine
    • (relational) rank and file,
      • рядово́́й соста́в ― rjadovój sostáv ― rank and file membership
    • (military) private (rank)
