жтва -> reaping, harvest
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: жтвы
Genitive: жтвы



Etymology: From Old East Slavic жѧтва (žętva), from Proto-Slavic *žętva, from *žęti +‎ *-tva. Equivalent to жать (žatʹ) +‎ -тва (-tva).



Definitions (English):
  • harvest (process of harvesting, gathering the ripened crop, harvest yield)
