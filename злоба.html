зло́ба -> spite, malice
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: зло́бы
Genitive: зло́бы



Etymology: From Old East Slavic зълоба (zŭloba), from Proto-Slavic *zъloba, from *zъlъ +‎ *-oba. Equivalent to злой (zloj) +‎ -оба (-oba).



Definitions (English):
  • spite, malice
    • rage, anger,
      • кипе́ть зло́бой ― kipétʹ zlóboj ― to boil with anger
    • grudge


      Definitions (Russian):
