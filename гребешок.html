Noun[edit]

гребешо́к • (grebešók) m anim or m inan (genitive гребешка́, nominative plural гребешки́, genitive plural гребешко́в)

  1. (inanimate) diminutive of гре́бень (grébenʹ): comb, tooth-comb
  2. crest (of a bird)
  3. (animate) scallop (mollusc)
