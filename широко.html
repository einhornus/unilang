широко -> widely, broadly

Etymology: From широ́кий (širókij) +‎ -о (-o) (stem-stressed variant) or from широ́кий (širókij) +‎ -о (-ó) (ending-stressed variant).



Definitions (English):
  • wide, widely, broadly
    • short neuter singular of широ́кий (širókij, “wide, broad, generous”)
      • short neuter singular of широ́кий (širókij, “too wide/loose”)
