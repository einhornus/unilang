проси́живать (16341)


Etymology

From просид(еть) (prosid(étʹ)) [и → и; д → ж] + -ивать (-ivatʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈsʲiʐɨvətʲ]

Verb

проси́живать • (prosíživatʹ) impf (perfective просиде́ть)

  1. (colloquial) to sit (for some time), to sit up (for some time), to spend one's time in a sitting position, to spend one's time under confinement, to stay (for some time)
    проси́живать над кни́гами
    prosíživatʹ nad knígami
    to sit (for some time) over books

Related terms

  • сиде́ть pf (sidétʹ)
