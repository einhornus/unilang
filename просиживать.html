просживать -> to sit (for a specified time)
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я просживаю
Ты просживаешь
Past: просживал/просживала



Etymology: From просид(еть) (prosid(étʹ)) [и → и д → ж] + -ивать (-ivatʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) to sit (for some time), to sit up (for some time), to spend one's time in a sitting position, to spend one's time under confinement, to stay (for some time)
    • проси́живать над кни́гами, prosíživatʹ nad knígamito sit (for some time) over books
