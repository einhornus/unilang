Verb[edit]

вре́зать • (vrézatʹ) pf (imperfective вре́зать or врезывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut in, to fit in, to cut (into), to fit (into)
    вре́зать но́вый за́мок
    vrézatʹ nóvyj zamók
    to fit a new lock in
  2. (colloquial) to hit, to paste it, to give a smack
    вре́зать кому-либо в че́люсть
    vrézatʹ komú-libo v čéljustʹ
    to give smb a smack on the jaw
  3. (colloquial) to give a piece of one's mind, to let have it, to give the business
