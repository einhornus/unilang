вреза́ть (6771)


Etymology 1

в- (v-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvrʲezətʲ]

Verb

вреза́ть • (vrézatʹ) pf (imperfective вреза́ть or врезывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut in, to fit in, to cut (intó), to fit (intó)
    вреза́ть но́вый замок
    vrézatʹ nóvyj zamók
    to fit a new lock in
  2. (colloquial) to hit, to paste it, to give a smack
    вреза́ть кому-либо в че́люсть
    vrézatʹ komú-libo v čéljustʹ
    to give smb a smack on the jaw
  3. (colloquial) to give a piece of one's mind, to let have it, to give the business

Etymology 2

вреза́ть (vrézatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vrʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

вреза́ть • (vrezátʹ) impf (perfective вреза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut in, to fit in, to cut (intó), to fit (intó)
    вреза́ть но́вый замок
    vrezátʹ nóvyj zamók
    to fit a new lock in

Related terms
