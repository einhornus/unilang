моло́дчик (19644)


Etymology[edit]

From молоде́ц (molodéc) +‎ -ик (-ik).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈlot͡ɕːɪk]

Noun[edit]

моло́дчик • (molódčik) m anim (genitive моло́дчика, nominative plural моло́дчики, genitive plural моло́дчиков)

  1. a young man (usually perceived as suspicious or dangerous)
