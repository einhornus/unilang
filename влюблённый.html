Definitions (English):
  • past passive perfective participle of влюбить (vljubítʹ)
    • in love, enamored (affected with love, experiencing love of a person)
      • (strongly) fond (of) (passionate about some hobby, profession, cause)
        • amorous (expressing love of a look, tone, etc.)
          • lover
