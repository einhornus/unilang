полоть (24735)


Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *pèlti.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlotʲ]

Verb

полоть • (polótʹ) impf (perfective прополоть)

  1. to weed, to weed out, to pull up (weeds)

Synonyms

  • выпалывать (vypályvatʹ)

Related terms

  • пропо́лка f (propólka)
