чемодн -> suitcase
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: чемодны
Genitive: чемодна



Etymology: Via a Turkic medium, from Persian جامه‌دان‎ (jâme-dân, “suitcase”).



Definitions (English):
  • suitcase, trunk
    • a heavy projectile, especially that of a naval gun (military slang since the first half of the 20th century, especially WWI often in quotes)
