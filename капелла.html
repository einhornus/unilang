капе́лла (15751)


Etymology

Perhaps borrowed from Italian cappella.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈpɛl(ː)ə] (phonetic respelling: капэлла)

Noun

капе́лла • (kapɛlla) f inan (genitive капе́ллы, nominative plural капе́ллы, genitive plural капе́лл)

  1. choir of singers (from the 18th century, mixed ensemble of singers and musicians)
  2. concert hall for chamber music
  3. chapel
  4. idiomatic; see а капе́лла (a kapɛlla)

Related terms

  • капеллан (kapellán)
  • капельме́йстер (kapelʹméjster)
