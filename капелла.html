капе́лла (15753)


Etymology

Perhaps borrowed from Italian cappella.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈpɛl(ː)ə] (phonetic respelling: капэ́лла)

Noun

капе́лла • (kapɛ́lla) f inan (genitive капе́ллы, nominative plural капе́ллы, genitive plural капе́лл)

  1. choir of singers (from the 18th century, mixed ensemble of singers and musicians)
  2. concert hall for chamber music
  3. chapel
  4. idiomatic; see а капе́лла (a kapɛ́lla)

Related terms

  • капелла́н (kapellán)
  • капельме́йстер (kapelʹméjster)
