годо́к (21849)


Etymology

год (god) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡɐˈdok]

Noun

годо́к • (godók) masculine gender">m inan or masculine gender">m anim (genitive годка́, nominative plural годки́, genitive plural годко́в)

  1. (inanimate) Diminutive of год (god, “year”)
  2. (low colloquial, animate) contemporary
  3. (animate) soldier who has served a full year
