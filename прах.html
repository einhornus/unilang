прах -> dust
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: прахи
Genitive: праха



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic прахъ (praxŭ), from Proto-Slavic *porxъ, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pers-. Doublet of по́рох (pórox), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • (poetic) dust, earth
    • (of the dead) ashes, remains,
      • мир пра́ху его́́ ― mir práxu jevó ― may he rest in peace
