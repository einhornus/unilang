мы -> we

Etymology: Cognate with Old Church Slavonic мꙑ (my). Also akin to Lithuanian mẽs and Old Armenian մեք (mekʿ).



Definitions (English):
  • I (only when used with с (s))
    • мы с тобо́й ― my s tobój ― you and I, мы с сестро́й ― my s sestrój ― me and my sister
