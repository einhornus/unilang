обрабо́тка -> treatment, processing
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: обрабо́тки
Genitive: обрабо́тки



Etymology: обрабо́тать (obrabótatʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • processing (act of taking something through a set of prescribed procedures)
    • adaptation (something which has been adapted, variation)
