раска́т (15326)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈskat]

Noun

раска́т • (raskát) m inan (genitive раска́та, nominative plural раска́ты, genitive plural раска́тов)

  1. reverberation, roll (a heavy, reverberatory sound)
  2. roar
  3. growl
