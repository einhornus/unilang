отки́нуться -> to lean back
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Past: отки́нулся/отки́нулась



Etymology: отки́нуть (otkínutʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to lean back, to settle back
    • (slang) to come out of prison after having served time
      • (slang) to die, kick the bucket. Synonyms:

      • passive of отки́нуть (otkínutʹ)
