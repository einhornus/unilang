клави́р (25001)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Klavier.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [klɐˈvʲir]

Noun

клави́р • (klavír) m inan (genitive клави́ра, nominative plural клави́ры, genitive plural клави́ров)

  1. keyboard instrument (piano, harpsichord, clavichord, etc.)
  2. piano arrangement of an opera
