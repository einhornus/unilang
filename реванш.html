рева́нш (19378)


Etymology

Borrowed from French revanche.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪˈvanʂ]

Noun

рева́нш • (revánš) m inan (genitive рева́нша, uncountable)

  1. revenge, retaliation
  2. rematch

Related terms

  • матч-реванш (matč-revánš)
  • реваншизм (revanšízm)
  • реваншист (revanšist)
