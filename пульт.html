пульт -> control panel, music-stand
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: пульты
Genitive: пульта



Etymology: Borrowed from German Pult, from Latin pulpitum (“platform, scaffold”).



Definitions (English):
  • lectern, music stand,
    • дирижёрский пульт ― dirižórskij pulʹt ― conductor's stand
  • console, (control) panel, benchboard
    • remote control
