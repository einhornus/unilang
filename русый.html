ру́сый -> light brown (hair)
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic русъ (rusŭ), from Proto-Slavic *rusъ, from earlier *rudsъ, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rowdʰ-s-o-.



Definitions (English):
  • fair(-haired), blond(e), light brown (of hair), dirty blond
