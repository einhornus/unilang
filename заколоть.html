заколо́ть (14769)


Etymology

East Slavic word inherited from Proto-Slavic *zakolti; synchronically analysable as за- (za-) +‎ коло́ть (kolótʹ). Doublet of заклать (zaklátʹ), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəkɐˈlotʲ]

Verb

заколо́ть • (zakolótʹ) impf (perfective закалывать)

  1. to kill by stabbing
  2. to slaughter (an animal)
    Imperfectives: коло́ть (kolótʹ), закалывать (zakályvatʹ)
  3. to attach with a pin
