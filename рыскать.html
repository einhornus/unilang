ры́скать (11332)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic рыскати (ryskati), from Proto-Slavic root *risk-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈrɨskətʲ]

Verb

ры́скать • (rýskatʹ) impf

  1. to prowl, to scour (about)
    ры́скать по берегу [ле́су] ― rýskatʹ po beregu [lesu] ― to scour the coast [woods]
  2. to rummage (in), to ransack
    ры́скать глаза́ми ― rýskatʹ glazámi ― to rove
  3. (nautical) to gripe, to yaw
