ти́на (10200)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic ти́на (tina), from Proto-Slavic *tīmnā or *tīnā

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲinə]

Noun

ти́на • (tína) f inan (genitive ти́ны, nominative plural ти́ны, genitive plural ти́н)

  1. scum, slime

Related terms

  • тинистый (tínistyj)
