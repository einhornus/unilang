dee (10017)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /diː/

Verb

dee (third-person singular simple present diz, present participle deein, simple past and past participle past|and|past|ptcp-form-of" lang="en">dyun)

  1. (Northumbria) To do.
    What are ye deein man!

Noun

dee (plural dees)

  1. The name of the Latin-script letter D.
  2. Something shaped like the letter D, such as a dee lock.
    the pommel is furnished with dees.
  3. (colloquial) Police detective.
    the dees are about.
