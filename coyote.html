coyote (14275)

Pronunciation

Noun

coyote m (plural coyotes)

  1. coyote (canine)
  2. (Mexico) coyote (smuggler of illegal immigrants)
  3. (Mexico) fixer, middleman
  4. (obsolete) a person of mixed Native American and mestizo descent
