отгу́л (21373)


Etymology[edit]

Deverbal from отгулять (otguljátʹ).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɐdˈɡul]

Noun[edit]

отгу́л • (otgúl) m inan (genitive отгу́ла, nominative plural отгу́лы, genitive plural отгу́лов)

  1. paid time off in compensation for overtime work

Related terms[edit]

  • отгуливать impf (otgúlivatʹ), отгулять pf (otguljátʹ)
