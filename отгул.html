отгу́л (21375)


Etymology

Deverbal from отгуля́ть (otguljátʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐdˈɡul]

Noun

отгу́л • (otgúl) m inan (genitive отгу́ла, nominative plural отгу́лы, genitive plural отгу́лов)

  1. paid time off in compensation for overtime work

Related terms

  • отгу́ливать impf (otgúlivatʹ), отгуля́ть pf (otguljátʹ)
