впрок (18550)


Etymology

в (v) +‎ прок (prok, “benefit, use”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fprok]

Adverb

впрок • (vprok)

  1. for future use, in reserve, in store
  2. to one's benefit
    э́то тебе́ не пойдёт впрок ― éto tebé ne pojdjót vprok ― it won't do you any good

Related terms

  • в (v)
  • прок m (prok)
