импера́тор -> emperor
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: импера́торы
Genitive: импера́тора



Etymology: Borrowed from Latin imperātor. The feminine form is influenced by the oblique stem (compare the ablative singular imperātrīce) of Latin imperātrīx.
