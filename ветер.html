ветер -> wind

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *větrъ, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂weh₁-.Compare to German Wetter or English weather.



Definitions (English):
  • (meteorology) wind
    • (plural only) the wind(s), flatus
      • (figuratively) the wave of change (usually in the phrase
        • ве́тёр переме́н (véter peremén))
