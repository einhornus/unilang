ла́дить -> to get on with (s.o.)
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я ла́жу
Ты ла́дишь
Past: ла́дил/ла́дила



Etymology: From лад (lad, “harmony, concord”) +‎ -ить (-itʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to get on (with), to agree (with), to be on good terms (with)
    • они не ла́дят, oní ne ládjatthey don't get on they are at odds
  • (colloquial) to prepare, to make ready, to fix, to tune
    • (colloquial) to plan,
      • он ла́дил в го́род е́хать, on ládil v górod jéxatʹhe planned to go to town
    • (colloquial) to repeat, to keep saying, to drone (on)
      • ла́дить одно́ и то же, láditʹ odnó i to žeharp on the same string
