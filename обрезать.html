обреза́ть (12421)


Etymology 1

об- (ob-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈbrʲezətʲ]

Verb

обреза́ть • (obrézatʹ) pf (imperfective обреза́ть or обрезывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to clip, to pare, to trim, to prune, to square
    обреза́ть но́гти ― obrézatʹ nógti ― pare one's fingernails
  2. to cut
    обреза́ть палец ― obrézatʹ pálec ― to cut one's finger
  3. to circumcise
  4. (colloquial) to snub, to cut short

Etymology 2

обреза́ть (obrézatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐbrʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

обреза́ть • (obrezátʹ) impf (perfective обреза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to clip, to pare, to trim, to prune, to square
    обреза́ть но́гти ― obrezátʹ nógti ― pare one's finger-nails
  2. to cut
    обреза́ть палец ― obrezátʹ pálec ― to cut one's finger
  3. to circumcise
  4. (colloquial) to snub, to cut short

Related terms

  • обрез (obréz)
  • обре́зок (obrézok), обрезочек (obrézoček)
  • обре́зка (obrézka)
  • обрезной (obreznój)
