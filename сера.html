сера (11041)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *sěra.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲerə]

Noun

сера • (séra) f inan (genitive се́ры, nominative plural се́ры, genitive plural се́р)

  1. sulphur, sulfur
  2. brimstone
  3. earwax, cerumen

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪˈra]

Adjective

сера • (será)

  1. short feminine singular of се́рый (séryj)
