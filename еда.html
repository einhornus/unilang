еда (1206)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *(j)ěsti, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *ēs-ti, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ed- (“to eat”). Cognates include Ancient Greek ἔδω (édō), Latin edō, Sanskrit अत्ति (átti), Lithuanian ėsti (“to eat (of animals), to swallow, to gobble”), Hittite hit">𒂊𒀉𒈪 (eidmi, “I eat”), Old English ang">etan (English eat).
