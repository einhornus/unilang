каре (28095)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from French carré (“square”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈrɛ] (phonetic respelling: карэ)

Noun

каре • (karɛ) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. (military) square
  2. (as an adjective) square
    вы́рез каре (у пла́тья) ― výrez kare (u plátʹja) ― square cut
  3. (women's haircut) bob
    носи́ть каре ― nosítʹ kare ― to wear one's hair in a bob
    сделать себе́ каре ― sdélatʹ sebé kare ― have one's hair bobbed
  4. (poker) four of a kind

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkarʲe]

Noun

каре • (káre) f inan

  1. dative singular of ка́ра (kára)
  2. prepositional singular of ка́ра (kára)
