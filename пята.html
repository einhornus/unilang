пята́ -> heel
Noun, declension 1b
Plural: пяты́
Genitive: пяты́



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *pęta. Cognate with Bulgarian пета (peta), Slovene peta, Serbo-Croatian peta, Slovak päta.



Definitions (English):
  • (archaic) heel foot,
    • с головы до пят ― s golový do pjat ― from head to toe
  • wide foot of a support, mainstay, pier, or buttress
