лом (8782)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *lomъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lom]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

лом • (lom) m inan (genitive ло́ма, nominative plural ломы́ or ломы́, genitive plural ломо́в)

  1. crowbar
  2. scrap, waste

Related terms

  • лома́ть (lomátʹ)
