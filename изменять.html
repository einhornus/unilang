изменя́ть -> to change, alter, (+d) betray
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я изменя́ю
Ты изменя́ешь
Past: изменя́л/изменя́ла



Etymology: измени́ть (izmenítʹ) +‎ -ять (-játʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to change, to alter, to modify, to edit
    • to vary
      • (with dative) to betray, to become unfaithful
        • to break, to violate (an oath, promise)
          • to fail (memory)
            • to desert
