станови́ться -> to become, to stand, p: begin
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: c

Я становлю́сь
Ты стано́вишься
Past: станови́лся/станови́лась



Etymology: Nasal present of стать (statʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja). See also Proto-Slavic *stati.



Definitions (English):
  • to stand, to take a stand
    • to get, to become, to grow
