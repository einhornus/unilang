ведать -> to manage, be in charge of, (+a) know
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я ве́даю
Ты ве́даешь
Past: ве́дал/ве́дала



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *věděti, from Proto-Indo-European *wóyde (“to know”), perfect of *weyd- (“to see, to know”).



Definitions (English):
  • (archaic or poetic) to know (be certain or sure about something)
    • (literary) to govern, to manage, to control.
      • Кто у нас ве́дает пожа́рной безопа́сностью? Kto u nas védajet požárnoj bezopásnostʹju? Who manages the fire safety among us? (instrumental case) Голова́ ве́дает волость. Golová védajet vólostʹ. A leader governs a volost. (archaic transitive usage)
