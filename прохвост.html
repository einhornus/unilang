прохво́ст (16955)


Etymology

From профос (profos), finally from German Profoss (cognate of provost)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐxˈvost]

Noun

прохво́ст • (proxvóst) masculine gender">m anim (genitive прохво́ста, nominative plural прохво́сты, genitive plural прохво́стов)

  1. (colloquial) scoundrel, rascal, fraud
