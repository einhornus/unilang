подъём -> rise
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: подъёмы
Genitive: подъёма



Etymology: Deverbal of поднима́ть (podnimátʹ). The combining form -ём is the normal deverbal equivalent of -нимать (-nimátʹ)/имать (imátʹ) compare archaic first singular present tense емлю (jemljú) of имать (imátʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • lifting, raise, raising
    • hoisting (of a flag)
      • salvaging (of a sunken ship)
        • ascent, rise, upgrade
          • climb (of a plane)
            • slope, rise (of a mountain)
              • величина́ подъёма ― veličiná podʺjóma ― up gradient
            • raising, development, upsurge
              • enthusiasm, animation
                • instep (of a foot)
                  • reveille
                    • (phonetics, phonology) height (of vowels)
                      • гласный сре́днего подъёма ― glásnyj srédnevo podʺjóma ― mid vowel (literally, “vowel of middle height”)
