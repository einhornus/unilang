оживи́ть (11511)


Etymology

о- (o-) +‎ живить (živítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐʐɨˈvʲitʲ]

Verb

оживи́ть • (oživítʹ) pf (imperfective оживля́ть)

  1. to bring back to life
  2. (figuratively) to revive, to bring back (something that has been lost or forgotten)
  3. (figuratively) to imbue physical or emotional energy intó (e.g. intó a person or street)
  4. (figuratively) to improve the appearance of (e.g. a building)
