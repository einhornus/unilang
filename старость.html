старость -> old age
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: старости
Genitive: старости



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *starostь, from *starъ +‎ *-ostь. Synchronically analyzable as ста́рый (stáryj) +‎ -ость (-ostʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • old age,
    • ста́рость — не ра́дость (proverb) ― stárostʹ — ne rádostʹ ― old age, boy, is no joy
  • dilapidation, decrepitude
    • ( Can we verify(+) this sense?) (informal) the elderly
