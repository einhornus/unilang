задува́ть (23048)


Etymology

заду́ть (zadútʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zədʊˈvatʲ]

Verb

задува́ть • (zaduvátʹ) impf (perfective заду́ть)

  1. to blow out (a candle, fire, etc.)
  2. (technical) to put (a blast furnace) into action
  3. (imperfective only, also impersonal) to gust
  4. (imperfective only) to blow, penetrating (в (v) + accusative, into something)
  5. (imperfective only) to carry by wafting

Related terms

  • задувка (zadúvka)
  • заду́в (zadúv)
  • дуть impf (dutʹ), поду́ть pf (podútʹ)
  • ду́тый (dútyj)
  • дутьё (dutʹjó)
