пого́ст (17663)


Etymology

Deverbal from погости́ть (pogostítʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈɡost]

Noun

пого́ст • (pogóst) m inan (genitive пого́ста, nominative plural пого́сты, genitive plural пого́стов)

  1. rural cemetery
  2. (dated) village church located awaý from the settlement, with a graveyard, its own land and a house for the clergy
  3. (historical, 10th century Ad) coaching inn for princes and important church officials
  4. (historical) former administrative-territorial unit in Russia, up through́ the 18th century, consisting of several villages
  5. (historical) large village in the center of such an administrative-territorial unit

Related terms

  • погости́ть (pogostítʹ)
