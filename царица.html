цари́ца -> tsarina, empress
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: цари́цы
Genitive: цари́цы



Etymology: From царь (carʹ) +‎ -ица (-ica).



Definitions (English):
  • tsarina, tsaritsa
    • empress, queen (in ancient or non-European monarchies)
      • цари́ца Савская ― caríca Sávskaja ― Queen of Sheba
    • (entomology) queen
