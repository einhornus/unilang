рай -> paradise
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ра́и
Genitive: ра́я



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *rajь, borrowed from Iranian, from Proto-Iranian *raHíš, from Proto-Indo-Iranian *raHíš, from Proto-Indo-European *reh₁ís (“wealth, goods”).



Definitions (English):
  • paradise, heaven
