ха́ус (28728)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxaʊs]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from English house.

Noun

ха́ус • (xáus) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ха́уса, uncountable)

  1. house music, house

Etymology 2

Noun

ха́ус • (xáus) masculine gender">m anim (genitive ха́уса, nominative plural ха́усы, genitive plural ха́усов)

  1. jungle cat, chaus
Synonyms
  • камышовый кот (kamyšovyj kot), болотная рысь (bolótnaja rysʹ)
