незако́нно (14345)


Etymology

незако́нный (nezakónnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪzɐˈkonːə]

Adverb

незако́нно • (nezakónno)

  1. illegally, unlawfully (in a manner contrary to the law)

Adjective

незако́нно • (nezakónno)

  1. short neuter singular of незако́нный (nezakónnyj)
