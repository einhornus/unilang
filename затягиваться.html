затгиваться -> to be covered (by), drag on, inhale (when smoking), heal (of wound), tighten one’s belt
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я затгиваюсь
Ты затгиваешься
Past: затгивался/затгивалась



Etymology: затя́гивать (zatjágivatʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to be tightened, to jam
    • to grow overcast
      • (medicine) to skin over, to heal (of wounds)
        • ра́на затя́гивалась ― rána zatjágivalasʹ ― the wound was skinning over / the wound was closing
      • to be delayed, to be dragged out
        • to inhale (cigarette, cigar, smoking pipe)
          • passive of затя́гивать (zatjágivatʹ)
