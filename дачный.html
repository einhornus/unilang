да́чный (8007)


Etymology

да́ча (dáča) +‎ -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈdat͡ɕnɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

да́чный • (dáčnyj)

  1. (relational) dacha
    да́чная ме́бель ― dáčnaja mébelʹ ― dacha furniture
  2. suburban (of means of transport; i.e. taking passengers to dachas)
    да́чный поезд ― dáčnyj pójezd ― suburban train
  3. summer (time when people go to dachas)
    да́чный сезо́н ― dáčnyj sezón ― summer season, dacha season
