впита́ть (20381)


Etymology

в- (v-) +‎ пита́ть (pitátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [f⁽ʲ⁾pʲɪˈtatʲ]

Verb

впита́ть • (vpitátʹ) pf (imperfective впи́тывать)

  1. to soak up, to soak in, to drink in, to absorb, to imbibe, to take in, to sop
    впита́ть с молоко́м матери ― vpitátʹ s molokóm máteri ― to imbibe with one's mother's milk/at one's mother's knees

Related terms

  • впитывание (vpítyvanije)
