скит (19545)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek σκήτη (skḗtē).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [skʲit]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

скит • (skit) masculine gender">m inan (genitive скита, nominative plural скиты, genitive plural скитов)

  1. skete (in Eastern Christianity">Eastern Christianity, a small hermitage at some distance from the main monastery)
  2. remote monastery among Believers">Old Believers
