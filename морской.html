морскй -> sea, marine, naval
Adjective



Etymology: мо́ре (móre) +‎ -ской (-skój), from Proto-Slavic *moře, from Proto-Indo-European *móri, whence English marine and maritime.



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) sea,
    • морско́й волк ― morskój volk ― sea dog, old salt
  • nautical
    • maritime
      • naval,
        • морско́й флот ― morskój flot ― navy
      • seaside
