барн -> ram (sheep)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: барны
Genitive: барна



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *baranъ.



Definitions (English):
  • ram (male domestic sheep)
    • sheep (an animal from the Ovis genus)
      • толсторогий бара́н ― tolstorógij barán ― bighorn sheep
    • (figuratively) mule namely someone who is very stubborn and foolish
