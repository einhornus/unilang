бара́н (4402)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *baranъ.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈran]
  • Audio

Noun[edit]

бара́н • (barán) m anim (genitive бара́на, nominative plural бара́ны, genitive plural бара́нов)

  1. ram (male domestic sheep)
  2. sheep (an animal from the Ovis genus)
    толсторогий бара́н ― tolstorógij barán ―
  3. (figuratively) mule; namely someone who is very stubborn and foolish

Synonyms[edit]

  • овен (ovén) (dated)
