то́же -> too
Definitions (English):
  • also, too, as well, likewise.
    • Я там был, Иван то́же. Ja tam byl, Iván tóže. I was there and so was Ivan.
  • (in negative constructions) either, neither, nor
    • expresses disbelief, sarcasm, irony, often combined with мне (mne)
      • то́же мне поэ́т! ― tóže mne poét! ― poet, yeah right! Synonym: вот ещё (vot ješčó)
