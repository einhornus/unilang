пост (1500)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [post]

Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *postъ.

Noun

пост • (post) masculine gender">m inan (genitive поста́, nominative plural посты́, genitive plural посто́в)

  1. fast (period of restricted eating)
    вели́кий пост ― velikij post ― Great Lent (lit. great fast)
Related terms
  • поститься (postítʹsja)
  • по́стный (póstnyj)

Etymology 2

thumb tright">
пост

Noun

пост • (post) masculine gender">m inan (genitive поста́, nominative plural посты́, genitive plural посто́в)

  1. post, office, appointment, position, job
  2. picket, outpost, guard post, station
Related terms
  • постово́й (postovój)

Etymology 3

Borrowed from English post.

Noun

пост • (post) masculine gender">m inan (genitive поста́, nominative plural посты́, genitive plural посто́в)
