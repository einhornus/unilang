пост -> post (job)
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: посты́
Genitive: поста́



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *postъ.
  • пост
  • Borrowed from English post.


Definitions (English):
  • fast (period of restricted eating)
    • вели́кий пост ― velikij post ― Great Lent (lit. great fast)
  • post, office, appointment, position, job
    • picket, outpost, guard post, station
      • (slang) post, message (online forums)
