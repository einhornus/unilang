трижды -> thrice, three times

Etymology: три (tri) +‎ -жды (-ždy)



Definitions (English):
  • three times, thrice, on three occasions,
    • три́жды в жи́зни ― tríždy v žízni ― three times in someone's life
  • 3x, three times as much,
    • три́жды пять — пятна́дцать ― tríždy pjatʹ — pjatnádcatʹ ― three times five is fifteen


Definitions (Russian):
  • три ра́за
