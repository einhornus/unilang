три́жды (4855)


Etymology

три (tri) +‎ -жды (-ždy)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtrʲiʐdɨ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

три́жды • (tríždy)

  1. three times, thrice, on three occasions
    три́жды в жи́зни ― tríždy v žízni ― three times in someone's life
  2. 3x, three times as much
    три́жды пять — пятна́дцать ― tríždy pjatʹ — pjatnádcatʹ ― three times five is fifteen
