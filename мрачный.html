мра́чный -> gloomy, baleful
Adjective



Etymology: мрак (mrak) +‎ -ный (-nyj). Compare морочный (móročnyj).



Definitions (English):
  • dark (without moral or spiritual light)
    • obscure
      • gloomy, somber, grim
