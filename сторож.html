сторо́ж -> watchman
Noun, declension 2c (pattern город)

Plural: сто́рожа
Genitive: сторо́жа



Etymology: From Old East Slavic сторожь (storožĭ), from Proto-Slavic *storžь (“guard”), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *storʔg-. Doublet of страж (straž), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • watch, watchman, guard, custodian


    Definitions (Russian):
