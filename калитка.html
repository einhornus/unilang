кали́тка -> gate, garden gate
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: кали́тки
Genitive: кали́тки



Etymology: Per Vasmer, most likely from *колита (*kolita, “door”) +‎ -ка (-ka) with akanye, where *колита (*kolita) comes from кол (kol, “stake, peg”) and its original meaning was "equipped with pegs".



Definitions (English):
  • small door in a fence wicket gate,
    • садовая кали́тка ― sadóvaja kalítka ― garden gate
  • (regional) a small pie
