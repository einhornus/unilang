успть -> to have time, to be in time

Etymology: у- (u-) +‎ спеть (spetʹ), from Proto-Indo-European *spewd- (“press, hurry”), whence English speed.



Definitions (English):
  • to have time, to find time
    • to manage (to do something), to succeed
      • to arrive in time (for), to be in time (for)
        • to catch (a train, bus)
