Usage notes[edit]

семна́дцать (semnadcatʹ) in the nominative case and accusative case governs the genitive plural of the noun. In other cases, it governs the corresponding plural case of the noun.

  • семна́дцать ма́льчиков ― semnádcatʹ málʹčikov ― seventeen boys
  • с семна́дцатью ма́льчиками ― s semnádcatʹju málʹčikami ― with seventeen boys
  • ма́льчиков семна́дцать ― málʹčikov semnádcatʹ ― approximately seventeen boys
