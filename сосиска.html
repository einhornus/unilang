сосиска (9517)


Etymology

Borrowed from French saucisse (“sausage”), from Medieval Latin">Medieval Latin salsīcia, from Latin salsus (“salted”), from sal (“salt”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈsʲiskə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

сосиска • (sosíska) f inan (genitive соси́ски, nominative plural соси́ски, genitive plural соси́сок)

  1. sausage, frankfurter (uncooked and smaller than sausage for slicing)
