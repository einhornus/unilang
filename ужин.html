ужин -> supper
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: ужины
Genitive: ужина



Etymology: From Old East Slavic у́жина (užina, “afternoon snack evening meal”), from Proto-Slavic *južina / *južinъ (“meal at noon”), from *jugъ (“South, south wind”). Compare Old East Slavic угъ (ugŭ, “South, noon”).The meaning evolved like this: "south" ⇒ "the time of the day when the sun is in the south" ⇒ "supper".



Definitions (English):
  • supper, late dinner (the evening meal)
