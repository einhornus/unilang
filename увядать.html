увяда́ть (24616)


Etymology

увяд- (uvjád-) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊvʲɪˈdatʲ]
  • Homophone: увида́ть (uvidátʹ)

Verb

увяда́ть • (uvjadátʹ) impf (perfective увя́нуть)

  1. to wither, to fade, to waste awaý (also figuratively)
