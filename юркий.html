ю́ркий (13120)


Etymology

From юр (jur, “busy place, market square; whirlpool, strong current”) + -кий (-kij).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈjurkʲɪj]

Adjective

ю́ркий • (júrkij) (rare/awkward comparative (по)ю́рче)

  1. agile, nimble
  2. quick, brisk (of a vehicle)

Related terms

  • юр (jur)
