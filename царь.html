царь -> tsar
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: цари
Genitive: царя



Etymology: From Old East Slavic цьсарь (cĭsarĭ), from цѣсарь (cěsarĭ), from Proto-Slavic *cěsařь, from a Germanic language, from Proto-Germanic *kaisaraz, from Latin Caesar.
