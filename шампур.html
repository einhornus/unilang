шампу́р (28204)


Etymology

Borrowed from Armenian շամփուր (šampʿur).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂɐmˈpur]

Noun

шампу́р • (šampúr) masculine gender">m inan (genitive шампу́ра, nominative plural шампу́ры, genitive plural шампу́ров)

  1. skewer (metal or wooden pin used to secure food during cooking)
