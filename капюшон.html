капюшо́н (8972)


Etymology

Borrowed from French capuchon, from Italian cappuccio.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kəpʲʊˈʂon]
  • IPA(key): [kəpʲɪˈʂon] (phonetic respelling: капишо́н)

Noun

капюшо́н • (kapjušón) m inan (genitive капюшо́на, nominative plural капюшо́ны, genitive plural капюшо́нов)
