аптека -> chemist’s, drugstore
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: аптеки
Genitive: аптеки



Etymology: Borrowed from Polish apteka, ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀποθήκη (apothḗkē).



Definitions (English):
  • pharmacy, chemist's, drugstore
