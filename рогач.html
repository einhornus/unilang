рогач (24638)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *rogačь, from *rogъ (“horn”) +‎ *-ačь. Equivalent to рог (rog, “horn”) +‎ -ач (-ač).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈɡat͡ɕ]

Noun

рогач • (rogáč) m anim (genitive рогача, nominative plural рогачи, genitive plural рогачей)

  1. stag, hart
  2. stag beetle

Related terms

  • рог (rog)
  • рога́тый (rogátyj)
