балага́н (14930)


Etymology[edit]

Ultimately from Persian بالاخانه‎ (bâlâxâne, “upper room”), via a Turkic language.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [bəlɐˈɡan]

Noun[edit]

балага́н • (balagán) m inan (genitive балага́на, nominative plural балага́ны, genitive plural балага́нов)

  1. booth, show-booth
  2. show; farce; low farce; buffoonery
