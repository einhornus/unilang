буква́льный -> literal
Adjective



Etymology: From бу́ква (búkva) +‎ -альный (-alʹnyj), a calque of French littéral. First attested in 1780s.



Definitions (English):
  • literal (exactly as stated read or understood without additional interpretation)
    • буква́льный перево́д ― bukválʹnyj perevód ― literal translation
