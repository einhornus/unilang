сейча́с -> now, right now

Etymology: Univerbation of сей (sej) + час (čas), ”this hour”, ”this time” (note: “сей” is dated, bookish or stilted (unlike “этот”), “час” in the sense of “time” is dated or poetic).



Definitions (English):
  • now, at present
    • presently, immediately, at once
      • just now
