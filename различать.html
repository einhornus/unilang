различать -> to distinguish
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я различаю

Past: различал/различала



Etymology: различи́́ть (različítʹ) +‎ -а́ть (-átʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to distinguish, to tell apart, to tell the difference between two entities
    • to differ
      • to discriminate
        • to recognize
