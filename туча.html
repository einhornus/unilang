тча -> storm cloud
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: тчи
Genitive: тчи



Etymology: From Old East Slavic ту́ча (tuča), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *tenk- (“to contract”). Compare the Slavic Old Church Slavonic тѫча (tǫča), Ukrainian ту́ча (túča), Polish tęcza and the Non-Slavic Gothic 𐌸𐌴𐌹𐍈𐍉𐌽 (þeiƕōn) and Old Norse þéttr
