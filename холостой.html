холостй -> unmarried
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *xolstъ(jь). Compare Old Church Slavonic хластъ (xlastŭ), Slovene hlȃst, Ukrainian хо́лост (xólost).



Definitions (English):
  • single (not married)
    • idle (not working)
      • (technical) idle, free-running,
        • холосто́й ход ― xolostój xod ― idling
      • (ballistics) blank
        • unmarried person, single person, bachelor
          • blank (bullet)
