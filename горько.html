горько -> bitter(ly)

Etymology: го́рький (górʹkij) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • bitter, bitterly
    • poignant, poignantly
      • short neuter singular of го́рький (górʹkij)
        • bitter! (an old Russian tradition: a shout by wedding guests urging the newlyweds to kiss, thus removing the "bitterness")
