пе́пел -> ash, ashes
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: пе́плы
Genitive: пе́пла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *pepelъ.



Definitions (English):
  • (usually uncountable) ash, ashes
