псенка -> song
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: псенки
Genitive: псенки



Etymology: пе́сня (pésnja) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • Diminutive of пе́сня (pésnja): (little) song,
    • твоя́ пе́сенка спета ― tvojá pésenka spéta ― you're finished you're history you're dead (literally, “your little song is sung”)
