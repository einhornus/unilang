пожалуйста -> please, please do, don’t mention it

Etymology: Lexicalization of пожа́луй (požáluj) + -ста (-sta), an obsolete clitic that was used to add emphasis and to signify politeness, of obscure origin. The clitic is no longer used or known to most speakers, even as passive vocabulary, and has essentially disappeared from the language. Accordingly, to modern speakers, the ending of the word пожа́луйста is merely a sequence of sounds of indeterminate meaning.



Definitions (English):
  • please.
    • Да, пожа́луйста. ― Da, požálujsta. ― Yes, please.
  • you're welcome, not at all, don't mention it, it's a pleasure (reply to thank you)
    • here you are (when handing something over)
