рове́сник (10765)


Etymology

Borrowed from Polish rówieśnik.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈvʲesʲnʲɪk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

рове́сник • (rovésnik) m anim (genitive рове́сника, nominative plural рове́сники, genitive plural рове́сников, feminine рове́сница)

  1. contemporary, age-mate (one who is the samé age as another)
