свиста́ть (27282)


Etymology

сви́стнуть (svístnutʹ) + -а́ть (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [svʲɪˈstatʲ]

Verb

свиста́ть • (svistátʹ) impf (perfective сви́стнуть)

  1. to whistle, to make a whistling sound (without the aid of a whistle)
    Synonym: свисте́ть (svistétʹ)
  2. to call someone by whistling
  3. (colloquial) to beat strongly with liquid

Related terms

  • свист m (svist)
  • свиста́ть все́х наве́рх (svistátʹ vsex navérx)
