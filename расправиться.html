распра́виться -> to deal with, straighten out
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я распра́влюсь
Ты распра́вишься
Past: распра́вился/распра́вилась



Etymology: расправить (rasprávitʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to beat violently, to punish
    • to get rid of (someone) by destroying them or rendering them harmless
      • (figuratively, colloquial) to deal with (something), to handle (something), to manage (something)
        • распра́виться без суда́ ― rasprávitʹsja bez sudá ― to take the law into one's own hands
      • (figuratively, colloquial) to eat up (something) without leaving a trace
        • to get smoothed out, to even out
          • to become straight, to straighten out,
            • пле́чи распра́вились ― pléči rasprávilisʹ ― one's shoulders straightened up
          • passive of расправить (rasprávitʹ)
