ва́та -> cotton wool
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ва́ты
Genitive: ва́ты



Etymology: Borrowing from German Watte or French ouate.



Definitions (English):
  • absorbent cotton, cotton wool, wadding, batting,
    • на ва́те ― na váte ― wadded, padded, пальто́ на ва́те ― palʹtó na váte ― wadded coat
  • (cloth) drugstore cotton
    • glass wool
