вата́ -> cotton wool
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: ваты
Genitive: ваты



Etymology: Borrowing from German Watte or French ouate.



Definitions (English):
  • absorbent cotton, cotton wool, wadding, batting,
    • на ва́те́ ― na váte ― wadded, padded, пальто́́ на ва́те́ ― palʹtó na váte ― wadded coat
  • (cloth) drugstore cotton
    • glass wool
