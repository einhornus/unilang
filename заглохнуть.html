загло́хнуть (11156)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ гло́хнуть (glóxnutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈɡloxnʊtʲ]

Verb

загло́хнуть • (zaglóxnutʹ) pf (imperfective гло́хнуть)

  1. (sound) to die awaý, to abate, to fade out, to subside
  2. to grow wild, to turn intó a wilderness, to run/go to seed, to decay, to grow lifeless
  3. (feeling, interest etc) to subside, to decline
  4. (engine) to fail, to cut out
