док (2602)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dok]
  • Homophones: дог

Etymology 1

Noun

док • (dok) m inan (genitive до́ка, nominative plural до́ки, genitive plural до́ков)

  1. dock
Related terms
  • докер (doker)

Etymology 2

Noun

док • (dok) m anim pl

  1. genitive plural of до́ка (dóka)
  2. accusative plural of до́ка (dóka)
