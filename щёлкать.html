щёлкать -> to flick, (+inst) click, crack, snap
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я щёлкаю
Ты щёлкаешь
Past: щёлкал/щёлкала



Etymology: Perhaps щёлка (ščólka) +‎ -ать (-atʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to click (to make a clicking sound)
    • to crack, to click, to snap, etc. (+ instrumental) (to cause to make such a sound)
      • to flick, to fillip (someone)
        • to crack (nuts)
          • (imperfective only) to warble (of a bird)
