wagon (7361)


Etymology

From English waggon, from Dutch wagen.

Pronunciation

  • (Belgium) IPA(key): /wa.ɡɔ̃/
  • (France, Switzerland) IPA(key): /va.ɡɔ̃/
  • wagon
    		(file)

Noun

wagon m (plural wagons)

  1. a railway carriage (note that the word voiture is preferred for passenger transport)
