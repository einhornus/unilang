тишина́ -> quiet, silence
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: тишины
Genitive: тишины



Etymology: ти́хий (tíxij) +‎ -ина (-ina)



Definitions (English):
  • silence, quietness, hush (the lack of any sound)
