неуклю́же (10002)


Etymology

неуклю́жий (neukljúžij) +‎ -е (-e)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪʊˈklʲuʐɨ]

Adverb

неуклю́же • (neukljúže)

  1. clumsily, awkwardly, uncouthly (done without́ care or finesse, often hurriedly or awkwardly)

Adjective

неуклю́же • (neukljúže)

  1. short neuter singular of неуклю́жий (neukljúžij)
