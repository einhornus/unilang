примкну́ть (15442)


Etymology

при- (pri-) + -мкнуть (-mknutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪmkˈnutʲ]

Verb

примкну́ть • (primknútʹ) pf (imperfective примыка́ть)

  1. (transitive) to affix, to join together, to close
    примкну́ть штыки́! ― Primknútʹ štykí! ― Fix bayonets!
  2. (intransitive) to join ranks, to side with someone; to stand next to
  3. (transitive, dated) to include amongst

Related terms

  • примыка́ние n (primykánije)
