несура́зный (21349)


Etymology

From не- (ne-, “not”) + суразный (suráznyj, “prominent, beautiful”), from су- (su-, “with”) + раз (raz, “(originally) stroke, cut”) + -ный (-nyj).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪsʊˈraznɨj]

Adjective

несура́зный • (nesuráznyj)

  1. (colloquial) absurd, ridiculous
  2. (colloquial) awkward, unsteady

Related terms

  • не- (ne-)
  • су- (su-)
  • раз (raz)
