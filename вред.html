вред -> harm
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: вреды́
Genitive: вреда́



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic врѣдъ (vrědŭ), from Proto-Slavic *verdъ. Doublet of веред (véred), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • harm, damage
    • detriment
