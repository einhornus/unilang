воскре́снуть -> to come back to life, revive
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я воскре́сну
Ты воскре́снешь
Past: воскре́с/воскре́сла



Etymology: Compare воскресить (voskresítʹ, “to resurrect”).



Definitions (English):
  • (intransitive) to revive, to resurrect, to rise from the dead
    • (intransitive) to revive, to feel reanimated, to rise again
      • (feelings, images etc.) to revive, to come back,
        • воскре́снуть в па́мяти ― voskrésnutʹ v pámjati ― to come back (recur) to one's memory
