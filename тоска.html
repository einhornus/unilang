тоска -> melancholy, depression, ennui, (по +d) yearning (for)
Noun, declension 1b
Plural: тоски
Genitive: тоски



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *tъska, which is perhaps related to то́щий (tóščij) or ти́скать (tískatʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • melancholy, depression
    • boredom, ennui, weariness
