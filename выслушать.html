вы́слушать (3092)


Etymology

вы- (vý-) +‎ слу́шать (slúšatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨslʊʂətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

вы́слушать • (výslušatʹ) pf (imperfective выслу́шивать)

  1. to hear out, to listen (to the end)
  2. to auscultate (to practice auscultation)
