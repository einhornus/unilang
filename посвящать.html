посвяща́ть -> to devote, dedicate (sth to sth/s.o.)
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я посвяща́ю
Ты посвяща́ешь
Past: посвяща́л/посвяща́ла



Etymology: посвяти́ть (posvjatítʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to devote, to dedicate (various senses)
    • Он посвяща́ет э́ти стихи́ да́ме сердца. On posvjaščájet éti stixí dáme sérdca. He dedicates these poems to his ladylove.
  • to initiate (into), to let (into)
    • to ordain (into), to consecrate (into)
