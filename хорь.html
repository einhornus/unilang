хорь (19904)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *dъxorь (literally “ferret, stinky”), cognate to дух (dux), до́хнуть (doxnútʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [xorʲ]
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

хорь • (xorʹ) m anim (genitive хоря́, nominative plural хори́, genitive plural хоре́й)

  1. ferret, polecat

Synonyms[edit]

  • хорёк (xorjók)
