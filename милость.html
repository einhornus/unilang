ми́лость -> favour
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: ми́лости
Genitive: ми́лости



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *milostь.



Definitions (English):
  • mercy, charity
    • (military) pardon, quarter
      • kindness
        • favor, grace
          • worship
