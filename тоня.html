то́ня (4629)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic тонꙗ (tonja), from Proto-Slavic *topnь or *tорni̯а, related to то́пить (topítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtonʲə]

Noun

то́ня • (tónja) f inan (genitive тони, nominative plural тони, genitive plural то́ней)

  1. fishery, fishing ground
  2. haul of fish
