залетть -> to fly in, (за +a) fly behind
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я залеч
Ты залетшь
Past: залетл/залетла



Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ лететь (letétʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to fly (in, into)
    • му́ха залете́ла в ко́мнату ― múxa zaletéla v kómnatu ― a fly flew into the room
  • to fly (as high as), to go up (as far as), to fly (as far as), to fly (behind, beyond)
    • самолёт залете́л за Северный поля́рный круг ― samoljót zaletél za Severnyj poljárnyj krug ― the aircraft crossed the Arctic circle, мяч залете́л за огра́ду ― mjač zaletél za ográdu ― the ball flew over the fence
  • to land (somewhere) on the way, to make a stopover (somewhere)
    • (colloquial) to get into a fix, to get into a mess, to get something one doesn't want (i.e. contract a sexually transmitted disease or become pregnant)
      • залете́ть на экза́мене ― zaletétʹ na ekzámene ― to fail an exam
