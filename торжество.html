торже́ств -> celebration, triumph
Noun, declension 2b
Plural: торже́ств
Genitive: торже́ств



Etymology: From Old East Slavic тържьство (tŭržĭstvo), торжество́ (toržestvo), from търгъ (tŭrgŭ) + -ьство (-ĭstvo). Equivalent to торг (torg) +‎ -ество (-estvo).



Definitions (English):
  • festival, celebration, festivity,
    • на долгожданном торжестве́ ― na dolgoždánnom toržestvé ― at a long-awaited celebration
  • triumph, victory
