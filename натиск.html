на́тиск (11236)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnatʲɪsk]

Noun

на́тиск • (nátisk) masculine gender">m inan (genitive на́тиска, nominative plural на́тиски, genitive plural на́тисков)

  1. onslaught, charge, onset, impact
  2. pressure
    под на́тиском ― pod nátiskom ― under pressure
  3. stamping, impression

Synonyms
