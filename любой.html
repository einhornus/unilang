любо́й -> any (any you like)

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *ľubъ, equivalent to люби́ть (ljubitʹ, “to like”) +‎ -ой (-oj). For semantic shift to "whichever" compare Spanish cualquiera.



Definitions (English):
  • any, either, whichever one wants
