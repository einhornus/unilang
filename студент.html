студе́нт (1099)


Etymology

Probably from Polish language">Polish student or German Student, from Latin studens.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stʊˈdʲent]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

студе́нт • (studént) masculine gender">m anim (genitive студе́нта, nominative plural студе́нты, genitive plural студе́нтов, feminine студе́нтка)

  1. student
  2. undergraduate

Related terms

  • Noun: студе́нтка f (studéntka), студе́нчество n (studénčestvo)
  • Adjective: студе́нческий (studénčeskij)
