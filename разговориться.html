разгово́риться -> to become talkative

Etymology: разговори́ть (razgovorítʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial, usually perfective) to enter into a conversation (with), to find plenty to talk about (with)
    • они́ разговори́лись ― oní razgovorílisʹ ― they got into a conversation
  • (colloquial) to speak more eagerly, to warm to one's topic
    • (colloquial) to become more fluent (in a language), to speak more easily/fluently
      • passive of разговори́ть (razgovorítʹ)
