слон -> elephant
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: слоны
Genitive: слона



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *slonъ, possibly a deverbative from Proto-Slavic *sloniti sę (“to lean against”), relating to а medieval story of а sleeping elephant who leaned against a tree. According to some other sources, it is related to the Turkish arslan (“lion”).
