намёк -> hint, allusion
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: намёки
Genitive: намёка



Etymology: Deverbal of намёка́ть (namekátʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • hint, suggestion
    • allusion, implication
      • tip (piece of private information)
        • inkling
          • insinuation
