босо́й -> barefoot
Adjective



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic босъ (bosŭ). Cognate to Lithuanian bãsas, Latvian bass, Old High German bar.



Definitions (English):
  • barefoot,
    • ходи́ть босиком ― xodítʹ bosikóm ― to walk barefoot
