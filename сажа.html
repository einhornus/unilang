са́жа -> soot
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: са́жи
Genitive: са́жи



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *saďa, cognate with Bulgarian сажди (saždi), Čakavian Serbo-Croatian sađa and saže, Slovene saje, Slovak sadza. Non-Slavic cognates include Old Norse sót (“soot”).



Definitions (English):
  • soot. Synonym:
    • копоть (kópotʹ)
