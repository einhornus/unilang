ку́рва (16281)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic ку́рва (kurva, “prostitute, whore”), from Proto-Slavic *kury (“whore”), from *kurъ (“bird, hen; cock”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkurvə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ку́рва • (kúrva) f anim (genitive ку́рвы, nominative plural ку́рвы, genitive plural ку́рв) (vulgar)

  1. whore, prostitute
  2. bitch, slut
  3. bastard
