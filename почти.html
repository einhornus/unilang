по́чти -> almost

Etymology: Imperative of поче́сть (počéstʹ, “count, consider”), literally ‘consider it so’ when something is almost true.



Definitions (English):
  • almost, nearly
    • second-person singular imperative perfective of поче́сть (počéstʹ)
      • second-person singular imperative perfective of почти́́ть (počtítʹ)
