administrator (2434)


Etymology

From administrō (“attend upoń, assist”), from ad- (“to”) + ministrō (“attend, manage”).

Pronunciation

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.mi.nisˈtraː.tor/, [ad̪.mɪ.nɪsˈt̪raː.t̪ɔr]
  • (Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.mi.nisˈtra.tor/, [ad̪.mi.nisˈt̪raː.t̪ɔr]

Noun

administrātor m (genitive administrātōris); third declension

  1. manager, conductor, administrator

Related terms

  • administer
  • administrātiō
  • administrātīvus
  • administrō
