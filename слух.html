слух -> hearing, rumour
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: слухи
Genitive: слуха



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *sluxъ, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- whence English listen and loud.



Definitions (English):
  • hearing, ear,
    • му́зыка́льный слух ― muzykálʹnyj slux ― ear for music
  • (often in the plural) rumor, hearsay
    • news, sign
