нева́жно (4346)


Etymology

нева́жный (nevážnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪˈvaʐnə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

нева́жно • (nevážno)

  1. no matter (how)
  2. poorly, not very well

Interjection

нева́жно • (nevážno)

  1. it doesn't matter, it's OK

Adjective

нева́жно • (nevážno)

  1. short neuter singular of нева́жный (nevážnyj)
