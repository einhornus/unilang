прилета́ть (11719)


Etymology

прилете́ть (priletétʹ) + -а́ть (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪlʲɪˈtatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

прилета́ть • (priletátʹ) impf (perfective прилете́ть)

  1. (aviation) to land; to touch down
  2. to descend to soil (flying).
  3. (used figuratively) to arrive swiftly, to abruptly (sometimes unexpectedly)

Related terms

  • взлета́ть (vzletátʹ) (antonym)
  • лета́ть (letátʹ)
  • прилёт (priljót)
