фо́рвард (28290)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈforvərt]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from English forward.

Noun

фо́рвард • (fórvard) m anim (genitive фо́рварда, nominative plural фо́рварды, genitive plural фо́рвардов)

  1. (sports) forward (player nearest to the opposing soccer team)
Synonyms
  • напада́ющий (napadájuščij)

Etymology 2

Noun

фо́рвард • (fórvard) m inan (genitive фо́рварда, nominative plural фо́рварды, genitive plural фо́рвардов)

  1. (finance, economics) forward
  2. (computing, email) forward
Related terms
  • форвардный (forvardnyj)
