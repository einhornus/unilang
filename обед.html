обед -> meal, dinner
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: обды
Genitive: обда



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *obědъ.



Definitions (English):
  • dinner, lunch ( The main meal of the day, which is served around 1 or 2 p.m. until 3 p.m. It may be called either "lunch" or "dinner" in English.)
    • (informal) noon
