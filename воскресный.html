воскре́сный (8894)


Etymology

From воскре́снуть (voskrésnutʹ, “to resurrect”) +‎ -ный (-nyj); meaning influenced by воскресе́нье (voskresénʹje, “Sunday”, literally “resurrection”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐˈskrʲesnɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

воскре́сный • (voskrésnyj)

  1. (relational) Sunday
    воскре́сный день ― voskrésnyj denʹ ― Sunday
