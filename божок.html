божо́к (24964)


Etymology

бог (box) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈʐok]

Noun

божо́к • (božók) m anim (genitive божка́, nominative plural божки́, genitive plural божко́в)

  1. Diminutive of бог (box): (a little) god
  2. (figuratively) idol, someone loved by the public
  3. a figurine or a small statue of a god
