тьма -> darkness
Noun, declension 1b
Plural: тьмы
Genitive: тьмы



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *tьma.
  • Borrowed in Old Russian from an ancestor of Mongolian түм (tüm).


Definitions (English):
  • dark, darkness
    • thousands, a multitude, a host, lots of
