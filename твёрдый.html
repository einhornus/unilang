твёрдый -> hard, firm, solid
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic твьрдъ (tvĭrdŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tvьrdъ (“hard, firm, solid”).



Definitions (English):
  • hard, solid
    • firm
      • steadfast, steady
        • fixed (prices)
          • sound, good
            • sure
              • (phonetics, phonology) unpalatalized
