нежена́тый (30757)


Etymology[edit]

не- (ne-) +‎ жена́тый (ženátyj)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪʐɨˈnatɨj]

Adjective[edit]

нежена́тый • (neženátyj)

  1. single (not married)
  2. celibate
  3. (colloquial, figuratively) unattached, associated with the single life (as of a bachelor)[1]

Synonyms[edit]

  • холосто́й (xolostój)
  • холостя́цкий (xolostjáckij)
