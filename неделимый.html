недели́мый (27560)


Etymology

не- (ne-) +‎ дели́мый (delímyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪdʲɪˈlʲimɨj]

Adjective

недели́мый • (nedelímyj)

  1. indivisible
  2. (mathematics) prime

Related terms
