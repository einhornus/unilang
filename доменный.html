доменный (31196)


Etymology 1

до́мна (dómna) +‎ -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈdomʲɪn(ː)ɨj]

Adjective

доменный • (dómennyj) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) blast furnace
Related terms
  • до́мна (dómna)

Etymology 2

до́мен (domén) +‎ -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dɐˈmʲenːɨj]

Adjective

доменный • (doménnyj) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) domain
Related terms
  • до́мен (domén)
