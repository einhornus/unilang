Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Etymology: бить (bitʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to fight, to struggle
    • to hit (against), to knock (against), to strike
      • (of heart) to beat, to pulsate
        • to writhe, to toss about
          • to exercise oneself, to drudge, to toil
            • to break, to smash
              • passive of бить (bitʹ)
