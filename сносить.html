сноси́ть (9177)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ носи́ть (nosítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [snɐˈsʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

сноси́ть • (snosítʹ) impf (perfective снести́)

  1. to carry down
  2. to carry to
  3. to pull down, to demolish, to raze
  4. to blow off, to blow awaý (of the wind)
  5. to carry awaý (of water)
  6. to tolerate, to endure
  7. to cut off
  8. to discard (a card)

Verb

сноси́ть • (snosítʹ) pf

  1. to carry somewhere and bring back
    Imperfective: носи́ть (nosítʹ)
  2. to wear out
    Imperfective: снашивать (snášivatʹ)
