мино́га (32245)


Etymology

Borrowed from Polish minóg, from German Neunauge.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈnoɡə]

Noun

мино́га • (minóga) f anim (genitive мино́ги, nominative plural мино́ги, genitive plural мино́г, related adjective миноговый or миножий)

  1. lamprey
