хитрость -> cunning, guile (noun), ruse, (coll) ingenuity
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: хитро́сти
Genitive: хитро́сти



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *xytrostь, from *xytrъ + *-ostь. Synchronically analysable as хи́трый (xítryj) +‎ -ость (-ostʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • craftiness, craft (shrewdness)
    • ruse
      • stratagem
