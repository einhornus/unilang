кроткий -> gentle, meek
Adjective



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic кротъкъ (krotŭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *krotъkъ. Cognate with Serbo-Croatian кро̀так / kròtak.



Definitions (English):
  • gentle, meek, mild
