щу́ка -> pike (fish)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: щу́ки
Genitive: щу́ки



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *ščuka, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *skew- (“to frighten”). Cognate with Old English scēoh, Old High German sciuhen.



Definitions (English):
  • pike (any freshwater fish of the genus Esox, a relatively large, predatory fish)
