мане́р (6381)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈnʲer]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from German Manier, from French manière, from Latin manus, from Proto-Indo-European *man-.

Noun

мане́р • (manér) m inan (genitive мане́ра, uncountable)

  1. (colloquial) manner

Etymology 2

Noun

мане́р • (manér) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of мане́ра (manéra)
