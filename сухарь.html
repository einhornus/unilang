суха́рь -> piece of dried bread, rusk
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: сухари́
Genitive: сухаря́



Etymology: сухо́й (suxój) +‎ -арь (-arʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • (inanimate, countable) a slice or piece of bread, dried or toasted until hard and crisp
    • (inanimate, countable, by extension) a piece of any dry bread-like food, such as a cracker, crouton, or crispbread
      • (colloquial, animate) dried-up man, a dry old stick a boring person
