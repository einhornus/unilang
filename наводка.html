наводка (15007)


Etymology

наводи́ть (navodítʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈvotkə]

Noun

наводка • (navódka) f inan (genitive наво́дки, nominative plural наво́дки, genitive plural наво́док)

  1. (of a weapon) laying, training
    пряма́я наводка ― prjamája navódka ― direct laying
    прямо́й наво́дкой ― prjamój navódkoj ― over open sights, point-blank
  2. tipping off, tip-off
  3. bridging operation
  4. focusing

Related terms

  • наводи́ть (navodítʹ), навести́ (navestí)
