малыш (2101)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈlɨʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

малы́ш • (malýš) m anim (genitive малыша́, nominative plural малыши́, genitive plural малыше́й, feminine малы́шка)

  1. (colloquial) kid, little boy
  2. (colloquial, derogatory) shorty, shrimp
