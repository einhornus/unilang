крепость -> fortress, strength
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: крепости
Genitive: крепости



Etymology: From Old East Slavic крѣпость (krěpostĭ, “strength”), from Proto-Slavic *krěpostь, from *krěpъ (“strong”) + *-ostь.



Definitions (English):
  • strength, stoutness
    • firmness, body, hardness
      • hardiness, fastness, tenacity
        • heartiness
          • stronghold, fortress, citadel, bastion, presidio
            • (law) deed
