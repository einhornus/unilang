бага́жник (8568)


Etymology

бага́ж (bagáž, “luggage”) + -ник (-nik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈɡaʐnʲɪk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

бага́жник • (bagážnik) m inan (genitive бага́жника, nominative plural бага́жники, genitive plural бага́жников)

  1. trunk (U.S.: luggage storage compartment of a sedan/saloon style car), boot (Gb)
  2. rack, carrier (of a bicycle)

Related terms

  • бага́жный (bagážnyj)
