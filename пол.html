пол -> floor, sex
Noun, declension 2c (pattern грод)

Plural: пол
Genitive: пла



Etymologies:
  • From Old East Slavic полъ (polŭ).
  • Related to пол- (pol-, “half”), from Old East Slavic полъ (polŭ), Old Church Slavonic полъ (polŭ)


Definitions (English):
  • floor,
    • па́дать на пол ― pádatʹ ná pol ― to fall to the floor
  • sex, gender,
    • же́нского пола ― žénskovo póla ― female, мужско́го пола ― mužskóvo póla ― male, обоего пола ― obójevo póla ― both sexes, прекра́сный пол ― prekrásnyj pol ― the fairer sex
  • short masculine singular of по́лый (pólyj)
    • genitive plural of пола (polá)
