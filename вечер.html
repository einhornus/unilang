вечер -> evening
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: вечера
Genitive: вечера



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *večerъ, from Proto-Indo-European *wekʷsperos. Cognates include Ancient Greek ἕσπερος (hésperos), Latin vesper, Old Armenian գիշեր (gišer).



Definitions (English):
  • evening (after 8 PM)
    • tonight, this evening.
      • Вечер обеща́ет быть прия́тным. ― Véčer obeščájet bytʹ prijátnym. ― Tonight is going to be a pleasant night.
    • (formal) party, social gathering, soiree
