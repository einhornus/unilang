кюве́т (25846)


Etymology

Borrowed from French cuvette.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kʲʉˈvʲet]

Noun

кюве́т • (kjuvét) masculine gender">m inan (genitive кюве́та, nominative plural кюве́ты, genitive plural кюве́тов)

  1. ditch (alonǵ a road)
  2. (military) cunette, cuvette
  3. (dated) moat

Related terms
