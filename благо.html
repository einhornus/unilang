Borrowed from Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic бла́го (blago). Displaced the inherited East Slavic form болого (bólogo), from Old East Slavic болого (bologo), бологъ (bologŭ), from Proto-Slavic *bolgъ.

Noun

бла́го • (blágo) n inan (genitive блага, nominative plural блага, genitive plural бла́г)

  1. (poetic) good, blessing, benefit
  2. (economics) good

Synonyms

Adjective

бла́го • (blágo)

  1. short neuter singular of благой (blagój)
