блго -> good (thing), benefit, (coll) since, seeing that
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лто)

Plural: блга
Genitive: блга



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic бла́го (blago). Displaced the inherited East Slavic form болого (bólogo), from Old East Slavic болого (bologo), бологъ (bologŭ), from Proto-Slavic *bolgъ.



Definitions (English):
  • (poetic) good, blessing, benefit
    • (economics) good
      • short neuter singular of благо́й (blagój)
        • (colloquial) since, now that,

        • (colloquial) the more so because
