зево́та (23747)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zʲɪˈvotə]

Noun

зево́та • (zevóta) f inan (genitive зево́ты, nominative plural зево́ты, genitive plural зево́т)

  1. (a fit of) yawning
