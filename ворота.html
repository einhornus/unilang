ворота -> gate, gates
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: ворота
Genitive:  —



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *vorta. Doublet of врата (vratá), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • gate
    • goal (in many sports, e.g. soccer, an area into which the players attempt to put an object)
      • пробива́ть ми́мо ворот ― probivátʹ mímo vorót ― to shoot past the goal to shoot wide
    • genitive singular of ворот (vórot)
