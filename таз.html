таз -> basin, pelvis
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: тазы́
Genitive: та́за



Etymologies:
  • From Old East Slavic тазъ (tazŭ), a Turkic borrowing. Compare Crimean Tatar tas, Turkish tas, Tatar тас (tas), etc. Ultimately from Persian تاس‎ (tâs).
