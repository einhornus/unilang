скля́нка -> phial, bottle
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: скля́нки
Genitive: скля́нки


Definitions (English):
  • vial, flask, bottle, glass
    • (naval) bell, half an hour,
      • пробило шесть скля́нок ― probilo šestʹ skljánok ― six bells went
    • (naval, obsolete) (plural) watch glass, hourglass
