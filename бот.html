Pronunciation

Etymology 1

Borrowed from Dutch boot.

Noun

бот • (bot) m inan (genitive бота, nominative plural бо́ты, genitive plural бо́тов)

  1. (nautical) boat

Etymology 2

Borrowed from French botte.

Бо́ты в камуфля́жной окра́ске

Noun

бот • (bot) m inan (genitive бота, nominative plural бо́ты, genitive plural бо́тов)

  1. overshoe
  2. a type of winter shoe with a zipper or Velcro fastener

Etymology 3

Borrowed from English bot.

Noun

бот • (bot) m anim (genitive бота, nominative plural бо́ты, genitive plural бо́тов)

  1. (computing, video games) bot
