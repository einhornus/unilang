оби́ть (10558)


Etymology

о- (o-) + бить (bitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈbʲitʲ]

Verb

оби́ть • (obítʹ) pf (imperfective обива́ть)

  1. to upholster, to pad
  2. to strike off, to beat off, to knock off, to shake off
    оби́ть поро́ги ― obítʹ porógi ― to importune, to beat down doors, to haunt somebody's threshold
