закла́дка -> laying, bookmark
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: закла́дки
Genitive: закла́дки



Etymology: заклад(ывать) (zaklád(yvatʹ)) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • bookmark (strip used to mark a place in a book)
    • (slang, recreational drugs) drug cache (to be collected by a client)
