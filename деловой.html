делово́й (2491)


Etymology

дело (délo) +‎ -овой (-ovój)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪlɐˈvoj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

делово́й • (delovój)

  1. (relational) business, work, working
    деловы́е круги́ ― delovýje krugí ― business community, business interest
  2. businesslike

Related terms

  • де́лать impf (délatʹ), сделать pf (sdélatʹ)
  • дело n (délo)
