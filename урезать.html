уреза́ть (21439)


Etymology 1

у- (u-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈrʲezətʲ]

Verb

уреза́ть • (urézatʹ) pf (imperfective уреза́ть or урезывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off
  2. to cut down, to reduce, to curtail, to skimp

Etymology 2

уреза́ть (urézatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊrʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

уреза́ть • (urezátʹ) impf (perfective уреза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off
  2. to cut down, to reduce, to curtail, to skimp

Related terms

  • урез (urez), урезание (urezánije), урезка (urezka), урезывание (urezyvanije)
