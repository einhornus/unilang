уреза́ть (21441)


Etymology 1

у- (u-) + ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈrʲezətʲ]

Verb

уре́зать • (urézatʹ) pf (imperfective уреза́ть or уре́зывать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off
  2. to cut down, to reduce, to curtail, to skimp

Etymology 2

уре́зать (urézatʹ) + -а́ть (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊrʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

уреза́ть • (urezátʹ) impf (perfective уре́зать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off
  2. to cut down, to reduce, to curtail, to skimp

Related terms

  • урез (urez), уреза́ние (urezánije), урезка (urezka), урезывание (urezyvanije)
