раб -> slave
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: рабы́
Genitive: раба́



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic рабъ (rabŭ), from Proto-Slavic *orbъ. Displaced Old East Slavic робъ (robŭ).



Definitions (English):
  • slave, serf, bondslave (in common sense and about male person, not female separately)
    • genitive plural of раба́ (rabá)
      • accusative plural of раба́ (rabá)
