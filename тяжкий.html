тя́жкий (4223)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic тѧжькъ (tęžĭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tęžьkъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲaʂkʲɪj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

тя́жкий • (tjážkij) (rare/awkward comparative (по)тяжче, superlative тягчайший)

  1. hard, grave
  2. terrible, painful

Related terms

  • тяжёлый (tjažólyj)
