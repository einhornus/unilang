развёртывать -> to unfold, expand
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я развёртываю
Ты развёртываешь
Past: развёртывал/развёртывала



Etymology: разверну́́ть (razvernútʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to unroll, to unwind, to unfold, to unwrap,
    • развёртывать ковёр ― razvjórtyvatʹ kovjór ― to unroll a carpet, развёртывать га́зе́ту́ ― razvjórtyvatʹ gazétu ― to unfold the paper, развёртывать паке́т ― razvjórtyvatʹ pakét ― to unwrap a parcel, развёртывать знамёна ― razvjórtyvatʹ znamjóna ― to spread out the banners
  • to show, to display,
    • развёртывать свои́́ си́лы ― razvjórtyvatʹ svoí síly ― to show/display/reveal one's strength
  • to start (up)
    • to put on a wide scale, to widen, to broaden, to expand
      • (military) to deploy, to extend
        • (military) to expand (into)
          • to turn, to swing about, to swing around, to slew (about), to make a U-turn
