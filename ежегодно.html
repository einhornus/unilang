ежего́дно (8691)


Etymology[edit]

ежего́дный (ježegódnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [(j)ɪʐɨˈɡodnə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb[edit]

ежего́дно • (ježegódno)

  1. annually, yearly (once every year)
  2. per annum, p.a.
