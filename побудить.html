Verb[edit]

побуди́ть • (pobudítʹ) pf (imperfective буди́ть)

  1. (colloquial, transitive) to spend some time awakening
  2. (colloquial) to wake up (all or many people, one by one)
  3. (low colloquial, transitive) to awaken
  4. (hunting) to drive (an animal) from its hole or lair, to flush out

Verb[edit]

побуди́ть • (pobudítʹ) pf (imperfective побужда́ть)

  1. to induce, to prompt, to impel, to spur

Related terms[edit]

  • побужде́ние (pobuždénije)
