напасть -> to attack, come upon
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: напасти
Genitive: напасти



Etymology: на- (na-) +‎ пасть (pastʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • misfortune, bad luck, trouble
    • to attack, to fall on, to assault, to descend (on)
      • to come across, to come upon, to hit on,
        • я напа́л на мысль ― ja napál na myslʹ ― the thought occurred to me
      • (feeling, condition) to come (over), to grip, to seize, to overcome
