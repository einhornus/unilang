матери́ть (23111)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mətʲɪˈrʲitʲ]

Verb

матери́ть • (materítʹ) impf (perfective обматери́ть)

  1. to dirty mouth, to badmouth, to abuse, to cuss out (to insult, to swear dirtily (at))

Related terms

  • мат (mat)
  • ма́терщина (máterščina)
