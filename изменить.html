изменть -> to change, alter, (+d) betray
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я изме́н
Ты изменшь
Past: изменл/изменла



Etymology: из- (iz-) + Old East Slavic мѣни́ти (měniti), from Proto-Slavic *měniti.



Definitions (English):
  • to change, to alter, to modify, to edit
    • to vary
      • (with dative) to betray, to become unfaithful, to cheat on
        • to break, to violate (an oath, promise)
          • to fail (memory)
            • to desert
