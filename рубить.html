рубить -> to chop
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я рублю
Ты рубишь
Past: рубил/рубила



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *rǫbiti. Compare Belarusian руба́ць (rubácʹ), Bulgarian ръ́бя (rǎ́bja), Ukrainian руби́́ти (rubýty), Czech roubit, Polish rąbać



Definitions (English):
  • to axe, to fell, to chop, to cut (with an axe)
    • (colloquial, figuratively) to not mince words, to speak the truth, to speak one's mind.
      • Он лю́би́т руби́́ть пра́вду-ма́тку. On ljúbit rubítʹ právdu-mátku. He doesn't like to mince words.(literally, “He likes to cut the mother truth.”)
