а́рмия -> army
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: а́рмии
Genitive: а́рмии



Etymology: From earlier армѣя (arměja)/армея (armeja), directly from French armée (“army armed forces”) or via German Armee (“army”).



Definitions (English):
  • army, armed forces (the complete military organization of a nation, including land, sea and air forces)
    • army (specifically the land-based forces of a nation's military)
