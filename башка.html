башка -> head

Etymology: From a Turkic language, probably a borrowing of a dative singular form attested in Kipchak languages compare Tatar баш (baş, “head”), Bashkir башҡа (bašqa, literally “head.1SG.DAT”).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial, derogatory) pate, noggin, bonce (slang word for head, part of the body)
    • Поду́май своё́й башко́й о после́дствиях! Podúmaj svojéj baškój o poslédstvijax! Use your noggin, think of the consequences!
