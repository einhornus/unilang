обтека́ть (23444)


Etymology

обте́к- (obték-) + -а́ть (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐptʲɪˈkatʲ]

Verb

обтека́ть • (obtekátʹ) impf (perfective обте́чь)

  1. to flow around, to circumvent
  2. (aviation) to streamline
  3. (mathematics) to circulate
  4. (figuratively) to bypass, to dodge, to ignore (something like obstacles or burdens)
  5. (colloquial) to feel strongly ashamed and try not to show it

Related terms

  • обтека́тель m (obtekátelʹ), обтека́емость f (obtekájemostʹ), обтека́ние n (obtekánije)
  • обтека́емый (obtekájemyj)
