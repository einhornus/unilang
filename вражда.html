вражда́ (11334)


Etymology

An Old Church Slavonicism, from Proto-Slavic *voržьda (the Old East Slavic form is found in the poetic Ukrainian ворожда (vorožda)), from *vorgъ + *-ьda. Cognate with Polish language">Polish wróżda, Czech vražda.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vrɐʐˈda]

Noun

вражда́ • (vraždá) f inan (genitive вражды́, uncountable)

  1. enmity, hostility, animosity, antagonism (hostile or unfriendly disposition)
