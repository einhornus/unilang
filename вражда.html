вражда́ -> enmity
Noun, declension 1b
Plural: вражды́
Genitive: вражды́



Etymology: An Old Church Slavonicism, from Proto-Slavic *voržьda (the Old East Slavic form is found in the poetic Ukrainian ворожда (vorožda)), from *vorgъ + *-ьda. Cognate with Polish wróżda, Czech vražda.



Definitions (English):
  • enmity, hostility, animosity, antagonism (hostile or unfriendly disposition)
