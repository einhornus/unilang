якорь -> anchor
Noun, declension 2c (pattern город)

Plural: якоря
Genitive: якоря



Etymology: From Old East Slavic ѧкорь (ękorĭ), from Latin ancora (“anchor”) via Old Swedish ankari (> Swedish ankare), Old Norse akkeri, from Ancient Greek ἄγκυρα (ánkura, “anchor hook”).



Definitions (English):
  • anchor,
    • я́корь мне в за́дницу! ― jákorʹ mne v zádnicu! ― an anchor into my ass! (expression of utter bewilderment or disapproval)
  • (electrical) armature core, armature
    • (acoustic) reed
