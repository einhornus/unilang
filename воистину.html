вои́стину (8713)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐˈisʲtʲɪnʊ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

вои́стину • (voístinu)

  1. (lofty) truly, in truth, indeed, verily
  2. (lofty, parenthesis) indeed

Related terms

  • и́стина f (ístina)
  • и́стинный (ístinnyj)
