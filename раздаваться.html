раздава́ться (3666)


Etymology

раздава́ть (razdavátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rəzdɐˈvat͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

раздава́ться • (razdavátʹsja) impf (perfective разда́ться)

  1. to sound, to ring out (usually unexpectedly), to go off (e.g. telephone, gunshot)
  2. (dated) to give way, to make way; to move aside
  3. to expand, to become wide; to gain weight
  4. passive of раздава́ть (razdavátʹ)
