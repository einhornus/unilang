опра́вка (21263)


Etymology

опра́вить (oprávitʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈprafkə]

Noun

опра́вка • (oprávka) f inan (genitive опра́вки, nominative plural опра́вки, genitive plural опра́вок)

  1. mandrel (object used as an aid for shaping a material)
  2. arbor (bar for supporting cutting tools)
  3. dolly (a camera cart)
