ружьё -> gun, rifle
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: ружья
Genitive: ружья



Etymology: From Old East Slavic оружиѥ (oružije), from Proto-Slavic *orǫžьje, from *orǫga / *orǫžь



Definitions (English):
  • shotgun, musket, rifle,
    • двуствольное ружьё ― dvustvólʹnoje ružʹjó ― double-barreled shotgun, помповое ружьё ― pompovoje ružʹjó ― pump-action shotgun, противотанковое ружьё ― protivotánkovoje ružʹjó ― antitank rifle, духовое ружьё ― duxovóje ružʹjó ― air rifle
  • a device resembling such a firearm,
    • фоторужьё ― fotoružʹjó ― "rifle stock" (for a camera)подво́дное ружьё ― podvódnoje ružʹjó ― spear gun
