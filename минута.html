мину́та (196)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin minuta, from minutus (“small”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈnutə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

мину́та • (minúta) f inan (genitive мину́ты, nominative plural мину́ты, genitive plural минут)

  1. minute
  2. moment, instant

Related terms

  • поминутный (pominútnyj)
