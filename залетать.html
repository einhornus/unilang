залета́ть (19258)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ лета́ть (letátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəlʲɪˈtatʲ]

Verb

залета́ть • (zaletátʹ) impf (perfective залете́ть)

  1. to fly (in, intó)
  2. to fly (as high as), to go up (as far as), to fly (as far as), to fly (behind, beyond)
  3. to land (somewhere) on the way, to make a stopover (somewhere)
  4. (colloquial) to get intó a fix, to get intó a mess, to get something one doesn't want (i.e. contract a sexually transmitted disease or become pregnant)

Verb

залета́ть • (zaletátʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial) to begin to fly

Related terms

  • залётный (zaljótnyj)
  • залёт (zaljót)
  • лететь impf (letétʹ), полете́ть pf (poletétʹ)
  • лета́ть (letátʹ)
  • лета́ющий (letájuščij)
  • лёт (ljot)
