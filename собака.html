соба́ка -> dog
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: соба́ки
Genitive: соба́ки



Etymology: From Middle Iranian compare Zoroastrian Dari [script needed] (sabah), Old Median σπάκα (spākəʰ) [the source of Old Armenian ասպակ (aspak, “dog”)], Avestan 𐬯𐬞𐬀𐬐𐬀‎ (spaka, “dog-like”). Cognates include Ukrainian соба́ка (sobáka), Belarusian сабака (sabáka), Polish sobaka (dialectal).
