подъзд -> entrance, doorway
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: подъзды
Genitive: подъзда



Etymology: Action noun from подъе́хать (podʺjéxatʹ, “to approach”), from под- (pod-) +‎ е́хать (jéxatʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • approach, access
    • main/front entrance of a building
      • stairwell
