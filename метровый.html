метро́вый (14018)


Etymology

метр (metr) +‎ -овый (-óvyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈtrovɨj]

Adjective

метро́вый • (metróvyj) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) meter
    метровые волны ― metróvyje vólny ― meter waves
  2. one-metre-long, one-metre-wide, one-metre-thick, one-metre-high

Related terms

  • метр (metr)
  • метраж (metráž)
