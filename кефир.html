кефир -> kefir, yoghurt-type drink
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: кефиры
Genitive: кефира



Etymology: Borrowed into Russian from a language of the Caucausus compare Mingrelian ქიფური (kipuri), Ossetian къӕпы (k’æpy), Karachay-Balkar гыпы. The ultimate origin is unknown.



Definitions (English):
  • kefir (a yogurt-like beverage made from fermented cows' milk)
    • Я про́сто обожа́ю кефи́р, но зато́́ терпе́ть не могу́́ молоко́́! ― Ja prósto obožáju kefír, no zató terpétʹ ne mogú molokó! ― I just love kefir, but, on the other hand, I hate milk!
