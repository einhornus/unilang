относи́ть (7995)


Etymology

от- (ot-) +‎ носи́ть (nosítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtnɐˈsʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

относи́ть • (otnosítʹ) impf (perfective отнести́)

  1. to refer
  2. to attribute, to ascribe (to associate ownership or authorship with)
  3. to relegate
  4. to take
  5. to charge
  6. to appropriate
  7. to take to
  8. to take back

Verb

относи́ть • (otnosítʹ) pf (imperfective носи́ть)

  1. to wear untiĺ worn off or untiĺ stopping to wear, to wear for a while
  2. to carry, to bear for a while
