Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -avə

Etymology 1

Inherited from Old East Slavic пра́во (pravo). Calque of German Recht and French droit, based on пра́вый (právyj, “right, correct; straight”). Cognate with Ukrainian пра́во (právo), Belarusian права (práva), Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic пра́во (pravo), Bulgarian пра́во (právo), Serbo-Croatian пра̏во, Slovene prȃvo (tonal orthography), Czech právo, Slovak právo, Polish language">Polish prawo.

Noun

пра́во • (právo) n inan (genitive права, nominative plural права, genitive plural пра́в)

  1. right; claim
  2. (law) law, right
  3. justice
  4. (in the plural) license

Etymology 2

пра́вый (právyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Adverb

пра́во • (právo)

  1. indeed, really

Adjective

пра́во • (právo)

  1. short neuter singular of пра́вый (právyj)
