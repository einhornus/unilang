лежа́ть (216)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *ležati, from Proto-Indo-European *legʰ-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈʐatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

лежа́ть • (ležátʹ) impf (perfective лечь or полежа́ть)

  1. to lie (to be in a horizontal position)
    ма́льчик лежа́л на излече́нии. ― Málʹčik ležál na izlečénii. ― A boy lay in recovery.
  2. to be situated
  3. to rest, to be incumbent

Related terms

  • лежа́нка (ležánka)
  • ле́жбище (léžbišče)
  • лёжка (ljóžka)
