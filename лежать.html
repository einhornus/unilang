лежа́ть -> to lie, (p) lie for a while
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я лежу́

Past: лежа́л/лежа́ла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *ležati, from Proto-Indo-European *legʰ-.



Definitions (English):
  • to lie (to be in a horizontal position)
    • Ма́льчик лежа́л на излечении. ― Málʹčik ležál na izlečénii. ― A boy lay in recovery.
  • to be situated
    • to rest, to be incumbent
