шала́ш -> cabin (made of branches)
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: шалаши́
Genitive: шалаша́



Etymology: From a Turkic source.



Definitions (English):
  • a simple wooden hut
    • (criminal slang) the bottom bunk of a bunk bed, with a blanket hanging down from the top bunk as a curtain
