куту́зовский (24594)


Etymology

куту́зов (Kutúzov) + -ский (-skij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kʊˈtuzəfskʲɪj]

Adjective

куту́зовский • (kutúzovskij) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) Kutuzov; Kutuzov's (Russian surname, especially referring to Mikhail Kutuzov, Field Marshal of the Russian Empire, or things named after him)
    куту́зовская изба́ ― Kutúzovskaja izbá ― Kutuzov's hut (Russian military museum)

Related terms

  • куту́зов (Kutúzov)
