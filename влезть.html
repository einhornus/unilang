влезть -> to climb in
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я влезу
Ты влезешь
Past: влез/влезла́



Etymology: в- (v-) +‎ лезть (leztʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to get into (something, also figurative), to climb into, to scramble into,
    • ско́лько вле́зет ― skólʹko vlézet ― as much as you like, влезть в долги́ ― vleztʹ v dolgí ― get into debts
  • to fit in, to go in
    • to meddle,
      • влезть не в своё де́ло ― vleztʹ ne v svojó délo ― to poke one's nose into other people's affairs to pry, влезть в ду́шу ― vleztʹ v dúšu ― to worm oneself into smb.'s confidence (to get into somebody's soul)
