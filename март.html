март -> March (month)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ма́рты
Genitive: ма́рта



Etymology: From Old East Slavic мартъ (martŭ), Old Church Slavonic мартъ (martŭ), from Byzantine Greek Μάρτιος (Mártios), from Latin mārtius, from Latin Mavors.
