перед -> in front of, before

Etymology: From Old East Slavic передъ (peredŭ), from Proto-Slavic *perdъ, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *per-, whence also пере- (pere-), перез (perez). Doublet of пред (pred), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • in front of, before
    • right/immediately before
      • front, forepart
