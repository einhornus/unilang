поле́но -> log
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: поле́нья
Genitive: поле́на



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic полѣно (polěno), related to полѣти (polěti, “burn, flame”).



Definitions (English):
  • billet, log, chock (of wood), wood block (for use as firewood)
    • log-shaped cake, e.g. a Yule log
