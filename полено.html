полено (8689)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic полѣно (polěno), related to полѣти (polěti, “burn, flame”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlʲenə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

полено • (poléno) n inan (genitive поле́на, nominative plural поле́нья, genitive plural поле́ньев)

  1. billet, log, chock (of wood), wood block (for use as firewood)
  2. log-shaped cake, e.g. a Yule log

Related terms

  • поле́нница (polénnica)
  • полешко (poléško)
  • рождественское полено (roždéstvenskoje poléno)
