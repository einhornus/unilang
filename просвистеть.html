просвисте́ть (19933)


Etymology

про- (pro-) +‎ свисте́ть (svistétʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəsvʲɪˈsʲtʲetʲ]

Verb

просвисте́ть • (prosvistétʹ) pf (imperfective просвистывать)

  1. (perfective only) to make a short whistling sound, to whistle for a short time
  2. to whistle (a musical piece)
  3. (perfective only) to whizz, to whistle while flying by
  4. (colloquial) to fritter awaý, to waste
  5. to whistle (for a specified amount of time)

Usage notes

With this verb, all meanings involving whistling refer to whistling with or without́ the aid of a whistle. Contrast просвистать (prosvistátʹ), with similar meanings but which may only be without́ the aid of a whistle.
