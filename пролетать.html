пролета́ть -> to fly through or past
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я пролета́ю
Ты пролета́ешь
Past: пролета́л/пролета́ла



Etymology: про- (pro-) +‎ лета́ть (letátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to fly (by, past, through), to fly (over)
    • to overfly, to overshoot,
      • пролета́ть ми́мо це́ли ― proletátʹ mímo céli ― to overshoot the target
    • to cover, to pass rapidly
      • (time) to fly by
        • (colloquial) to be passed over (for), to miss the mark, to miss the boat,
          • пролета́ть как фанера над Парижем, proletátʹ kak fanéra nad Parížemmiss the mark, miss the boat(literally, “to fly like plywood over Paris”)
