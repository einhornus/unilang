разгуля́ться (20196)


Etymology

разгуля́ть (razguljátʹ) + -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rəzɡʊˈlʲat͡sːə]

Verb

разгуля́ться • (razguljátʹsja) pf (imperfective разгу́ливаться)

  1. (colloquial) to give oneself free rein
  2. (low colloquial, figuratively) to binge
  3. (colloquial) to become very powerful (of a blizzard, a wave, etc.)
  4. (colloquial) to stop wanting to sleep
  5. (colloquial) to become clearer, to clear up (of the weather)
  6. passive of разгуля́ть (razguljátʹ)

Related terms

  • разгу́ливать impf (razgúlivatʹ), разгуля́ть pf (razguljátʹ)
  • разгу́л (razgúl), разгу́льный (razgúlʹnyj), разгу́лье (razgúlʹje)
  • гуля́ть (guljátʹ)
  • гуля́нье (guljánʹje)
  • гуля́ка (guljáka)
