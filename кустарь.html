куста́рь (26116)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle High German">Middle High German kunster (“connoisseur of the arts”), also kunstener; compare German Künstler (“artist”). Influenced by -арь (-árʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kʊˈstarʲ]

Noun

куста́рь • (kustárʹ) m anim (genitive кустаря́, nominative plural кустари́, genitive plural кустаре́й)

  1. artisan, handcraftsman

Synonyms

  • реме́сленник (reméslennik)
