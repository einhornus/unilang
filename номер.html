Noun

но́мер • (nómer) m inan (genitive номера, nominative plural номера, genitive plural номеро́в)

  1. ordinal number
  2. size (of clothes or shoes)
  3. room (in a hotel)
  4. number, issue (e.g. of a magazine or a newspaper)
  5. item, turn, trick (e.g., in a circus)

Usage notes

Frequently abbreviated to №. Doubling indicates plural: №№ (“номера (nomera)”).
