плуг -> plough
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: плу́ги
Genitive: плу́га



Etymology: From Old East Slavic плугъ (plugŭ), from Proto-Slavic *plugъ, borrowed from Proto-Germanic *plōgaz (“plough”), *plōguz.



Definitions (English):
  • plough, plow
