отпи́хивать (23806)


Etymology

отпих(нуть) (otpix(nútʹ)) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtˈpʲixʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

отпи́хивать • (otpíxivatʹ) impf (perfective отпихну́ть)

  1. (colloquial) to shove awaý, to shove off (e.g. a boat from the shore)
  2. (colloquial) to shove aside
  3. (colloquial, figuratively) to get rid of

Related terms

  • пиха́ть impf (pixátʹ), пихну́ть pf (pixnútʹ)
