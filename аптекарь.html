Noun[edit]

апте́карь • (aptékarʹ) m anim (genitive апте́каря, nominative plural апте́кари, genitive plural апте́карей, feminine аптекарша, related adjective апте́карский)

  1. pharmacist, chemist, druggist (male or female)

Usage notes[edit]

  • Grammatically the word is a masculine, but it is used to refer to both a man and a woman and it is declinable in both cases. The term аптекарша f (aptékarša) is also used for a woman in colloquial Russian.

Related terms[edit]

  • апте́ка (aptéka)
