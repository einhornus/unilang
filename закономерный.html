закономе́рный -> regular, predictable
Adjective



Etymology: зако́н (zakón) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ мерный (mérnyj)



Definitions (English):
  • regular, appropriate, natural, in accordance with the laws (e.g. of nature)
