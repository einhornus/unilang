пи́ки (8858)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpʲikʲɪ]

Etymology 1

Noun

пи́ки • (píki) f inan pl (genitive пик, plural only)

  1. (card games) spades (one of the four suits of playing cards, marked with the symbol ♠)
Synonyms
  • вини́ (víni)
Related terms
  • пи́ковый (píkovyj)

Noun

пи́ки • (píki) f inan or f inan pl

  1. nominative plural of пи́ка (píka)
  2. genitive singular of пи́ка (píka)
  3. accusative plural of пи́ка (píka)

Etymology 2

Noun

пи́ки • (píki) m inan pl

  1. nominative plural of пик (pik)
  2. accusative plural of пик (pik)
