щекота́ть -> to tickle
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я щекочу́
Ты щеко́чешь
Past: щекота́л/щекота́ла



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic скъкътати (skŭkŭtati).



Definitions (English):
  • to tickle, to titillate (touch repeatedly or stroke delicately)
    • (intransitive) to chirp, to twitter (said of nightingales, magpies and certain other birds)
