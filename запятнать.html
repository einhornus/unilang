запятна́ть (26054)


Etymology

за- (za-) +‎ пятнать (pjatnátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəpʲɪtˈnatʲ]

Verb

запятна́ть • (zapjatnátʹ) pf

  1. to stain, to soil
  2. (figuratively) to disgrace, to defame
  3. (informal) to touch or hit (in games of tag or lapta)
  4. (informal) to mark, to stamp, to brand

Related terms

  • пятно́ (pjatnó)
