пры́скать (29856)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *pryskati.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈprɨskətʲ]

Verb[edit]

пры́скать • (prýskatʹ) impf (perfective пры́снуть or попрыскать)

  1. to burst into laughter
  2. to dart, to dash
  3. to sprinkle (+ instrumental)
  4. (imperfective only) to drizzle, to fall in small drops (of rain)
