одино́чка -> person on his or her own, single person
Noun, declension 1a
Plural: одино́чки
Genitive: одино́чки



Etymology: одино́кий (odinókij) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • loner, singleton (one who lacks or avoids company)
    • (colloquial) solitary (confinement) cell, one-man cell
      • (boat) single-oar
