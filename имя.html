и́мя -> (first) name
Noun, declension 3c
Plural: имена́
Genitive: и́мени



Etymology: From Old East Slavic имѧ (imę), from Proto-Slavic *jьmę, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *inˀmen, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁nómn̥.
