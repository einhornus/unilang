отвести́ (2160)


Etymology

от- (ot-) + вести́ (vestí)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtvʲɪˈsʲtʲi]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Homophones: отвезти́ (otveztí)

Verb

отвести́ • (otvestí) pf (imperfective отводи́ть)

  1. to lead away; to take / draw aside; to take away
  2. to divert
  3. to avert, to parry; to deflect; to draw off; to ward off
  4. to allocate, to assign
  5. (idiomatic)
    отвести́ ду́шу ― otvestí dúšu ― to unburden one's heart, to pour out one's heart
Related terms
  • отво́д (otvód)
