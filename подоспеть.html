подоспе́ть (9201)


Etymology

подо- (podo-) + спеть (spetʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pədɐˈspʲetʲ]

Verb

подоспе́ть • (podospétʹ) pf (imperfective подоспева́ть)

  1. (colloquial) to come/arrive/be in time (for)
  2. (colloquial) (time) to come, to arrive
  3. (colloquial) to be mature/ripe

Related terms

  • спеть (spetʹ)
  • спе́лый (spélyj), спе́лость (spélostʹ)
