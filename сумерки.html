су́мерки -> twilight, dusk
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: су́мерки
Genitive:  —



Etymology: Related to Old East Slavic суморокъ (sumorokŭ), with vocalism change. See also су́мрак (súmrak).



Definitions (English):
  • dusk, twilight
