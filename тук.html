тук (11223)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic тукъ (tukŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tukъ

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tuk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

тук • (tuk) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ту́ка, nominative plural ту́ки, genitive plural ту́ков)

  1. (obsolete) fat, lard
  2. fertilizer

Related terms

  • ту́чный (túčnyj)
  • тучность (túčnostʹ)
  • тучнеть (tučnétʹ)
