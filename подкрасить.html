подкра́сить (23680)


Etymology

под- (pod-) +‎ кра́сить (krásitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐtˈkrasʲɪtʲ]

Verb

подкра́сить • (podkrásitʹ) pf (imperfective подкра́шивать)

  1. to touch up (something with paint, dye or makeup)
  2. to paint (something) more
  3. to tint
  4. (figuratively) to beautify, to embellish
