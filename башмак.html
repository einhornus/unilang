башмак -> shoe
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: башма́ки
Genitive: башма́ка



Etymology: Borrowed from a Turkic source. Compare Tatar башма́к (başmaq, “shoe”), dialectal Turkish başmak (“shoe”).



Definitions (English):

      • быть под башмако́м ― bytʹ pod bašmakóm ― to be henpecked
    • chock
