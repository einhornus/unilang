Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я совершу́

Past: соверши́л/соверши́ла



Etymology: From Old Church Slavonic съврьшити (sŭvrĭšiti), from съ- (sŭ-) + връшити (vrŭšiti) from Proto-Indo-European *wers-. Also related to верх (verx, “top, summit”) and to Old East Slavic вьрхъ (vĭrxŭ), Proto-Slavic *vrьxъ. Literally, "to top out".



Definitions (English):
  • to accomplish, to perform, to commit, to perpetrate, to make
