сви́стнуть (8664)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsvʲisnʊtʲ]
  • Homophones: свиснуть

Verb

сви́стнуть • (svístnutʹ) pf (imperfective свисте́ть)

  1. to whistle
  2. (colloquial) to smack, to slap
  3. (colloquial, figuratively) to sneak, to pinch, to steal

Related terms

  • свист (svist)
  • свисто́к (svistók)
  • свисту́н (svistun)
