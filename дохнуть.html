дохнуть -> to breathe
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я дохну
Ты дохнешь
Past: до́хнул (до́х)/дохла



Etymologies:
  • Ultimately from the same source as до́хну́ть (doxnútʹ) below.
  • From Proto-Slavic *dъxnǫti.


Definitions (English):
  • (derogatory, slang) to croak (to die)
    • to take a breath, to breathe
      • (figuratively) to blow (of a wind), to have a (single) blow
