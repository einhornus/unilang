до́хнуть -> to breathe
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я до́хну
Ты до́хнешь
Past: дохнул (дох)/до́хла



Etymologies:
  • Ultimately from the same source as до́хнуть (doxnútʹ) below.
  • From Proto-Slavic *dъxnǫti.


Definitions (English):
  • (derogatory, slang) to croak (to die)
    • to take a breath, to breathe
      • (figuratively) to blow (of a wind), to have a (single) blow
