вскрыть -> to unseal, disclose, dissect, open, reveal
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я вскро́ю
Ты вскро́ешь
Past: вскры́л/вскры́ла



Etymology: вс- (vs-) +‎ крыть (krytʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to open, to unseal,
    • вскрыть письмо́ ― vskrytʹ pisʹmó ― to open a letter, to tear a letter open
  • to reveal, to bring to light, to disclose, to uncover, to expose,
    • вскрыть недоста́тки ― vskrytʹ nedostátki ― to expose shortcomings, to lay bare shortcomings
  • to dissect, to make a postmortem, to perform an autopsy on
    • to burst, to cut, to open, to lance,
      • вскрыть нарыв ― vskrytʹ narýv ― to cut an abscess
