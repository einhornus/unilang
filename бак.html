бак -> cistern, tank
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ба́ки
Genitive: ба́ка



Etymologies:
  • Borrowed from Dutch bakboord or German Backbord.
  • Borrowed from Dutch bak or German Back or English back
  • Contraction of бакенбарда (bakenbárda).


Definitions (English):
  • (nautical) forecastle
    • container, receptacle (for liquids)
      • tank,
        • топливный бак ― tóplivnyj bak ― fuel tank, бензиновый бак / бензобак ― benzínovyj bak / benzobák ― petrol tank
      • boiler
        • sideburn. Synonym:
          • бакенбарда (bakenbárda)
