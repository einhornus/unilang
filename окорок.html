о́корок -> ham, gammon
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: окорока
Genitive: окорока



Etymology: From Old East Slavic окорокъ (okorokŭ), from Proto-Slavic *okorkъ, from *ob- + *korkъ (“leg”).
