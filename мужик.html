мужи́к -> (Russian) peasant
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: мужики́
Genitive: мужика́



Etymology: From муж (muž, “man (dated) husband”) +‎ -ик (-ik).



Definitions (English):
  • boor
    • coarse, uncouth person
      • (colloquial or derogatory) man (male person)
        • (colloquial) husband
          • (historical) peasant from tsarist Russia
            • (slang) macho
