побо́рник -> champion, keen supporter (of)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: побо́рники
Genitive: побо́рника



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic поборьникъ (poborĭnikŭ), from поборати (poborati, “to help in a struggle, to protect”), from борь (borĭ, “struggle”).



Definitions (English):
  • partisan (fervent supporter)
