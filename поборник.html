побо́рник (24323)


Etymology

Borrowed from Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic поборьникъ (poborĭnikŭ), from поборати (poborati, “to help in a struggle, to protect”), from борь (borĭ, “struggle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈbornʲɪk]

Noun

побо́рник • (pobórnik) m anim (genitive побо́рника, nominative plural побо́рники, genitive plural побо́рников)

  1. partisan (fervent supporter)

Related terms

  • борьба́ (borʹbá)
