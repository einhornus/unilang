бнка -> jar, can
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: бнки
Genitive: бнки



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *banъka, from *baňa +‎ *-ъka.
  • Borrowed from German Bank or Dutch bank.


Definitions (English):
  • jar, pot
    • can, tin
      • (medicine) cupping glass
        • (marine) sandbank, shoal
          • (marine)
            • устричная ба́нка oyster bed
          • (boats) thwart (oarsman’s transverse seat)
            • genitive singular of банк (bank)
