латви́йский (16841)


Etymology

Латвия (Látvija) +‎ -ийский (-íjskij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lɐtˈvʲijskʲɪj]

Adjective

латви́йский • (latvíjskij)

  1. Latvian (relating to the country Latvia as a state)

Usage notes

  • латви́йский is used to refer to the country Latvia as a state, its laws, constitution, flag, government, etc. To refer to ethnic Latvians and their language, culture, customs, traditions and so on, use латы́шский (latýšskij). Compare alsó российский (rossíjskij) and русский (rússkij).

Related terms

  • Латвия (Látvija)
  • латвиец (latvijec), латвийка (latvijka)
  • латы́ш (latýš), латышка (latýška), латы́шский (latýšskij)
