пёстрый -> many-coloured, motley
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic пьстръ (pĭstrŭ), from Proto-Slavic *pьstrъ (“variegated”).



Definitions (English):
  • variegated, motley
    • multicoloured
      • (colloquial, figuratively) mixed
        • (colloquial) florid
