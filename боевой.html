боево́й -> fighting, battle
Adjective



Etymology: бой (boj) +‎ -евой (-evój)



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) combat, battle, fighting,
    • боево́й поря́док ― bojevój porjádok ― combat formation, battle formation, боево́е иску́сство ― bojevóje iskússtvo ― martial art (literally, “combat art, fighting art”)
  • (figuratively) militant, bellicose, belligerent, active
