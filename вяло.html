вя́ло (5050)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʲalə]

Etymology 1

вя́лый (vjályj) + -о (-o)

Adverb

вя́ло • (vjálo)

  1. sluggishly, listlessly, inertly

Adjective

вя́ло • (vjálo)

  1. short neuter singular of вя́лый (vjályj)

Related terms

  • вя́лый (vjályj), вя́лость f (vjálostʹ)
  • вя́нуть impf (vjánutʹ), завя́нуть pf (zavjánutʹ)

Etymology 2

Verb

вя́ло • (vjálo)

  1. short neuter singular past indicative imperfective of вя́нуть (vjánutʹ)
