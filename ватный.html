ва́тный -> quilted, cotton wool (adj)
Adjective



Etymology: ва́та (váta) +‎ -ный (-nyj)



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) cotton wool, batting wadded, quilted,
    • ва́тный хала́т ― vátnyj xalát ― cotton wool bathrobe
  • (of limbs) powerless
