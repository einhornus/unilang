фру́нзенский (26412)


Etymology

Фрунзе (Frúnzɛ) +‎ -енский (-enskij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈfrun⁽ʲ⁾zʲɪnskʲɪj]

Adjective

фру́нзенский • (frúnzenskij) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) Frunze (referring to anyone with this name, especially Mikhail Frunze, Bolshevik military leader)
  2. (relational) Frunze (former name of Bishkek, capital of Kyrgyzstan)

Related terms

  • Фрунзе (Frúnzɛ)
