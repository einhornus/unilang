спешить -> to hurry
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я спешу
Ты спешишь
Past: спеши́л/спеши́ла



Etymologies:
  • From Old East Slavic спѣшити (spěšiti), Proto-Slavic *spěšiti (“to hurry”), from *spěxъ (“haste”), from *spěti (“to advance, ripen, hurry”) + *-xъ. Equivalently from спех (spex), from спеть (spetʹ).
  • с- (s-) +‎ пе́ший (péšij) +‎ -ить (-itʹ)


Definitions (English):
  • to hurry, to hasten, to make haste, to be in a hurry
    • to be fast (said of clocks, watches)
      • to cause to dismount (from a horse or bicycle, get out of a vehicle)
