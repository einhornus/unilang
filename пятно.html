пятно́ -> spot
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: пятна
Genitive: пятна



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic пятьно (pjatĭno, “brand, sign”), from Proto-Slavic *pętьno (“trace, sign”), from *pęta (“heel”).



Definitions (English):
