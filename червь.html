червь (6591)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *čьrvь, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷr̥mis (“worm”), whence English kermes.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡ɕerfʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

червь • (červʹ) m anim or m inan (genitive червя́, nominative plural че́рви, genitive plural червей)

  1. worm (invertebrate)
  2. (informal) caterpillar, maggot, helminth, grub
  3. worm (a feeling)
  4. worm (a person)
  5. (computing) worm (a computer program)
  6. (inanimate, archaic) name of the letter Ч.

Synonyms

  • (worm): червя́к (červják)

Related terms

  • червеобразный (červeobráznyj)
  • черви́вый (červívyj)
  • червяной (červjanój)
  • червиветь (červívetʹ)
  • червоточина (červotóčina)
