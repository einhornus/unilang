Noun[edit]

бычо́к • (byčók) m anim or m inan (genitive бычка́, nominative plural бычки́, genitive plural бычко́в)

  1. (animate) diminutive of бык (byk): bull calf, steer
  2. (animate) bullhead, miller's thumb, goby
  3. (slang, inanimate) cigarette butt, fag-end
  4. (colloquial, inanimate) ZIL-5301 light truck or related vehicle
