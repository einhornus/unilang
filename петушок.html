петушо́к (8413)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪtʊˈʂok]

Noun[edit]

петушо́к • (petušók) m anim (genitive петушка́, nominative plural петушки́, genitive plural петушко́в)

  1. Diminutive of пету́х (petúx): (young) rooster
  2. fighting fish, betta
  3. (slang) passive gay
