това́р -> commodity, article
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: това́ры
Genitive: това́ра



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *tovarъ, of Turkic origin compare Bashkir тауар (tawar, “merchandise, goods”), Turkish davar (“livestock”).



Definitions (English):
  • commodity, product, article
    • (plural) goods, wares
