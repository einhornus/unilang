едный -> single, sole, united
Adjective



Etymology: Derived from Old Church Slavonic ѥдинъ (jedinŭ) + -ый (-yj). Related to Russian оди́н (odín).



Definitions (English):
  • united, unified, common, indivisible
    • one, single, sole,
      • еди́ный день ― jedínyj denʹ ― a single day
