сазан (5152)


Etymology

From Turkic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈzan]

Noun

саза́н • (sazán) m anim (genitive саза́на, nominative plural саза́ны, genitive plural саза́нов)

  1. sazan, wild carp (Cyprinus carpio)

Synonyms

  • карп (karp)
