сектор -> sector
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: сектора
Genitive: сектора



Etymology: Borrowed from Latin sector, from Latin secō (“to cut”), from Proto-Indo-European *sek- (“to cut”). Cognates include Old Church Slavonic сѣщи (sěšti, “to cut, hack, chop off”) and Old English saga (English saw).



Definitions (English):
  • sector,
    • сектор обстре́ла ― séktor obstréla ― arc of fire
  • sphere, branch, department
