Verb[edit]

наплыва́ть • (naplyvátʹ) impf (perfective наплыть)

  1. to swim, to float (на (na) + accusative, into something/someone), to bump into while swimming or floating
  2. to move/sweep (на (na) + accusative, over something/someone, e.g. clouds over the sun)
  3. to accumulate while flowing, to flood (in some amount, often a lot; of liquid or a viscous material such as mud)
  4. to emanate (of sounds or smells)
  5. (intransitive) to materialize (of thoughts or feelings)
  6. to form a residue
