прикры́ться (13545)


Etymology

прикры́ть (prikrýtʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪˈkrɨt͡sːə]

Verb

прикры́ться • (prikrýtʹsja) pf (imperfective прикрыва́ться)

  1. to cover (oneself) (with)
  2. to use as a cover, to take refuge
  3. (colloquial) to close down
  4. passive of прикры́ть (prikrýtʹ)

Related terms

  • прикры́тие (prikrýtije)
