нету́ -> there isn’t/aren’t (= нет) (coll)

Etymology: From Old East Slavic нѣту́ (nětu) and нѣтъ (nětŭ). From older *не ѥ ту́ (it is not here), where ту́ (тут, also colloquial не́туть (nétutĭ)), was originally an ethical dative (-ть (-tĭ), dative -ти (-ti)).
