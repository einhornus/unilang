лак (6579)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lak]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from German Lack or Dutch lak or French laque, from Persian لاک‎ (lāk), from Hindi लाख (lākh), from Sanskrit लाक्षा (lākṣā).

Noun

лак • (lak) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ла́ка, nominative plural ла́ки, genitive plural ла́ков)

  1. varnish
  2. lacquer, lac, lake
  3. polish, nail polish
