заму́жний (17064)


Etymology

за́муж (zámuž) +‎ -ний (-nij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈmuʐnʲɪj]

Adjective

заму́жний • (zamúžnij)

  1. married (of women only)
    заму́жняя же́нщина ― zamúžnjaja žénščina ― a/the married woman

Usage notes

  • The masculine and neuter forms are only given for completeness.

Related terms

  • муж (muž, “husband”)
  • за́муж (zámuž, adverb) (of women; used in expressions about getting married)
  • за́мужем (zámužem, adverb) (of women; used predicatively or married women)
