секрета́рша -> (female) secretary (coll)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: секрета́рши
Genitive: секрета́рши



Etymology: From секрета́рь (sekretárʹ) +‎ -ша (-ša).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) secretary (female)
    • (colloquial) the wife of a secretary,
      • коллежская секрета́рша ― kolléžskaja sekretárša ― the wife of the collegiate secretary
