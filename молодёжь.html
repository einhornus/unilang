молодёжь -> young people
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жзнь)

Plural: молодёжи
Genitive: молодёжи



Etymology: From Old East Slavic молодёжь (molodežĭ), from Proto-Slavic *moldežь, from *moldъ (“young”) + *-ežь. Equivalent to молодо́й (molodój) +‎ -ёжь (-jóžʹ). Compare Polish młodzież.



Definitions (English):
  • the young, youth, young people (collective)
