спать -> to sleep
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b/c

Я сплю́
Ты спи́шь
Past: спа́л/спала́



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *sъpati, from Proto-Indo-European *swep- (“to sleep”), whence English soporific, hypnosis and somnolent.



Definitions (English):
  • to sleep,
    • ложи́ться спать ― ložítʹsja spatʹ ― to go to bed
  • (euphemistic) to have sex. Perfective:
    • переспать (perespátʹ) (also)
