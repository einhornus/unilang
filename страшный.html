стра́шный -> terrible, dreadful
Adjective



Etymology: From страх (strax) +‎ -ный (-nyj), from Proto-Slavic *straxъ, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *strākš-, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)treg-.



Definitions (English):
  • terrible, scary (+ instrumental or
    • в (v) + prepositional, in (e.g. one's rage))
  • ugly
