щади́ть -> to spare
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я щажу́

Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic щадѣти (ščaděti, “to keep, spare”), from Proto-Slavic *ščęditi, from Proto-Indo-European *skend-. See also ску́дный (skúdnyj).

    • щади́ть чьи-либо чу́вства ― ščadítʹ čʹí-libo čúvstva ― to spare somebody's feelings
