очнться -> to regain consciousness
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я очнсь
Ты очнёшься
Past: очнлся/очнлась



Etymology: From Old East Slavic ѡчхнꙋтисѧ (očxnutisę), from Proto-Slavic *otjьtnǫti sę.



Definitions (English):
  • to awaken
    • to come to, to gain consciousness
      • to come to one's senses
