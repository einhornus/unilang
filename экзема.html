экзе́ма (20562)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪɡˈzɛmə] (phonetic respelling: экзэма)

Noun

экзе́ма • (ekzɛma) f inan (genitive экзе́мы, nominative plural экзе́мы, genitive plural экзе́м)

  1. (pathology) eczema (acute or chronic inflammation of the skin)

Related terms
