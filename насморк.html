на́сморк -> cold (in the head)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: на́сморки
Genitive: на́сморка



Etymology: Deverbal of сморкать (smorkátʹ, “to blow one's nose”).



Definitions (English):
  • runny nose, rhinorrhea
