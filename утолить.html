утоли́ть (15262)


Etymology

From у- (u-) +‎ -толить (-tolítʹ), from Proto-Slavic *toliti (“to calm, to soothe”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊtɐˈlʲitʲ]

Verb

утоли́ть • (utolítʹ) pf (imperfective утоля́ть)

  1. to quench, to slake (thirst); to satisfy, to appease (hunger); to lull, to soothe, to alleviate (pain)

Synonyms

  • удовлетвори́ть (udovletvorítʹ)
