копьё -> spear
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: кпья
Genitive: копь



Etymology: From Old East Slavic копиѥ (kopije), from Proto-Slavic *kopьje (see details of derivation there), from *kopati, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *kop-, probably ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kep- (“to strike, beat”).



Definitions (English):
  • spear, lance, javelin,
    • метание копья ― metánije kopʹjá ― javelin throwing, би́ться на ко́пьях ― bítʹsja na kópʹjax ― to tilt, to joust
