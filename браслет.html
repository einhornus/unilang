брасле́т (6686)


Etymology

Borrowed from French bracelet.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brɐs⁽ʲ⁾ˈlʲet]

Noun

брасле́т • (braslét) masculine gender">m inan (genitive брасле́та, nominative plural брасле́ты, genitive plural брасле́тов)

  1. bracelet, bangle
