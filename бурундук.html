бурунду́к (26080)


Etymology

A languages">Turkic borrowing, to be compared with Tatar борындык (borındıq, “chipmunk”) or, less likely, a reshaping of Eastern Mari урымдо (úrymdo, “chipmunk”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʊrʊnˈduk]

Noun

бурунду́к • (burundúk) masculine gender">m anim (genitive бурундука́, nominative plural бурундуки́, genitive plural бурундуко́в)

  1. chipmunk
