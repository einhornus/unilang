растя́гивать (12810)


Etymology

растяг- (rastjag-) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈsʲtʲæɡʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

растя́гивать • (rastjágivatʹ) impf (perfective растяну́ть)

  1. to stretch (out)
    растя́гивать слова ― rastjágivatʹ slová ― to drawl
    растя́гивать удово́льствие ― rastjágivatʹ udovólʹstvije ― to prolong the pleasure
  2. to wear out, to strain (clothes)
  3. to strain, to sprain
  4. to stretch out, to spread out
  5. to protract, to prolong, to spin out, to drag out
