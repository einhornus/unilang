фа (30925)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fa]

Etymology 1

Noun

фа • (fa) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. (musical note) F
    фа диез ― fa dijéz/diéz ― F sharp
    фа бемоль ― fá bemólʹ ― F flat

Etymology 2

Abbreviation of фаши́ст (fašíst, “fascist”).

Noun

фа • (fa) masculine gender">m anim or f anim (indeclinable)

  1. (slang) fascist
Synonyms
