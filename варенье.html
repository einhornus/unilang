варе́нье -> jam, preserves
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: варе́нья
Genitive: варе́нья



Etymology: вари́ть (varítʹ) +‎ -енье (-énʹje)



Definitions (English):
  • jam, varenye, murabba (food usually not as thick as
    • джем, and with whole pieces of fruit or berry)
