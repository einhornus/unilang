папиро́ска (12140)


Etymology

папиро́са (papirósa) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəpʲɪˈroskə]

Noun

папиро́ска • (papiróska) f inan (genitive папиро́ски, nominative plural папиро́ски, genitive plural папиро́сок)

  1. Diminutive of папиро́са (papirósa): cigarette (strong Russian type made with dark tobacco, no filter)
