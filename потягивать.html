потя́гивать (13940)


Etymology

по- (po-) +‎ тяг- (tjag-) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈtʲæɡʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

потя́гивать • (potjágivatʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to sip
  2. (colloquial) to smoke, to pull (at), to draw (at)
