ссы́лка -> internal exile (inside Russia)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ссы́лки
Genitive: ссы́лки



Etymology: In the meaning "exile", from ссылать (ssylátʹ, “to exile”) +‎ -ка (-ka) in the meaning "reference", from ссылать(ся) (ssylátʹ(sja), “to refer to”) +‎ -ка (-ka) .



Definitions (English):
  • reference
    • (computing) link
      • (historical) exile, banishment (the state of being banished from one's home or country)
