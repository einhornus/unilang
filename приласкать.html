приласка́ть (20596)


Etymology

при- (pri-) +‎ ласка́ть (laskátʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪlɐˈskatʲ]

Verb

приласка́ть • (prilaskátʹ) pf (imperfective ласка́ть)

  1. to caress (touch, kiss or stroke lovingly)
  2. to fondle
  3. (colloquial, perfective only) to be nice to someone, to show a friendly disposition
