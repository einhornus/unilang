угоди́ть (4642)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *ugodìti; compare *godìti (“to please”). Synchronically analyzable as у- (u-) +‎ годить (godítʹ), but годить (godítʹ) no longer has its original meaning.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊɡɐˈdʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

угоди́ть • (ugodítʹ) pf (imperfective угожда́ть)

  1. to please, to oblige
    Imperfective: угожда́ть (ugoždátʹ)
  2. (colloquial) to get to (some place or situation, unexpectedly) (no imperfective)
  3. (colloquial) to hit (a target)
  4. (colloquial) to bump into/against
