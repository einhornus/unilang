угоди́ть -> to please, (coll) get into, end up (p only)
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Я угожу́
Ты угоди́шь
Past: угоди́л/угоди́ла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *ugodìti compare *godìti (“to please”). Synchronically analyzable as у- (u-) +‎ годить (godítʹ), but годить (godítʹ) no longer has its original meaning.



Definitions (English):
  • to please, to oblige. Imperfective:
    • угожда́ть (ugoždátʹ)
  • (colloquial) to get to (some place or situation, unexpectedly) (no imperfective)
    • (colloquial) to hit (a target)
      • (colloquial) to bump into/against
