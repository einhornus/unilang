впи́ться (8057)


Etymology

в- (v-) +‎ пить (pitʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈf⁽ʲ⁾pʲit͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

впи́ться • (vpítʹsja) pf (imperfective впива́ться)

  1. to stick (intó), to pierce, to bite (intó), to drive one's sting (intó), to dig one's fangs (intó)
    впи́ться зуба́ми ― vpítʹsja zubámi ― to dig one's teeth (intó)
    впи́ться когтя́ми ― vpítʹsja kogtjámi ― to dig one's claws (intó)
  2. to fix one's eyes (on), to fix with one's eyes, to fasten one's eyes (upoń), to glue one's eyes (to)
    он впился в неё глаза́ми ― on vpílsja v nejó glazámi ― he couldn't take his eyes off her
