ды́бом (14614)


Etymology

Apparently the instrumental case of a no-longer-existent word; compare на ды́бы (na dybý, “on its hind legs (of a horse)”), and see -ом (-om).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈdɨbəm]

Adverb

ды́бом • (dýbom)

  1. (standing) on end
    у него́ во́лосы вста́ли ды́бом ― u nevó vólosy vstáli dýbom ― his hair stood on end

Related terms

  • ды́бы (dybý)
