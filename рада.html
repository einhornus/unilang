ра́да (1370)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈradə]

Etymology 1

From Old East Slavic ра́да (rada), from Middle High German">Middle High German rât via Polish language">Polish rada, Czech rada, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *rēdaz.

Noun

ра́да • (ráda) f inan (genitive ра́ды, nominative plural ра́ды, genitive plural ра́д)

  1. rada, Rada (a parliamentary body in a number of Slavic countries)

Etymology 2

Adjective

ра́да • (ráda)

  1. feminine singular of ра́д (rad)

Noun
