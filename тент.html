тент (16726)


Etymology

Borrowed from English tent.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɛnt] (phonetic respelling: тэнт)
  • Rhymes: -ent

Noun

тент • (tɛnt) m inan (genitive те́нта, nominative plural те́нты, genitive plural те́нтов)

  1. awning, canopy, tent, baldachin (a rooflike cover)
