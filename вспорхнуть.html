вспорхнуть (21405)


Etymology

вс- (vs-) +‎ порхнуть (porxnútʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fspɐrxˈnutʲ]

Verb

вспорхнуть • (vsporxnútʹ) pf (imperfective вспархивать)

  1. to flutter out or up, to flit out or up (of birds, butterflies, snow, sheets of paper, etc.)
  2. (colloquial) to bound up, to quickly and easily jump up

Related terms

  • порха́ть impf (porxátʹ), порхнуть pf (porxnútʹ)
