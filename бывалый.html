быва́лый -> experienced
Adjective



Etymology: Perhaps derived from the past tense of быва́ть (byvátʹ, “to take place, to happen, to be (regularly)”).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) worldly-wise, experienced,
    • быва́лый солда́т ― byvályj soldát ― veteran
  • (colloquial) habitual, familiar,
    • э́то дело быва́лое ― éto délo byváloje ― this is nothing new
