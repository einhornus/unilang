мрамор -> marble
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: мраморы
Genitive: мрамора



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic мраморъ (mramorŭ), from Proto-Slavic *mormorъ, a borrowing from Latin marmor. Displaced the inherited Old East Slavic мороморъ (moromorŭ).
