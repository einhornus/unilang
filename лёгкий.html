лёгкий -> light, easy
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *lьgъkъ, from pre-Slavic *h₁ln̥gʷʰ-u-ko, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁lengʷʰ-.



Definitions (English):
  • light, lightweight
    • easy, facile, slight
      • (colloquial) lucky


        Definitions (Russian):
