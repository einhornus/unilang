север -> north
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: сверы
Genitive: свера



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *sěverъ, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱeh₁wero. Cognate with Lithuanian šiaurė, Latin caurus and English shower.



Definitions (English):

      • Arctic (northern parts of Russia)
        • Кра́йний Север ― Krájnij Séver ― Far North ( Russian territories that lie to the north of the Arctic circle)
