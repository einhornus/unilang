галифе́ (10672)


Etymology

From the name of French <small>[Term?] general Gaston de Galliffet, who purportedly invented breeches of such design.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡəlʲɪˈfɛ] (phonetic respelling: галифэ)

Noun

галифе́ • (galifɛ) n inan pl (indeclinable)

  1. (military) flared breeches

Synonyms

  • брюки-галифе (brjúki-galifɛ)
