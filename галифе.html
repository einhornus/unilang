галифе́ (10674)


Etymology

From the name of French [Term?] general Gaston de Galliffet, who purportedly invented breeches of such design.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡəlʲɪˈfɛ] (phonetic respelling: галифэ́)

Noun

галифе́ • (galifɛ́) n inan pl (indeclinable)

  1. (military) flared breeches

Synonyms

  • брю́ки-галифе́ (brjúki-galifɛ́)
