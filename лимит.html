лими́т (16694)


Etymology

Borrowed from French limite.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈmʲit]

Noun

лими́т • (limít) m inan (genitive лими́та, nominative plural лими́ты, genitive plural лими́тов)

  1. limit, quota

Related terms

  • лимитированный (limitirovannyj)
  • лимитировать (limitirovatʹ)
