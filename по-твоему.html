по-твоему -> in your opinion, as you want it

Etymology: From по (po) +‎ твоему́́ (tvojemú), with stress shift.



Definitions (English):
  • in [according to] your opinion, to your way of thinking, (in a question) do you think ...?
    • по-тво́ему́, я не име́ю на э́то права? ― po-tvójemu, ja ne iméju na éto prava? ― do you think I have no right to this?
  • as you wish, as you would have it, as you advise,
    • будь (пусть бу́дет) по-тво́ему́ ― budʹ (pustʹ búdet) po-tvójemu ― have it your own way let it be as you wish
