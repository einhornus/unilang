тру́бный (22306)


Etymology

труба́ (trubá) + -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtrubnɨj]

Adjective

тру́бный • (trúbnyj)

  1. (relational) trumpet
  2. (relational) pipe
  3. (figuratively) loud, booming
