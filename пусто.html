пу́сто (4224)


Etymology

пустой (pustój) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpustə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

пу́сто • (pústo) (comparative (по)пустее or (по)пусте́й)

  1. in an empty manner, emptily
  2. in an idle manner
  3. in a shallow or vain manner (of one's personality)
  4. futilely

Adjective

пу́сто • (pústo)

  1. short neuter singular of пустой (pustój)

Related terms

  • пустой (pustój), пустота́ (pustotá)
