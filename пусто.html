пу́сто -> (it’s) empty, emptily

Etymology: пусто́й (pustój) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • in an empty manner, emptily
    • in an idle manner
      • in a shallow or vain manner (of one's personality)
        • futilely
          • Predicative
            • пу́сто
              • pústo
                • it is empty, it is deserted.
                  • В ко́мнате бы́ло пу́сто. ― V kómnate býlo pústo. ― The room was empty.
                • one is lonely.
                  • Мне пу́сто. ― Mne pústo. ― I am lonely.
                • short neuter singular of пусто́й (pustój)


                  Definitions (Russian):
