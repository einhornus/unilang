исхо́д (3811)


Etymology

Deverbal of исходи́ть (isxodítʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪˈsxot]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ot

Noun

исхо́д • (isxód) m inan (genitive исхо́да, nominative plural исхо́ды, genitive plural исхо́дов)

  1. outcome, issue
  2. Exodus
  3. outlet
  4. way out from situation
