со (84)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Preposition

со • (so)

  1. with (+ instrumental case)
  2. off, from (+ genitive case)

Usage notes

This is a variant of с (s) beforé words beginning with certain awkward consonant clusters such as мн- (mn-), вс- (vs-), and сн- (sn-).
