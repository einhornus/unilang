отек (7536)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈtʲɵk]

Etymology 1

Noun

отёк • (otjók) m inan (genitive отёка, nominative plural отёки, genitive plural отёков)

  1. edema, dropsy, hydrops, hypostasis

Etymology 2

Verb

отёк • (otjók)

  1. masculine singular past indicative perfective of оте́чь (otéčʹ)
