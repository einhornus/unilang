опеча́тка (24637)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐpʲɪˈt͡ɕatkə]

Noun

опеча́тка • (opečátka) f inan (genitive опеча́тки, nominative plural опеча́тки, genitive plural опеча́ток)

  1. typó, misprint (a spelling or typographical error)

Synonyms

  • очепятка (očepjátka) (jocular, imitating a funny sounding typó in "опеча́тка")
