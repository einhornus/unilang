клие́нт (2399)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Klient.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [klʲɪˈjent]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

клие́нт • (klijént) masculine gender">m anim (genitive клие́нта, nominative plural клие́нты, genitive plural клие́нтов)

  1. client, customer
  2. (criminal, slang) target of a killing, target of extortion

Related terms

  • клие́нтка (klijentka)
  • клиентский (klijéntskij)
  • клиенту́ра (klijentúra)
