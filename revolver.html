revolver (11858)


Etymology

revolve +‎ -er

Pronunciation

  • (Us) IPA(key): /ɹɪˈvɒl.vɚ/

Noun

revolver (plural revolvers)

A revolver
  1. A handgun with a revolving chamber enabling several shots to be fired without́ reloading.
  2. (by extension) Any (personal) firearm with such a mechanism.
  3. (financé) Synonym of revolving line of credit
  4. Agent noun of revolve; something that revolves.

Synonyms

  • (handgun): wheelgun
