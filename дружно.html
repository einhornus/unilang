дру́жно (4285)


Etymology

дру́жный (drúžnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈdruʐnə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

дру́жно • (drúžno) (comparative (по)дружнее or (по)дружней)

  1. amicably, in a friendly manner
  2. simultaneously, hand in hand, (everybody) together, in a concerted effort

Adjective

дру́жно • (drúžno)

  1. short neuter singular of дру́жный (drúžnyj)
