пло́щадь -> square, area
Noun, declension 3e (pattern ночь)

Plural: пло́щади
Genitive: пло́щади



Etymology: From Old East Slavic пло́щадь (ploščadĭ), from Proto-Slavic *ploščadь, from *ploskъ (“flat”) + *-ědь.



Definitions (English):
  • square (open area in a town or city)
    • Кра́сная пло́щадь ― Krásnaja plóščadʹ ― Red Square
  • space, living space
    • (geometry) area
