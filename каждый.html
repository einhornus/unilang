ка́ждый (114)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic къжьдо (kŭžĭdo), from Proto-Slavic *kъžьdo

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkaʐdɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

ка́ждый • (káždyj)

  1. every, each
  2. either (of two)
  3. everybody, everyone
