Verb[edit]

засты́ть • (zastýtʹ) pf (imperfective застыва́ть or стыть)

  1. to thicken, to harden, to solidify
  2. to cool down
  3. (colloquial) to freeze, to feel cold; to catch cold
  4. to stiffen, to freeze, to be / become / stand stockstill
