брусни́ка (19579)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *brusьnica, from *brusьnъ + *-ica. Cognate with Czech brusnice, Slovak brusnica, Polish language">Polish brusznica, Serbo-Croatian brùsnica, Slovene brusníca, Bulgarian брусница (brusníca), and more distantly with Lithuanian brùknė and Latvian brūklene.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brʊsʲˈnʲikə]

Noun

брусни́ка • (brusníka) f inan (genitive брусни́ки, nominative plural брусни́ки, genitive plural брусни́к)

  1. cowberry, red whortleberry, lingonberry
