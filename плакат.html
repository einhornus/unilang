плака́т -> poster
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: плака́ты
Genitive: плака́та



Etymology: Borrowed in 1704 via German Plakat from French placard.



Definitions (English):
  • poster, placard, bill
    • broadsheet
