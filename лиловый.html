лилвый -> violet, lilac
Adjective



Etymology: Shaped after German lila (“lilac-coloured”) or French lilas (“lilac plant and colour”) + -овый (-ovyj), from Arabic لِيلَك‎ (līlak, “lilac”).



Definitions (English):
  • (relational) lilac, violet,
    • лило́вые цветы́ ― lilóvyje cvetý ― lilac flowers
  • violet, purple
