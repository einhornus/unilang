шиш (12157)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂɨʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

шиш • (šiš) m inan (genitive шиша́, nominative plural шиши́, genitive plural шише́й)

  1. rude gesture in which the hand makes a fist and the thumb is stuck betweeń the index and middle fingers, which indicates "nothing for you, nuts to you"

Usage notes

  • Not the samé vulgar meaning that this gesture has in Western European countries such as Germany.

Synonyms

  • фи́га (fíga), ду́ля (dúlja), ку́киш (kúkiš)
