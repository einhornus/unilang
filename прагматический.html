прагмати́ческий (32415)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from French pragmat(ique) +‎ -ический (-íčeskij).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [prəɡmɐˈtʲit͡ɕɪskʲɪj]

Adjective[edit]

прагмати́ческий • (pragmatíčeskij)

Related terms[edit]
