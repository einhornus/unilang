зе́ркало -> mirror
Noun, declension 2c
Plural: зеркала
Genitive: зеркала



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *zьrkadlo, from *zьrěti (whence Old Church Slavonic зьрѣти (zĭrěti, “to look at”)) + a suffix originating in Proto-Indo-European *-tlom. Probably a calque of Latin speculum.



Definitions (English):
  • mirror, looking glass
