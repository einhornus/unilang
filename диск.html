диск -> disk, discus
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: диски
Genitive: диска



Etymology: Borrowed from Ancient Greek δίσκος (dískos, “a circular plate suited for hurling”), from δικείν (dikeín, “to hurl, to launch”).



Definitions (English):
  • disk, disc
    • (sports) discus, platter
      • cartridge-drum
        • (informal) CD, compact disc
