семиле́тка (26965)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪmʲɪˈlʲetkə]

Noun

семиле́тка • (semilétka) f inan or f anim (genitive семиле́тки, nominative plural семиле́тки, genitive plural семиле́ток)

  1. seven-year period
  2. seven-year plan
  3. seven-year school, grade school (ages 7–13)
  4. seven-year-old child, child of seven

Related terms

  • семь (semʹ), ле́то (léto)
