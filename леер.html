ле́ер (31949)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch leier, from leiden (“to lead”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲe(j)ɪr]

Noun

ле́ер • (léjer) m inan (genitive ле́ера, nominative plural леера́, genitive plural лееро́в)

  1. (nautical) jackstay (taut wire, both ends of which are fixed to the ship structures)
