долгосрочный -> long-term
Adjective



Etymology: From до́лгий (dólgij) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ срок (srok) +‎ -ный (-nyj). Probably a calque of German langfristig.



Definitions (English):
  • long-dated, long-range, long-term
