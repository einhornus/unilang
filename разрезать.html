разреза́ть (7068)


Etymology 1

раз- (raz-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐzˈrʲezətʲ]

Verb

разреза́ть • (razrézatʹ) pf (imperfective разреза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to bisect

Etymology 2

разреза́ть (razrézatʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rəzrʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

разреза́ть • (razrezátʹ) impf (perfective разреза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to cut off, to bisect
