орех -> nut
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: орехи
Genitive: ореха



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *orěxъ. Compare Albanian arrë (“walnut”), Ancient Greek ἄρυα (árua, “walnut”), Latvian riẽksts, Serbo-Croatian òrah.
