отчуди́ть (18662)


Etymology

Synchronically, from от- (ot-) +‎ чуд- (čud-) +‎ -ить (-ítʹ). The middle part is cognate with чужо́й (čužój, “another's, alien”), but lacking the Early Proto-Slavic *-jь suffix, suggesting that the term originated in Early Proto Slavic or beforé.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐt͡ɕːʉˈdʲitʲ]

Verb

отчуди́ть • (otčudítʹ) pf (imperfective отчуждать)

  1. to alienate, to expropriate
  2. to estrange
  3. (colloquial, perfective only) to do something very strange
