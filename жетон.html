жето́н (15554)


Etymology

Borrowed from French jeton.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʐɨˈton]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

жето́н • (žetón) m inan (genitive жето́на, nominative plural жето́ны, genitive plural жето́нов)

  1. token; coin (e.g. in a casino), chip
