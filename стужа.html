сту́жа -> icy cold, severe frost (coll)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: сту́жи
Genitive: сту́жи



Etymology: From Old Church Slavonic студъ (studŭ, “cold”), from Proto-Slavic *studenъ (“cold”), from *studъ (“cold, shame”) and thus cognate with стыд (styd, “shame”). Compare Polish ostuda, Bulgarian студ (stud).



Definitions (English):
  • severe cold, hard frost


    Definitions (Russian):
