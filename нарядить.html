Verb[edit]

наряди́ть • (narjadítʹ) pf (imperfective наряжать)

  1. to dress up, to dress out, to apparel (to dress or clothe)
  2. to adorn, to decorate

Verb[edit]

наряди́ть • (narjadítʹ) pf (imperfective наряжать)

  1. (colloquial) to dispatch (someone) on an assignment
  2. (dated) to establish, to organize (e.g. an investigation)

Related terms[edit]

  • наря́д (narjád)
  • наря́дный (narjádnyj)
  • ряди́ть (rjadítʹ)
