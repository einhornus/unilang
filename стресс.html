стресс (8590)


Etymology

Borrowed from English stress.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [strɛs] (phonetic respelling: стрэсс)
  • IPA(key): [strʲes] (less-standard)
  • Rhymes: -es

Noun

стресс • (strɛss, stress) m inan (genitive стре́сса, nominative plural стре́ссы, genitive plural стре́ссов, related adjective стре́ссовый)

  1. stress, strain (emotional pressure)

Related terms

  • стрессовость (strɛssovostʹ, stréssovostʹ)
  • стрессово (strɛssovo, stréssovo)
