пло́тный -> compact, solid
Adjective



Etymology: Per Vasmer, probably inherited from Proto-Slavic *plъtьnъ, from Proto-Slavic *plъtь (whence Russian плоть (plotʹ, “flesh”)) compare Slovene póltǝn (“carnal, corporeal”) (tonal orthography) synchronically analyzable as плоть (plotʹ) +‎ -ный (-nyj).



Definitions (English):
  • dense
    • compact, solid
      • thickset
        • close, thick
