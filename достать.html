достать -> to get, obtain, take out, reach
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я достану
Ты достанешь
Past: достал/достала



Etymology: до- (do-) +‎ стать (statʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to reach, to touch.
    • Он ма́ленького ро́ста, не доста́нет до ве́рхней по́лки. On málenʹkovo rósta, ne dostánet do vérxnej pólki. He is of short stature and won't reach the top shelf.
  • to take, to go fetch, to take out.
    • Он доста́л из карма́на сига́ре́ты и закури́́л. On dostál iz karmána sigaréty i zakuríl. He took some cigarettes out of his pocket and started smoking.
  • to get, to procure, to obtain.
    • Где ты доста́ла э́ту́ кни́гу? (to a female) Gde ty dostála étu knígu? Where did you get (obtain) this book from?
  • (colloquial) to annoy someone badly.
    • Он меня́ доста́л со свои́́ми вопро́са́ми. On menjá dostál so svoími voprósami. He's a real pest with his questions.
  • (dated) to suffice
