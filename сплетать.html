сплета́ть (32273)


Etymology[edit]

сплет- (splet-) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [splʲɪˈtatʲ]

Verb[edit]

сплета́ть • (spletátʹ) impf (perfective сплести́)

  1. to weave (to create by weaving)
  2. (figuratively) to create (something complicated or confusing)
  3. to weave together, to interweave, to plait, to braid, to interlace
  4. (figuratively) to connect, to interlock
  5. (figuratively, colloquial) to invent, to compose

Related terms[edit]

  • сплете́ние (spleténije)
  • спле́тник (splétnik), сплетница (splétnica)
  • спле́тня (splétnja), спле́тничать (splétničatʹ)
  • плести́ impf (plestí), сплести́ pf (splestí)
  • плетёный (pletjónyj)
  • плетение (pleténije)
  • плеть (pletʹ)
  • плётка (pljótka)
  • плетёнка (pletjónka)
