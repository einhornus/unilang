моде́м (14385)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English modem.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈdɛm] (phonetic respelling: модэм)

Noun[edit]

моде́м • (modɛm) m inan (genitive моде́ма, nominative plural моде́мы, genitive plural моде́мов)

  1. (computing) modem
