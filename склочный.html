скло́чный (26615)


Etymology

From скло́ка (sklóka) +‎ -ный (-nyj).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsklot͡ɕnɨj]

Adjective

скло́чный • (sklóčnyj)

  1. (colloquial) quarrelsome, squabblesome, prone to fights, intrigues or gossip
