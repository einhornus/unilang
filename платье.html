пла́тье -> dress
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: пла́тья
Genitive: пла́тья



Etymology: From Old East Slavic *odeďa (*odeďa). See also Ukrainian плаття (pláttja), Belarusian плацце (pláccje) and Old Polish рłасiе.



Definitions (English):
  • (historical) (archaic) clothes, clothing
    • dress, gown, frock
