тут (81)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tut]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ut

Etymology 1

From Old East Slavic туто (tuto), тутъ (tutŭ, “here; thereto; herewith”), ту́ (tu, “there; thereto; then; here, herewith”), from Proto-Slavic *tuto, *tu

Adverb

тут • (tut)

  1. (location) here
  2. (time) then, at this point
    тут-же ― tút-že ― then and there
Synonyms
  • здесь (zdesʹ)

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Ottoman Turkish language">Ottoman Turkish طوت‎/توت‎ (tut), from Persian توت‎ (tut), related to Aramaic arc">תותא‎ /‎ ܬܘܬܐ‎ (tūṯā), Arabic تُوت‎ (tūt), Hebrew תּוּת‎ (tut).

Noun

тут • (tut) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ту́та, nominative plural ту́ты, genitive plural ту́тов)

  1. mulberry (fruit or tree)
    Synonyms: тутовое дерево (tutovoje dérevo), тутовник (tutovnik), тутовница (tutovnica), шелкови́ца (šelkovica)
Related terms
  • тутовник (tutovnik), тутовница (tutovnica)
  • тутовый (tutovyj)

Etymology 3

Noun

тут • (tut) f inan plural number">pl
