Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Finno-Ugric *perä. Cognates include Erzya пря (prja), Finnish perä, Komi-Zyrian бӧр (bör), Udmurt бер (ber), Khanty [script needed] (pĕrtäγ), Mansi [script needed] (pär).[1]
