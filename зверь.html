зверь -> wild animal
Noun, declension 2e
Plural: звери
Genitive: зверя



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *zvěrь. Cognate with Ancient Greek θήρ (thḗr) and Latin ferus.



Definitions (English):
  • animal, beast
    • (figuratively) brute
      • (sciences) mammal
