брани́ть (20210)


Etymology

брань (branʹ) +‎ -ить (-ítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brɐˈnʲitʲ]

Verb

брани́ть • (branítʹ) impf

  1. to scold
  2. to abuse, to curse
  3. (colloquial) to call names

Related terms

  • бра́нный (bránnyj)
