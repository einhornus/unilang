папа́ха (12355)


Etymology

Of Turkic origin: compare Ottoman Turkish پاپاق‎ (papak), Azerbaijani papaq.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈpaxə]

Noun

папа́ха • (papáxa) f inan (genitive папа́хи, nominative plural папа́хи, genitive plural папах)

  1. papakha, calpac
