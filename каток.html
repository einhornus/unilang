каток -> skating-rink, roller
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: катки
Genitive: катка



Etymology: From ката́ться (katátʹsja, “to skate”) and ката́ть (katátʹ, “to roll”).



Definitions (English):
  • skating rink, ice rink
    • steamroller, road roller, roller (compactor-type engineering vehicle)
      • mangle
