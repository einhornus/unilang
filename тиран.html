тиран (10497)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Tyrann, from Latin tyrannus, from Ancient Greek τύραννος (túrannos).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲɪˈran]

Noun

тиран • (tirán) masculine gender">m anim (genitive тира́на, nominative plural тира́ны, genitive plural тира́нов)

  1. tyrant (also figurative)
  2. bully

Synonyms

  • де́спот masculine gender">m (déspot)
  • сатра́п masculine gender">m (satráp)
