rob (3652)


Etymology

From West Slavic dialects, from Proto-Slavic *orbъ (“slave”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₃órbʰos (“orphan”). Compare English robot and Russian рабо́та (rabóta).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /rôb/

Noun

rȍb m (Cyrillic spelling ро̏б)

  1. slave
