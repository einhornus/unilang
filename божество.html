божество́ (15563)


Etymology

бо́жий (bóžij, “divine”) +‎ -ество (-estvó)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bəʐɨstˈvo]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

божество́ • (božestvó) n anim (genitive божества́, nominative plural божества́, genitive plural боже́ств)

  1. deity
  2. divinity, divine being, godhead
  3. idol

Related terms

  • бог (box)
  • бо́жий (bóžij)
