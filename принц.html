принц -> prince
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: при́нцы
Genitive: при́нца



Etymology: Borrowed from German Prinz, from French prince, from Latin princeps (“first head”), from primus (“first”) + capio (“I take”), literally, "one who takes the first part".



Definitions (English):
  • prince, crown prince (male ruler or head of a principality)
