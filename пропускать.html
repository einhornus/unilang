пропуска́ть (4170)


Etymology

про- (pro-) +‎ пуска́ть (puskátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəpʊˈskatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

пропуска́ть • (propuskátʹ) impf (perfective пропусти́ть)

  1. to let pass in, to let pass through́, to admit
  2. to run through́, to pass through́
  3. to omit, to leave out
  4. to miss, to fail to attend, to let slip

Related terms

  • про́пуск (própusk)
