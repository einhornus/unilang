загоре́лый (5404)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəɡɐˈrʲelɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

загоре́лый • (zagorélyj)

  1. sunburnt, tanned; (strongly) brown, bronzed

Usage notes

The singular short forms are usually avoided because they coincide with the past finite forms of загоре́ть (zagorétʹ).

Related terms

  • загора́ть impf (zagorátʹ), загоре́ть pf (zagorétʹ)
