боб (6077)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [bop]

Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *bobъ, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰabʰ-.

Noun[edit]

боб • (bob) m inan (genitive боба́, nominative plural бобы́, genitive plural бобо́в)

  1. bean, broad bean
Related terms[edit]
  • бобо́вый (bobóvyj)

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

боб • (bob) m inan (genitive боба́, nominative plural бобы́, genitive plural бобо́в)

  1. (informal) bobsled
