боб (6079)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bop]

Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *bobъ, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰabʰ-.

Noun

боб • (bob) m inan (genitive боба́, nominative plural бобы́, genitive plural бобо́в)

  1. bean, broad bean
Related terms
  • бобо́вый (bobóvyj)

Etymology 2

Noun

боб • (bob) m inan (genitive бо́ба, nominative plural бо́бы, genitive plural бо́бов)

  1. (informal) bobsled
