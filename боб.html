боб (6077)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bop]

Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *bobъ, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰabʰ-.

Noun

боб • (bob) masculine gender">m inan (genitive боба, nominative plural бобы, genitive plural бобов)

  1. bean, broad bean
Related terms
  • бобо́вый (bobóvyj)

Etymology 2

Noun

боб • (bob) masculine gender">m inan (genitive боба, nominative plural бобы, genitive plural бобов)

  1. (informal) bobsled
