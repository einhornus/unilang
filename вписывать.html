впи́сывать (24997)


Etymology

вписа́ть (vpisátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈf⁽ʲ⁾pʲisɨvətʲ]

Verb

впи́сывать • (vpísyvatʹ) impf (perfective вписа́ть)

  1. to enter, to insert́ (something to the text that is already written by writing)
  2. to record (something in a diary or a journal)
  3. to inscribe

Related terms

  • пи́сать impf (pisátʹ)
