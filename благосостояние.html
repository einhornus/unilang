благосостоние -> well-being, prosperity
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лто)

Plural: благосостония
Genitive: благосостония



Etymology: бла́го (blágo) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ состоя́ние (sostojánije)



Definitions (English):
  • well-being, prosperity, welfare (state of health, happiness and/or prosperity)
