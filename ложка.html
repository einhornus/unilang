ло́жка -> spoon
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ло́жки
Genitive: ло́жки



Etymology: From Old East Slavic лъжька (lŭžĭka), from Proto-Slavic *lъžьka, from *lъga + *-ьka.
