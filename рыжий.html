рыжий (10352)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic рꙑжии (ryžii), from Proto-Slavic *ryďь, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rūdʰ-yo-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈrɨʐɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

рыжий • (rýžij) (comparative (по)ры́жее or (по)ры́жей)

  1. red, sorrel
  2. (of hair) red, ginger
  3. (of a person or other thing that has hair) red-haired, red-headed, ginger

Related terms

► Russian terms derived from the Pie root *h₁rewdʰ-‎ (0 c, 8 e)

Noun

рыжий • (rýžij) m anim (genitive рыжего, nominative plural рыжие, genitive plural ры́жих, feminine рыжая)

  1. circus clown
