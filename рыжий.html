ры́жий -> red-haired
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic рꙑжии (ryžii), from Proto-Slavic *ryďь, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rūdʰ-yo-.



Definitions (English):
  • red, sorrel
    • (of hair) red, ginger
      • (of a person or other thing that has hair) red-haired, red-headed, ginger
        • circus clown
