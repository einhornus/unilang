ве́рмут (27248)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Wermut.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʲermʊt]

Noun

ве́рмут • (vérmut) m inan (genitive ве́рмута, nominative plural ве́рмуты, genitive plural ве́рмутов)

  1. vermouth
