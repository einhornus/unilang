скорлупа́ -> shell
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: скорлупы
Genitive: скорлупы



Etymology: From Old East Slavic скорлупа́ (skorlupa), from Proto-Slavic *skorupa, from *skora (“bast, skin”) + *-upa.



Definitions (English):
  • shell (of an egg), hull
