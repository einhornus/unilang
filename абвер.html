абвер (27727)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German Abwehr.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈabvɨr] (phonetic respelling: абвэр)
  • IPA(key): [ˈab⁽ʲ⁾vʲɪr]

Noun[edit]

абвер • (ábvɛr, ábver) m inan (genitive а́бвера, uncountable)

  1. (uncountable) Abwehr (a German intelligence organization from 1921 to 1944)
