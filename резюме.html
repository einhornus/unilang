резюме́ (16372)


Etymology

Borrowed from French résumé.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪzʲʊˈmɛ] (phonetic respelling: резюмэ)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

резюме́ • (rezjumɛ) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. summary
  2. curriculum vitae, CV, resume
