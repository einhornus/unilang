увести́ (4128)


Etymology

у- (u-) +‎ вести (vestí)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊvʲɪˈsʲtʲi]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Homophones: увезти́ (uveztí)

Verb

увести́ • (uvestí) pf (imperfective уводи́ть)

  1. to take awaý, to lead awaý, to withdraw (troops)
  2. to carry off, to lift
Related terms
  • увод m (uvód)
