пласт -> layer, stratum
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: пласты́
Genitive: пласта́



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic пластъ (plastŭ, “thin layer”), from Proto-Slavic *plastъ.



Definitions (English):
  • layer, sheet, stratum
    • bed (a deposit of ore, coal etc.)
