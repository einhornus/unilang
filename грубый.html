грбый -> coarse, rude
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *grǫbъ.Cognates include German grob, Lithuanian grubùs.



Definitions (English):
  • coarse, rough, rude
    • gross
      • crude
        • harsh, rough, gruff,
          • гру́бое сло́во ― grúboje slóvo ― harsh word
