сма́зать (8267)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ мазать (mázatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsmazətʲ]

Verb

сма́зать • (smázatʹ) pf (imperfective сма́зывать)

  1. to smear, to grease, to oil, to lubricate, to dub
    сма́зать йо́дом ― smázatʹ jódom ― to paint with iodine
  2. to smear, to wipe off
  3. (colloquial) to grease someone's palm
  4. (a question, a problem) to slur
    сма́зать вопро́с ― smázatʹ voprós ― to slur over a question
  5. (photography) to blur
  6. (colloquial) to bash, to dot

Related terms

  • мазать (mázatʹ)
  • сма́зка (smázka)
  • сма́зывание n (smázyvanije)
