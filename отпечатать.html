Verb[edit]

отпеча́тать • (otpečátatʹ) pf (imperfective отпечатывать)

  1. to print, to type
    отпеча́тать весь тира́ж кни́ги ― otpečátatʹ vesʹ tiráž knígi ― to print the whole edition of the book
  2. to imprint
  3. to unseal, to open up
  4. (colloquial) to say brusquely/resolutely/emphatically
    "не пойду́", - отпеча́тала она ― "ne pojdú", - otpečátala oná ― "I will not go," she said with emphasis
