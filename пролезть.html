проле́зть (9788)


Etymology

про- (pro-) +‎ лезть (leztʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈlʲesʲtʲ]

Verb

проле́зть • (proléztʹ) pf (imperfective пролеза́ть)

  1. to get through́, to wriggle through́, to climb through́, to squeeze through́
  2. to go (through́)
    шкаф не проле́зет в дверь ― škaf ne prolézet v dverʹ ― the wardrobe won't go through́ the door
  3. (colloquial) to worm oneself (intó)

Related terms

  • лезть impf (leztʹ), поле́зть pf (poléztʹ)
