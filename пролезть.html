проле́зть -> to climb through, go through
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я проле́зу
Ты проле́зешь
Past: проле́з/проле́зла



Etymology: про- (pro-) +‎ лезть (leztʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to get through, to wriggle through, to climb through, to squeeze through
    • to go (through)
      • шкаф не проле́зет в дверь ― škaf ne prolézet v dverʹ ― the wardrobe won't go through the door
    • (colloquial) to worm oneself (into)
