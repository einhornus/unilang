ма́лый -> small
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic малъ (malŭ), from Proto-Slavic *malъ, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)mal-, *(s)mel-, whence English small. Cognate to Ukrainian малий (malyj).



Definitions (English):
  • small, little
    • short
      • (short form only) too small smaller than necessary.
        • Э́ти сапоги́ мне малы́.Éti sapogí mne malý. These boots are too small for me.
