ша́вка (25603)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʂafkə]

Noun

ша́вка • (šávka) m anim or f anim (genitive ша́вки, nominative plural ша́вки, genitive plural шавок)

  1. (colloquial) small dog
  2. (figuratively, abuse) cur, insignificant person
