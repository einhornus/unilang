вы́говорить (5587)


Etymology

вы́- (vý-) + говори́ть (govorítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨɡəvərʲɪtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

вы́говорить • (výgovoritʹ) pf (imperfective выгова́ривать)

  1. to articulate, to pronounce, to utter
  2. (colloquial) to reserve for oneself, to stipulate, to set as a condition (in a contract, transaction, etc.)
  3. (colloquial) to censure, to reprimand, to rebuke
