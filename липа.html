ли́па (7204)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *lipa.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲipə]
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

ли́па • (lípa) f inan (genitive ли́пы, nominative plural ли́пы, genitive plural ли́п)

  1. lime tree, linden (tree, wood, or flower)
  2. forgery, fake

Related terms

  • ли́повый (lípovyj)
