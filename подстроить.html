подстро́ить (18412)


Etymology

под- (pod-) +‎ строи́ть (stróitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐt͡sˈstroɪtʲ]

Verb

подстро́ить • (podstróitʹ) pf (imperfective подстраивать)

  1. to build (to), to add (to)
  2. to tune (up)
  3. to bring about by secret plotting, to contrive, to plot, to cook up
