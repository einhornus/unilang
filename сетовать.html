се́товать (15784)


Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic сѣтовати (sětovati).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲetəvətʲ]

Verb[edit]

се́товать • (sétovatʹ) impf (perfective посе́товать)

  1. to lament, to complain (of)
