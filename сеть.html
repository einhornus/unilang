сеть -> net, network
Noun, declension 3e (pattern ночь)

Plural: се́ти
Genitive: се́ти



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *sětь (“net, snare”). Cognate with Upper Sorbian syć, Polish sieć, Czech síť, Old Church Slavonic сѣть (sětĭ), Lower Sorbian seś.



Definitions (English):
  • net (used for catching fish), mesh
    • grid
      • network, system
