polyester (0)


Etymology

poly- +‎ ester

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɛstə(ɹ)

Noun

polyester (countable and uncountable, plural polyesters)

  1. Any polymer whose monomers are linked together by ester bonds
  2. A material or fabric made from polyester polymer

Adjective

polyester (not comparable)

  1. Of, or consisting of polyesters
