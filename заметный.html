заметный -> noticeable
Adjective



Etymology: заме́тить (zamétitʹ) +‎ -ный (-nyj)



Definitions (English):
  • noticeable, perceptible, visible (capable of being seen or noticed)
    • marked, conspicuous
      • outstanding
