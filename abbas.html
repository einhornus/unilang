abbas (13489)


Etymology

From Ancient Greek ἀββᾶς (abbâs), from Aramaic אבא (’abbā, “father”).

Pronunciation

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈab.baːs/
  • (Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈab.bas/

Noun

abbās m (genitive abbātis); third declension

  1. An abbot.

Related terms

  • abbātia
  • abbātissa
  • abbātīzō
