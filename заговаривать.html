Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Etymology: заговори́́ть (zagovorítʹ) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to begin to talk
    • to tire (someone) with one's talk, to talk someone's ear off

        • загова́ривать зу́бы ― zagovárivatʹ zúby ― to get round, to distract (someone) purposely by talking, to fool (someone) with fine words, to fool (someone) with smooth talk
