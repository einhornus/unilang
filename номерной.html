номерно́й (21877)


Etymology

но́мер (nómer) + -но́й (-nój)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəmʲɪrˈnoj]

Adjective

номерно́й • (nomernój)

  1. (relational) ordinal number
  2. (relational) size (of clothes or shoes)
  3. (relational) number, issue (e.g. of a magazine or a newspaper)
  4. numbered

Noun

номерно́й • (nomernój) m anim (genitive номерно́го, nominative plural номерны́е, genitive plural номерны́х, feminine номерна́я)

  1. valet (of a hotel or a similar facility)

Related terms

  • безномерно́й (beznomernój)
  • но́мер m (nómer)
