дре́йфить (22747)


Etymology

From дрейф

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈdrʲejfʲɪtʲ]

Verb

дре́йфить • (dréjfitʹ) impf (perfective сдрейфить)

  1. to quail, to funk, to show the white feather, to show cowardice
