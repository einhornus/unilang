шля́ться (10128)


Etymology

Dialectally also сляться (sljátʹsja). Per Vasmer, quoting Sobolevsky, probably from Proto-Slavic *sъljati sę, from *sъlati (“to send”) (whence Russian слать (slatʹ)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʂlʲat͡sːə]

Verb

шля́ться • (šljátʹsja) impf

  1. (colloquial) to loaf about, to gad about
