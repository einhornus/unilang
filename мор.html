мор (14358)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic моръ (morŭ), from Proto-Slavic *morъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mor]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -or

Noun

мор • (mor) m inan (genitive мо́ра, nominative plural мо́ры, genitive plural мо́ров)

  1. (medicine) pestilence, plague, murrain

Synonyms

  • падёж (padjóž)

Related terms
