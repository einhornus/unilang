отре́бье (28757)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈtrʲeb⁽ʲ⁾je]

Noun

отре́бье • (otrébʹje) n inan (genitive отре́бья, nominative plural отре́бья, genitive plural отре́бьев)

  1. (alsó plural, dated) scum, worthless remnants, waste, rabble, refuse
  2. (alsó plural, derogatory) the dregs of society, morally debased or criminal elements
