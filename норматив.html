нормати́в (21164)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Normativ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nərmɐˈtʲif]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

нормати́в • (normatív) m inan (genitive нормати́ва, nominative plural нормати́вы, genitive plural нормати́вов)

  1. norm, standard
