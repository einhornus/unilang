очи́стка (9666)


Etymology

очи́стить (očístitʹ) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈt͡ɕistkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

очи́стка • (očístka) f inan (genitive очи́стки, nominative plural очи́стки, genitive plural очи́сток, related adjective очистной)

  1. cleaning, purification, clearing, refinement
  2. peeling
  3. (military) mopping-up
  4. (economics) clearance
