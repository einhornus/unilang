отнять -> to take away
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c/c

Etymology: от- (ot-) +‎ -ня́ть (-njátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to take (from)
    • to subtract to take away to abstract
      • to deduct, to take away, to take from, to take off
        • (also figuratively) to take away (from) to bereave (of)
          • Он о́тнял у неё после́днюю наде́жду́. On ótnjal u nejó poslédnjuju nadéždu. He has taken away her last hope.
        • to withdraw to remove (hands)
          • to rob (of), to deprive (of)
            • (colloquial) to amputate
