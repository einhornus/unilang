дипломт -> diplomat, attaché-case
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: дипломты
Genitive: дипломта



Etymology: Borrowed from French diplomate. The sense “briefcase” arose from the custom of diplomats to carry briefcases.



Definitions (English):
  • diplomat (person who is accredited to represent a government)
    • briefcase
