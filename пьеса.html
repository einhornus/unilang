пье́са -> play (theatre)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: пье́сы
Genitive: пье́сы



Etymology: Borrowed from French pièce, from Late Latin pettia, from Gaulish *pettyā‎, from Proto-Celtic *kʷezdis.



Definitions (English):
  • play (theatrical performance)
    • (music) piece (small instrumental composition)
