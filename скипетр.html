ски́петр (30468)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek σκῆπτρον (skêptron).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈskʲipʲɪtr]

Noun

ски́петр • (skípetr) m inan (genitive ски́петра, nominative plural ски́петры, genitive plural ски́петров)

  1. sceptre
