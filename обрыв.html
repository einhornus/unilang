обрв -> precipice
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: обрвы
Genitive: обрва



Etymology: Deverbal of обрыва́ть (obryvátʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • break, rupture
    • precipice, steep bluff, cliff
