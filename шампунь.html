шампу́нь (18375)


Etymology

From English shampooing, ultimately from Hindi चाँपो (cā̃po).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂɐmˈpunʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

шампу́нь • (šampúnʹ) m inan (genitive шампу́ня, nominative plural шампу́ни, genitive plural шампу́ней)

  1. shampoo
