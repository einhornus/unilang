нжка -> leg, stem (of wineglass, mushroom)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: нжки
Genitive: нжки



Etymology: нога́ (nogá) +‎ -ка (-ka)



Definitions (English):
  • Diminutive of нога́ (nogá): small leg
    • trotter (food)
      • (furniture) leg
        • stem of a glass or letter
          • stalk of a mushroom
            • drumstick (chicken leg)
