нака́тывать (21521)


Etymology[edit]

From наката́ть (nakatátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ) or from накати́ть (nakatítʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈkatɨvətʲ]

Verb[edit]

нака́тывать • (nakátyvatʹ) impf (perfective наката́ть or накати́ть)

  1. to make smooth (by rolling)
    Perfective: наката́ть (nakatátʹ)
  2. to roll on
    наката́ть кра́ску
    nakatátʹ krásku
    to roll the paint on
    Perfective: наката́ть (nakatátʹ)
  3. to roll (on, onto)
    Perfective: накати́ть (nakatítʹ)
  4. (waves) to roll in, to coast in
    Perfective: накати́ть (nakatítʹ)
  5. (weather) to set in, (events) to begin, to break out
    Perfective: накати́ть (nakatítʹ)
  6. (feelings, mood) to come (over), to be seized (with), to have an attack
    Perfective: накати́ть (nakatítʹ)
