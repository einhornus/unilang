обрз -> cut edge
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: обрзы
Genitive: обрза



Etymology: Deverbal of обрезать (obrézatʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • edge (of book pages, a roof, etc.)
    • в обре́з ― v obréz ― only just enough very short. У меня вре́мени в обре́з. ― U menjá vrémeni v obréz. ― I have just enough time. / I have no time to spare.
  • sawn-off rifle, sawn-off shotgun
