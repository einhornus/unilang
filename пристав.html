пристав (14119)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈprʲistəf]

Noun

пристав • (prístav) masculine gender">m anim (genitive пристава, nominative plural пристава or приставы, genitive plural приставов or приставов)

  1. (historical) the local police chief or constable in Pre-Revolutionary Russia
  2. (historical) bailiff, in Moscow in historical times
