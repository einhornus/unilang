совесть -> conscience
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: совести
Genitive: совести



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic съвѣсть (sŭvěstĭ).



Definitions (English):
  • conscience (moral sense)
    • У тебя́́ со́весть есть? ― U tebjá sóvestʹ jestʹ? ― Have you no conscience?
