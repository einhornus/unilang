Verb[edit]

прихвати́ть • (prixvatítʹ) pf (imperfective прихва́тывать)

  1. to catch up, to seize up, to grab, to borrow, to grip
  2. to fasten, to tie up
  3. to nip, to damage, to touch (of frost)
  4. to tack
