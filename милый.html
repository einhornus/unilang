ми́лый -> nice, dear
Adjective



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic милъ (milŭ), from Proto-Slavic *milъ.



Definitions (English):
  • dear, sweet (having a pleasing disposition)
    • наси́льно ми́л не бу́дешь (proverb) ― nasílʹno mil ne búdešʹ ― love cannot be compelled
  • beloved
    • (in direct address, also ironic) dear, darling
