свози́ть (16958)


Etymology

с- (s-) +‎ вози́ть (vozítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [svɐˈzʲitʲ]

Verb

свози́ть • (svozítʹ) impf (perfective свезти́)

  1. (transitive) to transport down, to drive down
  2. to transport awaý, to carry awaý (by vehicle)
  3. (transitive) to gather together

Verb

свози́ть • (svozítʹ) pf

  1. (transitive) to transport there and back, to drive there and back

Related terms

  • везти́ impf (veztí), повезти́ pf (poveztí)
  • вози́ть impf (vozítʹ), повозить pf (povozítʹ)
  • воз (voz)
