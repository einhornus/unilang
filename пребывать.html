пребыва́ть (4410)


Etymology

пребы́ть (prebýtʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪbɨˈvatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Homophone: прибыва́ть (pribyvátʹ)

Verb

пребыва́ть • (prebyvátʹ) impf (perfective пребы́ть)

  1. (literary) to be (at some place), to stay, to remain
  2. (literary) to be (in some state or condition), to stay, to remain

Related terms

  • пребыва́ние n (prebyvánije)
