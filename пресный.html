пре́сный -> unsalted, flavourless, bland
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic прѣснъ (prěsnŭ), from Proto-Slavic *prěsnъ.



Definitions (English):
  • sweet, fresh (water)
    • unleavened (bread)
      • tasteless (food)
        • stale, insipid, vapid
