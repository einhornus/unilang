штат -> staff (of institution), state (of the USA, Australia)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: штаты
Genitive: штата́



Etymology: Borrowed from German Staat (“state, country”), from Middle Low German stāt, from Middle Dutch staet, from Latin stātus. The semantic development was influenced by French état from the same Latin source.



Definitions (English):
  • state (a political division, e.g. a US state)
    • staff (personnel)
