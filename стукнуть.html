Definitions (English):
  • to knock,
    • сту́кнуть в дверь ― stúknutʹ v dverʹ ― knock at the door
  • to rap, to tap
    • to strike, to bang, to hit,
      • ско́лько тебе́ сту́кнуло? (colloquial) ― skólʹko tebé stúknulo? ― how old are you (now)? (literally, “how many did it hit you?”) Игорю Николаеву сего́дня сту́кнуло 50! ― Igorju Nikolájevu sevódnja stúknulo 50! ― Igor Nikolayev turned 50 today!ему́ что-то в го́лову сту́кнуло ― jemú štó-to v golovú stúknulo ― he got some crazy (new) idea (also used when somebody starts behaving strangely) (literally, “something hit his head”)
    • (slang) to squeal on
