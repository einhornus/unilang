Verb

сту́кнуть • (stúknutʹ) pf (imperfective стуча́ть or сту́кать)

  1. to knock
    сту́кнуть в дверь ― stúknutʹ v dverʹ ― knock at the door
  2. to rap, to tap
  3. to strike, to bang, to hit
    ско́лько тебе́ сту́кнуло? (colloquial) ― skólʹko tebé stúknulo? ― how old are you (now)? (literally, “how many did it hit you?”)
    Игорю никола́еву сего́дня сту́кнуло 50! ― Igorju Nikolájevu sevódnja stúknulo 50! ― Igor Nikolayev turned 50 today!
    ему́ что́-то в голову́ сту́кнуло ― jemú štó-to v golovú stúknulo ― he got some crazy (new) idea (also used when somebody starts behaving strangely) (literally, “something hit his head”)
  4. (slang) to squeal on

Related terms
