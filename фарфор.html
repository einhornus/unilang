Borrowed from Ottoman Turkish language">Ottoman Turkish فغفوری‎ (fağfûrî, “Chinese [porcelain]”), from Persian فغفوری‎ (fağfuri, “Chinese [porcelain]”), from فغفور‎ (fağfur, “emperor of China”) + ـی‎ (-i), from Sogdian [script needed] (βγpwr /βaγpūr/); see it for more.
