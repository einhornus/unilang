совме́стный -> joint, combined
Adjective



Etymology: со- (so-) +‎ вме́сте (vméste) +‎ -ный (-nyj)



Definitions (English):
  • joint, shared (done by two or more people or organisations working together)
    • common, conjoint, united
      • compatible, simultaneous
