юли́ть (11101)


Etymology

Perhaps from юла́ (julá) +‎ -ить (-ítʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [jʉˈlʲitʲ]

Verb

юли́ть • (julítʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to bustle, to fuss (about)
  2. (colloquial) to play up (to)
  3. (colloquial) to wriggle

Related terms

  • юла́ f (julá)
