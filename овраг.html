овра́г (3482)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic врагъ (vragŭ), from Proto-Slavic *vьragъ. Compare Bulgarian овра́г (ovrág)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈvrak]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

овра́г • (ovrág) m inan (genitive овра́га, nominative plural овра́ги, genitive plural овра́гов)

  1. ravine (a deep narrow valley)
