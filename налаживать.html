нала́живать (15884)


Etymology

нала́дить (naláditʹ) + -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈlaʐɨvətʲ]

Verb

нала́живать • (naláživatʹ) impf (perfective нала́дить)

  1. to adjust, to repair, to set right
  2. (colloquial) to organize, to arrange, to establish
    нала́живать дела́ ― naláživatʹ delá ― to set things going
  3. (colloquial) to normalize, to smooth (relations)
    нала́живать сотру́дничество ― naláživatʹ sotrúdničestvo ― to enter into cooperation
    нала́живать хоро́шие отноше́ния ― naláživatʹ xoróšije otnošénija ― to establish good relations; to get alonǵ well
  4. (colloquial) to tune, to adjust to a certain harmony (of a musical instrument, song, etc.)
  5. (low colloquial) to drive away

Related terms

  • нала́дка (naládka), нала́дчик (naládčik)
