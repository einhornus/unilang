Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Etymology: нала́дить (naláditʹ) +‎ -ивать (-ivatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to adjust, to repair, to set right
    • (colloquial) to organize, to arrange, to establish,
      • нала́живать дела́ ― naláživatʹ delá ― to set things going
    • (colloquial) to normalize, to smooth (relations)
      • нала́живать сотру́́дничество ― naláživatʹ sotrúdničestvo ― to enter into cooperation, нала́живать хоро́шие отноше́ния ― naláživatʹ xoróšije otnošénija ― to establish good relations to get along well
    • (colloquial) to tune, to adjust to a certain harmony (of a musical instrument, song, etc.)
      • (low colloquial) to drive away
