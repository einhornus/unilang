хранить -> to keep, store
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я храню
Ты хранишь
Past: хранил/хранила



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic хранити (xraniti), from Proto-Slavic *xorniti. Doublet of хорони́́ть (xoronítʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to keep (to maintain possession of), to preserve, to store,
    • храни́́ть де́ньги в ба́нке ― xranítʹ dénʹgi v bánke ― to keep one's money in a bank, храни́́ть в та́́йне ― xranítʹ v tájne ― to keep (something) a secret
  • to guard, to save, to protect,
    • храни́́ тебя́́ бог ― xraní tebjá box ― God save you
