бо́ртик (24238)


Etymology

борт (bort) +‎ -ик (-ik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbortʲɪk]

Noun

бо́ртик • (bórtik) m inan (genitive бо́ртика, nominative plural бо́ртики, genitive plural бо́ртиков)

  1. (colloquial) endearing diminutive of борт (bort, “side (of a ship), edge, border (of clothing)”)

Related terms

  • борт (bort), бортовой (bortovój)
