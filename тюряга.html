тюря́га (29948)


Etymology

Shortening of тюрьма́ (tjurʹmá).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲʉˈrʲaɡə]

Noun

тюря́га • (tjurjága) f inan (genitive тюря́ги, nominative plural тюря́ги, genitive plural тюря́г)

  1. (colloquial) joint, jug (prison, jail)
