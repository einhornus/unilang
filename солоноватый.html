солонова́тый (27884)


Etymology

From солон- (solon-) +‎ +-оватый (+-ovátyj). This term is built off of the irregular short stem солон- (solon-) of солёный (soljónyj); compare short masculine singular солон (sólon).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sələnɐˈvatɨj]

Adjective

солонова́тый • (solonovátyj)

  1. brackish (slightly salty)
