сарй -> shed
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Genitive: саря



Etymology: Borrowed from Persian سرای‎ (sarây).

  • shed, storage building (small wooden construction)
    • uncomfortable, untidy room, pigsty
      • ( Kazakhstan) palace
