чуткий -> sensitive
Adjective



Etymology: Related to чу́ять (čújatʹ), with -кий (-kij).



Definitions (English):
  • sensitive (of a person, instrument, etc.)
    • sharp, keen (sense of hearing, smell, etc.)
      • чу́ткая соба́ка ― čútkaja sobáka ― keen-nosed dog
    • sympathetic, thoughtful, tactful,
      • чу́ткий подхо́д ― čútkij podxód ― tactful approach
    • light (sleep)
      • чу́ткий сон ― čútkij son ― light sleep
