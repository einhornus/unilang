Verb[edit]

загля́дывать • (zagljádyvatʹ) impf (perfective загляну́ть)

  1. to glance (at), to peep (in), to look (into), to have a look (at)
    загля́дывать в бу́дущее ― zagljádyvatʹ v búduščeje ― to look to the future
    загля́дывать вперёд ― zagljádyvatʹ vperjód ― to look ahead
  2. (colloquial) to drop in (on), to look in (on), to call (on)
