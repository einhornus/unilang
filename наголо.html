наголо́ (13749)


Etymology 1

Apparently from на (na) +‎ го́ло (gólo); but compare also наго́й (nagój, “naked”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnaɡələ]

Adverb

наголо́ • (nágolo)

  1. close to the skin, closely shaven
