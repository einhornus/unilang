добы́тчик (25841)


Etymology

добы́ча (dobýča) + -щик (-ščik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dɐˈbɨt͡ɕːɪk]

Noun

добы́тчик • (dobýtčik) m anim (genitive добы́тчика, nominative plural добы́тчики, genitive plural добы́тчиков, feminine добы́тчица)

  1. (colloquial) someone who extracts resources (miner, hunter, fisherman, etc.)
  2. (colloquial) breadwinner, (good) earner (someone who is able to make good money)

Related terms

  • добыва́ть impf (dobyvátʹ), добы́ть pf (dobýtʹ)
