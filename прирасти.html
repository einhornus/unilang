прирасти́ (22728)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪrɐˈsʲtʲi]

Etymology 1

при- (pri-) +‎ расти́ (rastí)

Verb

прирасти́ • (prirastí) pf (imperfective прирастать)

  1. to adhere to , to grow to
  2. to grow, to increase, to accrue

Etymology 2

Verb

прирасти́ • (prirastí)

  1. second-person singular imperative perfective of прирастить (prirastítʹ)
