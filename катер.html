ка́тер (4724)


Etymology

Borrowed from English cutter.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkatʲɪr]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ка́тер • (káter) masculine gender">m inan (genitive катера, nominative plural катера, genitive plural катеро́в)

  1. (nautical) motorboat, boat (with an inboard engine)
  2. (nautical) cutter
