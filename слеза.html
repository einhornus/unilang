слеза́ -> tear
Noun, declension 1f
Plural: слёзы
Genitive: слезы



Etymology: From Old East Slavic сльза (slĭza), from Proto-Slavic *slьza.



Definitions (English):
  • tear (a drop of liquid from the eyes)
