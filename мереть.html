Verb[edit]

мере́ть • (merétʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to die, to perish (in large numbers)
  2. (informal) to stand still, to sink, to miss a beat

Related terms[edit]

  • мёртвый (mjórtvyj)
  • мор (mor)
  • смерть (smertʹ)
