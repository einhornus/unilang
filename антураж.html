антура́ж (25572)


Etymology

Borrowed from French entourage.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐntʊˈraʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

антура́ж • (anturáž) m inan (genitive антура́жа, nominative plural антура́жи, genitive plural антура́жей)

  1. entourage (retinue of attendants, associates or followers)

Значе́ние
