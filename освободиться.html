освободи́ться (4229)


Etymology

освободи́ть (osvobodítʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐsvəbɐˈdʲit͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

освободи́ться • (osvobodítʹsja) pf (imperfective освобожда́ться)

  1. to get free, to break free, to free oneself (from), to get loose
  2. to become empty; to become vacant
  3. to become free, to get free time (e.g. after work)
  4. passive of освободи́ть (osvobodítʹ)
