лиходе́й (30453)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic лиходѣй (lixoděj), from ли́хо (lixo, “bad”) + дѣꙗти (dějati, “to do”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪxɐˈdʲej]

Noun[edit]

лиходе́й • (lixodéj) m anim (genitive лиходе́я, nominative plural лиходе́и, genitive plural лиходе́ев, feminine лиходейка)

  1. (archaic, poetic) evildoer, villain

Synonyms[edit]

  • злоде́й (zlodéj)
