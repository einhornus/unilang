запада́ть (27825)


Etymology 1

запад- (zapad-) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəpɐˈdatʲ]

Verb

запада́ть • (zapadátʹ) impf (perfective запа́сть)

  1. to fall back, to fall behind, to sink down
    клавиша западает ― kláviša zapadájet ― the key is stuck; the key won't come up
  2. to sink deeply
    слова его́ запали мне в душу ― slová jevó zapáli mne v dúšu ― his words imprinted themselves in my mind [on my soul]; his words sank home
  3. (slang) to take (to), to fall (for)
Related terms
  • за́пад (západ)
