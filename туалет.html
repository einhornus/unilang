туале́т -> toilet
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: туале́ты
Genitive: туале́та



Etymology: Borrowed from French toilettes.



Definitions (English):
  • (countable) restroom, lavatory
    • (countable) dress, attire
      • (uncountable) toilet, grooming (care for one's personal appearance)
        • (countable) dressing table (also
          • туалетный сто́лик)
