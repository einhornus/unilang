округ -> okrug, region
Noun, declension 2c (pattern город)

Plural: о́круга
Genitive: окру́га



Etymology: From Old East Slavic округъ (okrugŭ), from Proto-Slavic *okrǫgъ, from *ob- + *krǫgъ (“circle”).

      • (law) circuit
