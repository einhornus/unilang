округ (3543)


Etymology 1

From Old East Slavic округъ (okrugŭ), from Proto-Slavic *okrǫgъ, from *ob- + *krǫgъ (“circle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈokrʊk]

Noun

о́круг • (ókrug) m inan (genitive о́круга, nominative plural округа́, genitive plural округо́в), округа (okrugá) pl

  1. district, okrug
  2. subprefecture (of Japan)
  3. (law) circuit
Related terms
  • круг m (krug)
  • окружно́й (okružnój)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈkruk]

Noun

окру́г • (okrúg) f inan pl
