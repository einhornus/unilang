факел -> torch
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: факелы
Genitive: факела



Etymology: Borrowed from German Fackel, from Old High German vackel or faccala, from Latin facula.



Definitions (English):
  • torch (stick with flame at one end)
    • jet, tongue, flare
      • flame body
