dictator (8004)


Etymology

From dictō (“I dictate”) +‎ -tor.

Pronunciation

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /dikˈtaː.tor/, [d̪ɪkˈt̪aː.t̪ɔr]
  • (Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /dikˈta.tor/, [d̪ikˈt̪aː.t̪ɔr]

Noun

dictātor m (genitive dictātōris); third declension

  1. an elected chief magistrate
  2. one who dictates.
