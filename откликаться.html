отклика́ться (9482)


Etymology

откли́кнуться (otklíknutʹsja) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtklʲɪˈkat͡sːə]

Verb

отклика́ться • (otklikátʹsja) impf (perfective откли́кнуться)

  1. to respond, to answer (to a call, challenge, appeal, etc.)
  2. to respond (with a letter), to comment (in the press)
  3. (impersonal) to sound as an answer, to like an echo

Related terms

  • о́тклик (ótklik)
