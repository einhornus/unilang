сиять -> to shine
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я сияю
Ты сияешь
Past: сиял/сияла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *sijati.



Definitions (English):
  • to shine, to beam
    • (facial expression) to shine, to beam, to be radiant,
      • сия́́ть от ра́дости ― sijátʹ ot rádosti ― shine with joy
    • to shine, to be resplendent
