хро́мать -> to limp
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я хро́маю
Ты хро́маешь
Past: хро́мал/хро́мала



Etymology: Perhaps from хро́мо́й (xromój) +‎ -а́ть (-átʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to limp, to be lame,
    • хрома́́ть на пра́вую но́гу ― xromátʹ na právuju nógu ― to be lame in the right leg
  • to be poor, to leave much to be desired, to be far from perfect, to not be up to standard,
    • у него́́ хрома́́ет орфогра́фия ― u nevó xromájet orfográfija ― his spelling is poor his spelling leaves much to be desired
