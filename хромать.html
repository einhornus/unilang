хрома́ть -> to limp
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я хрома́ю
Ты хрома́ешь
Past: хрома́л/хрома́ла



Etymology: Perhaps from хромо́й (xromój) +‎ -ать (-átʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • to limp, to be lame,
    • хрома́ть на пра́вую но́гу ― xromátʹ na právuju nógu ― to be lame in the right leg
  • to be poor, to leave much to be desired, to be far from perfect, to not be up to standard,
    • у него́ хрома́ет орфография ― u nevó xromájet orfográfija ― his spelling is poor his spelling leaves much to be desired
