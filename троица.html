тро́ица (10014)


Etymology

тро́е (tróje) + -ица (-ica)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtroɪt͡sə]

Noun

тро́ица • (tróica) f inan (genitive тро́ицы, nominative plural тро́ицы, genitive plural тро́иц)

  1. (colloquial, ironic) trio, the three of them
    бог лю́бит тро́ицу (proverb) ― box ljúbit tróicu ― all good things come in threes

Related terms

  • тро́ица (Tróica, “Trinity, Whitsunday”)
