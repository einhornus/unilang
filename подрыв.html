подры́в (18588)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈdrɨf]

Etymology 1

Deverbal of подрыва́ть (podryvátʹ).

Noun

подры́в • (podrýv) m inan (genitive подры́ва, nominative plural подры́вы, genitive plural подры́вов, related adjective подрывно́й)

  1. (the act of) blowing up, causing to explode
  2. (the act of) undermining (of influence, government, domination)

Etymology 2

Participle

подры́в • (podrýv)

  1. short past adverbial perfective participle of подрыть (podrýtʹ)
