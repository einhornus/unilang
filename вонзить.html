вонзи́ть (19521)


Etymology

Cognate with Old Church Slavonic въньзити pf (vŭnĭziti), въньзнѫти impf (vŭnĭznǫti). Synchronically composed of во- (vo-) + -нз- (-nz-) + -ить (-itʹ), where the root -нз- is also found in пронзи́ть (pronzítʹ) and is cognate with Polish nizać and further with нож (nož, “knife”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐn⁽ʲ⁾ˈzʲitʲ]

Verb

вонзи́ть • (vonzítʹ) pf (imperfective вонза́ть)

  1. to thrust into, to plunge into (в (v) + accusative)

Related terms

  • пронза́ть impf (pronzátʹ), пронзи́ть pf (pronzítʹ)
  • нож (nož)
