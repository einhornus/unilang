вонзи́ть (19519)


Etymology

Cognate with Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic въньзити pf (vŭnĭziti), въньзнѫти impf (vŭnĭznǫti). Synchronically composed of во- (vo-) +‎ -нз- (-nz-) +‎ -ить (-itʹ), where the root -нз- is also found in пронзи́ть (pronzítʹ) and is cognate with Polish language">Polish nizać and further with нож (nož, “knife”).
