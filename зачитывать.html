зачи́тывать (17501)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈt͡ɕitɨvətʲ]

Etymology 1

Verb

зачи́тывать • (začítyvatʹ) impf (perfective зачита́ть)

  1. to read out
  2. to start reading
  3. (colloquial) to wear out by reading too much
  4. (colloquial) to fail to return a borrowed book
Related terms
  • чита́ть impf (čitátʹ)
  • чте́ние n (čténije), прочте́ние n (pročténije)

Etymology 2

Verb

зачи́тывать • (začítyvatʹ) impf (perfective заче́сть)

  1. to credit, to accept as a credit (e.g. money towardś a debt)
  2. to pass, to give a passing grade to (e.g. coursework, a dissertation)
Related terms
  • зачёт m (začót)
