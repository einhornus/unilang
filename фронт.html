фронт -> front (war)
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: фронты́
Genitive: фро́нта



Etymology: Borrowed from German Front.



Definitions (English):
  • (military) front (large operational military command that controls several armies equivalent to U. S. army group)
    • battle front, front line
