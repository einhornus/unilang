отбы́тие (28068)


Etymology

отбы́ть (otbýtʹ) + -тие (-tije)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐdˈbɨtʲɪje]

Noun

отбы́тие • (otbýtije) n inan (genitive отбы́тия, nominative plural отбы́тия, genitive plural отбы́тий)

  1. departure
  2. completion, serving out (of a term in office, prison sentence, etc.)

Related terms

  • отбыва́ть impf (otbyvátʹ), отбы́ть pf (otbýtʹ)
