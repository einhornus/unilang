Noun[edit]

морко́вь • (morkóvʹ) f inan (genitive морко́ви, nominative plural морко́ви, genitive plural морко́вей)

  1. (countable) carrot (plant)
  2. (uncountable, collectively) carrots (roots of the plant used for eating)

Synonyms[edit]

  • морко́вка (morkóvka)
