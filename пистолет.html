пистолт -> pistol
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: пистолты
Genitive: пистолта



Etymology: Borrowed from French pistolet. Ultimately from Czech píšťala (“whistle”), from Proto-Slavic *piščalь, from *piskati, *piščati (“to squeak, whistle”), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *pīṣk-. Compare пищаль (piščálʹ)
