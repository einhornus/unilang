разный -> various, different
Adjective



Etymology: From Old Church Slavonic разьнъ (razĭnŭ), from Proto-Slavic *orzьnъ. Doublet of ро́зный (róznyj). See also рознь (roznʹ), раз (raz), ре́зать (rézatʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • different, diverse, unlike
    • various, varied, miscellaneous
