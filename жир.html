жир -> fat, grease
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: жиры́
Genitive: жи́ра



Etymology: From Old East Slavic жиръ (žirŭ), from Proto-Slavic *žirъ.



Definitions (English):
  • fat or oil from the body of an animal
    • grease
