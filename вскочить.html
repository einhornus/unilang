вскочи́ть (1401)


Etymology

в- (v-) +‎ -скочить (-skočítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fskɐˈt͡ɕitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

вскочи́ть • (vskočítʹ) pf (imperfective вска́кивать)

  1. (unexpectedly) to jump (up, ontó, out of), to leap (on, intó, ontó), to get up quickly
    вскочи́ть на ноги ― vskočítʹ ná nogi ― to leap / spring to one's feet
    вскочи́ть с постели ― vskočítʹ s postéli ― to jump out of bed
  2. (colloquial) to rise, to swell (up), to come up (e.g. a pimple on the face, a bump on the head)
    у меня́ вскочи́л прыщик на но́су ― u menjá vskočíl prýščik na nosú ― I've got a pimple on my nose (suddenly)

Related terms

  • вскакивание n (vskákivanije)
