мурлы́кать (13033)


Etymology

Onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʊrˈlɨkətʲ]

Verb

мурлы́кать • (murlýkatʹ) impf

  1. to purr
  2. (colloquial) to hum

Related terms

  • му́р (mur)
