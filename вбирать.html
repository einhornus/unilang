вбира́ть (18719)


Etymology

в- (v-) +‎ -бирать (-birátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [v⁽ʲ⁾bʲɪˈratʲ]

Verb

вбира́ть • (vbirátʹ) impf (perfective вобра́ть)

  1. to absorb, to imbibe
  2. to suck in, to drink in
  3. to inhale
