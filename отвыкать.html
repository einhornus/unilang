отвыка́ть (31303)


Etymology

отвы́кнуть (otvýknutʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtvɨˈkatʲ]

Verb

отвыка́ть • (otvykátʹ) impf (perfective отвы́кнуть)

  1. to get out of the habit (of), to break oneself of the habit (of)
  2. to become estranged (from)
    отвыка́ть от́ дома ― otvykátʹ ot dóma ― to become a stranger in one's own house
