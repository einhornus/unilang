ма́рля (18482)


Etymology

Borrowed from French marli (“gauze, muslin”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmarlʲə]

Noun

ма́рля • (márlja) f inan (genitive ма́рли, nominative plural ма́рли, genitive plural ма́рлей)

  1. gauze (thin fabric with open weave)

Related terms

  • марлевый (marlevyj)
