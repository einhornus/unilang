подра́ть (14289)

[pɐˈdratʲ]
|||||

по- (po-) + драть (dratʹ)


|||||

Verb

подра́ть • (podrátʹ) pf

  1. (low colloquial) to rip up , to tear up
  2. (low colloquial) to wear out (shoes, etc.)
  3. (low colloquial) to pull
  4. (low colloquial) to punish by pulling (hair, ears)
  5. (low colloquial) to run away
  6. (low colloquial) to devour (of animals)

Related terms

  • драть (dratʹ)
  • дра́ка (dráka)
  • драчли́вый (dračlívyj)
  • драчу́н (dračún)
