акведу́к (20026)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐkvʲɪˈduk]

Noun

акведу́к • (akvedúk) m inan (genitive акведу́ка, nominative plural акведу́ки, genitive plural акведу́ков)

  1. aqueduct (an artificial channel conveying water)
