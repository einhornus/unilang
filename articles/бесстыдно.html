Adverb

бессты́дно • (besstýdno)

  1. brazenly, shamelessly, immodestly

Predicative

бессты́дно • (besstýdno)

  1. one is shameless, one is brazen
    ему́ бессты́дно ― jemú besstýdno ― he is shameless
