непоколеби́мый (16691)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪpəkəlʲɪˈbʲimɨj]

Adjective

непоколеби́мый • (nepokolebímyj)

  1. firm, steadfast
  2. unflinching
  3. imperturbable, inflexible
