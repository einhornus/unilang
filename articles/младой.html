младо́й (17886)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic младъ (mladŭ), from Proto-Slavic *moldъ (“young”). Doublet of молодо́й (molodój).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mlɐˈdoj]

Adjective

младо́й • (mladój) (comparative (по)мла́дше)

  1. (archaic, poetic) young, youthful, new

Derived terms

  • мла́дость (mládostʹ)
