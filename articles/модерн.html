моде́рн (21239)

[mɐˈdɛrn]
|||||

Borrowed from French moderne.


|||||

Adjective

моде́рн • (modɛ́rn) (indeclinable)

  1. (colloquial) modern, contemporary, fashionable

Usage notes

  • Normally used either in the post-position or as a predicative adjective.

Synonyms

  • совреме́нный (sovreménnyj, “contemporary”)
  • мо́дный (módnyj, “fashionable”)
  • модерно́вый (modɛrnóvyj, “fashionable, contemporary (colloquial)”)

Noun

моде́рн • (modɛ́rn) m inan (genitive моде́рна, nominative plural моде́рны, genitive plural моде́рнов) or
моде́рн • (modɛ́rn) m inan (indeclinable)

  1. (art) modern style
    стиль моде́рн ― stilʹ modɛ́rn ― modern style
    в сти́ле моде́рн ― v stíle modɛ́rn ― in the modern style
    архитекту́ра моде́рна ― arxitektúra modɛ́rna ― modern architecture

Usage notes

  • In the post-position the term is also used in the sense of "modern" (adjective)
