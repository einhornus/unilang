ка́фель (16867)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Kachel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkafʲɪlʲ]

Noun

ка́фель • (káfelʹ) m inan (genitive ка́феля, uncountable, related adjective ка́фельный)

  1. tile, Dutch tile , stone / glazed tile
