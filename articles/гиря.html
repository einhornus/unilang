ги́ря (11082)


Etymology

Old Russian гыря, Bulgarian гiря. Vasmer connects it to the root of гвор (gvor) "bladder, tumor", ultimately PIE *gʷreh₂- "heavy". The phonology is difficult and may show Iranian influence (Indo-Iranian *gr̥Húš, modern Persian گران (gerân) "heavy").

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈɡʲirʲə]

Noun

ги́ря

ги́ря • (gírja) f inan (genitive ги́ри, nominative plural ги́ри, genitive plural гирь)

  1. weight for measuring
  2. kettlebell, girya

Related terms

  • гиревой (girevoj)
