гомони́ть (26089)


Etymology

го́мон (gómon) + -и́ть (-ítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡəmɐˈnʲitʲ]

Verb

гомони́ть • (gomonítʹ) impf

  1. to produce a hubbub, to rumble (of a crowd)
  2. to talk loudly
