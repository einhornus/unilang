волчо́к (19446)

[vɐlˈt͡ɕɵk]
|||||

волк (volk) + -о́к (-ók).


|||||

Noun

волчо́к • (volčók) m anim or m inan (genitive волчка́, nominative plural волчки́, genitive plural волчко́в)

  1. little wolf, wolf cub : diminutive of волк (volk) (animate)
  2. spinning top (a toy, inanimate)
  3. little bittern (a bird, animate)
  4. (regional) water sprout, epicormic shoot (inanimate)
  5. (colloquial) peephole in a prison cell door

Synonyms

  • волчо́нок (volčónok, “wolf cub”)
  • юла́ (julá, “spinning top”)
  • выпь (vypʹ, “bird”)
