кре́чет (15297)


Etymology

From Old Church Slavonic кречетъ (krečetŭ). Compare Ancient Greek κέρκνος (kérknos, “a Hierax or an Alektryon”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkrʲet͡ɕɪt]

Noun

кре́чет • (kréčet) m anim (genitive кре́чета, nominative plural кре́четы, genitive plural кре́четов)

  1. gyrfalcon (Falco rusticolus)

Значе́ние
