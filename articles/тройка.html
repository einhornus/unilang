тро́йка (4126)


Etymology

From трой- (troj-) + -ка (-ka).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtrojkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

тро́йка • (trójka) f inan (genitive тро́йки, nominative plural тро́йки, genitive plural тро́ек)

  1. (colloquial) the number three
  2. grade of three (corresponds to American C grade)
  3. (card games ) three, trey
  4. troika (Russian carriage drawn by a team of three horses abreast)
