слайд (26448)


Etymology

Borrowed from English slide.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [slajt]

Noun

слайд • (slajd) m inan (genitive сла́йда, nominative plural сла́йды, genitive plural сла́йдов)

  1. slide (transparent image for projecting)

Synonyms

  • диапозити́в m (diapozitív)
