се́рвер (6235)


Etymology

Borrowed from English server.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲervʲɪr]

Noun

се́рвер • (sérver) m inan (genitive се́рвера, nominative plural се́рверы, genitive plural се́рверов)

  1. (computing) server (a computer or a program which provides services to other programs or users)

Synonyms

  • серва́к (servák)

Related terms

  • серва́чок (serváčok)
  • блейд-се́рвер (blejd-sérver), се́рвис (sɛ́rvis)
  • се́рверный (sérvernyj)
