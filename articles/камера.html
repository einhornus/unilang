ка́мера (1248)


Etymology

From Latin camera.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkamʲɪrə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ка́мера • (kámera) f inan (genitive ка́меры, nominative plural ка́меры, genitive plural ка́мер)

  1. (photography) camera
  2. chamber, cell, compartment
  3. prison cell, ward
  4. room, office
  5. inner tube (of a tire)
  6. bladder (of a ball)
  7. barrel (of a pump)

Related terms

  • ка́мерный (kámernyj)
