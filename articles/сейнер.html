се́йнер (19482)


Etymology

Borrowed from English seiner.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲejnʲɪr]

Noun

се́йнер • (séjner) m inan (genitive се́йнера, nominative plural се́йнеры, genitive plural се́йнеров) (normal usage)
се́йнер • (séjner) m inan (genitive се́йнера, nominative plural сейнера́, genitive plural сейнеро́в) (professional usage)

  1. seiner (type of fishing boat)
