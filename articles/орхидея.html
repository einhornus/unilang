орхиде́я (26524)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek ὄρχις (órkhis, “orchid, testicle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐrxʲɪˈdʲejə]
  • IPA(key): [ɐrxʲɪˈdɛjə] (phonetic respelling: орхидэ́я)

Noun

орхиде́я • (orxidéja, orxidɛ́ja) f inan (genitive орхиде́и, nominative plural орхиде́и, genitive plural орхиде́й)

  1. orchid
