проду́мать (6824)


Etymology

про- (pro-) + ду́мать (dúmatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈdumətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

проду́мать • (prodúmatʹ) pf (imperfective проду́мывать)

  1. to think over , to think through , to consider carefully

Related terms
