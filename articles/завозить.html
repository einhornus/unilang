завози́ть (21403)

[zəvɐˈzʲitʲ]
|||||

за- (za-) + вози́ть (vozítʹ)


|||||

Verb

завози́ть • (zavozítʹ) impf (perfective завезти́)

  1. to take (to a place), to drop off (at a place)
  2. to supply, to deliver

Verb

завози́ть • (zavozítʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial) to begin to carry (by vehicle), to begin to transport
  2. (low colloquial) to dirty, to contaminate

Related terms

  • заво́з (zavóz)
  • вози́ть (vozítʹ), везти́ (veztí)
