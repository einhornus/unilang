миле́ди (23036)


Etymology

Borrowed from English milady.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈlʲedʲɪ]

Noun

миле́ди • (milédi) f anim (indeclinable)

  1. (historical) milady (English noblewoman)
