водола́з (21488)


Etymology

From вода́ (vodá, “water”) + -о- (-o-) + -лаз (-laz), combining form of ла́зить (lázitʹ, “to climb, to clamber”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vədɐˈlas]

Noun

водола́з • (vodoláz) m anim (genitive водола́за, nominative plural водола́зы, genitive plural водола́зов, feminine водола́зка)

  1. diver, frogman, plunger
  2. Newfoundland dog
