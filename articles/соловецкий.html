солове́цкий (26595)


Etymology

Соловки́ (Solovkí) + -ский (-skij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [səlɐˈvʲet͡skʲɪj]

Adjective

солове́цкий • (solovéckij) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) Solovki, Solovetsky Islands (archipelago in the White Sea )
    Солове́тские острова́ ― Solovétskije ostrová ― Solovetsky Islands
  2. (relational) Solovetsky District (district containing the Solovetsky Islands)
  3. (relational) Solovetsky Monastery (monastery on the Solovetsky Islands)
  4. (relational) Solovetsky, Solovetskaya, Solovetskoye (any of various Russian settlements with these names)

Related terms

  • Соловки́ (Solovkí)
