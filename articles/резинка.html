рези́нка (9542)


Etymology

рези́на (rezína) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪˈzʲinkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

рези́нка • (rezínka) f inan (genitive рези́нки, nominative plural рези́нки, genitive plural рези́нок)

  1. eraser (thing used to erase something written or drawn)
  2. elastic band; scrunchie (for hair); garter (for stockings)
  3. gum (for chewing)
    жева́тельная рези́нка ― ževátelʹnaja rezínka ― chewing gum
  4. (slang) condom, rubber
