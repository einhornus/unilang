те́хникум (10198)


Etymology

Possibly from German Technikum, cognate with Polish technikum.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲexnʲɪkʊm]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

те́хникум • (téxnikum) m inan (genitive те́хникума, nominative plural те́хникумы, genitive plural те́хникумов)

  1. technical college, technical school, vocational school, technicum (institution of [further education] at an intermediate level)
