интервью́ (3351)


Etymology

Borrowed from English interview.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪntɨrˈv⁽ʲ⁾ju] (phonetic respelling: интэрвью́)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

интервью́ • (intɛrvʹjú) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. interview

Synonyms

  • собесе́дование (sobesédovanije) (a formal meeting for the assessment of a candidate or applicant)

Related terms

  • интервьюи́ровать (intɛrvʹjuírovatʹ)
