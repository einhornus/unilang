теса́ть (31950)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic тесати (tesati), from Proto-Slavic *tesàti (“to hew”), from Proto-Indo-European *tetḱ- .

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲɪˈsatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

теса́ть • (tesátʹ) impf

  1. to hew (with an axe), to make (something) by cutting with an axe
  2. to shave the surface layer of (with an axe)
