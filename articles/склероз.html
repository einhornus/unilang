склеро́з (17004)

[sklʲɪˈros]

|||||

Noun

склеро́з • (skleróz) m inan (genitive склеро́за, nominative plural склеро́зы, genitive plural склеро́зов)

  1. (technical) sclerosis
  2. (colloquial) memory loss, especially in old age
