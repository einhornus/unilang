упря́мо (5694)


Etymology

упря́мый (uprjámyj) + -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈprʲamə]
  • Audio

Adverb

упря́мо • (uprjámo)

  1. obstinately (in an obstinate manner)

Adjective

упря́мо • (uprjámo)

  1. short neuter singular of упря́мый (uprjámyj)
Наре́чие
