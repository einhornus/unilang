пу́говка (14742)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpuɡəfkə]

Noun

пу́говка • (púgovka) f inan (genitive пу́говки, nominative plural пу́говки, genitive plural пу́говок)

  1. Diminutive of пу́говица (púgovica): (small) button (on clothing)
