нащу́пать (5447)


Etymology

на- (na-) + щу́пать (ščúpatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈɕːupətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

нащу́пать • (naščúpatʹ) pf (imperfective нащу́пывать)

  1. (literally and figuratively) to grope (for, after), to feel about (for)
