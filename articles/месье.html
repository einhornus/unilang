месье́ (1077)


Etymology

Borrowed from French monsieur.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈsʲje]

Noun

месье́ • (mesʹjé) m anim (indeclinable)

  1. (dated or French context) monsieur, sir
