пополня́ть (18782)


Etymology

попо́лнить (popólnitʹ) + -я́ть (-játʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəpɐlˈnʲætʲ]

Verb

пополня́ть • (popolnjátʹ) impf (perfective попо́лнить)

  1. to replenish, to fill up , to supplement, to enrich, to widen, to enlarge
  2. (military) to reman, to reinforce, to replace, to beef up

Related terms

  • пополне́ние n (popolnénije)
