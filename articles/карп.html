карп (8997)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [karp]

Noun

карп • (karp) m anim or m inan (genitive ка́рпа, nominative plural ка́рпы, genitive plural ка́рпов)

  1. (animate): Cyprinus carpio , common carp (species)
  2. (inanimate): common carp (meat)

Synonyms

Related terms
