заболева́ть (17822)


Etymology

заболе́ть (zabolétʹ) + -ва́ть (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəbəlʲɪˈvatʲ]

Verb

заболева́ть • (zabolevátʹ) impf (perfective заболе́ть)

  1. to fall ill (to become ill)
  2. to begin to hurt, to become hurt (of a body part)

Related terms

  • боле́ть impf (bolétʹ)
