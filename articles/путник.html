пу́тник (6465)


Etymology

Cognate with Old Church Slavonic пѫтьникъ (pǫtĭnikŭ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈputʲnʲɪk]

Noun

пу́тник • (pútnik) m anim (genitive пу́тника, nominative plural пу́тники, genitive plural пу́тников, feminine пу́тник)

  1. traveler, wayfarer

Derived terms
