возме́здие (11596)


Etymology

воз- (voz-) + мзда (mzda) + -ие (-ije)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐzˈmʲezʲdʲɪje]

Noun

возме́здие • (vozmézdije) n inan (genitive возме́здия, nominative plural возме́здия, genitive plural возме́здий)

  1. retribution, requital, punishment, payback, revenge (punishment inflicted in the spirit of moral outrage or personal vengeance)

Related terms

  • возме́здный (vozmézdnyj)
