турнике́т (25976)


Etymology

Borrowed from French tourniquet.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʊrnʲɪˈkʲet]

Noun

турнике́т • (turnikét) m inan (genitive турнике́та, nominative plural турнике́ты, genitive plural турнике́тов)

  1. turnstile
  2. (medicine) tourniquet, garrot
