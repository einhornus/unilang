наме́тить (5251)


Etymology

на- (na-) + ме́тить (métitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈmʲetʲɪtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

наме́тить • (namétitʹ) pf (imperfective намеча́ть)

  1. to design, to plan, to contemplate, to lay down (a program)
  2. to select, to nominate
  3. to mark, to make a mark on
