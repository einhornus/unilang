вы́мысел (20609)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨmɨsʲɪl]

Noun

вы́мысел • (výmysel) m inan (genitive вы́мысла, nominative plural вы́мыслы, genitive plural вы́мыслов)

  1. invention, fiction, fantasy, figment
  2. imagination, fancy
  3. falsehood, lie, fabrication

Related terms

  • вы́мышленный (výmyšlennyj)
