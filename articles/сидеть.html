сиде́ть (126)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic сидѣти (siděti), from Proto-Slavic *sěděti.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪˈdʲetʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Homophones: седе́ть (sedétʹ)

Verb

сиде́ть • (sidétʹ) impf (perfective сесть or посиде́ть)

  1. to sit
  2. (colloquial) to be imprisoned, to do time
  3. to stay at home with somebody, to look after somebody (с (s) + instrumental)
