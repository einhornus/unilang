блево́тина (22454)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [blʲɪˈvotʲɪnə]

Noun

блево́тина • (blevótina) f inan (genitive блево́тины, nominative plural блево́тины, genitive plural блево́тин)

  1. (colloquial) vomit, spew (regurgitated former contents of a stomach)
