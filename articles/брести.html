брести́ (5422)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic брести́ (bresti), from Proto-Slavic *brestì, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰredʰ-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [brʲɪˈsʲtʲi]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

брести́ • (brestí) impf (concrete verb)

  1. to plod, to lag
  2. to stroll

Usage notes

Брести́ is a Russian concrete verb. Its counterpart, броди́ть, is an abstract verb.
