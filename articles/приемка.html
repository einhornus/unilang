приёмка (23803)


Etymology

Deverbal of приня́ть (prinjátʹ) + -ка (-ka), based on the nominal combining stem приём- (prijom-), derived from the obsolete present tense stem прие́м- (prijém-).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪˈjɵmkə]

Noun

приёмка • (prijómka) f inan (genitive приёмки, nominative plural приёмки, genitive plural приёмок)

  1. takeover, taking over
  2. formal acceptance (of a completed construction project, a consignment of goods, etc)

Related terms

  • принима́ть (prinimátʹ), приня́ть (prinjátʹ)
  • приём (prijóm)
  • приёмный (prijómnyj), прие́млемый (prijémlemyj)
