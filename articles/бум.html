бум (12879)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bum]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from English boom.

Noun

бум • (bum) m inan (genitive бу́ма, nominative plural бу́мы, genitive plural бу́мов)

  1. (media) sensation, racket
  2. (economics) boom

Etymology 2

Onomatopoeic.

Interjection

бум • (bum)

  1. thud, crash
