напя́ливать (22280)


Etymology

напя́лить (napjálitʹ) + -ивать (-ivatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈpʲælʲɪvətʲ]

Verb

напя́ливать • (napjálivatʹ) impf (perfective напя́лить)

  1. to stretch (on)
  2. (vernacular) to pull (on), to get on
  3. (vernacular, deprecative) to trick out (in), to dude up (in), to fig up , to fig out (in)

Related terms

  • пя́лить (pjálitʹ)
  • пя́льцы (pjálʹcy)
