ри́кша (28518)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈrʲikʂə]

Noun

ри́кша • (ríkša) m anim or m inan (genitive ри́кши, nominative plural ри́кши, genitive plural рикш)

  1. (animate) person pulling a rickshaw
  2. (inanimate) rickshaw
