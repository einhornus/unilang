пра́пор (21937)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic *прапоръ (*praporŭ), from Proto-Slavic *porporъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈprapər]

Noun

пра́пор • (prápor) m inan or m anim (genitive пра́пора, nominative plural пра́поры, genitive plural пра́поров)

  1. (inanimate, obsolete) regimental colours
  2. (animate, colloquial) praporshchik

Derived terms

  • пра́порщик (práporščik)
