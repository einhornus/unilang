насыпать (9785)

[nɐˈsɨpətʲ]
[nəsɨˈpatʲ]
|||||

на- (na-) + сы́пать (sýpatʹ)


насы́пать (nasýpatʹ) + -а́ть (-átʹ)


|||||

Verb

насы́пать • (nasýpatʹ) pf (imperfective сы́пать or насыпа́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew, to pour (to distribute objects or pieces of something, of friable objects, not liquids)
  2. to sputter, to pelt, to spout

Verb

насыпа́ть • (nasypátʹ) impf (perfective насы́пать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew, to pour (to distribute objects or pieces of something, of friable objects, not liquids)
  2. to sputter, to pelt, to spout

Related terms

  • на́сыпь f (násypʹ)
