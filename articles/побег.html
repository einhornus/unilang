побе́г (4020)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈbʲek]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

побе́г • (pobég) m inan (genitive побе́га, nominative plural побе́ги, genitive plural побе́гов)

  1. flight, escape
  2. sprout, shoot
