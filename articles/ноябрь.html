ноя́брь (2230)


Etymology

Old East Slavic, Old Church Slavonic ноѩбрь (nojębrĭ), from Byzantine Greek Νοέμβριος (Noémbrios), from Latin november, from novem, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁néwn̥ (“nine”) + Latin -ber from -bris, an adjectival suffix.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈjabrʲ]
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

ноя́брь • (nojábrʹ) m inan (genitive ноября́, nominative plural ноябри́, genitive plural ноябре́й)
