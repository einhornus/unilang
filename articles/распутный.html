распу́тный (31702)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈsputnɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

распу́тный • (raspútnyj)

  1. dissolute, libertine, profligate, dissipated
  2. immoral
  3. lecherous
  4. wanton, promiscuous
  5. lewd, salacious
  6. rakish
  7. reprobate
  8. abandoned (wicked, self-abandoned, given to sin)
  9. lickerish
  10. Cyprian
  11. meretricious
  12. liquorish
