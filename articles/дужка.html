ду́жка (20730)


Etymology

дуга́ (dugá) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈduʂkə]

Noun

ду́жка • (dúžka) f inan (genitive ду́жки, nominative plural ду́жки, genitive plural ду́жек)

  1. Diminutive of дуга́ (dugá)
  2. side (of spectacles)
  3. (of a bucket, vessel) bail
  4. (croquet) hoop
