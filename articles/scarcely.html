scarcely (9034)


Наре́чие

Значе́ние

  1. едва́, почти́ не
    • ‘By the bye, Bob,’ said Hopkins, with a scarcely perceptible glance at Mr. Pickwick’s attentive face, ‘we had a curious accident last night. A child was brought in, who had swallowed a necklace.’
  2. едва́, с трудо́м
  3. едва́ ли, вряд ли
    • With these words, Mr. Weller placed the pocket-book in Mr. Pickwick’s hands, caught up his hat, and ran out of the room with a celerity scarcely to be expected from so corpulent a subject.
  4. то́лько, лишь
  5. едва́, как то́лько; то́лько что
    • They had scarcely taken their seats when there was another double knock. — Едва́ они успе́ли усе́сться, как сно́ва раздался двойно́й стук в дверь.
    • Scarcely had I started out when it began to rain. — Едва́ то́лько я вы́шел, начался́ дождь.
