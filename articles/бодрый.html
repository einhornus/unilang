бо́дрый (5739)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic бъдръ (bŭdrŭ), from Proto-Slavic *bъdrъ (“alert, cheerful”), from *bъděti (“to be awake”) + *-rъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbodrɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

бо́дрый • (bódryj) (comparative (по)бодре́е or (по)бодре́й)

  1. cheerful
  2. brisk
  3. sprightly
  4. awake
  5. vigorous, vivacious

Derived terms

  • бодри́ть (bodrítʹ)
  • бо́дрость (bódrostʹ)
  • бо́дрство (bódrstvo)
