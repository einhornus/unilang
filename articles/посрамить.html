[pəsrɐˈmʲitʲ]

Etymology

по- (po-) + срами́ть (sramítʹ)

$!$$

Verb

посрами́ть • (posramítʹ) pf (imperfective посрамля́ть)

  1. to shame, to disgrace, to dishonor
  2. to expose, to debunk
