возбуждённый (3694)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vəzbʊʐˈdʲɵnːɨj]
  		• (file)

Participle

возбуждённый • (vozbuždjónnyj)

  1. past passive perfective participle of возбуди́ть (vozbudítʹ)

Adjective

возбуждённый • (vozbuždjónnyj)

  1. excited (experiencing excitement) (person, crowd, etc.; normally animate)
  2. aroused, horny
  3. excited (expressing excitement) (speech, state, etc.; normally inanimate)
