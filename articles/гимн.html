гимн (9932)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek ὕμνος (húmnos).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡʲimn]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

гимн • (gimn) m inan (genitive ги́мна, nominative plural ги́мны, genitive plural ги́мнов)

  1. anthem
  2. hymn

Related terms

  • гимнописец (gimnopisec)
