рецензе́нт (22435)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪt͡sɨn⁽ʲ⁾ˈzʲent]
  • Rhymes: -ent

Noun

рецензе́нт • (recenzént) m anim (genitive рецензе́нта, nominative plural рецензе́нты, genitive plural рецензе́нтов)

  1. reviewer, referee (an expert reviewing the work prior to publishing), reader

Related terms

  • реце́нзия (recénzija)
