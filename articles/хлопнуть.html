хло́пнуть (2618)


Etymology

Perhaps хло́пать (xlópatʹ) + -нуть (-nutʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxlopnʊtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

хло́пнуть • (xlópnutʹ) pf (imperfective хло́пать)

  1. to flap, to clap
  2. to slam (door)

Derived terms

  • See хло́пать (xlópatʹ) for derived verbs.

Related terms

  • хлопо́к (xlopók)
