су́слик (18686)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic сусолу (susolu), probably onomatopoeic of its calls; related to Bulgarian съскам (sǎskam, “I hiss”) and Old Church Slavonic свистати (svistati, “to whistle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsus⁽ʲ⁾lʲɪk]

Noun

су́слик • (súslik) m anim (genitive су́слика, nominative plural су́слики, genitive plural су́сликов)

  1. suslik, ground squirrel , spermophile, gopher
