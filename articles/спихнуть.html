спихну́ть (19069)


Etymology

с- (s-) + пихну́ть (pixnútʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [spʲɪxˈnutʲ]

Verb

спихну́ть • (spixnútʹ) pf (imperfective спи́хивать)

  1. (colloquial) to shove off (e.g. a boat from the shore)
  2. (colloquial, also figuratively) to shove together, to cause to collide or clash
  3. (low colloquial, figuratively) to kick out ; to cause to resign
  4. (colloquial, figuratively) to get rid of (something bad)
  5. (colloquial) to get (something) over and done with

Related terms

  • пиха́ть impf (pixátʹ), пихну́ть pf (pixnútʹ)
