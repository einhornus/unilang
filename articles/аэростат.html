аэроста́т (29560)


Etymology

Probably borrowed from English aerostat or French aérostat.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐɪrɐˈstat]

Noun

аэроста́т • (aerostát) m inan (genitive аэроста́та, nominative plural аэроста́ты, genitive plural аэроста́тов)

  1. balloon, aerostat

Related terms

  • аэростати́ческий (aerostatíčeskij)
