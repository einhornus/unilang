поме́стье (12656)

[pɐˈmʲesʲtʲje]
||||||||||

Noun

поме́стье • (poméstʹje) n inan (genitive поме́стья, nominative plural поме́стья, genitive plural поме́стий)

  1. estate, manor, hall, property (a manor house)
  2. land granted by the state, originally temporarily (e.g. for life), in exchange for military or civil service
