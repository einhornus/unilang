рого́жа (24201)

[rɐˈɡoʐə]
|||||

Inherited from Old East Slavic рого́жа (rogoža), from Proto-Slavic *rogozia, from *rogozъ (“reed, rush”).


|||||

Noun

рого́жа • (rogóža) f inan (genitive рого́жи, nominative plural рого́жи, genitive plural рого́ж)

  1. matting, burlap (coarse fabric)

Related terms

  • рого́з (rogóz)
