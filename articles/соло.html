со́ло (14150)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian solo.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsolə]
  • IPA(key): [ˈsolo] (uncommon)

Adverb

со́ло • (sólo)

  1. solo

Noun

со́ло • (sólo) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. (music, dance) solo

Значе́ние
