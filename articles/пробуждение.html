пробужде́ние (10253)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəbʊʐˈdʲenʲɪje]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

пробужде́ние • (probuždénije) n inan (genitive пробужде́ния, nominative plural пробужде́ния, genitive plural пробужде́ний)

  1. awakening, waking
  2. arousal (the act of arousing or the state of being aroused)

Related terms
