дуло (6854)

[ˈdulə]
|||||

ду(ть) (du(tʹ), “to blow”) + -ло (-lo)



|||||

Noun

ду́ло • (dúlo) n inan (genitive ду́ла, nominative plural ду́ла, genitive plural дул)

  1. muzzle (of a firearm)
  2. (colloquial) barrel (of a firearm)
Synonyms
  • (barrel): ствол (stvol)

Verb

ду́ло • (dúlo)

  1. neuter singular past indicative imperfective of дуть (dutʹ)
