взмыть (15190)


Etymology

Perhaps from вз- (vz-) + мыть (mytʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vzmɨtʲ]

Verb

взмыть • (vzmytʹ) pf (imperfective взмыва́ть)

  1. to rocket, to soar up upwards, to shoot upwards
