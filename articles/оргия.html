о́ргия (25273)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek ὄργια (órgia).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈorɡʲɪjə]

Noun

о́ргия • (órgija) f inan (genitive о́ргии, nominative plural о́ргии, genitive plural о́ргий)

  1. orgy

Derived terms

  • оргиасти́ческий (orgiastíčeskij)
