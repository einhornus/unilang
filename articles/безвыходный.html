безвы́ходный (14641)


Etymology

без- (bez-) + вы́ход (výxod) + -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲɪzˈvɨxədnɨj]

Adjective

безвы́ходный • (bezvýxodnyj)

  1. having no way out
  2. (figuratively) hopeless, desperate
  3. (figuratively) unalterable
