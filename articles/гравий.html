гра́вий (16254)


Etymology

From French gravier.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈɡravʲɪj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

гра́вий • (grávij) m inan (genitive гра́вия, nominative plural гра́вии, genitive plural гра́виев)

  1. gravel

Usage notes

  • The word is usually only encountered in the singular.
