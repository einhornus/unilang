экзе́ма (20564)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪɡˈzɛmə] (phonetic respelling: экзэ́ма)

Noun

экзе́ма • (ekzɛ́ma) f inan (genitive экзе́мы, nominative plural экзе́мы, genitive plural экзе́м)

  1. (pathology) eczema (acute or chronic inflammation of the skin)

Related terms

  • экзематозный (ekzɛmatoznyj)
