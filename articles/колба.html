колба (17677)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Kolben.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkolbə]

Noun

ко́лба • (kólba) f inan (genitive ко́лбы, nominative plural ко́лбы, genitive plural колб)

  1. retort, bottle
  2. envelope, flask (of a lamp)
