срез (14025)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [srʲes]

Noun

срез • (srez) m inan (genitive сре́за, nominative plural сре́зы, genitive plural сре́зов)

  1. cut (in/off)
    срез криста́лла
    srez kristálla
    crystal cut
    срез ры́нка ― srez rýnka ― market profile
  2. microscopic section
  3. (physics) shear

Related terms

  • срезать (srezatʹ)
