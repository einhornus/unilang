инспе́кция (10318)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪnˈspʲekt͡sɨjə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

инспе́кция • (inspékcija) f inan (genitive инспе́кции, nominative plural инспе́кции, genitive plural инспе́кций)

  1. inspection
  2. organisation intended to carry out inspections
