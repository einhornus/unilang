дро́тик (28309)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈdrotʲɪk]

Noun

дро́тик • (drótik) m inan (genitive дро́тика, nominative plural дро́тики, genitive plural дро́тиков)

  1. dart (sharp-pointed missile weapon)
  2. javelin, spear
