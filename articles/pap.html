pap (15697)


Etymology

Borrowed from a Slavic (probably from a South Slavic) language. Compare Bulgarian поп (pop), Serbo-Croatian pop, Russian поп (pop).[1]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpɒp]
  • Rhymes: -ɒp

Noun

pap (plural papok)

  1. priest (in Catholic terminology)
