гурма́н (22499)


Etymology

Borrowed from French gourmand.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡʊrˈman]

Noun

гурма́н • (gurmán) m anim (genitive гурма́на, nominative plural гурма́ны, genitive plural гурма́нов, feminine гурма́нка)

  1. gourmet (gourmet; a person who appreciates good food)
