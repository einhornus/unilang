зам (6273)


Etymology

Clipping of замести́тель (zamestítelʹ, “deputy, assistant”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zam]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

зам • (zam) m anim (genitive за́ма, nominative plural за́мы, genitive plural за́мов)

  1. deputy, assistant
