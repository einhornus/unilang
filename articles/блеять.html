бле́ять (24619)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *blějati (“to bleat”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- (“to bleat”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈblʲe(j)ɪtʲ]

Verb

бле́ять • (bléjatʹ) impf (perfective забле́ять)

  1. to bleat

Derived terms

  • забле́ять pf (zabléjatʹ)
  • побле́ять pf (pobléjatʹ)
  • пробле́ять pf (probléjatʹ)

Related terms

  • бле́яние (bléjanije)
