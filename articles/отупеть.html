Etymology

Verb

отупе́ть • (otupétʹ) pf (imperfective тупе́ть)

  1. to become blunt (of instruments)
  2. to grow stupid; to become mentally insensitive
    Synonym: одуре́ть (odurétʹ)

Related terms

  • отупе́ние (otupénije)
  • тупо́й (tupój)
