обурева́ть (26792)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐbʊrʲɪˈvatʲ]

Verb

обурева́ть • (oburevátʹ) impf

  1. (literary) to seize, to grip, to overcome, to overwhelm (especially of feelings)
