сверло́ (19419)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *svьrdьlo.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [svʲɪrˈlo]

Noun

сверло́ • (sverló) n inan (genitive сверла́, nominative plural свёрла, genitive plural свёрл)

  1. drill, drill bit , auger, bore (carpenter's tool)

Related terms

  • сверли́ть (sverlítʹ)
