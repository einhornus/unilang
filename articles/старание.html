стара́ние (9428)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stɐˈranʲɪje]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -anʲɪje

Noun

стара́ние • (staránije) n inan (genitive стара́ния, nominative plural стара́ния, genitive plural стара́ний)

  1. effort, endeavour (the amount of work involved in achieving something)
