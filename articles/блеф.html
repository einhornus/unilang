блеф (24120)


Etymology

Since the 1920s from English bluff.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [blʲef]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

блеф • (blef) m inan (genitive бле́фа, uncountable)

  1. bluff

Derived terms

  • блефовый (blefovyj)
