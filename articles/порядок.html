поря́док (421)


Etymology

по- (po-) + ряд (rjad) + -ок (-ok)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈrʲadək]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

поря́док • (porjádok) m inan (genitive поря́дка, nominative plural поря́дки, genitive plural поря́дков)

  1. (proper) order
  2. order, sequence
  3. order, procedure, manner, way, form
  4. order, regime, system
  5. (plural) customs, usages, observances
  6. (military) order, array
  7. (mathematics) order
