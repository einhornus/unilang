отмени́ть (4651)


Etymology

от- (ot-) + -мени́ть (-menítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtmʲɪˈnʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

отмени́ть • (otmenítʹ) pf (imperfective отменя́ть)

  1. to abolish
  2. to cancel, to countermand, to disaffirm, to reverse
  3. law to repeal, to rescind, to abrogate, to revoke, to call off

Related terms

  • отме́на (otména)
