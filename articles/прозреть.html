Etymology

про- (pro-) + зреть (zretʹ)

$!$$

Verb

прозре́ть • (prozrétʹ) pf (imperfective прозрева́ть)

  1. to recover one's sight
  2. to begin to see clearly
    ту́т-то он и прозре́л ― tút-to on i prozrél ― then his eyes were opened; he was enlightened
