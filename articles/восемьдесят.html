во́семьдесят (4233)


Etymology

Compounded from во́семь + де́сять, or eight tens. Десят was the Old East Slavic genitive plural, which is required by numbers пять and up.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvosʲɪmʲdʲɪsʲɪt]
  • IPA(key): [ˈvosʲɪmdʲɪsʲɪt] (phonetic respelling: во́семдесят)
  • audio
    		(file)

Numeral

во́семьдесят • (vósemʹdesjat)

  1. eighty (80)

Usage notes

во́семьдесят (vosemʹdesjat) in the nominative case and accusative case governs the genitive plural of the noun. In other cases, it governs the corresponding plural case of the noun.

  • во́семьдесят ма́льчиков ― vósemʹdesjat málʹčikov ― eighty boys
  • с восемью́десятью ма́льчиками ― s vosemʹjúdesjatʹju málʹčikami ― with eighty boys
  • ма́льчиков во́семьдесят ― málʹčikov vósemʹdesjat ― approximately eighty boys

Derived terms

  • восьмидесятиле́тний (vosʹmidesjatilétnij)
