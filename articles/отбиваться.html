отбива́ться (9020)

[ɐdbʲɪˈvat͡sːə]
|||||

отбива́ть (otbivátʹ) + -ся (-sja)


|||||

Verb

отбива́ться • (otbivátʹsja) impf (perfective отби́ться)

  1. to fight off , to ward off , to beat off , to repulse, to defend oneself
  2. to come off , to fall off
  3. to fall behind from a group, to get lost
  4. passive of отбива́ть (otbivátʹ)
