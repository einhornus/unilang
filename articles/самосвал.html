самосва́л (11171)


Etymology

само- (samo-) + -свал (-sval), combining form of свали́ть (svalítʹ, “to dump”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [səmɐsˈval]

Noun

самосва́л (КамАЗ-55111)

самосва́л • (samosvál) m inan (genitive самосва́ла, nominative plural самосва́лы, genitive plural самосва́лов)

  1. dump truck (a heavy truck, for carrying loose, bulk cargo, having a hinged bed that allows the contents to be unloaded onto the ground)
