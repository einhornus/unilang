шко́да (22356)


Etymology

(This etymology is missing or incomplete. Please add to it, or discuss it at the Etymology scriptorium .)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʂkodə]

Noun

шко́да • (škóda) f inan or m anim or f anim (genitive шко́ды, nominative plural шко́ды, genitive plural шко́д)

  1. mischief, annoyance, monkey business
  2. mischief-maker , rascal, trickster, scamp, imp

Related terms

  • шкодить (škoditʹ), нашко́дить (naškoditʹ)
  • шкодливый (škodlivyj)
  • шкодник (škodnik)
