зуде́ть (15235)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zʊˈdʲetʲ]

Etymology 1

Perhaps from зуд (zud) + -е́ть (-étʹ).

Verb

зуде́ть • (zudétʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to itch
Derived terms
  • раззуде́ться (razzudétʹsja)
Related terms
  • зуд (zud)

Etymology 2

Onomatopoeic. See -е́ть (-étʹ).

Verb

зуде́ть • (zudétʹ) impf

  1. to hum, to buzz (to make a droning sound)
  2. (figuratively) to nag
