ольха́ (25031)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *elьxa, compare Serbo-Croatian јо́ха, jóha.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐlʲˈxa]

Noun

ольха́ • (olʹxá) f inan (genitive ольхи́, nominative plural о́льхи, genitive plural о́льх)

  1. alder (tree or wood)

Related terms

  • ольхо́вый (olʹxovyj)
