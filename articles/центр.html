центр (583)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin centrum.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡sɛntr]
  • Audio

Noun

центр • (centr) m inan (genitive це́нтра, nominative plural це́нтры, genitive plural це́нтров)

  1. center, centre
  2. (telephony) Charlie (C in the ICAO spelling alphabet)
  3. downtown, city centre

Derived terms

Related terms
