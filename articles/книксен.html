кни́ксен (32345)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Knickschen (“diminutive of Knicks - curtsey”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈknʲiksɨn] (phonetic respelling: кни́ксэн)

Noun

кни́ксен • (kníksɛn) m inan (genitive кни́ксена, nominative plural кни́ксены, genitive plural кни́ксенов)

  1. curtsey

Synonyms

  • ревера́нс m (reveráns)
