са́дик (8522)


Etymology

сад (sad) + -ик (-ik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsadʲɪk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

са́дик • (sádik) m inan (genitive са́дика, nominative plural са́дики, genitive plural са́диков)

  1. Diminutive of сад (sad) (small) garden, orchard
  2. (colloquial) kindergarten
