либре́тто (31418)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian libretto, diminutive of libro.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈbrʲet(ː)ə]
  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈbrɛt(ː)ə] (phonetic respelling: либрэ́тто)

Noun

либре́тто • (librétto, librɛ́tto) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. libretto
