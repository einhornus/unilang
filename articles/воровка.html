воро́вка (25252)


Etymology

вор (vor) + -о́вка (-óvka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐˈrofkə]

Noun

воро́вка • (voróvka) f anim (genitive воро́вки, nominative plural воро́вки, genitive plural воро́вок, masculine вор)

  1. female thief
  2. (obsolete) female traitor, apostate, outlaw
