лампа́с (19999)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lɐmˈpas]

Noun

лампа́с • (lampás) m inan (genitive лампа́са, nominative plural лампа́сы, genitive plural лампа́сов)

  1. A colored cloth stripe sewn along the outer seam of uniform trousers, lampasse
  2. lampas
