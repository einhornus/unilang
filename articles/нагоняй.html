нагоня́й (22389)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəɡɐˈnʲæj]

Noun

нагоня́й • (nagonjáj) m inan (genitive нагоня́я, nominative plural нагоня́и, genitive plural нагоня́ев)

  1. (colloquial) telling-off, rating, scolding
    дать нагоня́й ― datʹ nagonjaj ― to give () a telling-off/rating/scolding; to rate
    получи́ть нагоня́й ― polučitʹ nagonjaj ― to get a scolding; get into hot water; get it in the neck

Verb

нагоня́й • (nagonjáj)

  1. second-person singular imperative imperfective of нагоня́ть (nagonjátʹ)
