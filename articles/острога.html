острога (26623)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐstrɐˈɡa]

Noun

острога́ • (ostrogá) f inan (genitive остроги́, nominative plural остроги́, genitive plural остро́г)

  1. fishing spear, harpoon

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈstroɡə]

Noun

остро́га • (ostróga) m inan

  1. genitive singular of остро́г (ostróg)
