форту́на (16959)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin fortūna.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fɐrˈtunə]

Noun

форту́на • (fortúna) f inan (genitive форту́ны, nominative plural форту́ны, genitive plural форту́н)

  1. fortune (good luck, fate)
