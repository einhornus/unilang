хорь (19906)

[xorʲ]
|||||

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *dъxorь (literally “ferret, stinky”), cognate to дух (dux), дохну́ть (doxnútʹ)

Noun

хорь • (xorʹ) m anim (genitive хоря́, nominative plural хори́, genitive plural хоре́й)

  1. ferret, polecat
