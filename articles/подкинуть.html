подки́нуть (9381)


Etymology

под- (pod-) + ки́нуть (kínutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐtˈkʲinʊtʲ]

Verb

подки́нуть • (podkínutʹ) pf (imperfective подки́дывать)

  1. to toss up, to throw up
  2. to throw (under)
  3. to put secretly/stealthily, to plant (fake evidence)
    подки́нуть ребёнка кому́-либо ― podkínutʹ rebjónka komú-libo ― to abandon a baby at someone's door
  4. to add
    подки́нуть дро́в в пе́чку ― podkínutʹ drov v péčku ― to throw more (fire)wood on the fire
  5. (colloquial) to give a lift
  6. (colloquial) to bring up, to give some more
    подки́нуть деньжа́т ― podkínutʹ denʹžát ― to give some more money
  7. to add (a card) to be beaten

Related terms

  • подки́дыш m (podkídyš)
  • подкидно́й (podkidnój)
