делец (20276)


Etymology

де́лать (délatʹ) + -е́ц (-éc)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪˈlʲet͡s]

Noun

деле́ц • (deléc) m anim (genitive дельца́, nominative plural дельцы́, genitive plural дельцо́в)

  1. (usually derogatory) businessman, moneymaker, operator
