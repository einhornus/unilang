прили́чие (9487)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪˈlʲit͡ɕɪje]

Noun

прили́чие • (prilíčije) n inan (genitive прили́чия, nominative plural прили́чия, genitive plural прили́чий)

  1. decency, propriety, decorum, convenances

Related terms
