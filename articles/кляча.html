кля́ча (21881)

[ˈklʲæt͡ɕə]
||||||||||

Noun

кля́ча • (kljáča) f anim (genitive кля́чи, nominative plural кля́чи, genitive plural кляч)

  1. jade, old or worn-out horse
  2. (of a woman) jade, hag
