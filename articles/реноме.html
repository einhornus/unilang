реноме́ (30935)


Etymology

Borrowed from French renommée.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪnɐˈmɛ] (phonetic respelling: реномэ́)
  • IPA(key): [rɨnɐˈmɛ] (phonetic respelling: рэномэ́)

Noun

реноме́ • (renomɛ́, rɛnomɛ́) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. reputation, fame

Synonyms

  • репута́ция (reputácija), сла́ва (sláva)
