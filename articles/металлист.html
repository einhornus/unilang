металли́ст (29105)

[mʲɪtɐˈlʲist]
||||||||||

Noun

металли́ст • (metallíst) m anim (genitive металли́ста, nominative plural металли́сты, genitive plural металли́стов)

  1. metalworker
  2. specialist in metals or metallurgy
  3. (music) musician who plays metal; member of a metal band
  4. (music) metalhead, headbanger
