помо́щница (16695)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈmoʂnʲɪt͡sə] (phonetic respelling: помо́шница)

Noun

помо́щница • (pomóščnica) f anim (genitive помо́щницы, nominative plural помо́щницы, genitive plural помо́щниц, masculine помо́щник)

  1. female associate, assistant, deputy
  2. female helper, aid
  3. female helpmate
