подво́д (23209)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐdˈvot]
  • Rhymes: -ot

Etymology 1

Deverbal of подводи́ть (podvodítʹ).

Noun

подво́д • (podvód) m inan (genitive подво́да, nominative plural подво́ды, genitive plural подво́дов)

  1. supply, delivery
  2. admission, intake

Etymology 2

Noun

подво́д • (podvód) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of подво́да (podvóda)
