таве́рна (20027)


Etymology

Borrowed from French taverne.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐˈvʲernə]

Noun

таве́рна • (tavérna) f inan (genitive таве́рны, nominative plural таве́рны, genitive plural таве́рн)

  1. (dated or foreign context) tavern, bar, pub
