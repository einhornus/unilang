популя́ция (22352)


Etymology

Borrowed from Late Latin populatio, from Latin populus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəpʊˈlʲat͡sɨjə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

популя́ция • (populjácija) f inan (genitive популя́ции, nominative plural популя́ции, genitive plural популя́ций)

  1. population (usually of animals)
