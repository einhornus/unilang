би́тва (4135)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic *би́тва (*bitva), from Proto-Slavic *bitva, from *biti + *-tva . Equivalent to бить (bitʹ) + -тва (-tva).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbʲitvə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

би́тва • (bítva) f inan (genitive би́твы, nominative plural би́твы, genitive plural битв)

  1. battle, fight, combat

Related terms

  • бить (bitʹ), би́ться (bítʹsja)
