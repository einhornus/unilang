талму́д (21495)


Etymology

Borrowed from Hebrew תלמוד (talmud).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐlˈmut]

Noun

талму́д • (talmúd) m inan (genitive талму́да, nominative plural талму́ды, genitive plural талму́дов)

  1. Talmud
  2. (colloquial) a bulky and thick book
