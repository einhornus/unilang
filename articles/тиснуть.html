ти́снуть (27267)

[ˈtʲisnʊtʲ]
|||||

Perhaps from ти́скать (tískatʹ) + -нуть (-nutʹ).


|||||$!$!e

Etymology

Perhaps from ти́скать (tískatʹ) + -нуть (-nutʹ).

$!$$

Verb

ти́снуть • (tísnutʹ) pf (imperfective ти́скать)

  1. to squeeze once
  2. to squeeze
  3. to push, to shove once
  4. to push, to shove
  5. (colloquial) to publish once, to print once
  6. (colloquial) to publish, to print
  7. (colloquial) to filch, to pilfer, to swipe, to walk off, to walk away (with), to steal

$!$!d

Derived terms

  • See ти́скать (tískatʹ) for derived verbs.
  • тиснёный (tisnjónyj), тисне́ние (tisnénije)
