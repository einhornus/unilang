соне́т (20202)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian sonetto.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈnʲet]
  • IPA(key): [sɐˈnɛt] (phonetic respelling: сонэ́т)

Noun

соне́т • (sonét, sonɛ́t) m inan (genitive соне́та, nominative plural соне́ты, genitive plural соне́тов)

  1. sonnet
