караби́н (6706)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kərɐˈbʲin]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -in

Noun

караби́н • (karabín) m inan (genitive караби́на, nominative plural караби́ны, genitive plural караби́нов)

  1. carbine (weapon)
  2. carabiner (metal link with a gate)
