вар (12722)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic варъ (varŭ, “boiled liquid”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [var]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ar

Noun

вар • (var) m inan (genitive ва́ра, uncountable)

  1. tar, pitch
  2. cobbler's wax
  3. (colloquial) boiling water

Related terms

  • вари́ть (varítʹ)
