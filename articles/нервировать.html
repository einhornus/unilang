нерви́ровать (24996)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪrˈvʲirəvətʲ]

Verb

нерви́ровать • (nervírovatʹ) impf

  1. to make nervous, to unnerve
  2. to irritate
  3. to fidget
  4. to get on someone's nerves
