ка́рцер (10203)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin carcer via German Karzer.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkart͡sɨr]

Noun

ка́рцер • (kárcer) m inan (genitive ка́рцера, nominative plural ка́рцеры, genitive plural ка́рцеров)

  1. lockup; punishment cell; sweatbox, isolation ward
