четвёрка (8689)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡ɕɪtˈvʲɵrkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

четвёрка • (četvjórka) f inan (genitive четвёрки, nominative plural четвёрки, genitive plural четвёрок)

  1. (colloquial) four (digit)
  2. (group of) four
  3. (mark) four (out of five), B mark , B grade
    ста́влю тебе́ за э́то четвёрку
    stávlju tebé za éto četvjórku
    I give you four out of five for this
    он получи́л четвёрку по исто́рии
    on polučíl četvjórku po istórii
    he got a B for history
    на четвёрку ― na četvjórku ― rather well , quite well
  4. (card games ) four
    четвёрка черве́й ― četvjórka červéj ― the four of hearts
  5. No 4 bus (tram, etc)
  6. four-in-hand , team of four horses
  7. (boat) four
  8. (military, aviation) flight of four aircrafts
