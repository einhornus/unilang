мле́чный (16559)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *melčьnъ; synchronically, from мле́ко (mléko) + -ный (-nyj). Doublet of моло́чный (molóčnyj).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmlʲet͡ɕnɨj]

Adjective

мле́чный • (mléčnyj)

  1. (poetic, literary or dated) milky

Derived terms

  • мле́чный Путь (Mléčnyj Putʹ, “Milky Way”)
