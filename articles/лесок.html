лесок (6627)


Etymology 1

лес (les) + -ок (-ok)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈsok]

Noun

лесо́к • (lesók) m inan (genitive леска́, nominative plural лески́, genitive plural леско́в)

  1. grove, small wood
Related terms
  • лес (les)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲesək]

Noun

ле́сок • (lésok) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of ле́ска (léska)
