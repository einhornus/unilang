вы́манить (22338)

[ˈvɨmənʲɪtʲ]
||||||||||

Verb

вы́манить • (výmanitʹ) pf (imperfective выма́нивать)

  1. to beckon
  2. to wave (someone) away
  3. (colloquial) to manipulate, to wrap around one's little finger
