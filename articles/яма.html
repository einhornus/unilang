я́ма (2213)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈjamə]

Etymology 1

Inherited from Old East Slavic я́ма (jama), from Proto-Slavic *ama

Noun

я́ма • (jáma) f inan (genitive я́мы, nominative plural я́мы, genitive plural я́м)

  1. pit, hole (in the ground)
  2. dungeon
Derived terms
  • оркестро́вая я́ма f (orkestróvaja jáma)

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Sanskrit यम (yama).

Noun

я́ма • (jáma) f inan (indeclinable)

  1. yama

Etymology 3

Noun

я́ма • (jáma) m inan

  1. genitive singular of я́м (jam)
