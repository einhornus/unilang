ва́кса (30401)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Wachs.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvaksə]

Noun

ва́кса • (váksa) f inan (genitive ва́ксы, nominative plural ва́ксы, genitive plural ва́кс)

  1. shoe polish

Related terms

  • ваксить (vaksitʹ), наваксить (navaksitʹ)
