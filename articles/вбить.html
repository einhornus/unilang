вбить (9193)


Etymology

в- (v-) + бить (bitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [v⁽ʲ⁾bʲitʲ]

Verb

вби́ть • (vbítʹ) pf (imperfective вбива́ть)

  1. to drive in, to hammer in (also figuratively)
