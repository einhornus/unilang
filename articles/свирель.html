свирель (31555)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic свирѣль (svirělĭ), from Proto-Slavic *svirělь, from *svirati + *-ělь , ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *swer- (“to buzz, hum”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [svʲɪˈrʲelʲ]

Noun

свире́ль • (svirélʹ) f inan (genitive свире́ли, nominative plural свире́ли, genitive plural свире́лей)

  1. pipe, svirel (musical instrument)

Derived terms

  • свире́лка (svirélka)
  • свире́льник (svirélʹnik)
  • свире́льный (svirélʹnyj)
  • свире́льщик (svirélʹščik)

Related terms

  • свиристе́ть (sviristétʹ)
