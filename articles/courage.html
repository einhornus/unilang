courage (3907)


Etymology

cœur + -age or Middle French corage, from Old French corage, from Vulgar Latin *coraticum, from Latin cor.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ku.ʁaʒ/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

courage m (plural courages)

  1. courage
    Synonym: bravoure

Derived terms

  • courageux
    • courageusement
  • décourager
    • décourageant
    • découragement
  • encourager
    • encourageant
    • encouragement

Interjection

courage !

  1. chin up! keep going! take heart!

Usage notes

"bon courage !" has a slightly different meaning: "good luck!".
