мула́т (28458)


Etymology

From Spanish mulato or Portuguese mulato.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʊˈlat]

Noun

мула́т • (mulát) m anim (genitive мула́та, nominative plural мула́ты, genitive plural мула́тов, feminine мула́тка)

  1. mulatto
