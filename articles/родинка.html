ро́динка (13236)


Etymology

ро́дина (ródina) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈrodʲɪnkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ро́динка • (ródinka) f inan (genitive ро́динки, nominative plural ро́динки, genitive plural ро́динок)

  1. (anatomy) birthmark, mole, melanism, nevus (naevus) (dark spot on the skin)
