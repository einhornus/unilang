Etymology

Probably borrowed from German signalisieren + -овать (-ovatʹ); see also -и́ровать (-írovatʹ).

$!$$

Verb

сигнализи́ровать • (signalizírovatʹ) impf or pf (perfective сигнализи́ровать or просигнализи́ровать)

  1. to signal, to indicate (+ accusative or о (o) + prepositional or + clause, something; + dative, to someone/something)
  2. to warn
