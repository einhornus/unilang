сою́з (707)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic съѭзъ (sŭjǫzŭ). Compare у́зы (úzy).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈjus]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

сою́з • (sojúz) m inan (genitive сою́за, nominative plural сою́зы, genitive plural сою́зов)

  1. union
  2. alliance
  3. confederation, confederacy
  4. league
  5. (grammar) conjunction

Related terms

  • сою́зный (sojúznyj)
  • сою́зник (sojúznik), сою́зница (sojúznica)
