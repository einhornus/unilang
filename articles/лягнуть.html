лягну́ть (28005)


Etymology

ляга́ть (ljagátʹ) + -ну́ть (-nútʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪɡˈnutʲ]

Verb

лягну́ть • (ljagnútʹ) pf (imperfective ляга́ть)

  1. to kick
  2. (colloquial) to knock, to let out (at), to jump (on) (to tell something offensive of someone)

Synonyms

  • пнуть (pnutʹ)

Related terms

  • ляга́ться (ljagátʹsja)
