Etymology 1

у- (u-) + сы́пать (sýpatʹ)

$!$$

Verb

усы́пать • (usýpatʹ) pf (imperfective усыпа́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew (with), to bestrew (with)
    не́бо усы́пано звёздами ― nébo usýpano zvjózdami ― the sky is studded with stars
