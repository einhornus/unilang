брю́ква (26128)


Etymology

A regional wanderwort, see German Wruke.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbrʲukvə]

Noun

брю́ква • (brjúkva) f inan (genitive брю́квы, nominative plural брю́квы, genitive plural брю́кв)

  1. swede, rutabaga (Brassica napus, sometimes Brassica napus rapifera, plant and vegetable)
