р (1025)


Pronunciation

  • (phoneme) IPA(key): [r]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Letter

р • (r) (lower case, upper case Р)

  1. The eighteenth letter of the Russian Cyrillic alphabet.

Usage notes

Its name is эр (er) and it has the trilled sound of a Spanish r.
