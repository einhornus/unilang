фи́лин (6894)


Etymology

The origin is uncertain. Has been compared to Ukrainian квилити (kvylyty, “to groan, to moan”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈfʲilʲɪn]

Noun

фи́лин • (fílin) m anim (genitive фи́лина, nominative plural фи́лины, genitive plural фи́линов)

  1. eagle owl
