торча́ть (1360)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐrˈt͡ɕætʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

торча́ть • (torčátʹ) impf

  1. to stick out, to protrude, to jut out
  2. to stand on end, to bristle (of hair)
  3. to loiter, to stick around, to hang around
  4. (slang) to feel a euphoria, to get a kick, to get high

Derived terms
