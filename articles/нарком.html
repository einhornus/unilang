нарко́м (6744)


Etymology

Acronym of наро́дный комисса́р (naródnyj komissár).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐrˈkom]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

нарко́м • (narkóm) m anim (genitive нарко́ма, nominative plural нарко́мы, genitive plural нарко́мов)

  1. (historical) people's commissar
