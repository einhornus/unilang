чмо́кнуть (14000)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈt͡ɕmoknʊtʲ]

Verb

чмо́кнуть • (čmóknutʹ) pf (imperfective чмо́кать)

  1. (colloquial) to peck (to kiss)
  2. (colloquial) to smack (with the lips)
