старик (316)


Etymology

From старий (starij). Cognates include Czech stařík.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stɐˈrʲik]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

стари́к • (starík) m anim (genitive старика́, nominative plural старики́, genitive plural старико́в, feminine стару́ха)

  1. old man
  2. (slang) old-timer, that man who has worked or served somewhere more than his younger colleagues
  3. (colloquial) father, foozle
  4. (colloquial) elderly husband for a wife
  5. (colloquial, address) chap

Derived terms

  • старичо́к m (staričók)
  • ста́рческий (stárčeskij)
