тракти́р (8942)


Etymology

From Polish traktjer, from Latin tractōria. Compare Italian trattoria.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [trɐkˈtʲir]

Noun

тракти́р • (traktír) m inan (genitive тракти́ра, nominative plural тракти́ры, genitive plural тракти́ров)

  1. inn; hotel (somewhat dated)
  2. tavern; pub
