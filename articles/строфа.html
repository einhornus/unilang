строфа́ (18054)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin stropha, itself a borrowing from Ancient Greek στροφή (strophḗ, “a turn, bend, twist”).


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [strɐˈfa]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

строфа́ • (strofá) f inan (genitive строфы́, nominative plural стро́фы, genitive plural стро́ф)

  1. stanza (a unit of a poem)
  2. strophe
  3. verse

Synonyms

  • станс (stans)

Related terms

  • строфический (strofičeskij)
