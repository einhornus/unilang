вертел (8086)


Etymology 1

From верте́ть (vertétʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʲertʲɪl]

Noun

ве́ртел • (vértel) m inan (genitive ве́ртела, nominative plural вертела́, genitive plural вертело́в)

  1. spit, skewer
Synonyms
  • шампу́р (šampúr)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪrˈtʲel]

Verb

верте́л • (vertél)

  1. masculine singular past indicative imperfective of верте́ть (vertétʹ, “to turn, to twist”)
