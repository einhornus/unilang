изрека́ть (31278)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *reťi

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪzrʲɪˈkatʲ]

Verb

изрека́ть • (izrekátʹ) impf (perfective изре́чь)

  1. (poetic, archaic) to utter, to speak solemnly / pompously
    изрека́ть му́дрое сло́во ― izrekátʹ múdroje slóvo ― to utter a word of wisdom
  2. (colloquial) to mouth, to say
