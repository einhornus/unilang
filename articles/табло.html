табло́ (15770)


Etymology

Borrowed from French tableau.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐˈblo]

Noun

табло́ • (tabló) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. indicator board, panel, tableau
  2. scoreboard (on a stadium)
