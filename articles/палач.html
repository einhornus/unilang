пала́ч (5826)


Etymology

Often ascribed to Turkish раlа (“sword”) + -ач (-ač) or *раlаcı (“sword bearer”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlat͡ɕ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

пала́ч • (paláč) m anim (genitive палача́, nominative plural палачи́, genitive plural палаче́й)

  1. (also figuratively) executioner, hangman
