захвора́ть (22943)


Etymology

за- (za-) + хвора́ть (xvorátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəxvɐˈratʲ]

Verb

захвора́ть • (zaxvorátʹ) pf (imperfective хвора́ть)

  1. (colloquial) to become sick, to fall ill
  2. (colloquial) to contract, to come down with (an illness; + instrumental)

Related terms

  • хво́рый (xvóryj)
  • хворь (xvorʹ)
