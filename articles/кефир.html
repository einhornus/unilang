кефи́р (10447)


Etymology

Borrowed into Russian from a language of the Caucausus; compare Mingrelian ქიფური (kipuri), Ossetian къӕпы (k’æpy), Karachay-Balkar гыпы. The ultimate origin is unknown.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): [kʲɪˈfʲir]

Noun

кефи́р • (kefír) m inan (genitive кефи́ра, nominative plural кефи́ры, genitive plural кефи́ров)

  1. kefir (a yogurt-like beverage made from fermented cows' milk)
    Я про́сто обожа́ю кефи́р, но зато́ терпе́ть не могу́ молоко́! ― Ja prósto obožáju kefír, no zató terpétʹ ne mogú molokó! ― I just love kefir, but, on the other hand , I hate milk!
