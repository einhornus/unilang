зве́рски (15888)

[ˈzvʲerskʲɪ]
||||||||||

Adverb

зве́рски • (zvérski)

  1. monstrously, beastly, cruelly
  2. (colloquial) awfully, terribly
    он зве́рски го́лоден ― on zvérski góloden ― he is ravenous
    он зве́рски уста́л ― on zvérski ustál ― he is terribly tired; he is dead beat
