принц (2817)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Prinz, from French prince, from Latin princeps (“first head”), from primus (“first”) + capio (“I take”), literally, "one who takes the first part".

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲint͡s]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

принц • (princ) m anim (genitive при́нца, nominative plural при́нцы, genitive plural при́нцев, feminine принце́сса)

  1. prince, crown prince (male ruler or head of a principality)

Related terms

  • принце́сса (princéssa)
