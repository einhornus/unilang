ико́нка (21899)


Etymology

ико́на (ikóna) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪˈkonkə]

Noun

ико́нка • (ikónka) f inan (genitive ико́нки, nominative plural ико́нки, genitive plural ико́нок)

  1. Diminutive of ико́на (ikóna): (a small) icon (religious painting)
  2. (computing) icon (small picture)
