продро́гнуть (18156)


Etymology

про- (pro-) + дро́гнуть (drógnutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈdroɡnʊtʲ]

Verb

продро́гнуть • (prodrógnutʹ) pf (imperfective дро́гнуть)

  1. to be cold, to become frozen, to feel chilly; to freeze, to numb (by coldness), to shiver
