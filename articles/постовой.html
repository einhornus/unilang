постово́й (31099)


Etymology

пост (post) + -ово́й (-ovój)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəstɐˈvoj]

Adjective

постово́й • (postovój)

  1. on duty
  2. (relational) sentry, guard
    постова́я бу́дка ― postovája búdka ― sentry booth
  3. (relational) duty

Noun

постово́й • (postovój) m anim (genitive постово́го, nominative plural постовы́е, genitive plural постовы́х)

  1. sentry, guard, man on duty

Related terms

  • пост (post)

Значе́ние
