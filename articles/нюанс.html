нюа́нс (14006)


Etymology

Borrowed from French nuance (“nuance, shade, hue”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲʊˈans]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

нюа́нс • (njuáns) m inan (genitive нюа́нса, nominative plural нюа́нсы, genitive plural нюа́нсов)

  1. nuance, shade
