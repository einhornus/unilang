Etymology

стла́ть (stlátʹ) + -ся (-sja)

$!$$

Verb

стла́ться • (stlátʹsja) impf

  1. (intransitive) to spread, to spread out, to sprawl
  2. to lie on the ground
  3. to creep (of a plant)
  4. to speed over a surface (of animals and birds)
  5. (colloquial) to prepare for bed
    Perfective: постла́ться (postlátʹsja)
  6. passive of стла́ть (stlátʹ)
