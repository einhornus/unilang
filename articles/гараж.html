гара́ж (3515)


Etymology

Borrowed from French garage.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡɐˈraʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -aʂ

Noun

гара́ж • (garáž) m inan (genitive гаража́, nominative plural гаражи́, genitive plural гараже́й)

  1. garage
