кандида́т (2425)


Etymology

Borrowed from Polish kandydat.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kənʲdʲɪˈdat]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -at

Noun

кандида́т • (kandidát) m anim (genitive кандида́та, nominative plural кандида́ты, genitive plural кандида́тов, feminine кандида́тка)

  1. candidate (person running in an election)
  2. (informal) Candidate of Science (similar to a Ph.D.)

Derived terms

  • кандида́т нау́к (kandidát naúk)

Related terms

  • кандида́тский (kandidátskij)
