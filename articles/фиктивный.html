фикти́вный (13393)

[fʲɪkˈtʲivnɨj]
|||||

Perhaps from German fiktiv + -ный (-nyj).


|||||

Adjective

фикти́вный • (fiktívnyj)

  1. fictional, fictitious
  2. forged, counterfeit, fake, sham

Related terms
