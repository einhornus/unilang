российский (503)


Etymology

Росси́я (Rossíja) + -и́йский (-íjskij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈsʲijskʲɪj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

росси́йский • (rossíjskij)

  1. Russian (concerning the country, nationality)

Usage notes

The word росси́йский emphasizes belonging to Russia, as a citizen or resident regardless of ethnicity, as opposed to ру́сский (rússkij), which indicates ethnicity, etc. In some cases the words may be used interchangeably.

Related terms

  • всеросси́йский (vserossíjskij)
  • общеросси́йский (obščerossíjskij)
