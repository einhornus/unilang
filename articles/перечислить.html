перечи́слить (8961)


Etymology

пере- (pere-) + чи́слить (číslitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪrʲɪˈt͡ɕis⁽ʲ⁾lʲɪtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

перечи́слить • (perečíslitʹ) pf (imperfective перечисля́ть)

  1. to enumerate, to list (to specify each member of a sequence individually in incrementing order)
  2. to transfer (funds, wages)
