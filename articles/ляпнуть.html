ля́пнуть (12796)


Etymology

ляп (ljap) + -нуть (-nutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲapnʊtʲ]

Verb

ля́пнуть • (ljápnutʹ) pf (imperfective ля́пать)

  1. (colloquial, derogatory) to blab, to blurt out
  2. (colloquial, derogatory) to hit, to whack, to smack
  3. (colloquial, qualification to verb сказа́л, derogatory) to straight from the shoulder

Related terms

  • ляп (ljap)
  • тяп-ля́п (tjap-ljáp)
