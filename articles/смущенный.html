смущённый (8131)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [smʊˈɕːɵnːɨj]

Participle

смущённый • (smuščónnyj)

  1. past passive perfective participle of смути́ть (smutítʹ)

Adjective

смущённый • (smuščónnyj)

  1. embarrassed, confused (experiencing embarrassment or confusion) (usually of a person)
  2. embarrassed, confused (expressing embarrassment or confusion) (look, etc; usually inanimate)
