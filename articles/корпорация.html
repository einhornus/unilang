корпора́ция (4398)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kərpɐˈrat͡sɨjə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

корпора́ция • (korporácija) f inan (genitive корпора́ции, nominative plural корпора́ции, genitive plural корпора́ций, related adjective корпорати́вный)

  1. (economics) corporation, company
  2. association, organization (group of people with a common interest)
