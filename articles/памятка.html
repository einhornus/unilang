па́мятка (30411)


Etymology

па́мять (pámjatʹ) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpamʲɪtkə]

Noun

па́мятка • (pámjatka) f inan (genitive па́мятки, nominative plural па́мятки, genitive plural па́мяток)

  1. memo, memorandum
  2. instruction, written rules, instruction sheet
