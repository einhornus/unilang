люкс (14099)

[lʲuks]
||||||||||

Noun

люкс • (ljuks) m inan (genitive лю́кса, nominative plural лю́ксы, genitive plural лю́ксов)

  1. lux (unit of light)

Adjective

люкс • (ljuks) (indeclinable)

  1. deluxe
    но́мер люкс ― nómer ljuks ― deluxe suite

Usage notes

  • Usually used in the post-position.
