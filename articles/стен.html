стен (1573)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from French sthène, from Ancient Greek σθένος (sthénos, “strength”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stɛn] (phonetic respelling: стэн)

Noun

стен • (stɛn) m inan (genitive сте́на, nominative plural сте́ны, genitive plural сте́нов)

  1. (obsolete) sthène (unit of measure)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲtʲen]

Noun

стен • (sten) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of стена́ (stená)
