пюре́ (18942)


Etymology

Borrowed from French purée.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲʊˈrɛ] (phonetic respelling: пюрэ́)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

пюре́ • (pjurɛ́) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. purée
  2. mashed potato
