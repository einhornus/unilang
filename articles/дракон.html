драко́н (4155)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek δράκων (drákōn).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [drɐˈkon]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

драко́н • (drakón) m anim (genitive драко́на, nominative plural драко́ны, genitive plural драко́нов)

  1. dragon (mythical creature)

Synonyms

  • змей (zmej)
