то́нко (7134)


Etymology

то́нкий (tónkij) + -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtonkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

то́нко • (tónko) (comparative (по)то́ньше)

  1. thinly (not thickly)
  2. subtly
  3. delicately, finely (e.g. of produced workmanship)

Adjective

то́нко • (tónko)

  1. short neuter singular of то́нкий (tónkij)
