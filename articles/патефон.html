патефо́н (11956)

[pətʲɪˈfon]
|||||

From the French Pathéphone (trade mark) for a phonograph made by the Pathé Brothers Company.


|||||

Noun

патефо́н • (patefón) m inan (genitive патефо́на, nominative plural патефо́ны, genitive plural патефо́нов)

  1. (historical) portable gramophone, phonograph
