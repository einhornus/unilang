листа́ть (6199)


Etymology

лист (list) + -а́ть (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈstatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

листа́ть • (listátʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial) to leaf through, to flip through (a book, manuscript, etc.)
  2. (colloquial) to skim, to browse (a book, manuscript, etc.)
