желе́зо (2086)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic желѣзо (želězo), from Proto-Slavic *želězo.

Pronunciation

Noun

желе́зо • (želézo) n inan (genitive желе́за, nominative plural желе́за, genitive plural желе́з)

  1. iron
  2. any iron-like metal
  3. (computer slang) hardware
