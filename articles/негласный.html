негла́сный (15487)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪˈɡlasnɨj]

Adjective

негла́сный • (neglásnyj)

  1. unspoken, unwritten, oral, implicit (implicit or understood but not formally articulated)
    негла́сное пра́вило ― neglásnoje právilo ― unspoken rule, unwritten rule, oral law
