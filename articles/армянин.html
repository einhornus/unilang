армяни́н (10608)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic армѣнинъ (arměninŭ), рамѣнинъ (raměninŭ), from Ancient Greek Ἀρμένιος (Arménios, “Armenian”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐrmʲɪˈnʲin]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

армяни́н • (armjanín) m anim (genitive армяни́на, nominative plural армя́не, genitive plural армя́н, feminine армя́нка)

  1. Armenian (citizen, resident, or of heritage)

Related terms

  • арме́ния f (Arménija)
  • армя́нский (armjánskij)
