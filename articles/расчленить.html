расчлени́ть (27873)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rəɕt͡ɕlʲɪˈnʲitʲ]

Verb

расчлени́ть • (rasčlenítʹ) pf (imperfective расчленя́ть)

  1. to dismember
  2. to divide, to break up
  3. (military) to break up, to open out, to break down

Related terms

  • расчлене́ние (rasčlenénije)
