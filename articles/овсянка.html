овся́нка (29925)

[ɐfˈsʲankə]
||||||||||

Noun

овся́нка • (ovsjánka) f inan or f anim (genitive овся́нки, nominative plural овся́нки, genitive plural овся́нок)

  1. (inanimate) oatmeal (cereal)
  2. (inanimate) oatmeal porridge, gruel
  3. (animate) bunting, yellow bunting, yellowhammer
