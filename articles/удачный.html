уда́чный (4779)


Etymology

уда́ча (udáča) + -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈdat͡ɕnɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

уда́чный • (udáčnyj)

  1. successful
  2. fortunate
  3. lucky, prosperous
  4. felicitous, happy
  5. fortuitous
  6. chancy
  7. apposite (appropriate, relevant, well-suited)
  8. well-turned
