Verb

мотну́ть • (motnútʹ) pf (imperfective мота́ть)

  1. to shake
  2. (colloquial) to go on an outing (to a place)

Derived terms

  • мота́ться impf (motátʹsja), мотну́ться pf (motnútʹsja)
