прое́зжий (9129)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈjeʐːɨj], [prɐˈjeʑːɪj]

Adjective

прое́зжий • (projézžij)

  1. passing by, through (traveller)
  2. fit, used for traffic
    прое́зжая часть ― projézžaja častʹ ― carriageway
