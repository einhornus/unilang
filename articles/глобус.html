гло́бус (11029)


Etymology

First attested in 1697. Borrowed from Latin globus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈɡlobʊs]

Noun

гло́бус • (glóbus) m inan (genitive гло́буса, nominative plural гло́бусы, genitive plural гло́бусов)

  1. globe (model of Earth)

Derived terms

  • гло́бусный (glóbusnyj)
