[ʂɐˈlaʂ]
|||||

|||||

Noun

шала́ш • (šaláš) m inan (genitive шалаша́, nominative plural шалаши́, genitive plural шалаше́й)

  1. a simple wooden hut
  2. (criminal slang) the bottom bunk of a bunk bed , with a blanket hanging down from the top bunk as a curtain

Related terms

  • шала́шник (šalášnik)
  • шалашо́вка (šalašóvka)
