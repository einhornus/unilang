пролета́ть (7892)


Etymology

про- (pro-) + лета́ть (letátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəlʲɪˈtatʲ]

Verb

пролета́ть • (proletátʹ) impf (perfective пролете́ть)

  1. to fly (by, past, through), to fly (over)
  2. to overfly, to overshoot
    пролета́ть ми́мо це́ли ― proletátʹ mímo céli ― to overshoot the target
  3. to cover, to pass rapidly
  4. (time) to fly by
  5. (colloquial) to be passed over (for), to miss the mark, to miss the boat
    пролета́ть как фане́ра над пари́жем
    proletátʹ kak fanéra nad Parížem
    miss the mark, miss the boat
    (literally, “to fly like plywood over Paris”)

Related terms

  • пролёт (proljót), пролётка (proljótka)
