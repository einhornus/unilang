солида́рный (28426)


Etymology

Perhaps from German solidar(isch) + -ный (-nyj).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [səlʲɪˈdarnɨj]

Adjective

солида́рный • (solidárnyj)

  1. united, as one, solidary
  2. in full agreement (с (s) with)

Derived terms

  • солида́рность f (solidárnostʹ)

Related terms

  • солидаризи́роваться impf (solidarizírovatʹsja)
