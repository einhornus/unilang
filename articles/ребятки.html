ребя́тки (12311)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲɪˈbʲatkʲɪ]

Noun

ребя́тки • (rebjátki) n anim pl (genitive ребя́ток, plural only )

  1. Diminutive of ребя́та (rebjáta) children, kids; lads, boys

Synonyms

  • (children): де́тки (détki)
