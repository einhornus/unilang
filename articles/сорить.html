сори́ть (18224)

[sɐˈrʲitʲ]
||||||||||

Verb

сори́ть • (sorítʹ) impf (perfective насори́ть)

  1. to litter (drop or throw trash without properly disposing of it)
  2. (figuratively) to waste something (e.g. money) recklessly

Synonyms

  • му́сорить (músoritʹ)
