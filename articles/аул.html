ау́л (14091)


Etymology

From West (Kypchak) Turkic awul, awïl; compare Karachay-Balkar ауул (awul), Bashkir ауыл (awïl), Kazakh ауыл (awıl) and Turkish ağıl.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈul]

Noun

ау́л • (aúl) m inan (genitive ау́ла, nominative plural ау́лы, genitive plural ау́лов)

  1. aul (a type of village in the Caucasus or Central Asia )
