перебо́рка (21630)


Etymology

Deverbal of перебра́ть (perebrátʹ) + -ка (-ka), based on the nominal combining stem перебор- (perebor-), derived from the present tense stem перебер- (pereber-) with Indo-European o-vocalization characteristic of deverbal nouns.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪrʲɪˈborkə]

Noun

перебо́рка • (perebórka) f inan (genitive перебо́рки, nominative plural перебо́рки, genitive plural перебо́рок)

  1. (colloquial) sorting through
  2. (colloquial) sorting out
  3. (colloquial) collecting of disassembled parts (for the purpose of repair)
  4. (colloquial) bulkhead, partition

Related terms

  • перебира́ть impf (perebirátʹ), перебра́ть pf (perebrátʹ)
  • перебира́ться impf (perebirátʹsja), перебра́ться pf (perebrátʹsja)
