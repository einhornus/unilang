Deverbal of перебра́ть (perebrátʹ) + -ка (-ka), based on the nominal combining stem перебо́р- (perebor-), derived from the present tense stem перебер- (pereber-) with Indo-European o-vocalization characteristic of deverbal nouns.

Etymology

Deverbal of перебра́ть (perebrátʹ) + -ка (-ka), based on the nominal combining stem перебо́р- (perebor-), derived from the present tense stem перебер- (pereber-) with Indo-European o-vocalization characteristic of deverbal nouns.

$!$$

Noun

перебо́рка • (perebórka) f inan (genitive перебо́рки, nominative plural перебо́рки, genitive plural перебо́рок)

  1. (colloquial) sorting through
  2. (colloquial) sorting out
  3. (colloquial) collecting of disassembled parts (for the purpose of repair)
  4. (colloquial) bulkhead, partition
