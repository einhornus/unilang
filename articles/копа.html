Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *kopa.

$!$$

Noun

копа́ • (kopá) f inan (genitive копы́, nominative plural ко́пы, genitive plural коп)

  1. (regional) sixty pieces of something (e.g. eggs, cucumbers, sheaves of hay or rye, etc.)
