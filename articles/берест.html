берест (22231)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *berstъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbʲerʲɪst]

Noun

бе́рест • (bérest) m inan (genitive бе́реста, nominative plural бе́ресты, genitive plural бе́рестов)

  1. elm (tree or wood) (Ulmus minor )
