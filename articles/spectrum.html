spectrum (3710)


Etymology

From spec(iō) (“look at, behold”) + -trum. Confer with speculum.

Pronunciation

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈspek.trum/, [ˈspɛk.t̪rʊ̃ˑ]
  • (Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈspek.trum/, [ˈspɛk.t̪rum]

Noun

spectrum n (genitive spectrī); second declension

  1. appearance, image
  2. apparition, specter

Related terms

  • speciō
  • spectiō
  • spectō
