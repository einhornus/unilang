ножны (8377)


Pronunciation

  • но́жны: IPA(key): [ˈnoʐnɨ]
  • (archaic) ножны́: IPA(key): [nɐʐˈnɨ]

Noun

но́жны or ножны́* • (nóžny or nožný*) f inan pl (genitive но́жен or ножо́н*, plural only) (* Archaic.)

  1. sheath, scabbard

Related terms
