качну́ться (9077)


Etymology

качну́ть (kačnútʹ) + -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐt͡ɕˈnut͡sːə]

Verb

качну́ться • (kačnútʹsja) pf (imperfective кача́ться)

  1. (intransitive) to rock, to swing
  2. to stagger, to lurch, to reel
  3. (intransitive) to oscillate, to swing
  4. passive of качну́ть (kačnútʹ)
