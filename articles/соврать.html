совра́ть (6108)


Etymology

со- (so-) + врать (vratʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈvratʲ]
  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

совра́ть • (sovrátʹ) pf (imperfective врать)

  1. to lie
  2. to make a mistake
  3. to be inaccurate
