шлю́пка (10669)


Etymology

шлюп (šljup, “sloop”) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʂlʲupkə]

Noun

шлю́пка • (šljúpka) f inan (genitive шлю́пки, nominative plural шлю́пки, genitive plural шлю́пок)

  1. (ship's) boat, yawl, lifeboat
