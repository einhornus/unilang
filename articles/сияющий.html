сия́ющий (2431)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪˈjæjʉɕːɪj]

Participle

сия́ющий • (sijájuščij)

  1. present active imperfective participle of сия́ть (sijátʹ)

Adjective

сия́ющий • (sijájuščij)

  1. shining, sheeny
  2. radiant, refulgent
  3. alight ((figuratively) lit; on fire, burning)
  4. aglitter
  5. fulgent
  6. lambent
  7. auroral
  8. aglare
