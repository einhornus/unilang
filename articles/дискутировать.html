дискути́ровать (27054)


Etymology

From German diskutieren + -овать (-ovatʹ), ultimately borrowed from Latin discutere.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪskʊˈtʲirəvətʲ]

Verb

дискути́ровать • (diskutírovatʹ) impf

  1. to discuss, to debate (participate in a discussion or debate)
