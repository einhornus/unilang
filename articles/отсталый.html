отста́лый (12982)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐt͡sˈstalɨj]
  • Audio

Adjective

отста́лый • (otstályj)

  1. outdated, retrograde
  2. backward, retarded (in terms of development of a person)

Related terms
