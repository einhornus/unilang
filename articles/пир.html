пир (7278)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic пиръ (pirŭ), from Proto-Slavic *pirъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲir]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

пир • (pir) m inan (genitive пи́ра, nominative plural пиры́, genitive plural пиро́в)

  1. feast, banquet

Related terms

  • пирова́ть (pirovátʹ)
