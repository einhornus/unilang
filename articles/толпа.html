толпа́ (750)


Etymology

Inherited from Old East Slavic тълпа (tŭlpa), from Proto-Slavic *tъlpa

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐlˈpa]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

толпа́ • (tolpá) f inan (genitive толпы́, nominative plural то́лпы, genitive plural толп)

  1. crowd, throng
    то́лпы люде́й
    tólpy ljudéj
    crowds of people

Related terms

  • толпи́ться impf (tolpítʹsja), столпи́ться pf (stolpítʹsja)
