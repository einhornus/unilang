фут (15828)


Etymology

Borrowed from English foot.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fut]
  • Rhymes: -ut

Noun

фут • (fut) m inan (genitive фу́та, nominative plural фу́ты, genitive plural фу́тов)

  1. foot (unit of length)

фут
