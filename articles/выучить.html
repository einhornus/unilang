вы́учить (6439)


Etymology

вы́- (vý-) + учи́ть (učítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨʊt͡ɕɪtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

вы́учить • (výučitʹ) pf (imperfective выу́чивать or учи́ть)

  1. to learn (implying that an individual or a group learns about something with the help of inculcation with the intention of full accomplishment)
  2. to remember, to memorize
