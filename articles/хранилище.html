храни́лище (10867)


Etymology

храни́ть (xranítʹ) + -лище (-lišče)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [xrɐˈnʲilʲɪɕːe]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

храни́лище • (xranílišče) n inan (genitive храни́лища, nominative plural храни́лища, genitive plural храни́лищ)

  1. depository, repository, depot, storehouse, warehouse (a location for storage, often for safety or preservation)

Related terms

  • храни́ть impf (xranítʹ)
