здра́вый (5449)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic съдравъ (sŭdravŭ). Doublet of здоро́вый (zdoróvyj), the inherited Old East Slavic form.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈzdravɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

здра́вый • (zdrávyj) (comparative (по)здра́вее or (по)здра́вей)

  1. sensible, sound, sane, reasonable
    здра́вый смысл ― zdrávyj smysl ― common sense
    в здра́вом уме́ ― v zdrávom umé ― sane, in one's right mind
  2. (archaic) healthy

Derived terms

  • здра́во (zdrávo)
