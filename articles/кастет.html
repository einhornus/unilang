касте́т (15276)


Etymology

Borrowed from French casse-tête.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈsʲtʲet]

Noun

касте́т • (kastét) m inan (genitive касте́та, nominative plural касте́ты, genitive plural касте́тов)

  1. brass knuckles (weapon which reinforces the fist)
