шанс (2304)


Etymology

Borrowed from French chance.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʂans]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

шанс • (šans) m inan (genitive ша́нса, nominative plural ша́нсы, genitive plural ша́нсов)

  1. chance, prospect (chance for advancement, progress or profit)

Synonyms

  • возмо́жность (vozmóžnostʹ)
