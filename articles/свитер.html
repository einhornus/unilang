сви́тер (5703)


Etymology

Borrowed from English sweater with some phonetic changes.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsvʲitɨr] (phonetic respelling: сви́тэр)
  • IPA(key): [ˈsvʲitʲɪr]
  • Audio

Noun

сви́тер • (svítɛr, svíter) m inan (genitive сви́тера, nominative plural сви́теры or свитера́, genitive plural сви́теров or свитеро́в)
