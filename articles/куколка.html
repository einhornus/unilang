Etymology

Noun

ку́колка • (kúkolka) f anim or f inan (genitive ку́колки, nominative plural ку́колки, genitive plural ку́колок)

  1. pupa (an insect in its development stage)
  2. Diminutive of ку́кла (kúkla): small doll
  3. (colloquial, derogatory) an attractive woman or girl, dolly
