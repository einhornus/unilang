нагна́ть (10958)


Etymology

на- (na-) + гнать (gnatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐɡˈnatʲ]

Verb

нагна́ть • (nagnátʹ) pf (imperfective нагоня́ть)

  1. to catch up to
  2. to equal (someone) in success
  3. to make up for (lost time)
  4. (colloquial) to cause (an emotion) (на (na) someone)

Related terms

  • гнать (gnatʹ)
