цига́рка (8501)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡sɨˈɡarkə]

Noun

цига́рка • (cigárka) f inan (genitive цига́рки, nominative plural цига́рки, genitive plural цига́рок)

  1. (colloquial, regional) cigarette (especially hand-rolled)

Synonyms

  • сигаре́та (sigaréta), папиро́са (papirósa)
