аксессуа́р (28007)


Etymology

Borrowed from French accessoire.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐksʲɪsʊˈar]
  • IPA(key): [ɐksɨsʊˈar] (phonetic respelling: аксэссуа́р)

Noun

аксессуа́р • (aksessuár, aksɛssuár) m inan (genitive аксессуа́ра, nominative plural аксессуа́ры, genitive plural аксессуа́ров)

  1. accessory (that which belongs to something else deemed the principal)
