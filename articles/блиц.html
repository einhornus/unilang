блиц (28288)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Blitz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [blʲit͡s]

Noun

блиц • (blic) m inan (genitive бли́ца, nominative plural бли́цы, genitive plural бли́цев)

  1. (photography) flashlight
  2. quick chess game, sudden-death game

Related terms

  • блиц-турни́р m (blic-turnír), блицкри́г m (blickríg)
