парикма́хер (7758)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Perückenmacher (“wig-maker”), from French perruque (“wig”) + Macher (“maker”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pərʲɪkˈmaxʲɪr]
  • (colloquial) IPA(key): [pərʲɪxˈmaxʲɪr]

Noun

парикма́хер • (parikmáxer) m anim (genitive парикма́хера, nominative plural парикма́херы, genitive plural парикма́херов)

  1. friseur, barber, hairstylist, hairdresser

Related terms

  • парикма́херская (parikmáxerskaja)
