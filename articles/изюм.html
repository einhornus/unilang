изю́м (21722)


Etymology

A Turkic loan word. Cognate with Uyghur ئۈزۈم (üzüm, “grape”), Turkish üzüm.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪˈzʲum]
  • изю́м
    		(file)

Noun

изю́м • (izjúm) m inan (genitive изю́ма, nominative plural изю́мы, genitive plural изю́мов, diminutive изю́минка)

  1. raisin

Related terms

  • изюмина (izjumina)
  • изюмчик (izjumčik)
