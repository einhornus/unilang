но́жка (2825)


Etymology

нога́ (nogá) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnoʂkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

но́жка • (nóžka) f inan (genitive но́жки, nominative plural но́жки, genitive plural но́жек)

  1. Diminutive of нога́ (nogá): small leg
  2. trotter (food)
  3. (furniture) leg
  4. stem of a glass or letter
  5. stalk of a mushroom
  6. drumstick (chicken leg)
