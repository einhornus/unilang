скиф (28558)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [skʲif]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

скиф • (skif) m anim (genitive ски́фа, nominative plural ски́фы, genitive plural ски́фов)

  1. (historical) Scythian (an inhabitant of Scythia)
  2. wild, rough man
  3. (sports) skiff (boat)

Related terms

  • скифство (skifstvo)
  • скифский (skifskij)
