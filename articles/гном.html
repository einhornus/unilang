гном (2161)


Etymology

From New Latin gnomus, used by Paracelsus as a synonym for pygmaeus (“pygmy”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡnom]

Noun

гном • (gnom) m anim (genitive гно́ма, nominative plural гно́мы, genitive plural гно́мов)

  1. a gnome
  2. (obsolete, Magic: The Gathering) Synonym of ка́рлик (kárlik, “gnome”)
  3. a dwarf
  4. (Magic: The Gathering) a dwarf
