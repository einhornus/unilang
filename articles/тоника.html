то́ника (28189)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtonʲɪkə]

Etymology 1

From Italian tonica.

Noun

то́ника • (tónika) f inan (genitive то́ники, nominative plural то́ники, genitive plural то́ник)

  1. (music) tonic

Noun
