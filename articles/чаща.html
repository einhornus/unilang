ча́ща (8414)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *čęsťa, from *čęstъ (“frequent, dense”) + *-ja .

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈt͡ɕæɕːə]

Noun

ча́ща • (čášča) f inan (genitive ча́щи, nominative plural ча́щи, genitive plural чащ)

  1. thicket
