я́сень (28630)


Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *asenь

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈjæsʲɪnʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

я́сень • (jásenʹ) m inan (genitive я́сеня, nominative plural я́сени, genitive plural я́сеней)

  1. ash (tree or wood)

Related terms

  • ясеневый (jasenevyj)
