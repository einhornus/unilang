гольф (18750)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡolʲf]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from English golf.

Noun

гольф • (golʹf) m inan (genitive го́льфа, uncountable)

  1. (sports) golf

Etymology 2

Noun

гольф • (golʹf) m inan (genitive го́льфа, nominative plural го́льфы, genitive plural го́льфов)

  1. (chiefly in the plural) knee sock , knee high
