дизентери́я (22819)


Etymology

Perhaps borrowed from German Dysenterie; ultimately from Latin dysenteria, from Ancient Greek δυσεντερία (dusentería).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪzʲɪnʲtʲɪˈrʲijə]

Noun

дизентери́я • (dizenteríja) f inan (genitive дизентери́и, nominative plural дизентери́и, genitive plural дизентери́й)

  1. (medicine) dysentery

Derived terms

  • дизентери́йный (dizenteríjnyj)
