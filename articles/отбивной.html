отбивно́й (26685)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐdbʲɪvˈnoj]

Etymology 1

отбива́ть (otbivátʹ) + -но́й (-nój)

Adjective

отбивно́й • (otbivnój)

  1. intended for beating back (a blow) (of a weapon)
  2. beaten (made by beating, softened by beating, e.g. meat)
Derived terms
  • отбивна́я (otbivnája)
Related terms
  • отбива́ть impf (otbivátʹ), отби́ть pf (otbítʹ)

Etymology 2

Noun

отбивно́й • (otbivnój) f inan

  1. genitive singular of отбивна́я (otbivnája)
  2. dative singular of отбивна́я (otbivnája)
  3. instrumental singular of отбивна́я (otbivnája)
  4. prepositional singular of отбивна́я (otbivnája)
