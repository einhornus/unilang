взбить (14569)


Etymology

вз- (vz-) + бить (bitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vzbʲitʲ]

Verb

взби́ть • (vzbítʹ) pf (imperfective взбива́ть)

  1. to beat up, to fluff, to whip, to whisk (e.g. cream)
