ателье́ (13607)


Etymology

Borrowed from French atelier.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtɨˈlʲje] (phonetic respelling: атэлье́)

Noun

ателье́ • (atɛlʹjé) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. studio, atelier
  2. atelier, dressmaking and tailoring establishment
