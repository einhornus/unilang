премье́р (4628)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪˈm⁽ʲ⁾jer]

Etymology 1

Noun

премье́р • (premʹjér) m anim (genitive премье́ра, nominative plural премье́ры, genitive plural премье́ров)

  1. Ellipsis of премье́р-мини́стр (premʹjér-minístr): prime minister (chief member of the cabinet and head of the government)

Etymology 2

Noun

премье́р • (premʹjér) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of премье́ра (premʹjéra)
