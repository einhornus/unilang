хо́лмик (10145)


Etymology

холм (xolm) + -ик (-ik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈxolmʲɪk]

Noun

хо́лмик • (xólmik) m inan (genitive хо́лмика, nominative plural хо́лмики, genitive plural хо́лмиков)

  1. Diminutive of холм (xolm): (small) hill, hillock, knoll
