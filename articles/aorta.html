aorta (19003)


Etymology

From Russian ао́рта (aórta), from Ancient Greek ἀορτή (aortḗ, “the arteries springing from the heart”), from ἀορτέω (aortéō), lengthened form of ἀείρω (aeírō, “I lift, raise”).

Noun

aorta (plural aortalar)

  1. (anatomy) aorta
