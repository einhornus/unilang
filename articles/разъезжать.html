разъезжа́ть (15179)


Etymology

раз- (raz-) + -езжа́ть (-jezžátʹ, “to go (by vehicle)”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rəzjɪˈʐːatʲ], [rəzjɪˈʑːætʲ]

Verb

разъезжа́ть • (razʺjezžátʹ) impf

  1. (intransitive) to drive about, to ride about, to travel about
