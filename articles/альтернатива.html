альтернати́ва (13060)


Etymology

From Polish alternatywa.[1]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐlʲtɨrnɐˈtʲivə] (phonetic respelling: альтэрнати́ва)

Noun

альтернати́ва • (alʹtɛrnatíva) f inan (genitive альтернати́вы, nominative plural альтернати́вы, genitive plural альтернати́в)

  1. alternative (a situation which allows a choice between two or more possibilities)
