караме́ль (31553)


Etymology

Borrowed from French caramel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kərɐˈmʲelʲ]

Noun

караме́ль • (karamélʹ) f inan (genitive караме́ли, nominative plural караме́ли, genitive plural караме́лей)

  1. caramel
