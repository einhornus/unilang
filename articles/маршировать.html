марширова́ть (14257)


Etymology

From French marcher + -овать (-ovatʹ) via German marschieren + -овать (-ovatʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mərʂɨrɐˈvatʲ]

Verb

марширова́ть • (marširovátʹ) impf

  1. to march

Related terms
