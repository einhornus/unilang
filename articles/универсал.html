универса́л (26074)


Etymology

Ultimately borrowed from Latin ūniversālis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊnʲɪvʲɪrˈsal]

Noun

универса́л • (universál) m anim or m inan (genitive универса́ла, nominative plural универса́лы, genitive plural универса́лов, feminine универса́лка)

  1. (animate) jack of all trades, all-round craftsman, factotum
  2. (animate) universalist
  3. (automotive, inanimate) estate car, station wagon
  4. (historical, law, inanimate) type of legal decree or manifest issued in Poland and Ukraine

Related terms

  • универса́льный (universálʹnyj), универса́льность (universálʹnostʹ)
