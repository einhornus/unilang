проде́лывать (13303)


Etymology

проде́лать (prodélatʹ) + -ывать (-yvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈdʲelɨvətʲ]

Verb

проде́лывать • (prodélyvatʹ) impf (perfective проде́лать)

  1. to make (a hole), to break through (a hole)
  2. to do (a trick)
  3. to carry out (a task; work)
