Adjective

сладкова́тый • (sladkovátyj) (comparative (по)сладкова́тее or (по)сладкова́тей)

  1. somewhat sweet, sweetish
  2. sweet-smelling, fragrant
  3. falsely sentimental
  4. excessively courteous
