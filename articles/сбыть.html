сбыть (19914)


Etymology

с- (s-) + быть (bytʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zbɨtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

сбыть • (sbytʹ) pf (imperfective сбыва́ть)

  1. to sell, to market
  2. (colloquial) to get rid of

Derived terms

  • сбыть с ру́к pf (sbytʹ s ruk)
