канона́да (17475)


Etymology

Ultimately borrowed from French cannonade.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kənɐˈnadə]

Noun

канона́да • (kanonáda) f inan (genitive канона́ды, nominative plural канона́ды, genitive plural канона́д)

  1. cannonade
