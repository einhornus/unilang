кап (10691)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kap]

Etymology 1

Noun

кап • (kap) m inan (genitive ка́па, nominative plural ка́пы, genitive plural ка́пов)

  1. cape
  2. nodule, woodknob, burl

Etymology 2

Noun

кап • (kap) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of ка́па (kápa)
