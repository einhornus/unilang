те́ннис (12840)


Etymology

Borrowed from English tennis.

Pronunciation

Noun

те́ннис • (tɛ́nnis) m inan (genitive те́нниса, uncountable, related adjective те́ннисный)

  1. (sports) tennis

Derived terms
