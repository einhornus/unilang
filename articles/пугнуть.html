пугну́ть (29359)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʊɡˈnutʲ]

Verb

пугну́ть • (pugnútʹ) pf (imperfective пуга́ть)

  1. to frighten, to scare

Derived terms

  • See пуга́ть (pugátʹ) for derived verbs.
