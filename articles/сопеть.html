сопе́ть (6981)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic сопѣти (sopěti).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈpʲetʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

сопе́ть • (sopétʹ) impf

  1. to wheeze (to breathe heavily and noisily through the nose)
  2. to sniffle

Synonyms

  • (wheeze): хрипе́ть (xripétʹ)

Related terms

  • со́пли (sópli), сопли́вый (soplívyj)
  • сопло́ (sopló)
