зени́тный (9949)


Etymology

зени́т (zenít, “zenith”) + -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zʲɪˈnʲitnɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

зени́тный • (zenítnyj)

  1. (relational) zenith; zenithal
  2. antiaircraft / anti-aircraft (intended to attack aircraft)
