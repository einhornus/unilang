маскиро́вка (10875)


Etymology

From ма́ска (máska, “mask”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [məskʲɪˈrofkə]

Noun

маскиро́вка • (maskiróvka) f inan (genitive маскиро́вки, nominative plural маскиро́вки, genitive plural маскиро́вок)

  1. masking, disguising, concealment
  2. (military) camouflage
  3. (military) misinformation, disinformation
