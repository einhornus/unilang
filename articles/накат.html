нака́т (18035)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈkat]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

нака́т • (nakát) m inan (genitive нака́та, nominative plural нака́ты, genitive plural нака́тов)

  1. groundswell (nautical sense)
  2. (billiards) follow
  3. rolling (on, onto)
