колыха́ть (23952)

[kəlɨˈxatʲ]
||||||||||

Verb

колыха́ть • (kolyxátʹ) impf (perfective колыхну́ть)

  1. (transitive) to rhythmically sway, to rock gently
  2. (low colloquial) to excite, to emotionally move
