плед (16326)


Etymology

Borrowed from English plaid.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [plʲet]

Noun

плед • (pled) m inan (genitive пле́да, nominative plural пле́ды, genitive plural пле́дов)

  1. A rug, sheet or blanket of such material
  2. plaid, a type of twilled woollen cloth, often with a tartan or chequered pattern
