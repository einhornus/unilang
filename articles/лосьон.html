лосьо́н (26064)


Etymology

Borrowed from French lotion.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lɐˈsʲjɵn]

Noun

лосьо́н • (losʹón) m inan (genitive лосьо́на, nominative plural лосьо́ны, genitive plural лосьо́нов)

  1. makeup remover
  2. lotion
  3. aftershave, shaving lotion
