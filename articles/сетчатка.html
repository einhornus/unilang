сетча́тка (17490)


Etymology

Derived from се́тка (sétka), сеть (setʹ). Cognate with Belarusian сятча́тка (sjatčátka). (This etymology is missing or incomplete. Please add to it, or discuss it at the Etymology scriptorium .)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲɪˈt͡ɕatkə]

Noun

сетча́тка • (setčátka) f inan (genitive сетча́тки, nominative plural сетча́тки, genitive plural сетча́ток)

  1. retina (thin layer of cells at the back of the eyeball)

Synonyms

  • рети́на (retína)
