портмоне́ (23820)


Etymology

Borrowed from French porte-monnaie.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pərtmɐˈnɛ] (phonetic respelling: портмонэ́)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

портмоне́ • (portmonɛ́) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. wallet, purse, porte-monnaie

Synonyms

  • кошелёк (košeljók)
  • бума́жник (bumážnik)
