ле́ди (5485)


Etymology

Borrowed from English lady.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlʲedʲɪ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ле́ди • (lédi) f anim (indeclinable)

  1. lady (usually in British context; title or a woman of breeding and authority)
