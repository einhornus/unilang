ке́пка (5600)


Etymology

From ке́пи (képi) + -ка (-ka), from French képi, from German Käppi.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkʲepkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ке́пка • (képka) f inan (genitive ке́пки, nominative plural ке́пки, genitive plural ке́пок)

  1. peaked cap (headgear)
