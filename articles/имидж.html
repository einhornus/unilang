и́мидж (14877)


Etymology

Borrowed from English image.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈimʲɪt͡ʂʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

и́мидж • (ímidž) m inan (genitive и́миджа, nominative plural и́миджи, genitive plural и́миджей)

  1. image (characteristic as perceived by others)

Related terms

  • имиджевый (imidževyj)
