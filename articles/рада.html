ра́да (1372)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈradə]

Etymology 1

From Old East Slavic ра́да (rada), from Middle High German rât via Polish rada, Czech rada, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *rēdaz.

Noun

ра́да • (ráda) f inan (genitive ра́ды, nominative plural ра́ды, genitive plural рад)

  1. rada, Rada (a parliamentary body in a number of Slavic countries)

Etymology 2

Adjective

ра́да • (ráda)

  1. feminine singular of рад (rad)

Etymology 3

Noun

ра́да • (ráda) m inan

  1. genitive singular of рад (rad)
