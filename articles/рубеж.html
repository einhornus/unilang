рубе́ж (2821)


Etymology

Borrowed from Church Slavonic рубежь (rubežĭ), from Proto-Slavic *rǫbežь, from *rǫbiti (“to chop, hem”) + *-ežь . Doublet of рубёж (rubjóž).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʊˈbʲeʂ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

рубе́ж • (rubéž) m inan (genitive рубежа́, nominative plural рубежи́, genitive plural рубеже́й)

  1. boundary, border, borderline, frontier (the line or frontier area separating regions)
  2. line

Synonyms

  • грани́ца (graníca), преде́л (predél)
  • ве́ха (véxa), эта́п (etáp)
  • ли́ния (línija)

Related terms

  • рубежный (rubežnyj)
  • зарубе́жный (zarubéžnyj)
  • зарубе́жье (zarubéžʹje)
