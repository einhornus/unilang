бочо́нок (13011)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈt͡ɕɵnək]

Noun

бочо́нок • (bočónok) m inan (genitive бочо́нка, nominative plural бочо́нки, genitive plural бочо́нков)

  1. small barrel, keg, cask

Related terms
