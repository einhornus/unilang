пеший (9775)


Etymology

Ultimately from a Proto-Slavic *pěšь derivative of Proto-Indo-European *pṓds.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpʲeʂɨj]

Adjective

пеший • (péšij)

  1. unmounted
  2. pedestrian

Related terms

  • пешехо́д (pešexód)
  • пешко́м (peškóm)
