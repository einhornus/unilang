хитрый -> cunning
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic хытръ (xytrŭ), from Proto-Slavic *xytrъ. Cognate with Bulgarian хитър (hitǎr, “sly”), Serbo-Croatian хитар/hitar (“hasty”), Slovak and Czech chytrý (“clever”).



Definitions (English):
  • sly, cunning, artful
    • intricate, involved.
      • Э́то не хи́тро/хитро́. ― Éto ne xítro/xitró. ― That's simple. Э́то де́ло не хи́трое. ― Éto délo ne xítroje. ― It is very simple A child could do it.
    • skilful, resourceful
