владеть -> to possess, have command of, to master
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я владею

Past: владел/владела



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic владѣти (vladěti), from Proto-Slavic *volděti (“to rule”). Compare the inherited doublet володе́ть (volodétʹ).
