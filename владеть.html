владе́ть -> to possess, have command of, to master
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я владе́ю
Ты владе́ешь
Past: владе́л/владе́ла



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic владѣти (vladěti), from Proto-Slavic *volděti (“to rule”). Compare the inherited doublet володеть (volodétʹ).
