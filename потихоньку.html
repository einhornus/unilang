потихоньку -> slowly, quietly, secretly (coll)

Etymology: по (po) +‎ ти́хий (tíxij) +‎ -оньку (-onʹku)



Definitions (English):
  • OK, so-so (in answer to the question, "
    • как дела?" ("How are you?"), the response "потихо́ньку"—"OK" or "I'm getting along okay"—implies that things could be better and could be worse)
  • silently
    • gently
      • on the sly


        Definitions (Russian):
        • ти́хо, без шу́ма
