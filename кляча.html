кля́ча (21879)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈklʲæt͡ɕə]

Noun

кля́ча • (kljáča) f anim (genitive кля́чи, nominative plural кля́чи, genitive plural кля́ч)

  1. jade, old or worn-out horse
  2. (of a woman) jade, hag
