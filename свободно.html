свобо́дно (3403)


Etymology

свобо́дный (svobódnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [svɐˈbodnə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

свобо́дно • (svobódno)

  1. freely (without́ interference or restriction)
  2. freely, easily (without́ difficulties)
    Свобо́дно говори́т по-русски. ― Svobódno govorít po-rússki. ― He speaks Russian fluently.

Synonyms

  • во́льно (vólʹno)

Adjective

свобо́дно • (svobódno)

  1. short neuter singular of свобо́дный (svobódnyj)
