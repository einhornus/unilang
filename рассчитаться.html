Verb[edit]

рассчита́ться • (rassčitátʹsja) pf (imperfective рассчи́тываться)

  1. to settle accounts (with), to reckon (with), to square up (with), to settle up
  2. (colloquial) to get even, to get back some of one's own
  3. (military) to number (off)
  4. to pay (for)
  5. passive of рассчита́ть (rassčitátʹ)
