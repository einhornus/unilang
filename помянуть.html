помяну́ть -> to mention, recall
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я помяну́
Ты помя́нешь
Past: помяну́л/помяну́ла


Definitions (English):
  • (dated) to remember, to recall
    • (colloquial) to mention
      • (religion) to pray for the health living or the repose of the soul of the dead, to commemorate
