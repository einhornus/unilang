штурм -> storm, assault
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: штурмы
Genitive: штурма



Etymology: Borrowed from German Sturm (“assault, attack, storm”).



Definitions (English):
  • storm, storming of something
    • assault (a violent onset or attack)
      • взять шту́рмом что́-либо ― vzjatʹ štúrmom štó-libo ― to take something by storm
