густо́й -> thick, dense
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic гу́стꙑи (gustyi), from Proto-Slavic *gǫstъ (“dense”).



Definitions (English):
  • thick, dense (having a viscous consistency)
    • rich, deep, heavy
      • dark, deep (of a color)
