создть -> to create

Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic съзьдати (sŭzĭdati, “to build, to erect”), from Proto-Slavic *sъzьdati, from *zьdati. The modern conjugation is analogical after дать (datʹ), replacing the old present stem созижд-.



Definitions (English):
  • to create
    • to found, to originate
      • to set up, to establish
        • (literary) to build, to erect
