придти́ (19428)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪˈtʲːi]

Verb

придти́ • (pridtí)

  1. Obsolete spelling of прийти́ (prijtí). It had been prevailing untiĺ 1920s, when it gradually retreated in favor of притти́ (prittí). In 1956, new editions of officially recognized spelling rules[1] and spelling dictionary[2] finally eliminated both придти́ (pridtí) and притти́ (prittí) and approved only one form of spelling: прийти́ (prijtí).
