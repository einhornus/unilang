ольха́ (25029)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *elьxa, compare Serbo-Croatian јоха, jóha.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐlʲˈxa]

Noun

ольха́ • (olʹxá) f inan (genitive ольхи, nominative plural ольхи, genitive plural о́льх)

  1. alder (tree or wood)

Related terms

  • ольховый (olʹxovyj)
