тик (15097)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲik]

Etymology 1

Borrowed from French tic.

Noun

тик • (tik) m inan (genitive тика, nominative plural ти́ки, genitive plural тиков)

  1. tic (local and habitual convulsive motion)

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Dutch tijk or English tick.

Noun

тик • (tik) m inan (genitive тика, uncountable)

  1. tick, ticking (fabric)

Etymology 3

Borrowed from English teak.

Noun

тик • (tik) m inan (genitive тика, nominative plural ти́ки, genitive plural тиков)

  1. teak (tree; timber)
