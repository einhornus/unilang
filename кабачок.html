кабачок (13771)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kəbɐˈt͡ɕɵk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1

каба́к (kabák, “tavern, pub, bar”) + -ок (-ok)

Noun

кабачо́к • (kabačók) m inan (genitive кабачка́, nominative plural кабачки́, genitive plural кабачко́в)

  1. Diminutive of каба́к (kabák, “tavern, pub, bar”)
  2. little pub, little restaurant, saloon

Etymology 2

From Turkic kapgak, kapak + -ок (-ok) (compare Turkish kabak (“marrow, squash”)).

Noun

кабачо́к • (kabačók) m inan (genitive кабачка́, nominative plural кабачки́, genitive plural кабачко́в, related adjective кабачко́вый)

  1. vegetable marrow, squash, zucchini
Related terms
  • кабачко́вая икра́ (kabačkóvaja ikrá)
