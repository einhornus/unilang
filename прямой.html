прямо́й (949)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪˈmoj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1[edit]

Inherited from Old East Slavic прямъ (prjamŭ, “straight, correct, honest, simple”)

Adjective[edit]

прямо́й • (prjamój) (comparative (по)прямее or (по)прямей, superlative прямейший)

  1. straight
    прямо́й у́гол ― prjamój úgol ― right angle
  2. direct
  3. straightforward
  4. through (of a train)
  5. downright
  6. outspoken, frank
