сыр (5018)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *syrъ (“cheese”), from Proto-Balto-Slavic *sū́ˀras, from Proto-Indo-European *SuHrós.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɨr]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

сыр • (syr) m inan (genitive сы́ра, nominative plural сыры́, genitive plural сыро́в)

  1. cheese

Related terms

  • сырник (syrnik)
  • сырный (syrnyj)

Adjective

сыр • (syr)

  1. short masculine singular of сыро́й (syrój)
