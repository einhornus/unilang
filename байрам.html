байрам (22742)


Etymology

Borrowed from a languages">Turkic source. Compare Bashkir байрам (bayram), Tatar бәйрәм (bäyräm), Khakas пайрам (payram), Kazakh мейрам (meyram), Kyrgyz майрам (mayram), Azerbaijani bayram, Turkish bayram etc.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐjˈram]

Noun

байрам • (bajrám) m inan (genitive байра́ма, nominative plural байра́мы, genitive plural байра́мов)

  1. (Islam) Eid, Bayram (a Muslim religious festival), abbreviation of курбан-байрам (kurban-bajrám, “Eid Al-Adha”)
  2. (by extension) any Islamic festival

Synonyms

  • и́д (id) (from Arabic or Tajik), эйд (ejd) (from Persian), айт (ajt) (from Kazakh)
