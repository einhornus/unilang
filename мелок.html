мелок (21482)


Etymology 1

мел (mel) +‎ -ок (-ók)[1]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪˈlok]

Noun

мелок • (melók) m inan (genitive мелка́, nominative plural мелки, genitive plural мелко́в)

  1. chalk
  2. stick of pastel or similar
  3. (rare, by extension) crayon

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmʲelək]

Adjective

мелок • (mélok)

  1. short masculine singular of ме́лкий (mélkij)
