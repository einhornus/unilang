уни́женный (12078)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈnʲiʐɨn(ː)ɨj]

Participle

уни́женный • (unížennyj)

  1. past passive perfective participle of уни́зить (unízitʹ)

Adjective

уни́женный • (unížennyj)

  1. humiliated (experiencing or expressing humiliation)
  2. abject (cast down; rejected; low-lying)
