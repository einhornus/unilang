стукать -> to knock, bang
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я стукаю
Ты стукаешь
Past: стукал/стукала



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *stukati. Cognate with Ukrainian сту́кати́ (stúkaty), Belarusian сту́каць (stúkacʹ), Polish stukać. Of onomatopoeic origin see стук (stuk), стуча́́ть (stučátʹ). Compare Latvian stukât (“to walk with small steps (of children learning to walk)”), stuknît (“to push forward with blows”), stucinât (“to knock, to rumble”), stukme (“trampled place in a meadow”).



Definitions (English):
  • to knock
    • to rap, to tap
      • to strike, to bang, to hit
