сту́кать (22937)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *stukati. Cognate with Ukrainian стукати (stúkaty), Belarusian стукаць (stúkacʹ), Polish stukać. Of onomatopoeic origin; see стук (stuk), стуча́ть (stučátʹ). Compare Latvian stukât (“to walk with small steps (of children learning to walk)”), stuknît (“to push forward with blows”), stucinât (“to knock, to rumble”), stukme (“trampled place in a meadow”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈstukətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

сту́кать • (stúkatʹ) impf (perfective сту́кнуть)

  1. to knock
  2. to rap, to tap
  3. to strike, to bang, to hit

Related terms

  • стук (stuk)
  • стуча́ть impf (stučátʹ), постуча́ть pf (postučátʹ)
