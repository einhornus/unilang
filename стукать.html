сту́кать -> to knock, bang
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я сту́каю
Ты сту́каешь
Past: сту́кал/сту́кала



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *stukati. Cognate with Ukrainian стукати (stúkaty), Belarusian стукаць (stúkacʹ), Polish stukać. Of onomatopoeic origin see стук (stuk), стуча́ть (stučátʹ). Compare Latvian stukât (“to walk with small steps (of children learning to walk)”), stuknît (“to push forward with blows”), stucinât (“to knock, to rumble”), stukme (“trampled place in a meadow”).



Definitions (English):
  • to knock
    • to rap, to tap
      • to strike, to bang, to hit
