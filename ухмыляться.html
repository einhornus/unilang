ухмыля́ться (8707)


Etymology

Per Vasmer, cognate with Middle High German smollen (“to smile”), smielen (“to smile”), archaic Dutch smuylen (“to smile”), Bavarian schmollen (“to smile”), Norwegian smolla (“to smile”) (alsó smulla, smusla).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊxmɨˈlʲat͡sːə]

Verb

ухмыля́ться • (uxmyljátʹsja) impf (perfective ухмыльну́ться)

  1. to smirk, to grin

Synonyms

  • усмеха́ться (usmexátʹsja)
