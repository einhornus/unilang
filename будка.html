бдка -> booth
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: бдки
Genitive: бдки



Etymology: From буда (búda) +‎ -ка (-ka), which former has been displaced by the suffixed formation fast and is from Ukrainian буда (búda), from Polish buda, from Middle High German buode = German Bude, from Proto-Germanic *bōdō as English booth.



Definitions (English):
  • booth, cabin, kiosk, box (very small building)
    • (historical) small police facility (during pre-revolutionary Russia)
      • kennel, doghouse
