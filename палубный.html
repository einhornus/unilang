па́лубный (31049)


Etymology

па́луба (páluba) +‎ -ный (-nyj)

Pronunciation

IPA(key): [ˈpalʊbnɨj]

Adjective

па́лубный • (pálubnyj)

  1. (relational, nautical) deck
    па́лубные доски ― pálubnyje dóski ― deck planking
  2. (nautical) decked (having a deck)
    па́лубная я́хта ― pálubnaja jáxta ― a decked yacht
  3. (relational, aviation) carrieŕ; carrier-based (capable of taking off from and landing on an aircraft carrieŕ)
    па́лубный торпедоносец ― pálubnyj torpedonósec ― carrieŕ torpedo bomber
