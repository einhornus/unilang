тя́жко (7404)


Etymology

тя́жкий (tjážkij) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtʲaʂkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

тя́жко • (tjážko)

  1. sorely, gravely, heavily
  2. with difficulty

Adjective

тя́жко • (tjážko)

  1. short neuter singular of тя́жкий (tjážkij)
