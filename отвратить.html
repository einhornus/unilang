отврати́ть (31770)


Etymology

From от- (ot-) +‎ -вратить (-vratítʹ, “to turn”), which is alsó found in возврати́ть (vozvratítʹ), and is ultimately derived from Proto-Slavic *vortiti (whence вороти́ть (vorotítʹ)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtvrɐˈtʲitʲ]

Verb

отврати́ть • (otvratítʹ) pf (imperfective отвращать)

  1. to avert, to ward off, to prevent
  2. (obsolete) to avert, to turn aside, to turn awaý
  3. to keep (from), to prevent (from)
  4. to turn (off), to put (off), to cause aversion (in)
