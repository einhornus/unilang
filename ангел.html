а́нгел (2472)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek ἄγγελος (ángelos).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈanɡʲɪl]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

а́нгел • (ángel) masculine gender">m anim (genitive а́нгела, nominative plural а́нгелы, genitive plural а́нгелов)

  1. angel
  2. an innocent
  3. dear, darling
