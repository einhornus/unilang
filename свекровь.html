Noun[edit]

свекро́вь • (svekróvʹ) f anim (genitive свекро́ви, nominative plural свекро́ви, genitive plural свекро́вей)

  1. a woman’s mother-in-law (husband’s mother only)
    Synonym: свекруха (svekrúxa) (colloquial, can be disrespectful; or regional)

Related terms[edit]

  • свёкор (svjókor)
