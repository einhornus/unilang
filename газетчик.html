газе́тчик (14676)


Etymology

газе́та (gazéta) +‎ -щик (-ščik)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡɐˈzʲet͡ɕːɪk]

Noun

газе́тчик • (gazétčik) masculine gender">m anim (genitive газе́тчика, nominative plural газе́тчики, genitive plural газе́тчиков, feminine газетчица)

  1. newspaper seller, newsvendor, newsboy, newspaper boy
  2. (colloquial) journalist, newsman, pressman
