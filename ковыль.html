ковы́ль (32261)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *kovyljь.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈvɨlʲ]

Noun

ковы́ль • (kovýlʹ) m inan (genitive ковыля́, nominative plural ковыли́, genitive plural ковыле́й)

  1. feathergrass (Any of various perennial hermaphroditic grasses of the genus Stipa)
