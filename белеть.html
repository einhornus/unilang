бе́леть -> to grow white, be white
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я бе́лею
Ты бе́леешь
Past: бе́лел/бе́лела



Etymology: бе́лый (bélyj) +‎ -е́ть (-étʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to turn white, (intransitive) to whiten
    • (colloquial) to turn gray
      • (colloquial) to become brighter
        • (colloquial, impersonal) to dawn
          • (imperfective only) to gleam, to be visible (of something white)
