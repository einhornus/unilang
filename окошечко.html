окошечко -> little window
Noun, declension 2a (pattern лето)

Plural: окошечки
Genitive: окошечка



Etymology: о́ко́шко (okóško) +‎ -ко (-ko)



Definitions (English):
  • Diminutive of о́ко́шко (okóško), diminutive of окно́́ (oknó) (small) window counter (in a ticket office, bank, etc.)
