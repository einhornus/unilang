полотн -> canvas (painting), linen
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: полтна
Genitive: полотн



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *poltьno, from Proto-Indo-European *polto- (“cloth”). Doublet of платно (platnó), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • linen
    • cloth, bunting
      • canvas
        • roadbed (of a railroad) embankment
          • saw web, saw blade
            • panel
              • picture, painting
