рарите́т (27147)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Rarität, from Latin raritas ≃ rarus (“rare”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rərʲɪˈtʲet]

Noun

рарите́т • (raritét) m inan (genitive рарите́та, nominative plural рарите́ты, genitive plural рарите́тов)

  1. rarity, curiosity (rare or unusual object)
