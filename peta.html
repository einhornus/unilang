peta (14660)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *pęta. Cognate with Bulgarian пе́та (peta), Slovene peta, Russian пята́ (pjata), Slovak päta. Non-Slavic cognates include Pashto پونده (pūndaʿh, “heel”) and Old Prussian pentis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pěːta/
  • Hyphenation: pe‧ta

Noun

péta f (Cyrillic spelling пе́та)

  1. heel
