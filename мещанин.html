мещани́н -> petty bourgeois, lower middle class person, philistine
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: меща́не
Genitive: мещани́на



Etymology: Borrowed from Polish mieszczanin (“burgher, townsman”), from miasto (“town”).



Definitions (English):
  • (historical) petty bourgeois, petit bourgeois (member of a social estate in the Russian Empire comprising the urban lower middle class)
    • (figuratively) Philistine, narrow-minded person
