флюгер (21282)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch vleugel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈflʲʉɡʲɪr]

Noun

флюгер • (fljúger) m inan (genitive флюгера, nominative plural флюгера, genitive plural флюгеров)

  1. vane, weather vane, windvane, weathercock

Noun

флюгер • (fljúger) m anim (genitive флюгера, nominative plural флю́геры, genitive plural флюгеров)

  1. (colloquial, derogatory) weathercock, opportunist, timeserver
