Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *gorěti.



Definitions (English):
  • to burn.
    • Свеча́ горе́ла на столе́. ― Svečá goréla na stolé. ― A candle was burning on the table.
  • to be alight
    • to gleam, to shine (of eyes)
