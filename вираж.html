вира́ж (16446)


Etymology

Borrowed from French virage.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪˈraʂ]

Noun

вира́ж • (viráž) m inan (genitive виража, nominative plural виражи, genitive plural виражей) (bend, turn)
вира́ж • (viráž) m inan (genitive виража, nominative plural виражи, genitive plural виражей) (chemical solution)

  1. bend, curve
  2. (aviation) banking, turn, horizontal turn
  3. (photography) toning solution
