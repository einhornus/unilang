срам -> shame
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: сра́мы
Genitive: сра́ма



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic срамъ (sramŭ), from Proto-Slavic *sormъ. Doublet of сором (soróm), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • shame, disgrace
