срам (14621)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic срамъ (sramŭ), from Proto-Slavic *sormъ. Doublet of со́ром (soróm), the inherited East Slavic form.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sram]

Noun

срам • (sram) m inan (genitive сра́ма, uncountable)

  1. shame, disgrace

Synonyms

  • стыд (styd), позо́р (pozór)
