ну -> well

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *nъ, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *nu, from Proto-Indo-European *nu.



Definitions (English):
  • well.
    • Ну! ― Nu! ― Well! (indicating surprise and a little disbelief) Ну что ж? ― Nu što ž? ― Well, what? Ну так что́ же? ― Nu tak štó že? ― Well, what about it? Да ну́! ― Da nú! ― No, I don't want to! ("Совсе́м не хочу́́!" is synonymous) Да ну?! ― Da nu?! ― Really?!
  • (colloquial) yeah, yep (impolite, sometimes implying a question - so what?)
    • Ты э́то чита́л? — Ну? ― Ty éto čitál? — Nu? ― Have you read this? - Yeah, so what?
