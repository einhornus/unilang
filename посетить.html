Etymology

root">Pie word
*swé

From Proto-Slavic *posětiti. Compare Ukrainian посітити (positýty), Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic посѣтити (posětiti) (1sg pres.: посѣщѫ (posěštǫ)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pəsʲɪˈtʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

посети́ть • (posetítʹ) pf (imperfective посеща́ть)

  1. to visit, to call on
  2. to attend (lectures)

Related terms

  • посети́тель (posetítelʹ)
  • посещаемость (poseščájemostʹ)
  • посеще́ние (poseščénije)
