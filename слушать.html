слушать -> to listen to
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я слушаю
Ты слушаешь
Past: слушал/слушала



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *slušati, from Proto-Slavic *slouchēti, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- whence English listen.



Definitions (English):
  • to listen, to listen to (+ accusative)
    • to attend
      • (medicine) to auscultate.
        • Слу́шай! ― Slúšaj! ― Attention! Listen up! Слу́шаю! ― Slúšaju! ― Hello! (on the telephone) At your service! Very well! Слу́шаюсь! ― Slúšajusʹ! ― Yes, sir!
