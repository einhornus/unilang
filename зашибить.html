зашиби́ть (22674)


Etymology

за (za) +‎ -шибить (-šibítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəʂɨˈbʲitʲ]

Verb

зашиби́ть • (zašibítʹ) pf (imperfective зашибать)

  1. to bruise, to hurt
  2. (colloquial, intransitive) to drink hard, to hit the bottle
  3. (colloquial, intransitive or transitive) to make money
    Он зашиб бе́шеные бабки.
    On zašíb béšenyje bábki.
    He made heaps of money.
