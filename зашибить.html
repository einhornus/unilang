зашиби́ть (22676)


Etymology

за (za) + -шиби́ть (-šibítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəʂɨˈbʲitʲ]

Verb

зашиби́ть • (zašibítʹ) pf (imperfective зашиба́ть)

  1. to bruise, to hurt
  2. (colloquial, intransitive) to drink hard, to hit the bottle
  3. (colloquial, intransitive or transitive) to make money
    Он заши́б бе́шеные ба́бки.
    On zašíb béšenyje bábki.
    He made heaps of money.
