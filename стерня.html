стерня́ (25346)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *stьrnь.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sʲtʲɪrˈnʲa]

Noun

стерня́ • (sternjá) f inan (genitive стерни́, nominative plural стерни́, genitive plural стерней)

  1. stubble (short stalks left in a field after harvest)
    Synonym: жнивьё (žnivʹjó)
