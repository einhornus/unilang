оболчка -> cover, envelope
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: оболчки
Genitive: оболчки



Etymology: From Old Russian оболо́чка (oboločka), from оболочити (oboločiti) +‎ -ка (-ka) or from Proto-Slavic *obolčьka, from *obolka +‎ *-ьka, from *obelťi, from *ob- +‎ *velťi



Definitions (English):
  • cover, envelope, shell
    • (biology) capsule, outward form
      • (anatomy) membrane
        • (technology) jacket, casing
          • (computing) shell (environment)
