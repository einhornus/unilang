нарочи́тый (19998)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic [Term?]; compare нарокъ (narokŭ, “pebble used for divination”). Ultimately derived from на- (na-) and рок (rok).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nərɐˈt͡ɕitɨj]

Adjective

нарочи́тый • (naročítyj) (comparative (по)нарочитее or (по)нарочитей)

  1. intentional, deliberate, on purpose
  2. feigned

Related terms

  • наро́чно (naróšno, naróčno)
  • нарочный masculine gender">m (náročnyj)
