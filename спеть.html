спеть (10379)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [spʲetʲ]

Etymology 1

From Old East Slavic спѣти (spěti, “to hasten, to succeed”), from Proto-Indo-European *spewd- (“press, hurry”), whence English speed. Akin to Slovene speti (“to hasten”), Slovak dospieť (“grow mature”), Czech spět, spěch. Non-Slavic cognates include Sanskrit स्फायते (sphāyatē, “to grow fat, increase”) and German sich sputen (“to hasten”).

Verb

спеть • (spetʹ) impf (perfective поспе́ть)

  1. to ripen
Synonyms
  • зреть (zretʹ)
Related terms
  • спе́лый (spélyj)

Etymology 2

с- (s-) +‎ петь (petʹ)

Verb

спеть • (spetʹ) pf (imperfective петь)

  1. to sing
  2. to crow
Related terms
  • пе́сня (pésnja)
