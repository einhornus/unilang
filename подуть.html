подть -> to blow

Etymology: по- (po-) +‎ дуть (dutʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to blow.
    • Ветер подул нам в лицо́. Véter podúl nam v licó. The wind blew into our faces.
  • (impersonal) to be drafty
    • to go quickly, to scamper
      • (perfective only) to start blowing
