невеста -> bride, fiancee
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: невсты
Genitive: невсты



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *nevěsta. Probably from не (ne, “not”) + ведать (védatʹ, “to know”), indicating a strict hands-off relationship.



Definitions (English):
  • fiancée, bride
    • marriageable girl
