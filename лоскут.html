ло́скут -> rag, scrap
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ло́скуты
Genitive: ло́скута


Definitions (English):
  • rag, shred, scrap
    • (collective) scraps of cloth or leather in the textile and leather industries
