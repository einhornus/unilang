лоскут (13003)


Pronunciation

  • лоскут: IPA(key): [lɐˈskut]
  • лоскут: IPA(key): [ˈloskʊt]

Noun

лоскут • (loskút) m inan (genitive лоскута, nominative plural ло́скуты or лоску́тья, genitive plural ло́скутов or лоску́тьев)

  1. rag, shred, scrap

Noun

лоскут • (lóskut) m inan (genitive лоскута, uncountable)

  1. (collective) scraps of cloth or leather in the textile and leather industries
