Verb[edit]

перепа́сть • (perepástʹ) pf (imperfective перепа́дать)

  1. (colloquial) to fall to (someone's) lot, to become (someone's) responsibility (+ dative of person whose responsibility it is)
  2. (colloquial) to fall (of intermittent rain, snow, etc.)
  3. (low colloquial) to subside
  4. (low colloquial) to pass by
  5. (folk colloquial) to lose weight, to get thin
