ле́нта -> ribbon, tape
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ле́нты
Genitive: ле́нты



Etymology: From dialectal German Linte (“ribbon”). Compare Dutch lint (“ribbon”).



Definitions (English):
  • ribbon, band
    • (engineering) tape,
      • клейкая ле́нта ― kléjkaja lénta ― duct tape
    • (weaponry) belt
      • (culture) film, movie
