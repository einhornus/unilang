ба́тюшка -> father
Noun, declension 1a
Plural: ба́тюшки
Genitive: ба́тюшки



Etymology: ба́тя (bátja) +‎ -юшка (-juška)



Definitions (English):
  • (obsolete) father (parent)
    • Как вас по ба́тюшке? (dated) ― Kak vas po bátjuške? ― What is your patronymic?
  • father (term of address for a priest), priest.
    • Ба́тюшки (мои́ or свя́ты)! (colloquial) ― Bátjuški (moí or svjáty)! ― Good gracious! Goodness me!
  • dear friend old boy (respectful and affectionate term of address)
