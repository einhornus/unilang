стира́ть -> to rub off, grind down
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я стира́ю
Ты стира́ешь
Past: стира́л/стира́ла



Etymologies:
  • с- (s-) +‎ -тирать (-tirátʹ)
  • Probably ultimately the same as etymology 1, but reinterpreted as an unprefixed verb.


Definitions (English):
  • to erase something from a surface
    • to clean, to wash cloth or clothes
