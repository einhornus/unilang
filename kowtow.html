_кит. 1. _n.

1 де́лать ни́зкий покло́н (каса́ясь голово́й земли) (to) All the servants had to kowtow to the king when he entered the room.
2 раболепствовать (to) The natives keep in favour by kowtowing to the governing officials. Should a politician kowtow to the voters in order to win their votes?
