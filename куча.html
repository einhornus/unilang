кча -> heap
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: кчи
Genitive: кчи



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *kuča.
  • From Old East Slavic ку́ча (kuča), from Proto-Slavic *kǫťa. Doublet of куща (kúšča), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.

      • (vulgar) piece of…
        • (dialectal) a hut made of human-height poles covered with dirt
