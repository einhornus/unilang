неодушевленный (24401)


Etymology

не- (ne-) +‎ одушевлённый (oduševljónnyj)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nʲɪədʊʂɨˈvlʲɵnːɨj]

Adjective

неодушевлённый • (neoduševljónnyj)

  1. (grammar) inanimate
    неодушевлённый предме́т ― neoduševljónnyj predmét ― inanimate object
  2. inanimate (referring to the form in the accusative of a noun denoting nonliving things, or of an adjective that modifies it)
