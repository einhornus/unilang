цвет -> colour, blossom, prime
Noun, declension 2c (pattern грод)

Plural: цвет
Genitive: цвта



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *květъ, from *ḱweyt- (“white”), whence English white
  • From цвести́ (cvestí)


Definitions (English):
  • colour
    • blossom, bloom, flower
      • (figuratively or poetic) flower, cream, pick,
        • цвет на́ции ― cvet nácii ― cream of the nation
