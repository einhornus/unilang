пры́снуть (14829)


Etymology

Perhaps from прыс(кать) (prýs(katʹ)) +‎ -нуть (-nutʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈprɨsnʊtʲ]

Verb

пры́снуть • (prýsnutʹ) pf (imperfective пры́скать)

  1. to burst intó laughter
  2. to dart, to dash
  3. to sprinkle (+ instrumental)
  4. (perfective only) to gush, to spurt
