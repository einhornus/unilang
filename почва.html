по́чва -> soil
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: по́чвы
Genitive: по́чвы



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic подъшьва (podŭšĭva, “sole (bottom) foundation”), ultimately from Proto-Slavic *šiti (“to sew”). Doublet of подо́шва (podóšva, “sole, (mountain) foot”)



Definitions (English):
  • earth, soil, ground (mixture of sand and organic material)
    • basis, base, ground
