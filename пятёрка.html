пятёрка -> the number five, excellent (exam mark)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: пятёрки
Genitive: пятёрки


Definitions (English):
  • a grade in school of five, where five is the highest grade.
    • Я получи́л пятёрку по литерату́ре. Ja polučíl pjatjórku po literatúre. I got an A in literature.(literally, “I got a 5 in literature.”)
  • the figure five written out
    • fiver
