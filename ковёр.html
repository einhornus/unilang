ковёр -> carpet
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: ковры
Genitive: ковра



Etymology: Jooseppi Julius Mikkola, quoted by Vasmer and Karl BrugmannΏ], asserts that it is from Old Norse kǫgurrΐ]. A Turkic origin has also been theorised. Compare Slovak and Czech koberec, polish kobierzec, archaic Bulgarian губер (guber).
