забо́та -> care, trouble
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: забо́ты
Genitive: забо́ты



Etymology: From Old East Slavic зобота (zobota). Cognate with Proto-West Germanic *kapēn (“to watch, to look”), Proto-Germanic *kōpijaną (“to look after, care for”).



Definitions (English):
  • care
    • concern, anxiety, worry, trouble
