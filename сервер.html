се́рвер (6233)


Etymology

Borrowed from English server.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲervʲɪr]

Noun

се́рвер • (sérver) masculine gender">m inan (genitive се́рвера, nominative plural се́рверы, genitive plural се́рверов)

  1. (computing) server (a computer or a program which provides services to other programs or users)

Synonyms

  • сервак (servák)

Related terms

  • сервачок (serváčok)
  • блейд-сервер (blejd-sérver), се́рвис (sɛrvis)
  • серверный (sérvernyj)
