игра́ть -> to play
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *jьgrati. Cognate to Serbo-Croatian ȉgrati and Czech hrát.

    • to gamble
      • (of a storm) to rage
        • (of wine) to sparkle
