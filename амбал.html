амба́л (18006)


Etymology

Borrowed from Arabic حَمَّال (ḥammāl).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐmˈbal]

Noun

амба́л • (ambál) m anim (genitive амба́ла, nominative plural амба́лы, genitive plural амба́лов)

  1. (slang) strong, healthy man
  2. (dated, dialectal) Persian longshoreman working in Caspian Sea and lower Volga ports
  3. (dated, dialectal) loader, unskilled worker
