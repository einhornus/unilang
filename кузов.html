ку́зов -> body (of vehicle), basket
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: кузова
Genitive: кузова



Etymology: From Tatar kyzau̯ (“basket”). Compare Polish kozub, Slovene kozòɫ.



Definitions (English):
  • basket. Synonyms:
    • корзи́на (korzína), короб (kórob)
  • body (largest or most important part of anything (e.g. car bodywork))
