ста́ртер (22274)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from English starter.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈstartʲɪr]

Noun

ста́ртер • (stárter) m inan or m anim (genitive ста́ртера, nominative plural ста́ртеры, genitive plural ста́ртеров)

  1. starter (electric motor that starts an internal-combustion engine)
  2. (animate) starter (person who gives the signal to start)

Etymology 2

Noun

ста́ртер • (startjór) m inan or m anim

  1. Alternative spelling of стартёр (startjór)
