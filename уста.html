уста́ (5330)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *usta, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃éh₁os (“mouth”). Cognates include Sanskrit आस् (ās, “mouth”) and Latin os (“mouth”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈsta]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

уста́ • (ustá) n inan pl (genitive уст, plural only)

  1. (archaic) mouth, lips

Synonyms[edit]

  • рот (rot)
  • губы (guby)

Related terms[edit]

  • у́стный (ústnyj)
  • у́стье (ústʹje)
  • наизу́сть (naizústʹ)
