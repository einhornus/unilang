уста́ -> mouth, lips (poetic)
Noun, declension 2b
Plural: уста́
Genitive:  —



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *usta, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃éh₁os (“mouth”). Cognates include Sanskrit आस् (ās, “mouth”) and Latin os (“mouth”).



Definitions (English):
  • (archaic) mouth, lips
