тон -> tone, musical tone, colour tone, tint
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: тона
Genitive: тона



Etymology: Borrowed from Latin tonus, from Ancient Greek τόνος (tónos) cf. French ton. See also тонус (tónus).

  • (of a color) tone, shade, plural:

  • intonation
    • (physiology) muscle tone
