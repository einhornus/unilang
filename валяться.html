валя́ться (2197)


Etymology

валя́ть (valjátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vɐˈlʲat͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

валя́ться • (valjátʹsja) impf

  1. (intransitive) to roll around, to roll from side to side; to wallow
  2. (colloquial) to lie about (idly or as a result of being sick; regularly, periodically or for some amount of time)
    Perfective: проваляться (provaljátʹsja)
  3. (colloquial) to lie about (in disorder), to be strewn carelessly
  4. passive of валя́ть (valjátʹ)
Related terms
  • валяние (valjánije)
