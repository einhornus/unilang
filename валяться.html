валя́ться -> to roll, lie about
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я валя́юсь
Ты валя́ешься
Past: валя́лся/валя́лась



Etymology: валя́ть (valjátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • (intransitive) to roll around, to roll from side to side to wallow
    • (colloquial) to lie about (idly or as a result of being sick regularly, periodically or for some amount of time) Perfective:
      • проваляться (provaljátʹsja)
    • (colloquial) to lie about (in disorder), to be strewn carelessly
      • passive of валя́ть (valjátʹ)
