сократить -> to shorten, reduce
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я сокращу
Ты сократишь
Past: сократил/сократила



Etymology: From кра́ткий (krátkij). Compare the Belarusian скараці́ць (skaracícʹ) and Ukrainian скороти́ти (skorotýty).



Definitions (English):
  • to shorten, to curtail, to abridge, to abbreviate
    • to reduce, to cut, to curtail, to retrench
      • to dismiss, to discharge to lay off
        • (mathematics) to cancel, to abbreviate by cancellation
