Noun[edit]

то́рмоз • (tórmoz) m inan (genitive тормоза, nominative plural тормоза, genitive plural тормозов, related adjective тормозно́й)

  1. brake, brakes
  2. (figuratively) drag
  3. (figuratively) slow-witted person, retard
