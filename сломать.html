Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я слома́ю

Past: слома́л/слома́ла



Etymology: с- (s-) +‎ лома́ть (lomátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to break, to fracture,
    • язы́к слома́ешь ― jazýk slomáješʹ ― it's a real tongue-twister (literally, “you will break (my) tongue”)
  • to demolish, to pull down (a house)
    • (transitive) to destroy, to break down, to ruin
