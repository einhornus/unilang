просо́вывать (24716)


Etymology

про- (pro-) + -со́вывать (-sóvyvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈsovɨvətʲ]

Verb

просо́вывать • (prosóvyvatʹ) impf (perfective просу́нуть)

  1. to put/push through/in, to force through, to shove through/in, to thrust through/in, to pass through/in

Related terms

  • су́нуть impf (súnutʹ), сова́ть pf (sovátʹ)
