зае́хать (5295)


Etymology

From за- (za-) + е́хать (jéxatʹ, “to go (by vehicle)”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈjexətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

зае́хать • (zajéxatʹ) pf (imperfective заезжа́ть)

  1. to call in on the way
    Мы к вам зае́хали на ча́с.
    My k vam zajéxali na čás.
    We've called in to you (just) for an hour.
  2. to drive into
