способность -> ability
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: способности
Genitive: способности



Etymology: From спосо́бный (sposóbnyj) +‎ -ость (-ostʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • ability, capability (the quality or state of being able)
    • faculty
      • capacity,
        • покупа́тельная спосо́бность ― pokupátelʹnaja sposóbnostʹ ― purchasing power
      • talent (often plural)
        • power
          • quality
