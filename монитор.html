монито́р (7661)


Etymology

From English monitor.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mənʲɪˈtor]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

монито́р • (monitór) m inan (genitive монито́ра, nominative plural монито́ры, genitive plural монито́ров)

  1. (computing) monitor, a screen that displays graphics and images
  2. (engineering) hydraulic giant, hydraulic monitor, hydraulic gun

Synonyms

  • (computing) диспле́й (displéj, displɛj)
  • (engineering) гидромонитор (gidromonitór)
