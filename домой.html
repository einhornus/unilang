домо́й -> home, homewards

Etymology: From Old East Slavic домови (domovi), домовь (domovĭ), from Proto-Slavic *domovi, the u-stem dative singular of *domъ (“home”).



Definitions (English):
  • home, homewards, to the house.
    • Мне пора́ домо́й ― Mne porá domój ― It’s time for me to go home.
  • second-person singular imperative of домыть (domytʹ)
