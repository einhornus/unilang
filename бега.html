бега (22478)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲɪˈɡa]

Noun

бега • (begá) m inan pl (genitive бегов, plural only)

  1. races, horse-race
  2. hiding
    быть в бегах ― bytʹ v begáx ― to be on the run, to be in hiding, to be outlawed

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈbʲeɡə]

Noun

бега • (béga) m inan

  1. genitive singular of бег (beg)

Etymology 3

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʲɪˈɡa]

Noun

бега • (begá) m inan pl

  1. nominative plural of бег (beg)
  2. accusative plural of бег (beg)
