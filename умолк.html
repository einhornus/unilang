умолк (7006)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈuməlk]

Noun

умолк • (úmolk) m inan (genitive у́молка, nominative plural у́молки, genitive plural у́молков)

  1. (in expresions only)
    без у́молку ― bez úmolku ― unceasingly, incessantly, nonstop
    говори́ть без у́молку ― govorítʹ bez úmolku ― to talk without́ a stop

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈmolk]

Verb

умолк • (umólk)

  1. short masculine singular past indicative perfective of умо́лкнуть (umólknutʹ)
