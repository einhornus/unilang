па́че (14957)


Etymology

From Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic па́че (pače, “more”), comparative of пакы (paky, “again, moreover”), from Proto-Slavic *paky. Compare паки (páki).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpat͡ɕe]

Adverb

па́че • (páče)

  1. (archaic) more
    тем па́че ― tem páče ― the more so

Synonyms

  • больше (bólʹše)
