гварде́йский (9025)


Etymology

гварде(ец) (gvardé(jec)) +‎ -ский (-skij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡvɐrˈdʲejskʲɪj]

Adjective

гварде́йский • (gvardéjskij)

  1. (relational, military) guard (squad responsible for protecting something)
  2. guardsman's
  3. (relational, military) Guards (in the names of units)

Related terms

  • гварде́ец (gvardéjec)
  • гва́рдия (gvárdija)
