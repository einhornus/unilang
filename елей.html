елей (15559)


Etymology 1

Borrowing from Ancient Greek ἔλαιον (élaion, “oil”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [(j)ɪˈlʲej]

Noun

елей • (jeléj) m inan (genitive еле́я, nominative plural еле́и, genitive plural еле́ев)

  1. holy oil, chrism
  2. (figuratively) balm, unction
Related terms
  • елейный (jeléjnyj)
  • елейность (jeléjnostʹ)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

Noun

елей • (jélej) f inan pl

  1. genitive plural of ель (jelʹ)
