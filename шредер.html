шре́дер (17592)


Etymology

Borrowed from English shredder.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʂrɛdɨr] (phonetic respelling: шрэдэр)

Noun

шре́дер • (šrɛdɛr) m inan (genitive шре́дера, nominative plural шре́деры, genitive plural шре́деров)

  1. shredder (machine that tears up objects intó smaller pieces)
