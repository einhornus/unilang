беспарти́йный -> without party affiliation, independent
Adjective



Etymology: From без- (bez-) +‎ па́ртия (pártija) +‎ -ный (-nyj), a calque of German parteilos. First attested in 1901.



Definitions (English):
  • non-party, belonging to no party
