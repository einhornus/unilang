обло́м (17653)


Etymology

Deverbal from обломи́ть (oblomítʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈblom]

Noun

обло́м • (oblóm) m inan (genitive обло́ма, nominative plural обло́мы, genitive plural обло́мов)

  1. breaking off (breaking a piece off of something)
  2. something broken off, fragment
  3. place of fracture
  4. crack or bump at a place of fracture
  5. (architecture) molding
  6. (military) breastwork
  7. (slang) failure
  8. (colloquial) frustration

Synonyms

  • (fragment): обло́мок (oblómok)

Related terms

  • обла́мывать impf (oblámyvatʹ), обломи́ть pf (oblomítʹ), облома́ть pf (oblomátʹ)
