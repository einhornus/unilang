паро́ль (7401)


Etymology

Borrowed from French parole (“promise, word”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈrolʲ]
  • Rhymes: -olʲ

Noun

паро́ль • (parólʹ) m inan (genitive паро́ля, nominative plural паро́ли, genitive plural паро́лей)

  1. password
