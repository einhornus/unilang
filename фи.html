фи (21773)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [fʲi]

Etymology 1

Noun

фи • (fi) f inan (indeclinable)

  1. (Greek letter) phi

Etymology 2

Interjectioń

фи • (fi)

  1. Used to show (usually arrogant) lack of admiration, interest, or inspiration: meh, whatever
  2. Used to show (usually arrogant) disapproval, discontent: yuck, bah, pah, pfft (samé as фу)
