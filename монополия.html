моноплия -> monopoly
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: моноплии
Genitive: моноплии



Etymology: Borrowed from Latin monopōlium via Polish monopolia or New High German Monopolia, from Ancient Greek μονοπώλιον (monopṓlion).



Definitions (English):
  • monopoly (various senses)
    • exclusive domain
      • (historical, pre-Soviet Russia) a government liquor store
