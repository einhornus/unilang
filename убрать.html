убра́ть (2020)


Etymology

у- (u-, “awaý”) +‎ брать (bratʹ, “take, get, accept”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ʊˈbratʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

убра́ть • (ubrátʹ) pf (imperfective убира́ть)

  1. to remove, to take awaý
  2. to strike (nautical)
  3. to clean up, to tidy, to tidy up
  4. to put awaý
  5. to clear, to clear up, to clear out
  6. to deck out
  7. to decorate
  8. to take in

Related terms

  • убра́ться pf (ubrátʹsja)
