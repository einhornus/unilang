проня́ть (18709)


Etymology

про- (pro-) +‎ -нять (-njatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈnʲætʲ]

Verb

проня́ть • (pronjátʹ) pf (imperfective пронимать)

  1. (colloquial, figuratively) to penetrate (of the cold, the wind, etc.)
  2. (colloquial) to impress, to make a big impression on

Related terms

  • проём (projóm), проёмный (projómnyj)
  • -нять (-njatʹ)
  • ять (jatʹ)
