отдохну́ть (2500)


Etymology

от- (ot-) +‎ дохну́ть (doxnútʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐdːɐxˈnutʲ]
  • отдохну́ть
    		(file)

Verb

отдохну́ть • (otdoxnútʹ) pf (imperfective отдыха́ть)

  1. to rest, to relax, to have a rest
  2. to be resting, to be on holiday, to take a vacation

Related terms

  • о́тдых (ótdyx)
