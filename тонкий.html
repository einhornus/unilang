тнкий -> thin, slender, fine, subtle
Adjective



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic тънъкъ (tŭnŭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *tьnъkъ.



Definitions (English):
  • thin
    • slim, slender
      • small, fine, delicate
        • fine, high-quality (of food, wine, etc.)
          • subtle
            • keen
              • high (voice)
