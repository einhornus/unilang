су́дарь (7196)


Etymology

Contracted form of госуда́рь (gosudárʹ, “sire”). Rather archaic.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsudərʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

су́дарь • (súdarʹ) masculine gender">m anim (genitive су́даря, nominative plural су́дари, genitive plural су́дарей, feminine суда́рыня)

  1. sir

Usage notes

  • Not in common use since 1917. Despite some attempts, the political reforms in Russia and other Ex-USSR states have not revived the word. Still found in older fiction and is used by some speakers.

Related terms

  • суда́рыня (sudárynja)
