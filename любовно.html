любо́вно (12143)


Etymology

любо́вный (ljubóvnyj) +‎ -о (-o)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲʊˈbovnə]

Adverb

любо́вно • (ljubóvno)

  1. fondly, lovingly
  2. painstakingly
