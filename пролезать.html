пролеза́ть (21706)


Etymology

проле́зть (proléztʹ) +‎ -ать (-átʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prəlʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

пролеза́ть • (prolezátʹ) impf (perfective проле́зть)

  1. to get through́, to wriggle through́, to climb through́, to squeeze through́
  2. to go (through́)
    шкаф не пролеза́ет в дверь ― škaf ne prolezájet v dverʹ ― the wardrobe won't go through́ the door
  3. (colloquial) to worm oneself (intó)

Related terms

  • лезть impf (leztʹ), поле́зть pf (poléztʹ)
