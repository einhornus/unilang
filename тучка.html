ту́чка (15575)


Etymology

ту́ча (túča) + -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈtut͡ɕkə]

Noun

ту́чка • (túčka) f inan (genitive ту́чки, nominative plural ту́чки, genitive plural ту́чек)

  1. Diminutive of ту́ча (túča, “cloud”): cloudlet, small (rain) cloud

Related terms

  • ту́ча (túča)
