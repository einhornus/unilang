Noun[edit]

отве́рстие • (otvérstije) n inan (genitive отве́рстия, nominative plural отве́рстия, genitive plural отве́рстий)

  1. opening, aperture, hole, perforation
  2. mesh (of a screen)
  3. orifice, mouth, vent, passage, inlet, outlet, port
  4. break, gap
  5. bore
  6. span (of a bridge)

Related terms[edit]
