пневмати́ческий (25032)


Etymology

Probably from French pneumatique +‎ -еский (-eskij).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pnʲɪvmɐˈtʲit͡ɕɪskʲɪj]

Adjective

пневмати́ческий • (pnevmatíčeskij)

  1. (relational) air; pneumatic (relating to air or other gases)
    пневмати́ческий пистолет ― pnevmatíčeskij pistolét ― air pistol
    пневмати́ческий молоток ― pnevmatíčeskij molotók ― nail gun (literally, “pneumatic hammer”)
