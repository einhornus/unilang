прозва́ть (7481)


Etymology

про- (pro-) +‎ зва́ть (zvatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐzˈvatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

прозва́ть • (prozvátʹ) pf (imperfective прозывать)

  1. to name someone/something as something, to make a name or a nickname (to someone or something); to nickname

Related terms

  • про́звище (prózvišče)
