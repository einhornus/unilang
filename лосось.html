лосо́сь (23569)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *lososь, from Proto-Indo-European *laks-. Cognate with Old High German lahs, Icelandic lax, German Lachs, Yiddish לאַקס‎ (laks), English lox.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lɐˈsosʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

лосо́сь • (losósʹ) m anim (genitive лосо́ся, nominative plural лосо́си, genitive plural лосо́сей, related adjective лососёвый or лососий)

  1. salmon (the fish and the seafood)

Synonyms

  • сёмга (sjómga)
