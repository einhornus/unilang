копйка -> kopeck
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: копйки
Genitive: копйки



Etymology: From Middle Russian копе́йка, from копьё (spear) (from the image of a horseman carrying a spear on the coins minted by Moscow after the capture of Novgorod in 1478).



Definitions (English):
  • kopek, kopeck also copeck (one 100th of ruble, the monetary unit of Russia).
