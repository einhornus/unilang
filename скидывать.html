скдывать -> to throw off (coll)
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я скдываю
Ты скдываешь
Past: скдывал/скдывала



Etymology: скид- (skid-) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to throw down, to throw off,
    • ски́дывать снег с кры́ши ― skídyvatʹ sneg s krýši ― to throw the snow off the roof
  • (colloquial) (clothes, shoes) to take off, to throw off
    • (colloquial) to throw off, to dethrone
      • to throw together,
        • ски́дывать в ку́чу ― skídyvatʹ v kúču ― to pile heap
      • (colloquial) to knock off, to discount
        • (slang) to copy (to), to e-mail (to)
          • ски́дывать на диск ― skídyvatʹ na disk ― to save to a disk
