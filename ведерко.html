ведерко (16791)


Etymology

ведро́ (vedró) +‎ -ко (-ko)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪˈdʲɵrkə]

Noun

ведёрко • (vedjórko) n inan (genitive ведёрка, nominative plural ведёрки, genitive plural ведёрок)

  1. endearing diminutive of ведро́ (vedró): small bucket, small pail
  2. (colloquial) flashing light (e.g. on a police car)

Related terms

  • ведро́ (vedró)
