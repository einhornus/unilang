высыпа́ть (7261)


Etymology 1

вы- (vý-) +‎ сы́пать (sýpatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨsɨpətʲ]

Verb

высыпа́ть • (výsypatʹ) pf (imperfective высыпа́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to strew out, to pour out, to empty (to distribute objects or pieces of something)
  2. to pour out, to throng (of a crowd)
  3. to break out, to come out (of a rash, etc.)
