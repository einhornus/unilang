пи́кнуть (18357)


Etymology

пикать (píkatʹ) +‎ -нуть (-nutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpʲiknʊtʲ]

Verb

пи́кнуть • (píknutʹ) pf (imperfective пикать)

  1. (colloquial) to peep, to squeak
  2. (colloquial, usually negative) to utter a peep, to cry out, to moan
    Хотя́ ему́ бы́ло о́чень больно, он и не пи́кнул.
    Xotjá jemú býlo óčenʹ bólʹno, on i ne píknul.
    Although it hurt a lot, he didn't utter a peep.
  3. (colloquial, figuratively) to object, to express one's opinion

Related terms

  • пиканье (píkanʹje), пикание (pikánije)
