борщ -> borsch, beetroot soup
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язк)

Plural: борщ
Genitive: борщ



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *bъrščь, originally meaning “hogweed”, then “hogweed soup”, later “beetroot soup”, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *bursktis.



Definitions (English):
  • borscht, borsht, borshch, borsch (a soup made of beets and cabbage)
    • (dated) hogweed ( Heracleum sphondylium)
