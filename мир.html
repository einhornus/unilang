мир -> world, peace
Noun, declension 2c (pattern грод)

Plural: мир
Genitive: мра



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *mirъ (“peace world”), from Proto-Indo-European *meyH-ró-s, whence English mitigate.



Definitions (English):
  • (usually uncountable) peace,
    • война́ и мир ― vojná i mir ― war and peace, мир во всём ми́ре ― mir vo vsjóm míre ― world peace, оплот ми́ра ― oplót míra ― stronghold of peace
  • universe world planet,
    • происхожде́ние ми́ра ― proisxoždénije míra ― origin of the universe
