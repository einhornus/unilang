забега́ловка (15117)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zəbʲɪˈɡaləfkə]

Noun

забега́ловка • (zabegálovka) f inan (genitive забега́ловки, nominative plural забега́ловки, genitive plural забега́ловок)

  1. (colloquial) eatery, eating house, snack bar (usually of low quality)
  2. (colloquial) a place (e.g. a business, shop) with a low reputation
