макшка -> top, crown (of head)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: макшки
Genitive: макшки



Etymology: From мак (mak, “poppy”) +‎ -ушка (-úška) from the resemblance of the crown of the head to a poppy head (compare маковка (mákovka, “poppy, poppy seed, onion dome, cupola, crown of the head, top, summit”)).



Definitions (English):
  • crown, pate (of the head)
    • top, summit, vertex
