маку́шка (6602)


Etymology

From мак (mak, “poppy”) +‎ -ушка (-úška); from the resemblance of the crown of the head to a poppy head (compare ма́ковка (mákovka, “poppy, poppy seed, onion dome, cupola, crown of the head, top, summit”)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mɐˈkuʂkə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

маку́шка • (makúška) f inan (genitive маку́шки, nominative plural маку́шки, genitive plural маку́шек)

  1. crown, pate (of the head)
  2. top, summit, vertex
