макушка -> top, crown (of head)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: макушки
Genitive: макушки



Etymology: From мак (mak, “poppy”) +‎ -у́шка (-úška) from the resemblance of the crown of the head to a poppy head (compare ма́ковка (mákovka, “poppy, poppy seed, onion dome, cupola, crown of the head, top, summit”)).



Definitions (English):
  • crown, pate (of the head)
    • top, summit, vertex
