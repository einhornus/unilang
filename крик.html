крик -> cry, shout
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: крики
Genitive: крика



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *krȋkъ.
  • Borrowed from English creek.


Definitions (English):
  • cry (a shout or scream), outcry, scream, shout, shouting, yell
    • creek (in English and American creek names)
