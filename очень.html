очень -> very

Etymology: Unknown. Often interpreted as inherited from Old East Slavic *очьнь (*očĭnĭ) from о́ко (oko, “eye”) the semantic shift might be "visibly > obviously > prominently > very". The non-productive ending is similar to adverbs like вкривь (vkrivʹ), впрямь (vprjamʹ), встарь (vstarʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • very, much, very much
