солове́цкий (26593)


Etymology

Соловки (Solovkí) +‎ -ский (-skij)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [səlɐˈvʲet͡skʲɪj]

Adjective

солове́цкий • (solovéckij) (no comparative)

  1. (relational) Solovki, Solovetsky Islands (archipelago in the White Sea)
    Соловетские острова ― Solovétskije ostrová ― Solovetsky Islands
  2. (relational) District">Solovetsky District (district containing the Solovetsky Islands)
  3. (relational) Monastery">Solovetsky Monastery (monastery on the Solovetsky Islands)
  4. (relational) Solovetsky, Solovetskaya, Solovetskoye (any of various Russian settlements with these names)

Related terms

  • Соловки (Solovkí)
