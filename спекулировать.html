спекули́ровать (20577)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from German spekulieren +‎ -овать (-ovatʹ); see also -ировать (-írovatʹ).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [spʲɪkʊˈlʲirəvətʲ]

Verb[edit]

спекули́ровать • (spekulírovatʹ) impf

  1. (financial) to speculate, to profiteer, to gamble (in)
  2. (figuratively) to exploit, to take advantage of
  3. to speculate (on)

Related terms[edit]

  • спекуляти́вный (spekuljatívnyj)
  • спекулянт (spekuljánt)
