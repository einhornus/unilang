кудря́вый -> curly
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic кудрꙗвꙑи (kudrjavyi), from Proto-Slavic *kǫdrjavъ, from *kǫdrь + *-avъ.



Definitions (English):
  • having curls
    • curly (of smoke, water, hair)
      • budding, lush (of vegetation)
        • exquisitely or overly decorated, artsy
