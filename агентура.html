агенту́ра (10591)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐɡʲɪnˈturə]

Noun

агенту́ра • (agentúra) f inan (genitive агенту́ры, nominative plural агенту́ры, genitive plural агенту́р, related adjective агенту́рный)

  1. group/community of agents, collectively (in various senses of "agent", especially but not exclusively referring to secret or intelligence service agents)

Related terms

  • аге́нт m (agént)
