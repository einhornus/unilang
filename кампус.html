ка́мпус (18076)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin campus (“field”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkampʊs]

Noun

ка́мпус • (kámpus) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ка́мпуса, nominative plural ка́мпусы, genitive plural ка́мпусов)

  1. campus (grounds or property of a school, etc)
