отлета́ть (16931)


Etymology

от- (ot-) + лета́ть (letátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtlʲɪˈtatʲ]

Verb

отлета́ть • (otletátʹ) impf (perfective отлете́ть)

  1. to fly away, to fly off
  2. to rebound, to bounce back
  3. (colloquial) to come off, to break off

Verb

отлета́ть • (otletátʹ) pf (imperfective отлётывать)

  1. to complete a flight
  2. to fly (for a certain period)
    он отлета́л 20 лет ― on otletál 20 let ― he has done twenty years' flying
    отлета́ть своё ― otletátʹ svojó ― to be through with one's career of a pilot

Related terms

  • лете́ть impf (letétʹ), полете́ть pf (poletétʹ)

  • лётный (ljótnyj)
