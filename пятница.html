пя́тница (4852)


Etymology

From пять (pjatʹ, “five”) +‎ -ница (-nica), since Friday is the fifth day of the week (counting from Monday). Compare Ukrainian пятниця (pjátnycja), Old East Slavic пятъкъ, Old Church Slavonic пѧтъкъ (pętŭkŭ), Bulgarian петък (pétǎk) and петка (pétka), and Serbo-Croatian петак.
