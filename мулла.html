мулла́ (22669)


Etymology

Borrowed from Tatar мулла́ (mulla), from Arabic مولى‎. Attested since 17th century.

Replaced earlier (16th – 18th cc.) молла (molla).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʊˈlːa]

Noun

мулла́ • (mullá) m anim (genitive муллы́, nominative plural муллы́, genitive plural мулл)

  1. (Islam) mullah (a Muslim religious scholar and teacher)
