ка́чка (14049)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkat͡ɕkə]

Noun

ка́чка • (káčka) f inan (genitive ка́чки, nominative plural ка́чки, genitive plural ка́чек)

  1. (nautical) rolling, pitching
  2. (ornithology, regional) duck
Synonyms
  • утка́ (útka)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐt͡ɕˈka]

Noun

ка́чка • (kačká) m anim

  1. genitive singular of качок (kačók)
  2. accusative singular of качок (kačók)
