геро́й -> hero
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: геро́и
Genitive: геро́я



Etymology: Borrowed from French héros, from Latin hērōs, from Ancient Greek ἥρως (hḗrōs, “demi-god, hero”). Influenced by the displaced earlier ирой (irój), directly from the Greek. Cognate with English hero.



Definitions (English):
  • hero
    • (literature) character, protagonist
