броса́ть -> to throw, abandon, give up
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я броса́ю
Ты броса́ешь
Past: броса́л/броса́ла



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *brъsati.



Definitions (English):
  • to throw, to drop.
    • Я начал броса́ть мяч. Ja načal brosátʹ mjač. I started to throw the ball.
  • to send urgently
    • to abandon, to forsake
      • to give up, to quit, to leave off
        • to jilt
