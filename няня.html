ння -> nurse
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: нни
Genitive: нни



Etymology: Since ancient times, from reduplication in children’s speech. The related form ня́нька (njánʹka) was first attested in the 12th century.



Definitions (English):
  • nurse
    • nanny
      • babysitter
