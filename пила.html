пила́ (1808)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪˈla]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *pila, from Old High German fil (“file”).

Noun

пила́ • (pilá) f inan (genitive пилы, nominative plural пилы, genitive plural пил)

  1. saw
    двуручная пила́ ― dvurúčnaja pilá ― lumberman's saw

Etymology 2

Verb

пила́ • (pilá)

  1. feminine singular past indicative imperfective of пить (pitʹ)
