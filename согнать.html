согна́ть (12016)


Etymology

со- (so-) + гнать (gnatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐɡˈnatʲ]

Verb

согна́ть • (sognátʹ) pf (imperfective сгоня́ть)

  1. to drive away
  2. to drive together (cattle, etc.)
  3. (colloquial) to lose (weight)
  4. to remove (from the surface of)

Related terms

  • гнать (gnatʹ)
