иссле́дование -> investigation, research
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: иссле́дования
Genitive: иссле́дования



Etymology: From the verb иссле́довать (isslédovatʹ, “to explore, examine”), which in turn comes from the noun след (sled, “track, trail, mark”).



Definitions (English):
  • research, study
    • exploration, investigation
      • analysis
        • research paper, study
