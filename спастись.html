спасти́сь -> to escape, save os
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b/b

Etymology: спасти́ (spastí) +‎ -сь (-sʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to save oneself, to escape,
    • он едва́ спа́сся ― on jedvá spássja ― he had a narrow escape
  • to use as the thing that helps
    • passive of спасти́ (spastí)
