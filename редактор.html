реда́ктор -> editor
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: редактора
Genitive: редактора



Etymology: Borrowed from French rédacteur (“editor”) (compare Czech redaktor).



Definitions (English):
  • (animate) editor (person who edits)
    • (inanimate, computing) editor, processor editing program
