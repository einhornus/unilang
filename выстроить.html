вы́строить (6286)


Etymology

вы- (vý-) +‎ строи́ть (stróitʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvɨstrəɪtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

вы́строить • (výstroitʹ) pf (imperfective выстра́ивать or строи́ть)

  1. to build
  2. to line up, to draw up, to form up
  3. to arrange
  4. (a structure, a theory) to build, to develop
