блуди́ть (29757)


Etymology

From блуд (blud). Compare with Old Church Slavonic блѫдити (blǫditi).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [blʊˈdʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

блуди́ть • (bludítʹ) impf

  1. (somewhat dated) to wander, to roam

Verb

блуди́ть • (bludítʹ) impf

  1. to fornicate, to whore around

Related terms

  • блудни́ца (bludníca)
  • блу́дный (blúdnyj)
