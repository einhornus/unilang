ишь -> look (drawing attention to sth) (coll)

Etymology: Contraction of вишь (višʹ)., in turn contraction of ви́дишь (vídišʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) see! (just) look, listen there oh now,
    • ишь ты! ― išʹ ty! ― how d'you like that! oh come on! what are you talking about!
