дурак (888)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʊˈrak]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

дурак • (durák) masculine gender">m anim (genitive дурака́, nominative plural дураки́, genitive plural дурако́в, feminine ду́ра)

  1. fool, simpleton, idiot (stupid person with poor judgment)
  2. a simple card game in which the last player holding cards in their hand is the loser

Related terms

  • ду́ра (dúra)
  • дурень (dúrenʹ)
  • дура́цкий (duráckij)
