вла́га -> damp, moisture
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: вла́ги
Genitive: вла́ги



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic вла́га (vlaga), from Proto-Slavic *volga. Displaced the Old East Slavic inherited form волога (vológa).



Definitions (English):
  • moisture


    Definitions (Russian):
