влага -> damp, moisture
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: влаги
Genitive: влаги



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic вла́га (vlaga), from Proto-Slavic *volga. Displaced the Old East Slavic inherited form воло́га (vológa).
