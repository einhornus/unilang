глава́ -> head, chief, chapter
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: главы
Genitive: главы



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic глава́ (glava), from Proto-Slavic *golva, from Proto-Balto-Slavic *galˀwāˀ. Doublet of голова́ (golová), the regular East Slavic reflex.



Definitions (English):
  • head, top, summit
    • cupola, dome
      • chapter, section (of a book)
        • (heraldry) chief (on a shield)
          • head, chief (title)
