прбовать -> (+inf) to try, (+a) test, taste
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я прбую
Ты прбуешь
Past: прбовал/прбовала



Etymology: From про́ба (próba), from Polish próba or German Probe, from Middle Latin proba, from probō, from Proto-Indo-European *pro-bhwo- (“being in front”), from *pro-, a variant of the base *per-, + the base *bhu- (to be).



Definitions (English):
  • to test, to attempt, to try
    • to taste (to sample the flavor of something), to feel
