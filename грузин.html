грузи́н (6692)


Etymology

Modification of Old East Slavic гурзи plural number">pl (gurzi, “Georgians”). Compare also Old East Slavic гурзиискъ (gurziiskŭ, adj), гурзыньскаꙗ землꙗ (gurzynĭskaja zemlja). Ultimately of the same origin as Persian گرج‎ (Gorj), as the word Georgia.
