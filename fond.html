fond (8120)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fɔ̃/
  • audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1

From Old French, from Latin fundus, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰudʰmḗn.

Noun

fond m (plural fonds)

  1. back
  2. bottom
  3. fund; funding
  4. foundation
  5. (figurative) basics, essence
  6. background
  7. (cooking) base
  8. (music) foundation stop on a pipe organ
Related terms
  • à fond
  • au fond
  • du fond du cœur
  • fondation
  • fondement
  • fonder
  • toile de fond
