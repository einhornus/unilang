лави́ровать (19799)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lɐˈvʲirəvətʲ]

Verb

лави́ровать • (lavírovatʹ) impf

  1. (nautical) to tack (to turn the bow through́ the wind)
  2. (figuratively) to manoeuvre
