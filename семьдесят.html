се́мьдесят (3368)


Etymology

Compounded from семь + де́сять, or seven tens. Десят was the Old East Slavic genitive plural, which is required by numbers пять and up.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲemʲdʲɪsʲɪt]
  • IPA(key): [ˈsʲemdʲɪsʲɪt] (phonetic respelling: се́мдесят)
  • audio
    		(file)

Numeral

се́мьдесят • (sémʹdesjat)

  1. seventy (70)

Usage notes

се́мьдесят (semʹdesjat) in the nominative case and accusative case governs the genitive plural of the noun. In other cases, it governs the corresponding plural case of the noun.

  • се́мьдесят ма́льчиков ― sémʹdesjat málʹčikov ― seventy boys
  • с семью́десятью ма́льчиками ― s semʹjúdesjatʹju málʹčikami ― with seventy boys
  • ма́льчиков се́мьдесят ― málʹčikov sémʹdesjat ― approximately seventy boys
