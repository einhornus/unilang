ладо́нь -> palm (of the hand)
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: ладо́ни
Genitive: ладо́ни



Etymology: Metathesis of Proto-Slavic *dolnь the expected form долонь (dólonʹ) occurs dialectally. Doublet of длань (dlanʹ), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic.



Definitions (English):
  • palm (of the hand)
