взвести́ (20605)


Etymology

вз- (vz-) + вести́ (vestí)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vzvʲɪˈsʲtʲi]

Verb

взвести́ • (vzvestí) pf (imperfective взводи́ть)

  1. (rare) to lead (someone/something) (на (na) + accusative, up something)
  2. to lift up, to cock (a trigger)
  3. (literary) to direct (one's eyes, etc.) up
  4. to unfairly direct (a charge, slander, etc.) (на (na) + accusative, against someone/something)

Related terms

  • води́ть (vodítʹ)
  • вести́ impf (vestí), повести́ pf (povestí)
