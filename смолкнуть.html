смо́лкнуть (6469)


Etymology[edit]

с- (s-) +‎ молкнуть (mólknutʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈsmolknʊtʲ]

Verb[edit]

смо́лкнуть • (smólknutʹ) pf (imperfective смолка́ть)

  1. (sound, noise) to cease, to stop
  2. to grow silent, to fall into silence, to fall silent

Related terms[edit]

  • смолча́ть (smolčátʹ)
