рвно -> evenly, precisely

Etymology: ро́вный (róvnyj) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • smoothly.
    • Доро́га идёт дово́льно ро́вно ― Doróga idjót dovólʹno róvno ― The road is quite smooth.
  • evenly, regularly, uniformly
    • sharp, exactly, precisely.
      • Я приду́ ро́вно в де́вять ― Ja pridú róvno v dévjatʹ ― I'll be there at nine sharp.
    • short neuter singular of ро́вный (róvnyj)
      • (vernacular) as if, as though, like (expresses an uncertain assumption)
        • (colloquial) absolutely, at all, completely, quite
