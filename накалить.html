накали́ть (19557)


Etymology

на- (na-) +‎ калить (kalítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nəkɐˈlʲitʲ]

Verb

накали́ть • (nakalítʹ) pf (imperfective накаливать or накалять)

  1. (transitive) to heat to a very high temperature (metal or something metallic)
    накали́ть докрасна ― nakalítʹ dokrasna ― to make red-hot
    накали́ть добела́ ― nakalítʹ dobelá ― to make white-hot
  2. to agitate, to stir up, to build up tension in
