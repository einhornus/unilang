сре́дство (496)


Etymology

среда́ (sredá) +‎ -ство (-stvo)

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Noun

сре́дство • (srédstvo) n inan (genitive средства, nominative plural средства, genitive plural сре́дств)

  1. mean, facility
  2. tool, instrument
  3. remedy
  4. (pl.) means (money)
