карту́з (13008)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐrˈtus]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

карту́з • (kartúz) m inan (genitive картуза́, nominative plural картузы́, genitive plural картузо́в, diminutive картузик)

  1. type of a peaked cap with high cap band
  2. (archaic) paper bag for various loose materials
  3. (archaic) powder bag
