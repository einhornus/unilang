зареза́ть (6678)


Etymology 1

за- (za-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈrʲezətʲ]

Verb

зареза́ть • (zarézatʹ) pf (imperfective зареза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in the word stress in the infinitive and the conjugation)

  1. to stab (to death), to knife, to kill, to carve up; to butcher, to stick
  2. (figuratively) to axe, to kill (a part in a play, a bill, etc.)
