отпусти́ть -> to let go, release
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я отпущу́
Ты отпу́стишь
Past: отпусти́л/отпусти́ла



Etymology: от- (ot-) +‎ пусти́ть (pustítʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to let go, to let off
    • to release, to set free,
      • отпусти́ть на во́лю ― otpustítʹ na vólju ― to set at liberty, to liberate, отпусти́ть на поруки ― otpustítʹ na porúki ― to take bail
    • to dismiss, to give leave
      • to supply, to serve, to issue, to give out, to sell,
        • отпусти́ть шу́тку ― otpustítʹ šútku ― to crack a joke, отпусти́ть комплимент ― otpustítʹ komplimént ― to make a compliment, to pay a compliment
      • to allot, to assign, to allow, to provide
        • to slacken, to relax, to turn loose, to loosen
          • (obsolete) to remit, to forgive,
            • отпусти́ть грехи́ ― otpustítʹ grexí ― to give absolution
          • (hair or beard) to grow, to let grow
            • (metal) to temper, to draw
              • (pain) to lessen, to ease


                Definitions (Russian):
