затво́р (7837)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐtˈvor]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

затво́р • (zatvór) masculine gender">m inan (genitive затво́ра, nominative plural затво́ры, genitive plural затво́ров)

  1. bolt, bar, latch (for locking a door, window, etc.)
  2. (engineering) gate, valve
  3. camera shutter
    спуск затво́ра ― spusk zatvóra ― shutter release
  4. (plumbing) trap
  5. (firearms) breechblock
  6. (electronics) gate (of a field-effect transistor)

Related terms

  • затворный (zatvórnyj)
  • затворять (zatvorjátʹ), затвори́ть (zatvorítʹ)
