проспе́кт (3099)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin prospectus (“view, sight”), probably via German Prospekt

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈspʲekt]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

проспе́кт • (prospékt) m inan (genitive проспе́кта, nominative plural проспе́кты, genitive plural проспе́ктов)

  1. avenue
  2. prospectus, outline
  3. booklet, folder, advertisement
    рекла́мные проспе́кты ― reklámnyje prospékty ― advertisements, promotional material
