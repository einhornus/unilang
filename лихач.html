лиха́ч (24025)


Etymology

лихой (lixój) +‎ -ач (-ač).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈxat͡ɕ]

Noun

лиха́ч • (lixáč) m anim (genitive лихача́, nominative plural лихачи́, genitive plural лихаче́й, feminine лихачка)

  1. (colloquial) daredevil, brave man
  2. (colloquial) roadhog; a reckless driver
