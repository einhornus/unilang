Verb[edit]

заве́сить • (zavésitʹ) pf (imperfective завешивать)

  1. to close (e.g. a window) by hanging something over it
  2. (figuratively) to obscure, to veil (of a cloud of smoke, the fog, the rain, etc.)
  3. (colloquial) to weigh
