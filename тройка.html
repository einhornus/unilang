тройка -> three, group of three, troika
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: тройки
Genitive: тройки



Etymology: From трой- (troj-) +‎ -ка (-ka).



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) the number three
    • grade of three (corresponds to American C grade)
      • (card games) three, trey
        • troika ( Russian carriage drawn by a team of three horses abreast)
