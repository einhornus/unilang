вор -> thief
Noun, declension 2e
Plural: во́ры
Genitive: во́ра



Etymologies:
  • Probably related to врать (vratʹ, “to lie”).
  • Borrowed from English vore.


Definitions (English):
  • thief
    • (obsolete) traitor, apostate, outlaw
      • Clipping of вор в зако́не (vor v zakóne).
        • vore (sexual fetish)
