сажа́ть (4805)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈʐatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

сажа́ть • (sažátʹ) impf (perfective посади́ть)

  1. to seat, to set
  2. to plant
  3. to put in jail

Synonyms[edit]

  • сади́ть (sadítʹ) (colloquial)
