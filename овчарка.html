овча́рка (7969)


Etymology

овчар (ovčár) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐfˈt͡ɕarkə]

Noun

овча́рка • (ovčárka) f anim (genitive овча́рки, nominative plural овча́рки, genitive plural овча́рок)

  1. sheepdog, herding dog
  2. German Shepherd
