пришива́ть (22004)


Etymology

приши́ть (prišítʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪʂɨˈvatʲ]

Verb

пришива́ть • (prišivátʹ) impf (perfective приши́ть)

  1. to sew on; to nail on
  2. (slang) to kill, to murder, to waste
  3. (slang) to falsely accuse of a crime, to pin an accusation
