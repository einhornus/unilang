грусть -> sadness
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: грусти
Genitive: грусти



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *grustь. Vasmer adduces гру́да (grúda) and/or грызть (gryztʹ) as possible cognates.



Definitions (English):
  • sadness (state/emotion)
