хрип (9326)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [xrʲip]

Etymology 1

Noun

хрип • (xrip) m inan (genitive хри́па, nominative plural хри́пы, genitive plural хри́пов)

  1. wheeze (piping or whistling sound caused by difficult respiration)

Etymology 2

Verb

хрип • (xrip)

  1. short masculine singular past indicative imperfective of хрипнуть (xrípnutʹ)
  2. short masculine singular past indicative perfective of хрипнуть (xrípnutʹ)
