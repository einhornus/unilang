сты́чка -> skirmish, squabble
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: сты́чки
Genitive: сты́чки



Etymology: Per Dal, from стыкать (stykátʹ, “to connect, to join”) +‎ -ка (-ka) presumably the sense development was "joining" → "meeting" → "skirmishing".



Definitions (English):
  • skirmish (brief battle between small groups)
