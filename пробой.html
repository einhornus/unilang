пробой (29526)


Etymology 1[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈboj]

Noun[edit]

пробой • (probój) m inan (genitive пробо́я, nominative plural пробо́и, genitive plural пробо́ев)

  1. (for a lock) holdfast, clamp, hasp
  2. rupture, breakdown, punch-through, runaway, puncture, disruption, burst
    пробой ка́беля ― proboj kábelja ― cable fault

Related terms[edit]

  • пробива́ть impf (probivátʹ), проби́ть pf (probítʹ)
  • пробойник m
  • пробойный
