реши́ть -> to decide, solve
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я решу́
Ты реши́шь
Past: реши́л/реши́ла



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic рѣшити (rěšiti, “to untie, absolve”), from Proto-Indo-European *wreyḱ- (“to twist, bend”).



Definitions (English):
  • to solve, to settle
    • to decide, to resolve, to make up one's mind, to determine
