пу́стошь (18349)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpustəʂ]

Noun

пу́стошь • (pústošʹ) f inan (genitive пу́стоши, nominative plural пу́стоши, genitive plural пу́стошей)

  1. wasteland; uninhabited, abandoned or uncultivated area
