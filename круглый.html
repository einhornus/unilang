кру́глый -> round
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *krǫglъ synchronically analysable as круг (krug) +‎ -лый (-lyj). Compare Old Church Slavonic крѫглъ (krǫglŭ), Ukrainian круглий (krúhlyj), Bulgarian кръгъл (krǎgǎl), Serbo-Croatian округао, Slovene krógǝl, Czech okrouhlý, Polish krągły.



Definitions (English):
  • round
    • (colloquial) perfect, complete
