круглый -> round
Adjective



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *krǫglъ synchronically analysable as круг (krug) +‎ -лый (-lyj). Compare Old Church Slavonic крѫглъ (krǫglŭ), Ukrainian кру́глий (krúhlyj), Bulgarian кръ́гъл (krǎ́gǎl), Serbo-Croatian окру́гао, Slovene krógǝl, Czech okrouhlý, Polish krągły.



Definitions (English):
  • round
    • (colloquial) perfect, complete
