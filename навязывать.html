Etymology: навяза́ть (navjazátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to tie (on), to fasten (to)
    • to make (a number of), to knit,
      • навя́зывать узло́в ― navjázyvatʹ uzlóv ― to make a lot of knots, навя́зывать носко́в ― navjázyvatʹ noskóv ― to knit a lot of socks
    • to impose (on), to thrust (on), to foist (off) (on)
      • навя́зывать свою́ во́лю ― navjázyvatʹ svojú vólju ― to impose/dictate one's will (on)продаве́ц навя́зывает ей неисправный агрега́т ― prodavéc navjázyvajet jej neisprávnyj agregát ― the salesman is foisting (off) the faulty machine on her
