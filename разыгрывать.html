Etymology: разыгра́ть (razygrátʹ) +‎ -ывать (-yvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to play, to perform,
    • разы́грывать дурака́ ― razýgryvatʹ duráka ― to play the fool, make a fool of oneself, разы́грывать сце́ну ― razýgryvatʹ scénu ― to put up an act
  • to raffle off, to draw
    • (colloquial) to play a trick, to play a practical joke, to pull one's leg
