зали́в (5770)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [zɐˈlʲif]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1

From the vb. лить (litʹ, “to pour, shed, cast”), ultimately from Pie *h₂leybʰ-. Cognate with Latin lībō (“I sprinkle, spill”) and Ancient Greek ἀλείφω (aleíphō, “to anoint”).

Noun

зали́в • (zalív) masculine gender">m inan (genitive зали́ва, nominative plural зали́вы, genitive plural зали́вов)

  1. gulf
    Перси́дский зали́в ― Persídskij zalív ― The Persian Gulf
  2. bay

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Participle

зали́в • (zalív)

  1. short past adverbial perfective participle of зали́ть (zalítʹ)
