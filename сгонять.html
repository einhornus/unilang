Verb[edit]

сгоня́ть • (sgonjátʹ) impf (perfective согна́ть)

  1. to drive away
  2. to drive together (cattle, etc.)
  3. (colloquial) to lose (weight)
  4. to remove (from the surface of)

Related terms[edit]

  • гнать (gnatʹ)

Verb[edit]

сгоня́ть • (sgonjátʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial, intransitive) to move quickly
  2. (colloquial, transitive) to cause to go somewhere quickly and come back

Related terms[edit]

  • гнать (gnatʹ)
