ба́тя -> dad, father (coll) (= ба́тюшка)
Noun, declension 1a
Plural: ба́ти
Genitive: ба́ти



Etymology: From Old East Slavic батѧ (batę), from Proto-Slavic *batę, compare to Bulgarian баща (baštá). Alternatively from *brat(r)ъ.



Definitions (English):
  • (familiar) dad
    • familiar term of address for an elderly man
