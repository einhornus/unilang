автозаво́д (31235)


Etymology

First attested in 1926. авто- (avto-) + заво́д (zavód), calque of German Autowerk.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐftəzɐˈvot]
  • IPA(key): [ˌaftəzɐˈvot]

Noun

автозаво́д • (avtozavód) m inan (genitive автозаво́да, nominative plural автозаво́ды, genitive plural автозаво́дов)

  1. car factory, motor-car factory, automobile plant, automobile works, motor works
