пята́к (9934)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪˈtak]

Noun

пята́к • (pjaták) m inan (genitive пятака́, nominative plural пятаки́, genitive plural пятако́в, diminutive пятачо́к)

  1. five-kopeck coin
  2. five roubles, five dollars, etc.
  3. (slang) muzzle, mug (face); schnozzle, conk, honker, schnoz, snoot (nose)
