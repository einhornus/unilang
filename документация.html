документа́ция (12453)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dəkʊmʲɪnˈtat͡sɨjə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

документа́ция • (dokumentácija) f inan (genitive документа́ции, nominative plural документа́ции, genitive plural документа́ций)

  1. documentation (something transposed from a thought to a document)
  2. (computing) documentation (documents that explain the operation of a particular software program)
