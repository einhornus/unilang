перец (4695)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic пьпьрьць (pĭpĭrĭcĭ), from пьпьрь (pĭpĭrĭ, “pepper”) + -ьць (-ĭcĭ), from Proto-Slavic *pьpьrь. Cognate with English pepper.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpʲerʲɪt͡s]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

перец • (pérec) m inan (genitive пе́рца, nominative plural пе́рцы, genitive plural пе́рцев)

  1. pepper (including sweet peppers, chilis as well as black pepper)

Related terms

  • перчик (pérčik)
  • перечница (pérečnica)
  • перечный (pérečnyj)
  • перцовый (pércovyj)
  • перчить (perčítʹ)
