пе́рец -> pepper
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: пе́рцы
Genitive: пе́рца



Etymology: From Old East Slavic пьпьрьць (pĭpĭrĭcĭ), from пьпьрь (pĭpĭrĭ, “pepper”) + -ьць (-ĭcĭ), from Proto-Slavic *pьpьrь. Cognate with English pepper.



Definitions (English):
  • pepper (including sweet peppers, chilis as well as black pepper)
