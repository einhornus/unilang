кро́тко -> short, briefly

Etymology: From коро́ткий (korótkij) +‎ -о (-o), with stress shift in the adverb paralleling the short neuter adjective.



Definitions (English):
  • shortly, briefly, in brief
    • short, close (length of cut)
      • short neuter singular of коро́ткий (korótkij)
