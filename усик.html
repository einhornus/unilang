у́сик -> whisker, antenna, feeler
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: у́сики
Genitive: у́сика



Etymology: ус (us) +‎ -ик (-ik)



Definitions (English):
  • Diminutive of ус (us): little/short moustache or whisker
    • endearing form of ус (us)
      • barbel (of fish)
        • feeler, antenna (feeler organ on the head of an insect)
          • (botany) tendril, awn, runner
