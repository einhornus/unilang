Verb[edit]

поверну́ть • (povernútʹ) pf (imperfective повора́чивать)

  1. (transitive) to turn, to swing
  2. to turn (to change direction of one's motion)

Synonyms[edit]

  • поворотить (povorotítʹ) (low colloquial)

Related terms[edit]

  • поворо́т (povorót)
