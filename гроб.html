Noun[edit]

гроб • (grob) m inan (genitive гроба, nominative plural гробы́, genitive plural гробо́в)

  1. coffin
  2. (poetic) grave
    вогна́ть в гроб ― vognátʹ v grob ― to drive to the grave
    до гроба, по гроб жи́зни ― do gróba, po grob žízni ― until death, as long as one shall live
    Synonym: моги́ла (mogíla)
