гроб -> coffin
Noun, declension 2c (pattern го́род)

Plural: гроба
Genitive: гроба



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *grobъ.



Definitions (English):
  • coffin
    • (poetic) grave,
      • вогнать в гроб ― vognátʹ v grob ― to drive to the grave, до гроба, по гроб жи́зни ― do gróba, po grob žízni ― until death, as long as one shall live. Synonym: моги́ла (mogíla)
