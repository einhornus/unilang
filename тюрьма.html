тюрьма́ -> prison
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: тюрьмы
Genitive: тюрьмы



Etymology: Traditionally derived from Polish turma, from Middle High German turm, turn, from Old French *torn, from Latin turrem/turrim, accusative singular of turris, whence English tower. But more likely from Proto-Turkic *türmä (“prison”) compare Tatar төрмә (törmä, “prison”), Kyrgyz түрмө (türmö, “prison”).



Definitions (English):
  • prison, jail
