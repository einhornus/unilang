Participle[edit]

уважа́емый • (uvažájemyj)

  1. present passive imperfective participle of уважа́ть (uvažátʹ)

Adjective[edit]

уважа́емый • (uvažájemyj)

  1. dear, esteemed (a formal way of addressing somebody at the beginning of a letter)
  2. respectable
