кресто́вый (19267)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [krʲɪˈstovɨj]

Etymology 1

крест (krest, “cross”) +‎ -овый (-óvyj)

Adjective

кресто́вый • (krestóvyj)

  1. (relational) cross
    кресто́вая бу́лочка ― krestóvaja búločka ― hot cross bun (literally, “cross bun”)
  2. (relational) crusade (military expedition undertaken by the Christians of Europe in the 11th to 13th centuries)
    кресто́вый похо́д ― krestóvyj poxód ― crusade (literally, “crusade campaign”)

Etymology 2

крести́ (krésti, “clubs”) +‎ -овый (-óvyj)

Adjective

кресто́вый • (krestóvyj)

  1. (relational) clubs (card game)
    кресто́вый вале́т ― krestóvyj valét ― jack of clubs
Synonyms
  • трефовый (trefóvyj)
