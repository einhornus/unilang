ру́пор (15590)


Etymology

Borrowed from Dutch roeper, from roepen (“to shout”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈrupər]

Noun

ру́пор • (rúpor) m inan (genitive ру́пора, nominative plural ру́поры or рупора́, genitive plural ру́поров or рупоро́в)

  1. megaphone
