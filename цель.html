цель -> target, purpose
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: це́ли
Genitive: це́ли



Etymology: Borrowed from Polish cel (“aim, goal”), in turn fromMiddle High German zil.



Definitions (English):
  • goal, target, aim, end, object,
    • не попа́дать/попа́сть в цель ― ne popadátʹ/popástʹ v celʹ ― to miss (one's aim)
  • purpose
    • destination (of traveller)
      • second-person singular imperative imperfective of целить (célitʹ)
