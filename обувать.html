Etymology: обу́ть (obútʹ) +‎ -вать (-vátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to put shoes on somebody's feet, to help somebody put on his/her shoes,
    • обува́ть ноги ― obuvátʹ nogi ― to put on shoes
  • to provide with shoes
    • (slang) to trick, to take in, to snooker
