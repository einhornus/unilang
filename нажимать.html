нажима́ть (6085)


Etymology[edit]

на- (na-) +‎ -жимать (-žimátʹ)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [nəʐɨˈmatʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

нажима́ть • (nažimátʹ) impf (perfective нажа́ть)

  1. to press, to push
  2. to urge, to impel, to influence, to put pressure on
  3. to press on, to press ahead
  4. to make by pressing

Related terms[edit]

  • жать (žatʹ)
  • нажа́тие (nažátije)
  • нажа́тый (nažátyj)
