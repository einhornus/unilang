дре́безг (28854)


Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *drebězgъ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈdrʲebʲɪsk]

Noun

дре́безг • (drébezg) m inan (genitive дре́безга, nominative plural дре́безги, genitive plural дре́безгов)

  1. (colloquial) tinkling, sound of broken glass
  2. (colloquial) shard, fragment, smithereen

Related terms

  • дребезжа́ть (drebezžátʹ)
  • дребезжание (drebezžánije)
