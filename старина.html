старина (5868)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [stərʲɪˈna]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

старина • (stariná) f inan (genitive старины, nominative plural старины, genitive plural старин)

  1. olden time, olden days, antiquity
    в старину ― v starinú ― in olden times, formerly, in the old days
    тряхну́ть стариной ― trjaxnútʹ starinój ― to revive a bit of the past, to go back to the good old days
  2. antiquities, antique

Related terms

  • стари́нный (starínnyj)
  • ста́рый (stáryj)

Noun

старина • (stariná) masculine gender">m anim (genitive старины, nominative plural старины, genitive plural старин)

  1. (colloquial, address) old man, old boy, old chap, old fellow

Synonyms

  • старик (starík)
