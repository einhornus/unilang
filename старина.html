ста́рина -> bygone times, (coll) old fellow
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ста́рины
Genitive: ста́рины


Definitions (English):
  • olden time, olden days, antiquity,
    • в ста́рину ― v starinú ― in olden times, formerly, in the old days, тряхну́ть ста́риной ― trjaxnútʹ starinój ― to revive a bit of the past, to go back to the good old days
  • antiquities, antique
    • (colloquial, address) old man, old boy, old chap, old fellow
