пересе́чь -> to intersect, cross, cut across
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: b/b

Я пересеку́
Ты пересечёшь
Past: пересёк/пересекла́



Etymology: пере- (pere-) +‎ сечь (séčʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to intersect
    • to pierce into objects that are arranged in a line
      • to move across in a transverse direction,
        • пересе́чь грани́цу ― pereséčʹ granícu ― to cross the border
      • to move through any space vigorously
        • (colloquial) to divide into many parts
          • to whip, to flog (all or many)
