пень (5938)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic пьнь (pĭnĭ), from Proto-Slavic *pь̏ňь

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲenʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -enʲ

Noun

пень • (penʹ) masculine gender">m inan (genitive пня́, nominative plural пни́, genitive plural пне́й)

  1. stump, stub, rampike
  2. blockhead
  3. (slang) Pentium processor
