ягненок (23879)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic агнѧ (agnę), ꙗгнѧ (jagnę) +‎ -ёнок (-jónok), from Proto-Slavic *agnę, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂egʷn-, the stem of *h₂egʷnós. Compare а́гнец (ágnec).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [(j)ɪɡˈnʲɵnək]
  • (dialectal) IPA(key): [(j)ɪˈnʲɵnək] (phonetic respelling: янёнок)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ягнёнок • (jagnjónok) m anim (genitive ягнёнка, nominative plural ягня́та, genitive plural ягня́т)

  1. lamb
