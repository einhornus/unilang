топи́ть -> to stoke, heat, (p) sink (sth), drown (s.o.)
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я топлю́
Ты то́пишь
Past: топи́л/топи́ла



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *topìti (“to heat”).
  • From Proto-Slavic *topìti (“to heat”) (same as Etymology 1).
  • From Proto-Slavic *topìti (“to drown”).


Definitions (English):
  • to stoke (an oven)
    • to heat
      • to melt, to render (fat)
        • to sink
          • to drown (transitive)
            • (slang) to support (a club or political party)
              • to topple (a person involved in politics, a student during exam)
                • (slang) to drive (as qualifier "against the rules" or "much quicker than usually")
