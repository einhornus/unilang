Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈmɨlə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *mydlo, from *myti (“to wash”). Synchronically analyzable as мыть (mytʹ) +‎ -ло (-lo).

Noun[edit]

мы́ло • (mýlo) n inan (genitive мыла, nominative plural мыла, genitive plural мыл)

  1. (usually uncountable) soap
  2. (usually uncountable) lather
  3. (slang) e-mail (by consonance with English mail)
Related terms[edit]
  • мы́льница (mýlʹnica)
  • мы́льный (mýlʹnyj)

Verb[edit]

мы́ло • (mýlo)
