мы́ло -> soap
Noun, declension 2c
Plural: мыла
Genitive: мыла



Etymology: Inherited from Proto-Slavic *mydlo, from *myti (“to wash”). Synchronically analyzable as мыть (mytʹ) +‎ -ло (-lo).



Definitions (English):
  • (usually uncountable) soap
    • (usually uncountable) lather
      • (slang) e-mail (by consonance with English mail)
        • neuter singular past indicative imperfective of мыть (mytʹ)
