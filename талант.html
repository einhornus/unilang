тала́нт -> talent
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: тала́нты
Genitive: тала́нта


Definitions (English):
  • talent, gift,
    • зарывать свой тала́нт в зе́млю ― zaryvátʹ svoj talánt v zémlju ― to bury one's talent ≈ to hide one's light under a bushel ≈ to hide one's talents in a napkin
  • (colloquial) man or woman of talent, gifted person, talent
    • (historical) talent (measure of weight and monetary unit)
