Noun[edit]

молодня́к • (molodnják) m inan (genitive молодняка́, nominative plural молодняки́, genitive plural молодняко́в)

  1. (collective) young animals, cubs
  2. (collective) underwood, saplings
  3. (collective, colloquial) offspring, youngsters, young generation
