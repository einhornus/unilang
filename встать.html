встать -> to get up, stand up
Verb, perfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я вста́ну
Ты вста́нешь
Past: вста́л/вста́ла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic възстати (vŭzstati), from въз- (vŭz-) + стати (stati). Synchronically, from вз- / вс- (vz- / vs-, “up”) +‎ стать (statʹ). Compare восстать (vosstátʹ), with the Church Slavonic version of the prefix.



Definitions (English):
  • to stand up, to get up, to rise
    • to rise (sun)
      • to arise, to come up (question)
        • to stop
