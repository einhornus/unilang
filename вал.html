вал -> billow, rampart, gross output (econ)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: валы
Genitive: ва́ла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic валъ (valŭ), from Proto-Slavic *valъ.



Definitions (English):
  • billow, roller
    • barrage fire
      • rampart
        • shaft, axle
          • gross output,
            • вы́полнить план по ва́лу ― výpolnitʹ plan po valu ― to implement the plan by bulk output
