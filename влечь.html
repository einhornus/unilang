влечь -> to draw, attract
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: b/b

Я влёку
Ты влечёшь
Past: влёк/влекла



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic влѣщи (vlěšti), from Proto-Slavic *velťi. Doublet of воло́чь (volóčʹ), the inherited East Slavic form.
