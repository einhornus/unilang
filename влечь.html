влечь -> to draw, attract
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: b/b

Я влеку́
Ты влечёшь
Past: влёк/влекла́



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic влѣщи (vlěšti), from Proto-Slavic *velťi. Doublet of волочь (volóčʹ), the inherited East Slavic form.



Definitions (English):
  • to attract, to draw, to pull, to haul
    • to entail, to bring about.

Unless a valid reason is given, not attending the polling stations entails a fine in Luxembourg. Perfective: повлечь (povléčʹ)
