сток -> flow, drain
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: стки
Genitive: стка



Etymology: Deverbal of стечь (stečʹ, “to flow down, to drain”) (compare stem стек- (stek-)), with Indo-European o-vocalization characteristic of deverbal nouns.



Definitions (English):
  • flow, flowing, drainage
    • drain, gutter, sewer
