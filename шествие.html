ше́ствие -> procession
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: ше́ствия
Genitive: ше́ствия



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic шьствие (šĭstvie). Constructed from the root of Proto-Slavic *šьdlъ (resultative participle, later past tense, of *jьti (“to go”)) + *-ьstvo + *-ьje (compare Russian идти́ (idtí), -ство (-stvo), -ие (-ije)).



Definitions (English):
  • procession, demonstration


    Definitions (Russian):
    • прохожде́ние, как пра́вило, торже́ственное
