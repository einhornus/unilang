дико́винка (20288)


Etymology

дико́вина (dikóvina) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪˈkovʲɪnkə]

Noun

дико́винка • (dikóvinka) f inan (genitive дико́винки, nominative plural дико́винки, genitive plural дико́винок)

  1. endearing diminutive of дико́вина (dikóvina): (small) wonder, a strange or unusual thing or phenomenon, gimmick
