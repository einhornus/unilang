смелый -> brave, bold
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic съмѣлъ (sŭmělŭ), from Proto-Slavic *sъmělъ synchronically, from сметь (smetʹ, “to dare”) +‎ -лый (-lyj). Compare Bulgarian сме́л (smel), Czech smělý, Polish śmiały.



Definitions (English):
  • courageous, daring, audacious, bold, brave
