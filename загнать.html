Etymology: за- (za-) +‎ гнать (gnatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to drive into
    • to hammer into
      • to herd into
        • (colloquial) to sell, to flog,
          • он загна́л своё пальто́ ― on zagnál svojó palʹtó ― he sold his coat
        • to exhaust, to fatigue to drive to exhaustion, to drive too hard (a horse)
