дека́брь (2308)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic декѧбрь (dekębrĭ) from Medieval Greek">Byzantine Greek Δεκέμβριος (Dekémbrios) from Latin december from decem from Proto-Indo-European *déḱm̥ (“ten”) + Latin -ber, from -bris, an adjectival suffix.

Pronunciation

Noun

  1. December
