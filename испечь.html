испе́чь (17212)


Etymology

ис- (is-) + печь (pečʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɪˈspʲet͡ɕ]

Verb

испе́чь • (ispéčʹ) pf (imperfective печь)

  1. to bake
  2. to scorch (said of the sun)

Related terms

  • спечь (spečʹ)
