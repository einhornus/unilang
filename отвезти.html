отвезти́ (4241)


Etymology

From от- (ot-) + везти́ (veztí, “to carry, transport”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtvʲɪˈsʲtʲi]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Homophone: отвести́ (otvestí)

Verb

отвезти́ • (otveztí) pf (imperfective отвози́ть)

  1. to take away (by vehicle), to drive away, to take (somebody or something by vehicle)
