влетть -> to fly in
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я влетю
Ты влетешь
Past: влетл/влетла



Etymology: в- (v-) +‎ лета́ть (letátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to fly into
    • (colloquial) to run into (to enter while running), to burst into
      • (colloquial, impersonal) to get into trouble,
        • ему́ влета́ет ― jemú vletájet ― he is getting into trouble
