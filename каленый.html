каленый (20496)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [kɐˈlʲɵnɨj]

Adjective[edit]

калёный • (kaljónyj)

  1. red-hot, tempered (of iron), made from tempered steel
    • 1899, A. V. Zheleznova, “Сон Стеньки Разина”, in Songs of the Ural Cossacks, 19th-century Russian folk song:
      Ой, разыпались калёныя стрѣлы / какъ по матушкѣ сыро́й землѣ."
      Oj, razýpalisʹ kaljónyja strěly / kak po mátuškě syrój zemlě."
      Ah, the tempered arrows were scattered / as on damp mother earth.
  2. roasted
    калёные оре́хи ― kaljónyje oréxi ― roasted nuts

Related terms[edit]

  • калильный (kalílʹnyj)
  • калить impf (kalítʹ)
