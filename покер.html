по́кер (25483)


Etymology

Borrowed from English poker.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpokʲɪr]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

по́кер • (póker) m inan (genitive по́кера, nominative plural по́керы, genitive plural по́керов, related adjective покерный)

  1. (card games) poker
  2. (card games) four cards of a kind in a poker hand
  3. (soccer) four goals scored by one player in a soccer match
