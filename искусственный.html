исксственный -> artificial
Adjective



Etymology: From иску́сство (iskússtvo) +‎ -енный (-ennyj).



Definitions (English):
  • artificial, man-made.
    • Свечи - иску́сственные исто́чники све́та. ― Svéči - iskússtvennyje istóčniki svéta. ― Candles are artificial light sources.
  • (dentistry) false (in terms of teeth)
    • imitation, fake
      • (figuratively, derogatory) unnatural, insincere, contrived
