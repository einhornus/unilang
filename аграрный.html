агра́рный (16311)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin agrarius (“agrarian”), from ager (“field, farm”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éǵros.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈɡrarnɨj]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

агра́рный • (agrárnyj)
