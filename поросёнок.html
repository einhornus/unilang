поросёнок -> piglet
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: порося́та
Genitive: поросёнка



Etymology: Proto-Slavic *porsę (“piglet”) + -онок/-ёнок (-ónok/-jónok, “diminutive suffix for baby animals”). Cognate with Serbo-Croatian prȃse, Czech prase, Slovak prasa, Polish prosię, Slovene prašič, Bulgarian прасе (prase).



Definitions (English):
  • piglet, young pig
