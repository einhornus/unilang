бутерброд -> (open) sandwich
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: бутерброды
Genitive: бутерброда



Etymology: Borrowed from German Butterbrot (“open sandwich, slice of bread with butter”, literally “butter bread”).
