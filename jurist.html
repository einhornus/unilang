jurist (16134)


Etymology

From Middle French juriste

Pronunciation

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˈdʒʊəɹ.ɪst/
  • (Us) IPA(key): /ˈdʒəɹ.ɪst/

Noun

jurist (plural jurists)

  1. (law) An expert of law or someone who researches jurisprudencé.
  2. (Us, Canada, law) A judge.
