боржо́ми (26654)


Etymology

Borrowed from Georgian ბორჯომი (borǯomi).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐrˈʐomʲɪ]

Noun

боржо́ми • (boržómi) f inan (indeclinable)

  1. Borjomi (a brand, well-known in the USSR, of naturally carbonated mineral water from Georgia (country)).
