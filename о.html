о -> about, concerning

Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *o(b), from Proto-Indo-European *h₃ebʰi.



Definitions (English):
  • The Cyrillic letter
    • О ( O), о (o).
  • The Roman letter O, o.
    • oh.
      • О бо́же! ― O bóže! ― Oh God!
    • (+ prepositional case) about, of, on.
      • Я чита́л о нём ― Ja čitál o njóm ― I’ve read about him.
    • (literary) (+ accusative case) against, upon,
      • опира́ться о сте́ну ― opirátʹsja o sténu ― to lean against the wall
