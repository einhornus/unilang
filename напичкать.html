напи́чкать (23947)


Etymology

на- (na-) + пи́чкать (píčkatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈpʲit͡ɕkətʲ]

Verb

напи́чкать • (napíčkatʹ) pf (imperfective пи́чкать)

  1. (colloquial) to overfeed (usually forcibly or against someone's desires)
  2. (colloquial, transitive) to overdose, to give too much medicine to (someone)
  3. (colloquial) to stuff, to cram (someone with useless or unnecessary knowledge or information)
  4. (colloquial) to oversupply (useless or unnecessary knowledge or information)

Related terms

  • пи́чканье (píčkanʹje), пи́чкание (píčkanije)
