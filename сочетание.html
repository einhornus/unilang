сочета́ние -> combination
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: сочета́ния
Genitive: сочета́ния



Etymology: сочета́ть (sočetátʹ) +‎ -ание (-ánije)



Definitions (English):
  • combination (one or more elements selected without regard of the order)
    • conjunction
