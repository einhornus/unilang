лёгкое -> lung
Noun, declension 0a
Plural: лёгкие
Genitive: лёгкого



Etymology: From the adjective лёгкий (ljóxkij, “light, easy”). Compare Proto-Indo-European *lengʷʰ- (“light, not heavy, agile, nimble”), with a sense of "the light organ" developing later. Compare Polish lekkie, English lung.
