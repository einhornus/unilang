водоём -> reservoir
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: водоёмы
Genitive: водоёма



Etymology: From вода́ (vodá) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ -ём (-jóm), combining form of имать (imatʹ, “to take, originally to have”) (compare 1sg. present емлю (jémlju)).



Definitions (English):
  • water reservoir, basin, pond, body of water
