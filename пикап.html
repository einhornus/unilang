пика́п (22569)


Etymology

Borrowed from English pickup.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪˈkap]

Noun

пика́п • (pikáp) m inan (genitive пика́па, nominative plural пика́пы, genitive plural пика́пов, diminutive пикапчик)

  1. pickup truck (truck with an open cargo bed)
  2. pickup (seduction technique, especially as practiced by pickup artists)
