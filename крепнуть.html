кре́пнуть (17233)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈkrʲepnʊtʲ]

Verb

кре́пнуть • (krépnutʹ) impf (perfective окре́пнуть)

  1. to grow stronger
  2. to get firmly established

Related terms

  • кре́пкий (krépkij)
