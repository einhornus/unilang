Noun[edit]

весть • (vestʹ) f inan (genitive вести, nominative plural вести, genitive plural весте́й)

  1. news, piece of news, message; tidings
    Марко отовсю́ду несёт ему́ весте́й ― Márko otovsjúdu nesjót jemú vestéj ― Marco brings him news from everywhere
  2. (in the plural) news (presentation of news, e.g. on television)
