весть -> piece of news
Noun, declension 3e (pattern ночь)

Plural: вести
Genitive: вести



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *věstь.



Definitions (English):
  • news, piece of news, message tidings.
    • Марко отовсю́ду несёт ему́ весте́й ― Márko otovsjúdu nesjót jemú vestéj ― Marco brings him news from everywhere
  • (in the plural) news (presentation of news, e.g. on television)
