два́дцать (405)


Etymology[edit]

From два (dva) +‎ -дцать (-dcatʹ), or "twice ten". This was an Old East Slavic [Term?] dual form.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈdvat͡s(ː)ɨtʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Numeral[edit]

два́дцать • (dvádcatʹ)

  1. twenty (20)

Usage notes[edit]

два́дцать (dvadcatʹ) in the nominative case and accusative case governs the genitive plural of the noun. In other cases, it governs the corresponding plural case of the noun.

  • два́дцать ма́льчиков ― dvádcatʹ málʹčikov ― twenty boys
  • с двадцатью ма́льчиками ― s dvadcatʹjú málʹčikami ― with twenty boys
  • ма́льчиков два́дцать ― málʹčikov dvádcatʹ ― approximately twenty boys
