светило -> luminary
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: свети́ла
Genitive: свети́ла



Etymology: свети́ть (svetítʹ) +‎ -ло (-lo)



Definitions (English):
  • heavenly body, celestial body, lamp,
    • дневно́е светило ― dnevnóje svetílo ― the star of day, ночны́е свети́ла ― nočnýje svetíla ― the moon and the stars the lamps of night
  • luminary, notability,
    • светило нау́ки ― svetílo naúki ― a luminary [leading light] in science
  • neuter singular past indicative imperfective of свети́ть (svetítʹ)
