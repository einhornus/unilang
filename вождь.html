вождь -> leader
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язк)

Plural: вожд
Genitive: вожд



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic вождь (voždĭ), from Proto-Slavic *voďь. Displaced inherited Old East Slavic вожь (vožĭ).



Definitions (English):
  • chief, chieftain (of a tribe)
    • leader (inspiring or commanding a movement)
      • (dated) captain (leading an army)
