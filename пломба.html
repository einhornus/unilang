пло́мба (24564)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Plombe.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈplombə]

Noun

пло́мба • (plómba) f inan (genitive пло́мбы, nominative plural пло́мбы, genitive plural пло́мб)

  1. lead, seal, stamp
  2. (medicine) filling, stopping, inlay, plug

Related terms

  • пломбировать (plombirovatʹ)
