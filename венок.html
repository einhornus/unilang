венок -> wreath, garland
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: венки́
Genitive: венка́



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic вѣнъкъ (věnŭkŭ), from Proto-Slavic *vĕnъkъ, from *vĕnъ (“wreath”) + *-ъkъ, details of further origin unclear (possibly from Proto-Indo-European *wóh₁y-nós or derived later) but ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weh₁y- (“to twist, wind, weave”).
