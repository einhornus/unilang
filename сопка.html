сопка -> knoll, mound, volcano
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: сопки
Genitive: сопки



Etymology: From соп (sop, “mound (dialectal)”) +‎ -ка (-ka), from Proto-Slavic *sъpъ, related to сы́пать (sýpatʹ, “to pour, to strew”). Per Vasmer, meaning "volcano" presumably influenced by сопе́ть (sopétʹ, “to wheeze”) Czech sopka (“volcano”), Slovak sopka (“volcano”) are borrowings from Russian.



Definitions (English):
  • knoll, hill, mound
    • volcano
