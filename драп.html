Noun[edit]

драп • (drap) m inan (genitive дра́па, nominative plural дра́пы, genitive plural дра́пов)

  1. thick woolen cloth

Noun[edit]

драп • (drap) m inan (genitive дра́па, nominative plural дра́пы, genitive plural дра́пов)

  1. (colloquial) fleeing, skedaddling, runner (as in “to do a runner”)
    дать дра́пу ― datʹ drápu ― to flee, to make a run for it

Noun[edit]

драп • (drap) m inan (genitive дра́па, nominative plural дра́пы, genitive plural дра́пов)

  1. (slang) marijuana

Noun[edit]
