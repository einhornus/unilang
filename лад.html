лад (23634)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lat]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -at

Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *ladъ, of unknown origin.

Noun

лад • (lad) m inan (genitive ла́да, nominative plural лады, genitive plural ладо́в)

  1. harmony, concord
  2. manner, way
  3. (music) mode; fret
Synonyms
  • (harmony, concord): согласие (soglásije), мир (mir)
  • (manner, way): спо́соб (spósob), метод (métod), приём (prijóm), путь (putʹ), сре́дство (srédstvo)

Etymology 2

Noun

лад • (lad) f anim pl

  1. genitive plural of ла́да (láda)
  2. accusative plural of ла́да (láda)
