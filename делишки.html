делишки (17797)


Etymology

дела (delá) +‎ -ишки (-íški)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪˈlʲiʂkʲɪ]

Noun

делишки • (delíški) n inan pl (genitive дели́шек, plural only)

  1. (colloquial) Diminutive of дела (delá): affairs, matters, things
    Как делишки?
    Kak delíški?
    How are you? How is it going?
