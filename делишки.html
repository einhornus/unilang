делишки (17799)


Etymology

дела́ (delá) + -и́шки (-íški)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [dʲɪˈlʲiʂkʲɪ]

Noun

дели́шки • (delíški) n inan pl (genitive дели́шек, plural only)

  1. (colloquial) Diminutive of дела́ (delá): affairs, matters, things
    Как дели́шки?
    Kak delíški?
    How are you? How is it going?
