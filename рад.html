рад -> glad (short adj)
Adjective



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *radъ.
  • Clipping of радиан (radian).


Definitions (English):
  • glad.
    • Бу́дем о́чень ра́ды ви́деть Вас на конфере́нции. Búdem óčenʹ rády vídetʹ Vas na konferéncii. We will be very glad to see you at the conference. (at the bottom of an invitation card)
  • rad (non-SI unit of absorbed radiation dose)
    • radian
      • genitive plural of ра́да (ráda)
