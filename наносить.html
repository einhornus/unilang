наноси́ть (6354)


Etymology

на- (na-) +‎ носи́ть (nosítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nənɐˈsʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

наноси́ть • (nanosítʹ) impf (perfective нанести́)

  1. to deposit, to pile up (sand or snow)

Related terms

  • нанос m (nanós)

Verb

наноси́ть • (nanosítʹ) pf (imperfective нанашивать)

  1. to bear, to carry (in some quantity, in several stages)
    наноси́ть дрова́ ― nanosítʹ drová ― to carry firewood (a bit at a time)
