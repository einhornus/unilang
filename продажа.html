продажа -> sale
Noun, declension 1a (pattern девочка)

Plural: продажи
Genitive: продажи



Etymology: From Old East Slavic прода́жа (prodaža), from Proto-Slavic *prodaďa.



Definitions (English):
  • sale, selling (exchange of goods or services for currency or credit)
    • на прода́жу, na prodážufor sale
