мо́лвить (8219)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *mъlviti, cognate to Polish mówić, Czech mluvit, Ukrainian розмовляти (rozmovljáty)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmolvʲɪtʲ]

Verb

мо́лвить • (mólvitʹ) impf (perfective промо́лвить)

  1. (archaic, poetic) to say, to declare, to quethe

Related terms

  • молва́ (molvá)
  • мол (mol)
