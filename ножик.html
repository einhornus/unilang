но́жик (8584)


Etymology[edit]

нож (nož) +‎ -ик (-ik)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈnoʐɨk]

Noun[edit]

но́жик • (nóžik) m inan (genitive но́жика, nominative plural но́жики, genitive plural но́жиков)

  1. diminutive of нож (nož): little knife
