капка́н -> trap
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: капка́ны
Genitive: капка́на



Etymology: From Turkic. Compare Crimean Tatar qapqan and Turkish kapan (“trap, snare”). Cognate with Georgian ხაფანგი (xapangi, “trap”).



Definitions (English):
  • trap, bear trap, leg trap (device designed to catch or kill animals)
