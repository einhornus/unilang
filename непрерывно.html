непреры́вно -> continuously

Etymology: непреры́вный (neprerývnyj) +‎ -о (-o)



Definitions (English):
  • incessantly (in a manner without pause or stop)
    • short neuter singular of непреры́вный (neprerývnyj)


      Definitions (Russian):
