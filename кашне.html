кашне́ (29610)


Etymology

Borrowed from French cache-nez.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɐʂˈnɛ] (phonetic respelling: кашнэ)

Noun

кашне́ • (kašnɛ) n inan (indeclinable)

  1. scarf, muffler

Synonyms

  • шарф (šarf)
