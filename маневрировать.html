маневри́ровать (21441)


Etymology

From French manœuvrer, probably through́ German manövrieren. Analyzable as манёвр (manjóvr) +‎ -ировать (-írovatʹ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mənʲɪˈvrʲirəvətʲ]

Verb

маневри́ровать • (manevrírovatʹ) impf (perfective сманеврировать)

  1. to manoeuvre, to maneuver
  2. (figuratively) to redistribute
  3. (figuratively) to manage, to use
  4. (rail transport) to shunt

Related terms

  • манёвр (manjóvr)
  • манёвренный (manjóvrennyj), манёвренность (manjóvrennostʹ)
