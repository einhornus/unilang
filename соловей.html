солове́й (8849)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic соловии (solovii), from Proto-Slavic *solvьjь, from *solvъ (“cream-colored”) +‎ *-ьjь, from Proto-Indo-European *saluo-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [səlɐˈvʲej]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

солове́й • (solovéj) m anim (genitive соловья́, nominative plural соловьи́, genitive plural соловьёв)

  1. nightingale
