соловей (8849)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic соловии (solovii), from Proto-Slavic *solvьjь, from *solvъ (“cream-colored”) +‎ *-ьjь, from Proto-Indo-European *saluo-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [səlɐˈvʲej]

Noun
