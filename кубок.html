кубок -> goblet, bowl
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: кубки
Genitive: кубка



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic кубъкъ (kubŭkŭ, “drinking vessel”) [14th century]. Logically куб (kub, “boiler, large tank”) +‎ -ок (-ok).



Definitions (English):
  • goblet, bowl, beaker
    • cup (a trophy in the shape of an oversized cup)
