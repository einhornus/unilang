отчи́зна (18332)


Etymology[edit]

From Old East Slavic отьчизна (otĭčizna, “(fore)fathers, lineage, patrimony”). Cognate to Ukrainian вітчизна (vitčýzna). Synchronically analysable as оте́ц (otéc) +‎ -изна (-izna).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ɐˈt͡ɕːiznə]

Noun[edit]

отчи́зна • (otčízna) f inan (genitive отчи́зны, nominative plural отчи́зны, genitive plural отчи́зн)

  1. fatherland, motherland, homeland, home country

Synonyms[edit]

  • оте́чество n (otéčestvo)
  • ро́дина n (rodina)
