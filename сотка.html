со́тка (19870)


Etymology

сто (sto) +‎ -ка (-ka)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈsotkə]

Noun

со́тка • (sótka) f inan (genitive сотки, nominative plural сотки, genitive plural со́ток)

  1. (colloquial) are, one hundred square metres
    огород/сад в пять со́ток
    ogoród/sad v pjatʹ sótok
    a garden (with an area) of five hundred square metres

Related terms

  • сто (sto)
  • со́тня (sótnja)
