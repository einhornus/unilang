сачо́к (12207)


Etymology

сак (sak) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sɐˈt͡ɕɵk]

Noun

сачо́к • (sačók) masculine gender">m anim or masculine gender">m inan (genitive сачка́, nominative plural сачки́, genitive plural сачко́в)

  1. (inanimate) butterfly net, hand net, net (for butterflies, insects or small fish)
  2. (animate, colloquial) loafer, idler, shirker

Related terms

  • сачковать (sačkovátʹ)
