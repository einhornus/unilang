ритм (4908)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin rhythmus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rʲitm]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ритм • (ritm) m inan (genitive ри́тма, nominative plural ри́тмы, genitive plural ри́тмов)

  1. rhythm (various senses)
  2. (physiology) heartbeat

Related terms

  • ритми́чный (ritmíčnyj), ритми́ческий (ritmíčeskij)
