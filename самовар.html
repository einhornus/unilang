самова́р -> samovar
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: самова́ры
Genitive: самова́ра



Etymology: From само- (samo-, “self-”) + a deverbal form of вари́ть (varítʹ, “to boil”).



Definitions (English):
  • samovar (a large coal- or woodfired metal urn with a spigot for boiling water for tea)
