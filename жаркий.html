жркий -> hot
Adjective


Definitions (English):
  • hot, make one feel hot (such as of the surrounding air or the sun)
    • жа́ркий во́здух ― žárkij vózdux ― hot air, жа́ркое со́лнце ― žárkoje sólnce ― the hot sun
  • having a hot climate, southern, tropical,
    • жа́ркие страны ― žárkije strány ― countries with a hot climate
  • fervent, warm, passionate
