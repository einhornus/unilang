теря́ться (5774)


Etymology

теря́ть (terjátʹ) + -ся (-sja)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tʲɪˈrʲat͡sːə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

теря́ться • (terjátʹsja) impf (perfective потеря́ться)

  1. to get lost, to disappear
  2. to lose one's way
  3. to lose one's presence of mind, to get flustered
  4. (imperfective only) to fail, to weaken (eyesight, hearing, memory, etc.)
  5. passive of теря́ть (terjátʹ)
