терться -> to get lost, panic
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: a

Я терюсь
Ты терешься
Past: терлся/терлась



Etymology: теря́ть (terjátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • to get lost, to disappear
    • to lose one's way
      • to lose one's presence of mind, to get flustered
        • (imperfective only) to fail, to weaken (eyesight, hearing, memory, etc.)
          • passive of теря́ть (terjátʹ)
