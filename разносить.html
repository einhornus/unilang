разноси́ть (9813)


Etymology

раз- (raz-) +‎ носи́ть (nosítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rəznɐˈsʲitʲ]

Verb

разноси́ть • (raznosítʹ) impf (perfective разнести́)

  1. to carry, to convey, to deliver
  2. (colloquial) (news, rumor etc) to spread around
  3. to enter, to book
  4. (impersonal, colloquial) to cause to swell
  5. to smash, to destroy
    разноси́ть в ще́пки (на куски́) ― raznosítʹ v ščépki (na kuskí) ― to break/smash to pieces
  6. (colloquial) to scatter, to disperse
  7. to give a rating/dressing-down, to dress down, to blow up

Verb

разноси́ть • (raznosítʹ) pf (imperfective разнашивать)

  1. to break in (shoes)

Related terms

  • разно́с (raznós)
  • разноска (raznóska)
  • разносный (raznósnyj)
  • разно́счик (raznósčik)

  • нести́ impf (nestí), понести́ pf (ponestí)
