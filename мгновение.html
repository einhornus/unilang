мгнове́ние -> moment
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: мгнове́ния
Genitive: мгнове́ния



Etymology: From Old East Slavic мьгновениѥ (mĭgnovenije), from Proto-Slavic *mьgnovenьje, from *mьgnǫti (“to blink”), from *migati (“to blink”).



Definitions (English):
  • moment, instant, twinkling
