просу́нуть (8692)


Etymology

про- (pro-) + су́нуть (súnutʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prɐˈsunʊtʲ]

Verb

просу́нуть • (prosúnutʹ) pf (imperfective просо́вывать)

  1. to put/push through/in, to force through, to shove through/in, to thrust through/in, to pass through/in

Related terms

  • су́нуть impf (súnutʹ), сова́ть pf (sovátʹ)
