вилок (6336)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vʲɪˈlok]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

вилок • (vilók) m inan or m anim (genitive вилка, nominative plural вилки, genitive plural вилко́в)

  1. (colloquial) head of cabbage
  2. (low colloquial, animate) weak, wretched man; pussy
