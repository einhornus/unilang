одолеть -> to overcome
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я одолею
Ты одолеешь
Past: одолел/одолела



Etymology: From Old East Slavic одолѣти (odolěti), from Proto-Slavic *odolěti.



Definitions (English):
  • to overcome, to overpower, to conquer,
    • одоле́ть врага́́ ― odolétʹ vragá ― to overpower the enemy
  • to seize, to overcome,
    • его́́ одоле́л сон ― jevó odolél son ― he was overcome by sleepiness
  • (colloquial) to cope, to manage,
    • он ника́к не одоле́ет э́ту́ кни́гу ― on nikák ne odoléjet étu knígu ― he cannot get through this book
