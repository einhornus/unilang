шшка -> sabre, (pl) draughts, checkers (board game)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern двочка)

Plural: шшки
Genitive: шшки



Etymologies:
  • шах (šax, “check”) +‎ -ка (-ka).
  • Кавка́зская ша́шка

        • (military) smoke grenade
          • (construction) paving block

              • (military, dated) rider, cavalryman (as a counting unit of military strength)
