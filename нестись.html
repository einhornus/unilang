нести́сь -> to rush
Verb, imperfective, intransitive, stress pattern: b/b

Я несу́сь
Ты несё́шься
Past: нёсся/несла́сь



Etymology: нести́ (nestí) +‎ -сь (-sʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to hurtle, to dash, to race, to tear, to scurry (to move rapidly, violently, or without control)
    • to come, (sound) to reach one's ears, to be heard,
      • из окна несё́тся за́пах гари, iz okná nesjótsja zápax gárithere's the smell of burning coming from the window
    • to lay eggs. Perfective:
      • снестись (snestísʹ)
    • passive of нести́ (nestí)
