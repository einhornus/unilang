смакова́ть (19085)


Etymology

смак (smak) +‎ -овать (-ovátʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [sməkɐˈvatʲ]

Verb

смакова́ть • (smakovátʹ) impf

  1. (colloquial, also figuratively) to relish, to savor

Related terms
