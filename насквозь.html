наскво́зь (4307)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐskˈvosʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

наскво́зь • (naskvózʹ)

  1. all the way through́, throughout́, completely
    Я наскво́зь промо́к.
    Ja naskvózʹ promók.
    I'm soaking wet. (male speaker)
    Я ви́жу её наскво́зь.
    Ja vížu jejó naskvózʹ.
    I see through́ her (i.e. know her true intentions).

Related terms

  • сквозь (skvozʹ)
