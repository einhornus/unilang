ка́ша -> kasha (porridge made of buckwheat, semolina or oatmeal)
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: ка́ши
Genitive: ка́ши



Etymology: From Old East Slavic ка́ша (kaša), from Proto-Slavic *kaša. Cognates include Slovak kaša and Serbo-Croatian kaša



Definitions (English):
  • porridge, gruel
    • mash, mush
      • (figuratively) muddle, mess, confusion (e.g.
        • ка́ша в голове́)
