ба́рхат -> velvet
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: ба́рхаты
Genitive: ба́рхата



Etymology: Borrowed from Middle High German barchât, from Medieval Latin barracanus, from Arabic بَرَّكَان‎ (barrakān, “a kind of black gown”).
