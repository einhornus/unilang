следопы́т (7706)


Etymology

след (sled) + -о- (-o-) + -пыт (-pyt), combining form of пыта́ть (pytátʹ, “(originally) to examine”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [s⁽ʲ⁾lʲɪdɐˈpɨt]

Noun

следопы́т • (sledopýt) m anim (genitive следопы́та, nominative plural следопы́ты, genitive plural следопы́тов)

  1. (hunting) tracker
  2. someone who searches historical materials for traces of past events
