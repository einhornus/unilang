мирско́й (18741)


Etymology

мир (mir) + -ско́й (-skój)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [mʲɪrˈskoj]

Adjective

мирско́й • (mirskój)

  1. (relational, dated) society, community
  2. (relational) world
  3. secular, worldly
  4. (relational, law) arbitration

Related terms

  • мир (mir)
  • мирозда́ние (mirozdánije)
