сда́вать -> to hand in, hand over, rent, let, surrender, give up, i: take (exam), p: to pass (exam)
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: b

Я сдаю
Ты сдаёшь
Past: сда́вал/сда́вала



Etymology: сдать (sdatʹ) +‎ -ва́ть (-vátʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to deliver, to hand in, to hand over
    • to return, to turn in,
      • сдава́ть ве́щи в бага́ж ― sdavátʹ véšči v bagáž ― to check/register one's luggage, сдава́ть дела́ ― sdavátʹ delá ― to turn over one's duties, сдава́ть внаём ― sdavátʹ vnajóm ― to let, to let out, to hire out, сдава́ть в аре́нду ― sdavátʹ v aréndu ― to lease, to grant on lease, to rent
    • (a fortress, city etc.) to surrender, to yield
      • (card games) to deal
        • to take/pass (an exam)
          • to be weakened, to be in a reduced state
