лях (18742)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic *ляхъ (*ljaxŭ), nominative plural ляси (ljasi), accusative plural ляхꙑ (ljaxy). See Polish Lach for more.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [lʲax]

Noun

лях • (ljax) m anim (genitive ля́ха, nominative plural ля́хи, genitive plural ля́хов, feminine ля́шка)

  1. (historical, now sometimes slightly derogatory or ironic) Pole, Polish man, Polack

Synonyms

  • (regular term) поля́к (polják)
