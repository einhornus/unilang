вольница (31074)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvolʲnʲɪt͡sə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

вольница • (vólʹnica) f inan (genitive вольницы, nominative plural вольницы, genitive plural вольниц)

  1. (historical) community of people who escaped the harsh conditions of serfdom and settled on the outskirts of Russia, e.g. Cossacks

Noun

вольница • (vólʹnica) m anim or f anim (genitive вольницы, nominative plural вольницы, genitive plural вольниц)

  1. (dated) strong-willed person
