вольница (31076)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvolʲnʲɪt͡sə]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

во́льница • (vólʹnica) f inan (genitive во́льницы, nominative plural во́льницы, genitive plural во́льниц)

  1. (historical) community of people who escaped the harsh conditions of serfdom and settled on the outskirts of Russia, e.g. Cossacks

Noun

во́льница • (vólʹnica) m anim or f anim (genitive во́льницы, nominative plural во́льницы, genitive plural во́льниц)

  1. (dated) strong-willed person
