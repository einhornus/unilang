плени́ть (19066)


Etymology

плен (plen) +‎ -ить (-ítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [plʲɪˈnʲitʲ]

Verb

плени́ть • (plenítʹ) pf (imperfective пленя́ть)

  1. (dated) to capture, to take prisoner, to deprive of liberty
  2. (dated) to conquer (a land, a country)
  3. to captivate, to fascinate, to charm

Related terms

  • плен (plen), пле́нный (plénnyj)
  • пле́нник (plénnik)
