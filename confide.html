confide


_v.
1 доверя́ть, поверя́ть кому-л. (in); полага́ться на кого-л. (in) You can confide in me, my dear, your affairs will be kept private.
2 вверять; поруча́ть (to) You had better confide your jewels to the bank in future. The boy was confided to the care of his aunt.
3 признаваться, сообща́ть по секре́ту (to) She confided her secret to her best friend. The firm's plans were confided only to the chairman and the directors. _Syn: entrust
