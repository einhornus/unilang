храбрый -> brave
Adjective



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic храбръ (xrabrŭ, “brave”), from Proto-Slavic *xorbrъ (“brave”). Displaced the native East Slavic form хоро́брый (xoróbryj, “brave”).



Definitions (English):
  • brave, courageous (strong in the face of fear)


    Definitions (Russian):
