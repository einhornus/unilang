Noun[edit]

сиг • (sig) m anim (genitive сига́, nominative plural сиги́, genitive plural сиго́в)

  1. Coregoninae cisco, whitefish (family)
  2. Coregonus vandesius, vendace (species)
  3. Coregonus lavaretus or Coregonus pennantii, gwyniad/gwuniad (species)
  4. Coregonus pollan, pollan (species)
