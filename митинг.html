митинг -> political meeting
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: митинги
Genitive: митинга



Etymology: Borrowed from English meeting.



Definitions (English):
  • (politics) rally, mass-meeting (a demonstration an event where people gather together to protest against a given cause or express solidarity)
    • (neologism) meeting (gathering among business people to discuss their business)
