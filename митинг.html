ми́тинг (4818)


Etymology

Borrowed from English meeting.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmʲitʲɪnk]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

ми́тинг • (míting) masculine gender">m inan (genitive ми́тинга, nominative plural ми́тинги, genitive plural ми́тингов)

  1. (politics) rally, mass-meeting (a demonstration; an event where people gather together to protest against́ a given cause or express solidarity)
  2. (neologism) meeting (gathering among business people to discuss their business)
