прейскура́нт (25298)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Preiskurant, from French prix courant.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [prʲɪjskʊˈrant]

Noun

прейскура́нт • (prejskuránt) m inan (genitive прейскура́нта, nominative plural прейскура́нты, genitive plural прейскура́нтов)

  1. price list
