вывозть -> to transport away, export
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: c

Я вывож
Ты вывзишь
Past: вывозл/вывозла



Etymologies:
  • From вы- (vý-) +‎ вози́ть (vozítʹ) by rule, вы- attracts the stress in perfective but not imperfective verbs.
  • See вывози́ть (vývozitʹ) above.


Definitions (English):
  • to take out (by vehicle), to remove,
    • вывози́ть на тачке (pejorative)vyvozítʹ na táčketo protest by removing someone in a wheelbarrow from an administrative post(literally, “to cart away in a wheelbarrow”)
  • to deliver,
    • вывози́ть в свет (dated, high-style)vyvozítʹ v svetto introduce (young girls) to the community(literally, “to deliver into the light”)
  • to export
    • to bring back
      • (colloquial) to rescue, to save
        • (colloquial) to soil, to bedraggle
