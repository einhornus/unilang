водоем (10883)


Etymology

From вода́ (vodá) +‎ -о- (-o-) +‎ -ём (-jóm), combining form of имать (imatʹ, “to take, originally to have”) (compare 1sg. present емлю (jémlju)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [vədɐˈjɵm]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

водоём • (vodojóm) m inan (genitive водоёма, nominative plural водоёмы, genitive plural водоёмов)

  1. water reservoir, basin, pond, body of water
