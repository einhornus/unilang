печа́тать (4389)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪˈt͡ɕatətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

печа́тать • (pečátatʹ) impf (perfective напеча́тать or распеча́тать)

  1. to print (to copy something on a surface, especially by machine)
    Perfective: распеча́тать (raspečátatʹ) (alsó, especially on a printer)
  2. to type (to use a typewriter or to enter text or commands intó a computer using a keyboard)

Related terms

  • печа́ть (pečátʹ)
  • печа́тный (pečátnyj)
