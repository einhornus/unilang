куку́шка -> cuckoo
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: куку́шки
Genitive: куку́шки



Etymology: Onomatopoeic from ку-ку (ku-kú) +‎ -шка (-ška).



Definitions (English):
  • cuckoo,
    • куку́шка кукует: ку-ку, ку-ку! ― kukúška kukújet: ku-kú, ku-kú! ― A cuckoo cuckoos!
  • (railroad) cuckoo (a term used for the old «
    • Ку» series locomotives)
  • (railroad) cuckoo (a term for a suburban light-rail with several cars)
    • (military) sniper whose firing position is located in a tree
