сжатие (23401)


Etymology

сжать (sžatʹ) +‎ -тие (-tije)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʐːatʲɪje]

Noun

сжатие • (sžátije) n inan (genitive сжа́тия, nominative plural сжа́тия, genitive plural сжа́тий)

  1. contraction (reduction in size or activity)
  2. compression, pressing

Related terms

  • сжима́ть impf (sžimátʹ), сжать pf (sžatʹ)
