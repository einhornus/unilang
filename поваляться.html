поваля́ться -> to roll, lie about

Etymology: повалять (povaljátʹ) +‎ -ся (-sja)



Definitions (English):
  • (colloquial) to spend some time rolling about or wallowing.
    • Дава́й поваляемся в сне́гу! ― Daváj povaljájemsja v snegú! ― Let's roll in the snow!
  • (colloquial) to spend some time lying about (of a person — idly or due to illness of objects — due to being carelessly strewn)
    • passive of повалять (povaljátʹ)
