худжник -> artist
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мльчик)

Plural: худжники
Genitive: худжника



Etymology: From Old East Slavic художьникъ (xudožĭnikŭ, “artist, painter, master”), from худогъ (xudogŭ, “skillful, knacky experienced versed, adept, conversant lucky”), from Proto-Slavic *xǫdogъ, from a reflex of Proto-Germanic *handugaz.



Definitions (English):
  • artist, painter
