батаре́я -> battery, radiator
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: батаре́и
Genitive: батаре́и



Etymologies:
  • From French batterie, possibly via German Batterie.
  • Borrowed from Russian батаре́я (bataréja), from French batterie.

  • (electrical) battery
