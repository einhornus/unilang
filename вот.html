вот -> here, there (pointing)

Etymology: From Old East Slavic ото (oto) or вото (voto), compare with Polish oto.



Definitions (English):
  • here (is), this (is)
    • Вот мой дом. ― Vot moj dom. ― Here/this is my house.
  • there.
    • Вот тебе́ раз! ― Vot tebé raz! ― There you are! Вот и всё! ― Vot i vsjó! ― That’s all! Вот челове́к! ― Vot čelovék! ― What a man!вот где ― vót gde ― here’s where, вот-вот ― vot-vót ― just about to on the point of
  • here, look (at it)
    • take it! (when offering something, casual)
