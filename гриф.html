гриф (12826)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɡrʲif]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

гриф • (grif) m anim or m inan (genitive гри́фа, nominative plural гри́фы, genitive plural гри́фов)

  1. vulture (bird)
  2. (mythology) griffin, gryphon
  3. fingerboard, neck (the extension of any stringed instrument on which a fingerboard is mounted)
  4. signature stamp
