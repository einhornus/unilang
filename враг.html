враг -> enemy
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язык)

Plural: враги
Genitive: врага



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic врагъ (vragŭ), from Proto-Slavic *vorgъ. Compare the inherited doublet во́рог (vórog, “enemy, foe”) (poetic).



Definitions (English):
  • enemy, foe
