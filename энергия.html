эне́ргия -> energy
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: эне́ргии
Genitive: эне́ргии



Etymology: Borrowed from German Energie, from Latin energīa, from Ancient Greek ἐνέργεια (enérgeia).



Definitions (English):
  • energy, power
    • vitality
      • vigor
