расти́ть (5521)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɐˈsʲtʲitʲ]

Verb

расти́ть • (rastítʹ) impf (perfective вы́растить)

  1. to grow, to raise, to cultivate
  2. to breed, to rear, to raise
  3. to bring up
  4. to train, to prepare, to form

Related terms

  • расти́ (rastí)
  • рост (rost)
