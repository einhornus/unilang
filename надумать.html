наду́мать (10097)


Etymology

на- (na-) + ду́мать (dúmatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [nɐˈdumətʲ]

Verb

наду́мать • (nadúmatʹ) pf

  1. (colloquial) to make up one's mind, to decide
  2. (colloquial) to devise, to invent
