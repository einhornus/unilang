честь -> honour
Noun, declension 3a (pattern жизнь)

Plural: чести
Genitive: чести



Etymologies:
  • From Proto-Slavic *čьstь, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kʷit-tis.
  • From Proto-Slavic *čьsti (“to read, to count”).


Definitions (English):
  • honor
    • regard, respect
      • (obsolete) to read
        • (obsolete) to consider, to think, to suppose
