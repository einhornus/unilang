а́лый -> scarlet
Adjective



Etymology: From Old East Slavic алъ (alŭ, “bright red crimson”), from a Turkic source - compare Bashkir а́л (al, “bright red”), Turkish al (“crimson”).



Definitions (English):
  • crimson, scarlet, vermilion
    • reddish, blood-red, ruby-colored
