левый -> left, left-wing
Adjective



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic лѣвъ (lěvŭ, “left, wrong, evil”), from Proto-Slavic *lěvъ, Proto-Indo-European *leh₂iwo-. Cognate to Latin laevus, Greek λαιός (laiós)



Definitions (English):
  • left, left-hand
    • (nautical) port
      • left-wing, leftist
        • counterfeit
          • (slang) wrong, strange, unsuitable, impertinent
            • leftist, one who believes in the political left
