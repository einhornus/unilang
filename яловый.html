я́ловый (23513)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic ꙗловыи (jalovyi), from Proto-Slavic *jalovъ (“barren, infertile”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈjaləvɨj]

Adjective

я́ловый • (jálovyj) (no comparative)

  1. barren (of animals)
  2. made of the hide of a young calf
