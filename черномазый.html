чернома́зый (24234)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [t͡ɕɪrnɐˈmazɨj]

Noun

чернома́зый • (černomázyj) m anim (genitive чернома́зого, nominative plural чернома́зые, genitive plural чернома́зых, feminine чернома́зая)

  1. (offensive, ethnic slur) wog, nigger (an offensive term for a dark-skinned person, an immigrant from Central Asia or the Caucasus)

Adjective

чернома́зый • (černomázyj)

  1. (offensive, ethnic slur) Of or related to dark-skinned people, immigrants from Central Asia or the Caucasus
