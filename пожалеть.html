пожалть -> to pity, feel sorry for, (o +pr) regret
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я пожалю

Past: пожалл/пожалла



Etymology: по- (po-) +‎ жале́ть (žalétʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to feel sorry, to be sorry, to be sad (about something), to regret
    • to pity, to commiserate
      • to save, to spare (e.g. time, energy)
