е -> her
Definitions (English):
  • (possessive) her, its.
    • Где её кни́га? ― Gde jejó kníga? ― Where is her book? Я уже́ ви́дел её кни́гу. ― Ja užé vídel jejó knígu. ― I have already seen her book.
  • accusative of она́ (oná): her, it.
    • Он её ви́дит. ― On jejó vídit. ― He can see her.
  • genitive of она́ (oná): of her, of it
