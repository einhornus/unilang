валькирия (28079)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Norse valkyrja.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [vɐlʲˈkʲirʲɪjə]

Noun[edit]

валькирия • (valʹkírija) f anim (genitive вальки́рии, nominative plural вальки́рии, genitive plural вальки́рий)

  1. Valkyrie
