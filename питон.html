пито́н (10615)


Etymology

Borrowed from Ancient Greek Πύθων (Púthōn).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pʲɪˈton]

Noun

пито́н • (pitón) m anim (genitive пито́на, nominative plural пито́ны, genitive plural пито́нов)

  1. python (constricting snake)
