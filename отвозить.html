отвози́ть (15880)


Etymology

от- (ot-) +‎ вози́ть (vozítʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ɐtvɐˈzʲitʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

отвози́ть • (otvozítʹ) impf (perfective отвезти́)

  1. to take away (by vehicle), to drive away, to take (somebody or something by vehicle)
