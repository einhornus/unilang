о́ткуп (27721)


Etymology

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈotkʊp]

Noun

о́ткуп • (ótkup) masculine gender">m inan (genitive откупа, nominative plural откупа, genitive plural откупо́в)

  1. (historical, economics) tax farming
  2. lease
  3. payoff

Related terms
