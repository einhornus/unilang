полог (15535)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *pologъ. Composed of по- (po-) + a nominalized form of the verb лежа́ть (ležátʹ, “to lie”), with typical Indo-European nominal o-ablaut.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpolək]

Noun

полог • (pólog) masculine gender">m inan (genitive полога, nominative plural пологи, genitive plural по́логов)

  1. canopy
Related terms
  • по- (po-)
  • ложить (ložítʹ)
  • лежа́ть (ležátʹ)
