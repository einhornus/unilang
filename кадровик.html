Noun[edit]

кадрови́к • (kadrovík) m anim (genitive кадровика́, nominative plural кадровики́, genitive plural кадровико́в)

  1. (colloquial, military) regular officer
  2. (colloquial) member of a company's personnel, staff worker
  3. (colloquial) human resources officer
  4. (colloquial) member of the permanent staff

Related terms[edit]

  • кадр (kadr), ка́дры (kádry), ка́дровый (kádrovyj)
