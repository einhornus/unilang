вя́жущий (26805)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʲaʐʊɕːɪj]

Participle

вя́жущий • (vjážuščij)

  1. present active imperfective participle of вяза́ть (vjazátʹ, “to knit, to bind”): knitting, binding
    вя́жущие материа́лы ― vjážuščije materiály ― binding/cementing materials

Adjective

вя́жущий • (vjážuščij)

  1. astringent (having the effect of drawing tissue together)
