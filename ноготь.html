но́готь (2895)


Etymology

From Proto-Slavic *nogъtь, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₃nogʰ-, *h₃nogʷʰ-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈnoɡətʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

но́готь • (nógotʹ) m inan (genitive но́гтя, nominative plural но́гти, genitive plural ногте́й)

  1. nail (of finger or toe)
