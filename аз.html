аз (13235)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [as]

Etymology 1

From the pronoun with the same spelling, see beloẃ.

Noun

аз • (az) masculine gender">m inan (genitive аза́, nominative plural азы́, genitive plural азо́в)

  1. (archaic) name of the letter А (A)
  2. (plural only) basics, fundamentals

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic азъ (azŭ), Proto-Slavic *(j)azъ

Pronoun

аз • (az)
