Etymology

Unknown. Cognates include Ukrainian жабри (žábry), Belarusian жебры (žjébry), Bulgarian жабри (žábri), Czech žábra, Slovak žiabra, but a Proto-Slavic form hasn't been reconstructed.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈʐabrə]

Noun

жа́бра • (žábra) f inan (genitive жа́бры, nominative plural жа́бры, genitive plural жа́бр)
