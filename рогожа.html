рого́жа -> matting
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: рого́жи
Genitive: рого́жи



Etymology: Inherited from Old East Slavic рого́жа (rogoža), from Proto-Slavic *rogozia, from *rogozъ (“reed, rush”).



Definitions (English):
  • matting, burlap (coarse fabric)


    Definitions (Russian):
    • гру́бая хозя́йственная ткань из мочала
