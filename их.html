их -> their
Definitions (English):
  • (possessive) their.
    • Где их кни́га? ― Gde ix kníga? ― Where is their book? Я уже́ ви́дел их кни́гу. ― Ja užé vídel ix knígu. ― I've already seen their book.
  • accusative of они (oní): them.
    • Он их ви́дит. ― On ix vídit. ― He sees them.
  • genitive of они (oní) they, of them.
    • В до́ме их нет. ― V dóme ix net. ― They are not in the house.


Definitions (Russian):
