чу́вство -> feeling
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ле́то)

Plural: чу́вства
Genitive: чу́вства



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *čuvьstvo, from *čuti (“to sense, feel, notice”) + *-ьstvo. Cognate to чу́ять (čújatʹ), чутьё (čutʹjó).



Definitions (English):
  • sense, feeling (sensation)
    • feeling, emotion
