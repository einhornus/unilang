страна́ -> country
Noun, declension 1d (pattern страна́)

Plural: страны
Genitive: страны



Etymology: Borrowed from Old Church Slavonic страна́ (strana), from Proto-Slavic *storna. Compare the regular East-Slavic reflex with pleophony сторона́ (storoná).
