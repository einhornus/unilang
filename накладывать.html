накла́дывать -> to lay sth on sth, put on, superimpose
Verb, imperfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я накла́дываю
Ты накла́дываешь
Past: накла́дывал/накла́дывала



Etymology: на- (na-) +‎ -кладывать (-kládyvatʹ)



Definitions (English):
  • to lay (something) on/over (something else), to superimpose
    • to apply (bandage, face cream, etc.)
      • to pack, to load (a fair amount of something)
        • to put in (stitches)
