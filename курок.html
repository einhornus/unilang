курок (11469)


Etymology

кур (kur) +‎ -ок (-ok)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kʊˈrok]

Noun

курок • (kurók) masculine gender">m inan (genitive курка́, nominative plural курки́, genitive plural курко́в)

  1. cock, cocking piece (hammer of a firearm trigger mechanism)
  2. (colloquial, proscribed) trigger (a finger-operated lever used to fire a gun)
