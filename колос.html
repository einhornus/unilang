ко́лос -> ear (of corn)
Noun, declension 2a (pattern ма́льчик)

Plural: коло́сья
Genitive: ко́лоса



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *kȏlsъ, from Proto-Indo-European *kolH-so-. Related to коло́ть (kolótʹ).



Definitions (English):
  • ear (fruiting body of a grain plant)
