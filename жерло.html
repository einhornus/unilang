жерло́ -> mouth, muzzle
Noun, declension 2d
Plural: жерла
Genitive: жерла



Etymology: From Old East Slavic жьрло (žĭrlo), from Proto-Slavic gerlo.



Definitions (English):
  • (geology) crater, orifice, vent
    • narrow and deep aperture
      • (military) muzzle
