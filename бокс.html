бокс (6107)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [boks]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

бокс • (boks) masculine gender">m inan (genitive бо́кса, nominative plural бо́ксы, genitive plural бо́ксов)

  1. (sports) boxing
  2. (medicine) box (isolation ward in a hospital)
  3. box cut (men’s flattop hairstyle in which the hair at the temples and nape of the neck is shaved off)
  4. small box (such as for computer disks)

Related terms

  • боксёр (boksjór)
  • боксировать (boksírovatʹ)
  • полубокс (poluboks)
