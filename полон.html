полон (7306)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Slavic *pelnъ. Doublet of плен (plen), a borrowing from Old Church Slavonic плѣнъ (plěnŭ).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [pɐˈlon]

Noun

поло́н • (polón) m inan (genitive поло́на, nominative plural поло́ны, genitive plural поло́нов)

  1. (archaic, poetic) Alternative form of плен (plen)

Etymology 2

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpolən]

Adjective

по́лон • (pólon)

  1. short masculine singular of по́лный (pólnyj)
