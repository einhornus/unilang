амбар -> barn, storehouse
Noun, declension 2a (pattern мальчик)

Plural: амбары
Genitive: амбара



Etymology: Via Turkic (compare Tatar амба́р (ambar)) from Persian انبار‎ (ambâr).



Definitions (English):
  • barn, granary, warehouse, storehouse
