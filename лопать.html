Verb

ло́пать • (lópatʹ) impf (perfective сло́пать)

  1. (low colloquial) to devour, to gobble

Verb

ло́пать • (lópatʹ) impf (perfective ло́пнуть)

  1. (regional) Alternative form of ло́паться (lópatʹsja)
  2. (regional) to cause to burst
