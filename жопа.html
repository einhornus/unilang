жо́па (6517)


Etymology[edit]

Uncertain, presumed to derive from a Proto-Slavic form which cannot be easily reconstructed. According to Vasmer, two probable hypotheses are:

  • Cognate with Polish gap (“rubbernecker”) and gapić się (“to stare”)
  • Cognate with Old Church Slavonic жупа (župa, “hole”) and Ukrainian жупа (žúpa, “salt mine”)

Noun[edit]

жо́па • (žópa) f inan (genitive жо́пы, nominative plural жо́пы, genitive plural жоп, related adjective жопный, diminutive жопка)

  1. (vulgar) ass, arse (the buttocks of a person or animal)
    Подви́нь жо́пу! ― Podvínʹ žópu! ― Move your ass!
  2. (vulgar, figuratively) middle of nowhere, Bumfuck
    У него́ дом в какой-то жо́пе. ― U nevó dom v kakój-to žópe. ― His house is someplace in the middle of nowhere.
  3. (vulgar, figuratively) shit (a difficult situation)
    попа́сть в глубо́кую жо́пу ― popástʹ v glubókuju žópu ― to be

Synonyms[edit]

  • (ass): за́дница (zádnica), (vulgar) срака (sráka), (slightly less vulgar) зад (zad), (not vulgar) попа́ (pópa), гузно (gúzno), а́нус (ánus), (vulgar) пердалище (perdališče), пердак (perdák)
  • (middle of nowhere): дыра́ (dyrá), (highly vulgar) ебеня (jebenjá), (vulgar) Мухосранск (Muxosránsk)
  • (a difficult situation): (highly vulgar) пиздец (pizdéc)
