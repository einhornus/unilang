Verb[edit]

поджа́ть • (podžátʹ) pf (imperfective поджима́ть)

  1. to tuck up
    сиде́ть, поджа́в ноги ― sidétʹ, podžáv nogi ― to sit with one's legs tucked up beneath one
    поджа́ть хвост ― podžátʹ xvost ― to have one's tail between one's legs
    поджа́ть губы ― podžátʹ guby ― to purse one's lips, press up one's lips
  2. to tuck in
    подожми́ живо́т ― podožmi živót ― to tuck in your stomach
  3. (colloquial) to press
    ло́дку поджа́ло к берегу ― lódku podžálo k beregu ― the boat was pressed/pushed close to the bank
  4. (colloquial) to tighten
  5. to put/exert pressure (on), to press, to put the screws (on)

Verb[edit]

поджа́ть • (podžátʹ) pf (imperfective поджинать)

  1. (colloquial) to harvest further
  2. (colloquial) to harvest what remains
