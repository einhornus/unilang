зелень (3822)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈzʲelʲɪnʲ]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: зе‧лень

Noun

зелень • (zélenʹ) f inan (genitive зе́лени, nominative plural зе́лени, genitive plural зеленей)

  1. verdure (greenness, vegetation)
  2. greens; vegetables, edible grass (i. e. dill, parsley, etc.)
  3. (colloquial) dollars, greenbacks
