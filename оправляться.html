Verb[edit]

оправля́ться • (opravljátʹsja) impf (perfective опра́виться)

  1. to recover, to pick up
  2. (clothes) to put in order
  3. (colloquial) to ease oneself, to do the deed
  4. passive of оправля́ть (opravljátʹ)
