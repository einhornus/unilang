близне́ц -> twin
Noun, declension 2b (pattern язы́к)

Plural: близнецы́
Genitive: близнеца́



Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *bliznьcь. Compare Bulgarian близнак (bliznák), Polish bliźniak, Serbo-Croatian blizanac.



Definitions (English):
