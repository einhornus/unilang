бачо́к (12110)


Etymology

бак (bak) +‎ -ок (-ók)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bɐˈt͡ɕɵk]

Noun

бачо́к • (bačók) m inan (genitive бачка́, nominative plural бачки́, genitive plural бачко́в)

  1. diminutive of бак (bak): a (small) cistern; a (small) tank
