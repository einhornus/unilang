кно́пка -> button, knob, drawing-pin
Noun, declension 1a (pattern де́вочка)

Plural: кно́пки
Genitive: кно́пки



Etymology: Borrowed from German Knopf.



Definitions (English):
  • button (a mechanical device meant to be pressed with a finger)
    • drawing pin, thumbtack
      • press button, press stud, snap fastener
