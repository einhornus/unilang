тони́ровать (29899)


Etymology

тон (ton) +‎ -ировать (-írovatʹ)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [tɐˈnʲirəvətʲ]

Verb

тони́ровать • (tonírovatʹ) impf or pf

  1. to reproduce the full sound of (a musical or spoken text)
  2. to decorate, to tint, to tone (to touch up, giving a desired color or tint)
  3. (dated) to set the tone, to prescribe the manner of behavior

Related terms

  • тон (ton), тоновый (tónovyj), тоновой (tonovój)
