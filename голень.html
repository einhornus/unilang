го́лень (16265)


Etymology

From Old East Slavic голѣнь (golěnĭ), го́лень (golenĭ), from Proto-Slavic *golěnь / *golenь (“shin”), from *golъ (“naked”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈɡolʲɪnʲ]

Noun

го́лень • (gólenʹ) f inan (genitive го́лени, nominative plural го́лени, genitive plural го́леней, diminutive голя́шка)

  1. (anatomy) shin, shank

Synonyms

  • лы́сто (lýsto)
