му́льтик (30522)


Etymology

From a clipping of мультфи́льм (mulʹtfílʹm) +‎ -ик (-ik).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈmulʲtʲɪk]

Noun

му́льтик • (múlʹtik) m inan (genitive му́льтика, nominative plural му́льтики, genitive plural му́льтиков)

  1. (colloquial or childish) animated cartoon, animated film
