Etymology: From Proto-Slavic *plestì see there for cognates.



Definitions (English):
  • to braid, to plait
    • (figuratively, derogatory) to weave, to spin,
      • плести́́ интри́ги ― plestí intrígi ― to spin intrigues
    • (colloquial, derogatory) to chatter, to tell nonsense, to lie
