миновать -> to pass, pass by
Verb, perfective, transitive, stress pattern: a

Я ми́ную
Ты ми́нуешь
Past: миновал/миновала



Etymology: From Old East Slavic миновати (minovati), from Proto-Slavic *minovati.



Definitions (English):
  • to pass, to elapse (of time)
    • to go by, to go past (without any affect)
      • Чему́́ быть, того́́ не минова́ть.Čemú bytʹ, tovó ne minovátʹ. What must be, must be What happens, happens.
    • (colloquial or dated) to turn (an age)
      • (chiefly in the negative) to evade
        • (intransitive) to end, to finish
